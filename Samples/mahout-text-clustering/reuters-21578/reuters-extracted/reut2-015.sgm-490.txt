ROPAK <ROPK> FORMS JAPANESE UNIT

Ropak corp said it has formed a new Tokyo-based subsidiary called Ropak Nippon Ltd to market its North American products in Japan.     It said the new unit has started importing plastic pans and other products for the packaging of seafood and will also market rigid-plastic shipping pails for a variety of packaging uses.
