HEXCEL CORP <HXL> PLANS NEW PLANT

Hexcel Corp said it plans to build a manufacturing facility in Chandler, Arizona.     The company said construction will begin this summer, with completion scheduled for early in 1988.     The facility will produce engineered and structural products and employ about 200 people, it said.  Reuter &#3;
