MERCK <MRK> COMBINATION HEART DRUG AVAILABLE

Merck and Co Inc said a combination heart drug is available by prescription in the U.S.     The drug is Vaseretic, a once-a-day medication combining an angiotensin converting enzyme inhibitor (ACE) and a diuretic to treat high blood pressure. The inhibitor is a new class of antihypertensive drugs. Last year Merck introduced Vasotec, an ACE inhibitor.     The new drug combined Vasotec and HydroDiuril, a frequently prescribed diuretic.
