ERICSSON <ERICY> WINS ORDER FROM WEST GERMANY

L.M. Ericsson Telephone Co of Sweden said it was awarded a 7.5 mln dlr contract to supply its Alfaskop computer terminals to the West German Bundespost, West Germany's telecommunications and postal administration.     Ericsson said the contract covers 2,000 terminals, which will be used by West German post offices.  Reuter &#3;
