RJR NABISCO <RJR> TO BUY FIVE MLN OF ITS SHARES

RJR Nabisco Inc said it will purchase up to five mln of its outstanding common shares.     The company said purchases under the ongoing program will be made on the open market or in negotiated transactions. It now has about 250.3 mln shares outstanding.  Reuter &#3;
