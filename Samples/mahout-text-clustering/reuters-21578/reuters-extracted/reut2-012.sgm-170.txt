AUSIMONT COMPO <AUS> SELLS UNIT

Ausimont Compo NV said it has sold the assets of its Equipment and Machinery Division for about six mln dlrs to an investment group including the unit's management, resulting in a modest pretax gain that will be included in first quarter results.     The unit had sales of five mln dlrs in 1986.  Reuter &#3;
