VISTA CHEMICAL <VC> IN PUBLIC OFFERING

Vista Chemical Co said it plans to file a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission in early covering the sale of about 4.5 mln shares of its common stock to the public.     The shares will be sold by institutional investors who partially financed the company upon its formation in 1984 and by the trustee of its savings and investment plan on behalf of employee participants.     None of the shares will be sold by the company, it said.  Reuter &#3;
