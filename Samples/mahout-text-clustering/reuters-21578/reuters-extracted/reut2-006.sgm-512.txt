CEREALS MCAS TO BE UNCHANGED NEXT WEEK

Monetary compensatory amounts (mcas) for cereals will be unchanged for the week starting March 23, European Commission officials said.     Cereals mcas are plus 2.4 points for West Germany and the Netherlands, minus two points for Denmark, minus eight points for France, minus nine points for Ireland, minus 5.7 points for Italy, minus 25.7 points for Britain, minus 44.1 points for Greece and minus 10.5 points for Spain.
