FORD <F> EXTENDS INCENTIVES ON SOME MODELS

Ford Motor Co said it extended its current buyer incentive program on selected cars and light trucks to April Six.     It also said it added the Ford Taurus and Mercury Sable to the program which gives customers a choice of 3.9- to 9.9-annual percentage rate financing.     Ford said buyers of all 1986 and 1987 Taurus and Sable models can choose between the low financing rates or cash allowances of 400 dlrs.     Buyers of all other eligible cars will get a cash allowance of up to 600 dlrs, plus the low financing rates. It said those buying trucks can choose between the low financing or a cash rebate of up to 600 dlrs.     Ford said the program takes effect now and applies to most vehicles delivered to retail customers from dealer stock. A program for Mustang ends March 31 as announced earlier.  Reuter &#3;
