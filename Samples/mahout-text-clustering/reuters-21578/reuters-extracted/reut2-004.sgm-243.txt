CONSUMERS POWER<CMS> TO REDEEM PREFERRED STOCK

Consumers Power Co said it will redeem 200,000 shares of its 3.85 dlr preference stock at 27.50 dlrs a share plus accrued dividends of 0.320833 dlrs a share.     It said the redemption will be made May One to holders of record March 23, with shares selected by random lot.     It said some 100,000 of the shares are being redeemed under mandatory sinking fund provisions. The balance will be redeemed at the company's option as part of its efforts to reduce its cost of capital.  Reuter &#3;
