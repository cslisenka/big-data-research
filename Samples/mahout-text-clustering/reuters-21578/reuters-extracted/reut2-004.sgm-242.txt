VISHAY INTERTECHNOLOGY <VSH> CLARIFIES OFFER

Vishay Intertechnology Inc said it wished to clarify its pending offer to exchange shares of its Class B stock for its outstanding common stock     The company said shareholders who already have or plan to take advantage of the offer, may withdraw their offer by the close of business March 25, 1987.
