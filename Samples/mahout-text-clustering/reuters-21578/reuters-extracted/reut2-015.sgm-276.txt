CORVUS SYSTEMS <CRVS> NAMES NEW PRESIDENT

Corvus Systems Inc said it named Lewis Lipton its president and chief operating officer.     Lipton had been consulting for Corvus for the past nine months and prior to that was the chief executive officer of Trimedia Corp.     He reports to James Siehl, who had been acting as president and continues as chief executive officer. Siehl was also named Chairman of the Board, which he had also been acting as.  Reuter &#3;
