SYNERGY GROUP SELLS SUBORDINATED NOTES

Synergy Group is offering 85 mln dlrs of senior subordinated notes due 1997 with an 11-5/8 coupon and par pricing, said sole manager L.F. Rothschild, Unterberg, Towbin Inc.     Non-redeemable for five years, the notes are rated B-3 by Moody's Investors Service Inc and B-plus by Standard and Poor's Corp.  The issue was increased from an initial offering of 75 mln dlrs.
