BAXTER <BAX> SETS BRIEFING FOR NEW PRODUCT

Baxter Travenol Laboratories Inc said it will hold a press conference June 29 to introduce a new information systems product that will "dramatically improve hospital efficiency."     No other details were disclosed.     Baxter currently offers computerized information systems that link hospital medical and business departments and doctors' offices.  Reuter &#3;
