U.S. ENERGY SECRETARY IN MOROCCO AFTER GULF TOUR

U.S. Energy Secretary John Herrington arrived in Rabat on Monday after a tour of the Gulf and the Middle East.     Herrington, who is scheduled to meet King Hassan during his 24-hour official visit, said on arrival he would discuss regional and international issues with Moroccan leaders.     Morocco is the last stop of a tour that has taken Herrington to Iraq, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, Saudi Arabia and Egypt.  REUTER &#3;
