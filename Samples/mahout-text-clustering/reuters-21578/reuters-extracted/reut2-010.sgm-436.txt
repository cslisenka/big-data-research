LONE STAR INDUSTRIES <LCE> PROMISARY NOTES

Lone Star Industries Inc said it has arranged 150 mln dlrs of financing through the issuance of promisary notes to a consortium of 22 lenders.     Lone Star said the 8.75 pct notes will mature on March 26, 1992. The proceeds will be used to refinance and retire debt, and other general corporate purposes, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
