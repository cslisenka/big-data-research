BRANIFF INC <BAIR.O> MAY TRAFFIC ROSE

Braniff Inc  aid its May load factor rose to 59.15 pct from 57.16 pct. Revenue passenger miles rose 25.7 pct to 268.9 mln miles from 213.8 mln miles and available seat miles rose 21.5 pct to 454.5 mln miles from 374.1 mln miles.     Year-to-date load factor rose 56.22 pct from 55.35 pct. Revenue passenger miles rose 34.2 pct to 1.3 billion from 934.2 mln and available seat miles rose 32.2 pct to 2.2 billion from 1.7 billion.
