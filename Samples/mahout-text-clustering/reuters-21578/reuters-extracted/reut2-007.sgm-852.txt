UNIFORCE <UNFR> TO FILE REGISTRATION

Uniforce Temporary Personnel Inc said it will file in April a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an offering of 128,250 shares of common stock issuable upon exercise of common stock purchase warrants.     The offering is being made at the request of a holder of the warrants which were issued in 1984 at the time of Uniforce's initial public offering.  Reuter &#3;
