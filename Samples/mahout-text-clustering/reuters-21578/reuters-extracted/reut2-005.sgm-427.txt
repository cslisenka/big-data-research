GM <GM>, PPG <PPG> SIGN AUTO PAINT PACT

General Motors Corp and PPG Industries Inc announced jointly that they had signed an agreement that will give PPG responsibility for operating the paint shop in GM's Buick Reatta Craft Center here.     The Reatta is a two-seat luxury car that will be introduced in the spring of 1988, said GM.     The companies described the agreement as an arrangement aimed at integrating GM's auto production expertise with PPG's coatings expertise.     Under the agreement, PPG's and GM's paint operating staff at the center will be consolidated under the PPG area manager.      Reuter &#3;
