STOCKS PLUNGE IN EARLY TOKYO TRADE

Share prices plunged in early trade as an opening foreign exchange record low of 143.75 yen to the dollar and a weak stockmarket in New York withered investors' confidence, brokers said.     After 32 minutes' trade, the average plummetted 313.00 to 22,609.20. Yesterday the market gained 9.21 to a record close.     Brokers said the dollar's fall against the yen prompted a large selloff of export-related shares. But fears had spread throughout the market, depressing almost all issues.  REUTER &#3;
