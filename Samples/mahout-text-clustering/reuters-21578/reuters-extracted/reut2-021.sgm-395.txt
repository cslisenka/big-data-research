MILLIPORE <MILI> FILES FOR DEBENTURE OFFERING

Millipore Corp filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a proposed offering of up to 75 mln dlrs of convertible subordinated debentures due November 15, 2012.     First Boston Corp and Alex. Brown and Sons Inc will act as underwriters for the proposed offering.     Millipore said it will use proceeds from the offering to repay debt and for general corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
