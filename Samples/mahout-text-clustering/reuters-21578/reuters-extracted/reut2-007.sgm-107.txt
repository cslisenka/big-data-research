PAGURIAN TO BUY BACK UP TO 10 PCT OF STOCK

<Pagurian Corp Ltd> said it filed a notice with the Toronto Stock Exchange to buy back up to 10 pct of its publicly held class A non-voting shares over a one-year period starting April three.     About 27.9 mln of the company's 47.2 mln class A shares are publicly held at present.  Reuter &#3;
