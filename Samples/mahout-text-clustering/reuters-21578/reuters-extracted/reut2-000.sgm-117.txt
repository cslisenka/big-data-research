FLUOR <FLR> UNIT GETS CONSTRUCTION CONTRACT

Fluor Corp said its Fluor Daniel unit received a contract from Union Carbide Corp <UK> covering design, procurement and construction of a 108 megawatt combined cycle cogeneration facility in Seadrift, Texas.     The value of the contract was not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
