SOUTHERN UNION <SUG> TO SELL NOTES, DEBENTURES

Southern Union Co said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering a 50 mln dlr offering of 10-year notes and a same-sized issue of 30-year debentures.     Proceeds will be used to redeem three outstanding debenture issues and repay two outstanding note issues.     The company named Goldman, Sachs and Co as sole manager of the note and debenture offerings.  Reuter &#3;
