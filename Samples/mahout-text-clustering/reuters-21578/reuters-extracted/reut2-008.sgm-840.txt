BANCA CATTOLICA IN 100 MLN DLR CD ISSUE

Banca Cattolica del Veneto SpA said it had signed an accord with Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co of New York covering a 100 mln dlr certificates of deposit issue in London.     Maturities on the certificates will be between seven and 365 days.     The bank said the issue would be placed with international institutional and private investors.  REUTER &#3;
