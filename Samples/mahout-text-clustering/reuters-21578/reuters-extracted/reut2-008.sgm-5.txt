TEXAS INSTRUMENTS <TXN> BEGINS BUILDING PLANT

Texas Instruments Inc said construction will begin in April on a new plant near Denton, Texas, on 193 acres owned by the company.     It said the plant will initially employ between 1,000 and 1,500 people, and will be used by its Defense Systems and Electronics Group for producing electronic equipemnt. The plant is expected to be operational in late 1988.     Separately, the company said it leased about 185,000 square feet of a complex in Texas for its Defense Systems group. It plans to transfer about 700 employees to work at the site by the third quarter of 1987.  Reuter &#3;
