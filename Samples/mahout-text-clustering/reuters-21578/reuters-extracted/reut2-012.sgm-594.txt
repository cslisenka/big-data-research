BRAZIL TO EXPORT POULTRY, MEAT TO IRAQ FOR OIL

Brazil will export 6,000 tonnes of poultry and 10,000 tonnes of frozen meat to Iraq in exchange for oil, Petrobras Commercial Director Carlos Sant'Anna said.     Brazil has a barter deal with Iraq and currently imports 215,000 barrels per day of oil, of which 170,000 bpd are paid for with exports of Brazilian goods to that country.  Reuter &#3;
