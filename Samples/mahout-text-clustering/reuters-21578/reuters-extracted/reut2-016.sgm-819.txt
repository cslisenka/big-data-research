MARTIN PROCESSING <MPI> SEES YEAR SALES GROWTH

Martin PRocessing Inc said it expects significant sales growth for the rest of the year, particularly for its peak periods, the second and third quarters.     The company reported first quarter sales of 11.2 mln dlrs, up from 11.1 mln dlrs a year before.  For all of 1986 it had sales of 46.6 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
