CANADIAN AIRLINES TO PURCHASE SIX BOEING<BA>JETS

Canadian Airlines International said it agreed to purchase six Boeing Co 767-300ER (extended range) aircraft, and options on eight more, for 1.6 billion Canadian dlrs, including spare parts and training.     Delivery will begin in April 1988 and run through April 1989, the company said.     The airline said it plans to finance the new aircraft by means of oeprating leases. Canadian Airlines was recently formed through the merger of Canadian Pacific Airlines and Pacific Western Airlines.  Reuter &#3;
