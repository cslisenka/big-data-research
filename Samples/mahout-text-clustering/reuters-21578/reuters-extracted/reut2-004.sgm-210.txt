NASD NAMES NEW BOARD MEMBER

The <National Association of Securities Dealers> said it has named Jaguar Cars Inc president Graham W. Whitehead as one of its three governors-at-large representing companies listed on the NASDAQ system.     Jaguar Cars is the U.S. marketing arm of Jaguar PLC <JAGRY>, which was NASDAQ's third-most-actively traded issue in 1986.  Reuter &#3;
