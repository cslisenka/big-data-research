WAL-MART STORES <WMT> MARCH SALES RISE

Wal-Mart Stores Inc said March sales were up 32 pct to 1.13 billion dlrs from 855 mln dlrs a year earlier, with same-store sales up eight pct.     The company said sales for the first two months of its fiscal year were up 37 pct to 2.01 billion dlrs from 1.47 billion dlrs a year before, with same-store sales up 11 pct.  Reuter &#3;
