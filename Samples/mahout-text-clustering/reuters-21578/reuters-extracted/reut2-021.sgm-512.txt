HYUNDAI AUTO CANADA TO RAISE 100 MLN DLR LOAN

<Hyundai Auto Canada Inc>, a unit of South Korea's Hyundai Motor Co <HYUN.SE>, is expected to award Chemical Asia Ltd a mandate later this week to arrange a 100 mln U.S. Dlr loan, banking sources said.     The nine-year loan will carry interest of 1/4 point over the London interbank offered rate. No other details were available.     A Chemical spokesman declined comment.   REUTER &#3;
