ROSS STORES <ROST> FIVE WEEK SALES UP FIVE PCT

Ross Stores Inc said its five-week sales for the period ended April 4 totaled 44 mln dlrs, up five pct from a year earlier.     The company said for the two months ended April 4, sales totaled 85 mln dlrs, up nine pct from a year ago.     Ross stores operates 121 retail stores. It closed 25 of its stores earlier this year.     It said comparing sales to a year ago, not counting the 25 stores now closed, sales were up 22 pct in the five-week period and up 29 pct in the two month period.  Reuter &#3;
