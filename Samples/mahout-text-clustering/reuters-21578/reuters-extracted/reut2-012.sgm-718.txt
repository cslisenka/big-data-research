ARBITRAGER ACQUIRES 9.98 PCT OF DECISION <DCF>

Decision/Capital Fund Inc said entities associated with Mario Gabelli, an arbitrager, have acquired 9.98 pct of the shares in a friendly transaction.     In a 13D filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission, The Gabelli Group Inc of New York, said it acquired 200,000 shares of the fund in stock exchange transactions on March 25 "in the ordinary course of business and not to acquire control" of the fund.     Decision has said it will seek shareholder approval to convert to an operating company. It also plans to acquire Hopper, Soliday and Co Inc, a regional stock brokerage.  Reuter &#3;
