FORTUNE FINANCIAL <FORF.O> EXTENDS REPURCHASE

Fortune Financial Group Inc said it extended its stock repurchase program for an additional six months.     The program, which began last April, will extend to April 16, 1988, the company said.     Last April the board authorized the repurchase of up to 400,000 common shares, of which 111,200 were repurchased, the company said.     The company said it may purchase another 288,800 shares in the open market.      Reuter &#3;
