GAMING AND TECHNOLOGY <GATI.O> PRESIDENT RESIGN

Gaming and Technology Inc said Stan Fulton has resigned as president of the company.     Chairman Alfred Wilms said he will assume the additional position of president.     Fulton will remain a director of the company and will work on special projects, a Gaming and Technology spokeswoman said.  Reuter &#3;
