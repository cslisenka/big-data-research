GENERAL INSTRUMENT <GRL> UNIT GETS NAVY AWARD

General Instrument Corp's government systems division said it received a 35.9 mln dlr contract from the Naval Air Command Systems.     The company said the contract calls for continued production of General Instrument's family of electronics warfare systems for application in naval aircraft.     The contract also contains options for more than 100 systems to be ordered within the next several years, the company added.  Reuter &#3;
