ALCATEL CIT WINS SWEDISH MILITARY CONTRACT

Alcatel Cit <ALCP.PA>, the newly established French subsidiary of Alcatel NV which now runs the worldwide telecommunications activities of ITT Corp <ITT.N>, has won a 15 mln franc contract from the Swedish armed forces for transmission equipment, Alcatel said in a communique here.     The order is for multiplexing equipment and falls within the framework of a 50 mln franc sales contract obtained by Alcatel in 1982, it added.     Alcatel NV is a joint venture between ITT and France's state-owned Cie Generale d'Electricite <CGE>.  REUTER &#3;
