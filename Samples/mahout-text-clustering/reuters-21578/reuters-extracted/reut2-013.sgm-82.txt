NISSAN RAISES U.S. CAR, TRUCK PRICES 1.9 PCT

Nissan Motor Corp said it is raising the suggested retail price for its cars and trucks sold in the United States by 1.9 pct, or an average 212 dlrs per vehicle, effective April 6.     The company said the increase is the result of the continued strengthening of the Japanese yen against the U.S. dollar.  Reuter &#3;
