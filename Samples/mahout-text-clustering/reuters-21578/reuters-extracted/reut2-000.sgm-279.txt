SINO-U.S. VENTURE IN CHINA TO MAKE RINSING AGENTS

<Ecolab Co> of the United States signed a contract with North China Industrial Co to set up the first Sino-U.S. Joint venture in China to make rinsing agents, the New China News Agency said.      It said total investment in the new venture, <Ecolab Chemical Industrial Co>, is 2.4 mln dlrs. It said the venture will be based in Shanghai and produce agents for use in hotels and industries.     It gave no further details.  REUTER &#3;
