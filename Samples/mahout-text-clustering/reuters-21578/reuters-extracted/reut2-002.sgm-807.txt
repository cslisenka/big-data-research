GATX <GMT> TO REPURCHASE UP TO 750,000 SHARES

GATX Corp said it will buy back up to 750,000 common shares in the open market from time to time within the next year.     Morgan Stanley and Co Inc will act as agent.     As of December 31, 1986, GATX had nine mln shares outstanding.  Reuter &#3;
