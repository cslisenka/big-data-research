INVESTORS SAVINGS <ISLA> TO REDEEM PREFERRED

Investors Savings Bank said it will redeem on May 8 its 95 ct Series A cumulative convertible preferred stock at 10.72 dlrs plus accrued interst of 10 cts per share.     The company said each share may be converted instead into 1.333 common shares through May 1.  Investors have outstanding 1,166,857 preferred shares convertible into 1,555,420 common shares.
