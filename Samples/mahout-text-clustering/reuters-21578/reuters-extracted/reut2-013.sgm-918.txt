TURKISH CENTRAL BANK SETS LIRA/DOLLAR, DM RATES

The Turkish Central Bank set a Lira/Dollar rate for April 8 of 784.50/788.42 to the Dollar, down from the previous 784.35/788.27.     The Bank also set a Lira/Mark rate of 430.60/432.75 to the Mark, down from the previous 429.35/431.50. &#3;
