ORS <ORSC> CHAIRMAN RESIGNS AFTER CONVICTION

Robert A. Alexander resigned yesterday as chairman, president and director of ORS Corp following his conviction in the United States District Court in Tulsa of seven counts of mail and wire fraud, the company said.     The company, which will appoint an interim president, said Alexander plans to appeal the conviction.     The company said it was not charged in Alexander's indictment.  Reuter &#3;
