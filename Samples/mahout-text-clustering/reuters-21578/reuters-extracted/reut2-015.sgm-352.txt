FORD <F> EXTENDS BUYER INCENTIVE PROGRAM

Ford Motor Co said it has extended to April 30 its buyer incentive program on 1986 and 1987 Ford Escort and Tempo, and Mercury Lynx and Topaz.     Consumers will have a choice of 3.9 pct to 9.9 pct annual percentage rate financing or a cash rebate of up to 600 dlrs.     Earlier Ford extended its incentive program to April 30 on its Taurus and Mercury Sable and several lines of light trucks.  Reuter &#3;
