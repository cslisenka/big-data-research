ROCKWELL <ROK>, U S WEST <USW> UNIT IN PACT

Rockwell International Corp said it and U S West Knowledge Engineering Inc, a U S West Inc subsidiary, agreed to jointly develop an operator workstation that combines voice and data capabilities for directory assistance, call intercept and other operator functions in a single terminal.     It said the systems will be offered as integrated elements of Rockwell's telephone- company-oriented ISS-3000 Automatic Call Distribution switches.   Reuter &#3;
