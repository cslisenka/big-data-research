VICTORY MARKETS <VMKT> SELLS DEBENTURES

Victory Markets Inc is raising 60 mln dlrs through an offering of subordinated debentures due 1999 with a 12-1/2 pct coupon and par pricing, said sole underwriter Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc.     Non-callable for five years, the debt is rated B-2 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. The issue was increased from an initial offering of 50 mln dlrs because of investor demand.  Reuter &#3;
