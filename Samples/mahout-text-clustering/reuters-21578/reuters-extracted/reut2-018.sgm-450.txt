OLYMPIC CO ISSUES 50 MLN SWISS FRANC WARRANT NOTES

Olympic Co Ltd of Tokyo is issuing 50 mln Swiss francs of five year notes with an indicated one pct coupon, lead manager Morgan Stanley said.     The issue is guaranteed by Mitsubishi Trust and Banking Corp.     Terms will be fixed on June 22 with payment due July 9. The warrant exercise period is from August 3, 1987 until June 30, 1992.
