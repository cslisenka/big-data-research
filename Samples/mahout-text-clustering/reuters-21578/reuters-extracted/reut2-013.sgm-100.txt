USX <USX> TO SHUT DOWN UTAH STEEL PLANT

USX Corp's USS division said it it intends to close the Geneva Works near Provo, Utah, effective July one.     The company said it is continuing talks with local interests in Utah concerning a possible sale of the Geneva works plant.     The company had announced the indefinite idling of the integrated steel mill in February. The plant, which produced flat rolled steel products and small diameter pipe, had employeed 2,200 workers, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
