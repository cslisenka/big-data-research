EMERSON RADIO <EME> SELLS NOTES PRIVATELY

Emerson Radio Corp said it has sold 10 mln dlrs of eight pct senior notes due March 1992 to a major institutional investor it did not name.     The company also said 5,800,000 dlrs of its convertible subordinated notes due 1997 have been converted into 728,476 common shares.  Reuter &#3;
