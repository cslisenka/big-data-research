BRUNSWICK CORP <BC> SELLS NOTES AT 8.199 PCT

Brunswick Corp is raising 100 mln dlrs through an offering of notes due 1997 yielding 8.199 pct, lead manager Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc said.     The notes have an 8-1/2 pct coupon and were priced at 99.50 to yield 96 basis points over comparable Treasury securities.     The issue is non-callable for life and rated Baa-1 by Moody's Investors Service Inc and BBB-plus by Standard and Poor's Corp.     Salomon Brothers co-managed the deal.  Reuter &#3;
