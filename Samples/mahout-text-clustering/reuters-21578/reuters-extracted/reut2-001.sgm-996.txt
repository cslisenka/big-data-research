PAYLESS CASHWAYS INC <PCI> FEBRUARY SALES RISE

Payless Cashways Inc said its sales for the four weeks ended February 28 rose six pct on a comparable store basis to 108.6 mln dlrs from 89.3 mln dlrs a year ago.     Year to date, it said sales advanced to 332.1 mln dlrs from 274.5 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
