NEWCOR <NEW> NAMES NEW CHAIRMAN

Newcor Inc said its board elected William Mitchell as chairman succeeding Frank Gofrank, who is retiring as chairman but remains a director.     In other action, Newcor's board approved payment of a regular quarterly dividend of eight cts on May one, record April 15.  Reuter &#3;
