MCA INC <MCA> TO BUY BACK TEN MLN COMMON SHARES

MCA Inc said its board has authorized management to buy up to ten mln shares of MCA's common stock in the open market or through private transactions.     The company said ten mln shares is about 13 pct of current outstanding common stock.     The shares are intended to be used for general corporate purposes, MCA said.  Reuter &#3;
