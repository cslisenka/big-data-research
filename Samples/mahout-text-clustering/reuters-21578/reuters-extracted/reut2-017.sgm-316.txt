HUNTINGDON <HRCLY.O> IN ACQUISITION TALKS

Huntingdon International Holdings plc said it has entered into discussions with a view to acquiring Northern Engineering and Testing Inc, a company based mainly in the northwestern United States.     The company said Northern Engineering had sales of approximately 6.5 mln dlrs in the year ended March 31.  Reuter &#3;
