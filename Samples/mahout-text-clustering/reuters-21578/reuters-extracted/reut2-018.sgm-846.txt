AMVESTORS <AVFC.O>OVERALLOTMENT OPTION EXERCISED

AmVestors Financial Corp said underwriters Smith Barney, Harris Upham and Co Inc and Morgan Keegan Inc <MOR> have exercised their overallotment option for an additional 10,000 common shares at 9.50 dlrs each in connection with a recent offering of 100,000 AmVestors shares.     It said it realized net proceeds of 886,000 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
