ENGINEERED SYSTEMS <ESD> UNIT GETS LOAN NOTICE

Engineered Systems and Development Corp said Wells Fargo and Co's <WFC> Wells Fargo Bank has demanded immediate payment of a 1,750,000 dlr loan made to Engineered's Seaborn Development Inc unit under a revolving credit arrangment that expired on April 30, 1986.     The company said Wells Fargo has also said it will sue to collect if it does not receive payment immediately.     The parties involved also began negotiations to restructure the repayment over an 18 month period and the talks are ongoing, Engineered Systems also said.  Reuter &#3;
