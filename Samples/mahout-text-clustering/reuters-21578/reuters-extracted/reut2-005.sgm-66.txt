IOMEGA <IMOG> PRESIDENT RESIGNS FOR NEW POST

Iomega Corp said its president, Gabriel Fusco, resigned as president to become chairman and chief executive officer of <Sequoia Systems>.     Fusco was president and chief executive officer of Iomega between April 1983 and January 1987, and will remain on the company's board of directors.   Reuter &#3;
