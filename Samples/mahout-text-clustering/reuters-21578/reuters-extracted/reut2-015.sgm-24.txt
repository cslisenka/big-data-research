HELEN OF TROY <HELE> FILES CONVERTIBLE OFFERING

Helen of Troy Corp said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering a 20 mln dlr issue of covertible subordinated debentures due 2007.     Proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes, including possible repayment of bank debt, product development and possible acquisitions, Helen of Troy said.     The company named Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc as sole underwriter of the offering.  Reuter &#3;
