WTC SHAREHOLDERS APPROVE PITTSTON <PCO> BUYOUT

Pittston Co said WTC International N.V. shareholders approved the acquisition of the company by Pittston.     Terms of the transaction call for Pittston to exchange 0.523 of its own common shares for each outstanding WTC share, Pittston said.     WTC is engaged in domestic international air freight, ocean and surface freight forwarding and consolidation and distribution services.  Reuter &#3;
