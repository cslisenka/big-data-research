ENDOTRONICS <ENDO> TO CUT WORKFORCE BY 33 PCT

Endotronics Inc said it is restructuring its workforce to reduce payroll costs by terminating 75 full-time and four part-time employees, or 33 pct of its total ranks.     The company said despite the cutbacks it remains commited to development of promising new areas and it intends "to maintain our research commitment to our programs in cancer immunotherapy and a new Hepatitis-B vaccine."
