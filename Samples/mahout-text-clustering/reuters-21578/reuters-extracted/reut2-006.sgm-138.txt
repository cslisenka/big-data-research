AMEX OPTIONS SEAT SOLD FOR RECORD AMOUNT

American Stock Exchange said a seat was sold yesterday for 315,000 dlrs, a record amount and up 10,000 dlrs from the last sale on March Three, which had been the record.     The market is now 275,000 dlrs bid and 340,000 dlrs offered, it said.  Reuter &#3;
