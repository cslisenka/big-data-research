U.S. ALUMINUM STOCKS HIGHER IN DECEMBER - ABMS

Total U.S. aluminum stocks held by integrated and non-integrated producers and smelters including scrap, ingot, metal in process and finished products rose to 2,470,000 short tons in December from 2,451,000 short tons in November, American Bureau Metal Statistics said.     Primary production rose to 278,000 tons in December from 263,000 tons in November. Imports for consumption declined to 114,000 tons in December from 150,000 tons in November.     Net shipments eased to 605,000 tons in December from 608,000 tons in November. Ingot shipments were 87,000 tons versus 99,000 tons, mill products shipments were 435,000 tons versus 420,000 tons and casting shipments were 83,000 versus 89,000.  Reuter &#3;
