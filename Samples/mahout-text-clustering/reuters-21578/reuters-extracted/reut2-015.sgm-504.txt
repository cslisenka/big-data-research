WEST GERMANY'S THESING ISSUES 10 MLN SFR BOND

<Thesing-Verwaltungsgesellschaft GmbH> of Coesfeld, West Germany, is issuing a 10 mln Swiss franc eight year bond with an indicated 6-3/8 pct coupon and par issue price, joint lead managers Fintrelex SA and E.Gutzwiller und Cie, Banquiers said.     Subscription is from May 7 until May 13.     The issue is guaranteed by Schallplattentruhe A. Thesing GmbH und Co Kommanditgesellschaft, Martin Thesing Handelsgesellschaft.  REUTER &#3;
