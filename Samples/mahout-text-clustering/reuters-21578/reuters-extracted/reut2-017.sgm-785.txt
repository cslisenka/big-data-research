UNIVERSAL HEALTH <UHT> TO BUY BACK SHARES

Universal Health Realty Income Trust said its board approved the repurchase over the next 12 months of up to one mln shares of beneficial interest of the trust in the open market or subject to obtaining necessary financing.     Universal also declared a regular quarterly dividend of 33 cts a share payable June 30 to shareholdres or record June 15.  Reuter &#3;
