SPORTS NEWS, CYBERNETIC IN DEAL

Sports News Network International Inc said it signed an exclusive agreement to distribute its electronic news service, Sportswatch, through Cybernetic Data Products.     The agreement calls for Sports News Network to provide its sports news service to more than 1,300 existing electronic board owned by Cybernetic Data Products and operated under the name, SilentRadio.
