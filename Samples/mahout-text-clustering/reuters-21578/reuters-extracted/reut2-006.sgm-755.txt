NO ACCEPTABLE BIDS FOR CCC PEANUTS - USDA

The Commodity Credit Corporation, CCC, received no acceptable bids on the offer to sell about 16,610 short tons of 1986-crop farmers stock peanuts for domestic crushing, the U.S. Agriculture Department said.     An additional offering of 1986-crop peanuts will be made at a later date, the department said.  Reuter &#3;
