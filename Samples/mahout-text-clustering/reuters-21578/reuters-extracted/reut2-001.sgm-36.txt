DIMIS <DMS> EXTENDS LIFE OF WARRANTS BRIEFLY

Dimis Inc said it has extended by five business days the expiration of its common stock purchase warrants until March Nine.     It said over one mln have already been exercised.  The warrants became effective February 17.     Holders exercising will receive a new warrant expiring March 31 allowing the purchase of half a common share at one dlr.  Reuter &#3;
