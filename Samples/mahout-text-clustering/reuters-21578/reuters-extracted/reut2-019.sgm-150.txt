NIPPON OIL AND FATS ISSUES EQUITY WARRANT EUROBOND

Nippon Oil and Fats Co Ltd is issuing a 70 mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due July 15, 1992 with an indicated coupon of 1-3/8 pct and par pricing, lead manager Yamaichi International (Europe) Ltd said.     The issue is guaranteed by Fuji Bank Ltd and final terms will be fixed on June 25. The selling concession is 1-1/2 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 3/4 pct.     The issue is available in denominations of 5,000 dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg. The payment date is July 15 and the warrants are exercisable from July 29, 1987 until June 30, 1992.
