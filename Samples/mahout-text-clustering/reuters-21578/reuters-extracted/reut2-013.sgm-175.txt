RESIDENTIAL <RMI> ISSUES 54.3 MLN MORTGAGE

Residential Mortgage Investments Inc's Mortgage Acceptance Inc unit said it is offering a 54.3 mln dlrs collaterized mortgage obligation using mortgage pass-through certificates evidencing undivided interests in pools of conventional mortgage loans on single-family residential properties as collateral.     It is expected the issue will close on April 28.  Reuter &#3;
