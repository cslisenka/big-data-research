<DIGITAL OPTRONICS CORP> SETS INITIAL OFFERING

Digital Optronics Corp said it has filed for an initial public offering of 850,000 common shares at an expected price of six to 6.50 dlrs a share through underwriters led by Yorke McCarter Owen and Bartels Inc and Norris and Hirshberg Inc.     The company said Acme-Cleveland Corp <AMT> and Digital Signal Corp are investors in Digital Optronics. which makes artifical vision sensors and laser radar.  Reuter &#3;
