COCA-COLA <KO> EXECUTIVE RESIGNS

Coca-Cola Co said Eugene V. Amoroso has resigned as president and chief executive officer of its Coca-Cola Foods division to pursue other business interests.     The company said Harry E. Teasley Jr., who had headed its bottling subsidiary serving southern England, will succeed Amoroso.  Reuter &#3;
