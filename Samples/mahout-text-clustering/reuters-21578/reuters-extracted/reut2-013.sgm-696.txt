U.S. FEEDER STEER PRICE

The U.S. Feeder Steer Price, or USFSP, as posted by the CME is calculated by Cattle Fax and represents the price used for cash settlement of the CME Feeder Cattle contract.     The USFSP is a seven-calendar-day average of feeder steer prices from 27 states, based on auction and direct country sales for feeder steers that weigh between 600 and 800 lbs and are estimated to grade between 60 and 80 pct choice when fed to slaughter weight.
