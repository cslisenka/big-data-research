RJR NABISCO INC ISSUES 100 MLN DLR EUROBOND

RJR Nabisco Inc is issuing a 100 mln dlr eurobond due June 30, 1994 paying 8-3/4 pct and priced at 101-3/8 pct, lead manager of Bank of America International Ltd said. This confirms an earlier report by bond market sources.     The issue has an investor put option after four years at par but is non-callable. The selling concession is 1-1/8 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 1/2 pct.     The issue is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 dlrs and listing will be in Luxembourg. The payment date is June 30.  REUTER &#3;
