PERCEPTRONICS <PERC> UNIT GETS NAVY CONTRACT

Perceptronics Inc said its FAAC Perceptronics subsidiary received a 2.7 mln dlr contract from the Naval Air Development Center in Warminster, Pa.     The contract calls for the company to develop software for Naval aircraft training simulators and F-14D weapons systems analysis, Perceptronics said.    Reuter &#3;
