AMERICAN CAPITAL CORP <ACC> MAKES SWAP OFFER

American Capital Corp said it will offer to holders of its 13,325,367 outstanding common stock purchase warrants 2.10 dlrs cash and 0.09 shares of common stock for each outstanding warrant.     The company said the offer will remain open until July 21.     It said the offer is subject to certain conditions including completion of the pending public offering of a new series of preferred stock.  Reuter &#3;
