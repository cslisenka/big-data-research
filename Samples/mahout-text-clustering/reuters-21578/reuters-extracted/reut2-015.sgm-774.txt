NUTMEG INDUSTRIES <NUTM> IN LICENSING DEAL

Nutmeg Industries Inc said it has agreed in principle on a license deal with United Media/United Feature Syndicate Inc under which it would have the right to sell a line of men's spectator sportswear bearing the likenesses of characters from Charles M. Schulz' "Peanuts" comic strip.     The company said the license runs through December 1988 and has renewal options for two more years.  Reuter &#3;
