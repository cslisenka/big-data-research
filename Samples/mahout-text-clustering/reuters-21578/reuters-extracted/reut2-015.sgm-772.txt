FEDERATED GROUP <FEGP> POSTS HIGHER MARCH SALES

Federated Group Inc said its sales for the five weeks ended April five totaled 33.9 mln dlrs, compared with 33.8 mln during the same period last year.     Comparable store sales fell eight pct to 29.4 mln dlrs from 32.1 mln a year earlier, the company said.     Federated said it now has 65 stores in operation compared with 59 during the like period a year ago.                 Reuter &#3;
