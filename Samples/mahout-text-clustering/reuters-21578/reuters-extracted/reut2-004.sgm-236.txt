BIOTECH CAPITAL <BITC> TO BUY MAGAZINE

Biotech Capital Corp said it agreed to buy High Technology magazine from the Goldhirsh Group of Boston.     Terms were not disclosed.     The magazine publishes information about emerging technologies and their impact on business. It has a circulation of 200,000 and is the largest of its kind in the world, the company said.
