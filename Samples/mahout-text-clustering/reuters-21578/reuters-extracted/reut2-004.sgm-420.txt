E-SYSTEMS <ESY> CHAIRMAN TO RETIRE

E-Systems Inc said John W. Dixon will retire April 21 as chairman and chief executive officer but remain on the board as chairman emeritus.     The company said president and chief operating officer David R. Tacke will succeed Dixon as chairman and chief executive and senior vice president E. Gene Keigger will become president and chief operating officer.  Reuter &#3;
