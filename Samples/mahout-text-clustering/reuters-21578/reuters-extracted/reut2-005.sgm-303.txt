NORTHERN AIR FREIGHT <NAFI> GETS, REJECTS, BID

Privately-held <Pilot Air Freight> said it met with officials of Northern Air Freight Inc to try to negotiate a friendly acquisition of Northern, but Northern's management had no interest in the proposal.     Northern has annual revenues of about 60 mln dlrs and is based in Seattle.        Reuter &#3;
