STE-GENEVIEVE UPS OFFER FOR SULLIVAN <SUM.TO>

Ste-Genevieve Resources Ltd said it increased its cash takeover bid for Sullivan Mines Inc shares to 7.25 dlrs a share. It did not give the original bid.     The offer is for a minimum five mln common shares and a maximum six mln shares, the company said.     It gave no further details.  Reuter &#3;
