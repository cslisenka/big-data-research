CBT SOYPRODUCT FUTURES EXPECTED STEADY/EASIER

CBT soyproduct futures were called steady to slightly easier in sympathy with expected weakness in soybeans, traders said.     Soybean crush ratios remained under pressure yesterday, so further decline in the U.S. soybean crush rate is needed before soyproducts can begin to show strength relative to soybeans, they added.  ----  Malaysian palm oil trimmed initial gains on trade-talk that Pakistan passed on its tender for 6,000 tonnes of PBD palm oil...Rotterdam vegetable oils were steady to slightly firmer at midday, with meals and feeds mixed.
