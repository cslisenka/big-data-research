PIEDMONT AIR <PIE> FEBRUARY LOAD FACTOR RISES

Piedmont Aviation Inc's Piedmont Airlines said its February load factor, or percentage of seats filled, rose to 55.5 pct, from 52.6 pct for the same month last year.     The airline said revenue passenger miles for the month were up 20.1 pct, to 780 mln, from 649.3 mln. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.     Piedmont said its capacity increased 13.8 pct, to 1.40 billion available seat miles, from 1.23 billion.     Year to date, Piedmont said its load factor was 54.1 pct vs 51.6 pct last year.     It said revenue passenger miles totaled 1.57 billion vs 1.34 billion and capacity was 2.90 billion available seat miles vs 2.61 billion.  Reuter &#3;
