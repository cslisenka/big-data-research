WILSON FOODS <WILF> SETS FINAL CREDITOR PAYMENT

Wilson Foods Corp said it made the final creditor payment under its 1984 plan of reorganization.     The plan resulted from its 1983 filing under Chapter 11 filing of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code.     Including the final payment, the company said it has paid its creditors 31 mln dlrs plus interest at 9.5 pct, repaying all outstanding debt owed under the plan.  Reuter &#3;
