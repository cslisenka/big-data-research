CISTRON BIOTECHNOLOGY <CIST.O> IN MARKETING DEAL

Cistron Biotechnology Inc said it has reached a marketing agreement for a "major multinational consumer products company" it did not name to seek U.S. Food and Drug Administration approvals for over the counter sales of Cistron's home test for detection of vaginal infections.     The company said product commercialization could take place within 18 months.  The new tests are expected to retail for 10 to 20 dlrs each and take about 30 minutes for results to become known.  It said its agreement with the major company also covers worldwide sales.  Reuter &#3;
