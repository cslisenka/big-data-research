ALITALIA PILOTS START WEEK OF INDUSTRIAL ACTION

Alitalia <AZPI.MI> pilots started five days of industrial action today causing cancellations to dozens of domestic flights and chaos to several international services, Italian airport officials said.     Pilots working for the national airline are striking for four hours during the busy morning period every day until Saturday in protest at working conditions. Almost all of Italy's airports are affected by the action.     Alitalia said it had cancelled 96 domestic flights and warned of severe disruption to other services.  REUTER &#3;
