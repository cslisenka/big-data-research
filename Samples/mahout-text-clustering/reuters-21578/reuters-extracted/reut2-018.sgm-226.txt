AMR <AMR> COMPLETES PREFERRED STOCK PLACEMENT

AMR Corp, parent company of American Airlines, said it has completed a private placement of 150 mln dlrs of preferred auction rate stock through Bankers Trust Co NY <BT>.     AMR said the preferred stock was sold in three series of 50 mln dlrs each. Each series is perpetual and will pay dividend rates which will be determined every seven weeks through a Dutch Auction process, AMR added.     The company said it will add the proceeds from the issue to working capital and use it for capital expenditures and for general corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
