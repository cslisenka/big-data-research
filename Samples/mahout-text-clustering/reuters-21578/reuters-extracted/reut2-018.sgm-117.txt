IMMUNOMEDICS <IMMU.O> HAS NEW LINKER TECHNOLOGY

Immunomedics Inc said it has developed a technology that makes it possible to join chemical molecules to antibodies in a more selective manner than now possible.     It said it has filed a patent application, which is expected to be issued shortly as a patent for the technology.  Reuter &#3;
