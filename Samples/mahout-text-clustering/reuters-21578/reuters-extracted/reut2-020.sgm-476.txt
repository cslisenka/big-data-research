AMERICAN FILTRONA<AFIL.O> TO BUY BACK STOCK

American Fltrona Corp said its board authorized the repurchase of up to 100,000 shares of company's stock for use in the its employee benefit plans and for other general corporate purposes.     The company had 3,690,000 shares outstanding as of September 30.     American also said it declared a regular quarterly dividend of 19 cts a share payable November 25 to shareholders of record November 10.  Reuter &#3;
