SYMBOL TECHNOLOGIES <SMBL> GETS CONTRACT

Symbol Technologies Inc said it has received an order worth over five mln dlrs to install its LS-7000 hand-held laser scanners and related equipment in all 271 U.S. Toys "R" Us Inc <TOY> stores by the end of calendar 1987.  Reuter &#3;
