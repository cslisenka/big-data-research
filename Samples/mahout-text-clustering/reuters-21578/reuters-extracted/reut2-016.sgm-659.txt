LOCKHEED <LK> UNIT GETS 275 MLN DLR CONTRACT

Lockheed Corp's Lockheed Missiles and Space Co is being awarded a 275 mln dlr modification to a Navy contract for development and production of Trident II missiles, the Defense Department said.     It said work on the contract is expected to be completed in March 1990.   Reuter &#3;
