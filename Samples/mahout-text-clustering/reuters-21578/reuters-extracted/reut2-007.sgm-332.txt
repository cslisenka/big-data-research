MARKITSTAR <MARK> FILES TO OFFER SHARES

MarkitStar Inc said it has filed for an offering of 1,700,000 common shares through underwriters led by Woolcott and Co Inc.     The company said it will sell 1,500,000 shares and shareholders the rest.  Company proceeds will be used for the development and expansion of marketing and sales operations and facilities, for research and for working capital.  Reuter &#3;
