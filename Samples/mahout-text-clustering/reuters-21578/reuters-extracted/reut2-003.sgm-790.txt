LONE STAR <LCE> TO ADD FIVE CEMENT TERMINALS

Lone Star Industries Inc said it signed an agreement to buy or lease five import cement terminals from Hanson Industries' <HAN> Kaiser Cement Corp.     Terms of the agreement were not disclosed.     Lone Star said three of the terminals are located in Alaska, one in Seattle and the other in Portland, Ore.  Reuter &#3;
