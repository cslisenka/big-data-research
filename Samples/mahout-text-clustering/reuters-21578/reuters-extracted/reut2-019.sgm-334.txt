BEST BUY <BBUY.O> FILES 30 MLN DLR DEBT OFFERING

Best Buy Co Inc said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a proposed offering of 30 mln dlrs of subordinated extendable notes.     Underwriters are led by Piper, Jaffray and Hopwood Inc. Financing is expected to be completed within 30 days, the company said.     Proceeds will be used to fund the company's planned expansion.
