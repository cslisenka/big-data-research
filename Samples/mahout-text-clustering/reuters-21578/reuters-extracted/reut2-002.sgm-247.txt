ALCAN <AL> UNIT BEGINS PREFERRED SHARE OFFER

Aluminum Co of Canada Ltd, a unit of Alcan Aluminium Ltd, said it began an offer of 400,000 shares of a new series of preferred stock after its board approved the final prospectus.     The offering, which is being made in Canada, is for 400,000 shares of cumulative redeemable retractable preference shares at 25 dlrs apiece.     The quarterly dividend on the stock, to be known as series F, will be 50 cts Canadian, equal to eight pct a year, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
