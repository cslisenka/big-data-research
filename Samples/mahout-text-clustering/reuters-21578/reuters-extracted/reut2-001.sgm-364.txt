CBOE MEMBERSHIP SEAT SELLS FOR RECORD HIGH

A membership seat on the Chicago Board Options Exchange (CBOE) sold for a record 342,000 dlrs Friday, the exchange said today.     The previous record price of 308,000 dlrs was set on February 17.     The current bid for a CBOE seat is 315,000 dlrs and the offer is 345,000 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
