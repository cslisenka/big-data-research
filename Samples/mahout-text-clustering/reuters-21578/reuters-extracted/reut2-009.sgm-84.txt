NMS PHARMACEUTICAL <NMSI> SETS NEW BLOOD TEST

NMS Pharmaceuticals Inc said it has launched a new over-the-counter urinary blood test that will be sold through drugstores soon.     The test, called EZ Detect Urinary Blood Test, detects minute amounts of blood in the urine that cannot be detected by the naked eye.     It has been approved by the Food and Drug Administration.
