BRIXTON ESTATE LAUNCHES UNLIMITED STG CP PROGRAM

Brixton Estate Plc is establishing a sterling commercial paper program for an unlimited amount, J. Henry Schroder Wagg and Co Ltd said as arranger.     Dealers will be Schroder Wagg, S.G. Warburg and Co Ltd and County Natwest Capital Markets Ltd.     The paper will be issued in denominations of 500,000 and one mln stg and will have maturities between seven and 364 days.  REUTER &#3;
