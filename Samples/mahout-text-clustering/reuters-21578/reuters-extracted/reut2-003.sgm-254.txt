<BARTER RESOURCES> FORMS HOME SHOPPING UNIT

Barter Resources said it formed a television home shopping unit, Television Catalogue Inc.     The company said an investor group paid 500,000 dlrs in funding for a 51 pct interest in the unit.     Barter said 100,000 dlrs of the funding has been paid, with the balance due within sixty days.  Reuter &#3;
