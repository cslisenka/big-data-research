TAP AIR PORTUGAL SIGNS CONTRACT FOR AIRBUS

TAP Air Portugal said it signed a contract for three <Airbus Industrie> A310-300 aircraft, confirming a commitment made in January.     Air Portugal said it has taken options on two further aircraft which may be either A310-300s or A340s.  Reuter &#3;
