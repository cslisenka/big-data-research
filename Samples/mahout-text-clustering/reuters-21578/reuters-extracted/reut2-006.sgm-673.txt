BIONOMIC SCIENCES <BSII> SEES PROFITABILITY

Bionomic Sciences International Inc said it expects to start operating profitably by the fourth quarter.     The company today reported a profit of 165,600 dlrs for the first half ended January 31 -- after a 396,000 dlr gain on the sale of assets and a 10,000 dlr extraordinary loss.  A year before it lost 409,100 dlrs after a 106,300 dlr extraordinary gain.  Reuter &#3;
