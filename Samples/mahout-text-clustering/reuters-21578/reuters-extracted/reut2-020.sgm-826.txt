MDS HEALTH <MHGA.TO> GETS ORDER

MDS Health Group Ltd said that mass spectrometry equipment developed and made by its Sciex division will be used in British-made contraband detection systems being purchased by the Japanese government.     The company said the Japanese customs bureau placed an initial 21 mln dlr order.     The equipment is able to detect illegal drugs in cargo, the company said. The systems are made by British Aerospace <BAEL.L>.  Reuter &#3;
