AIRGAS <AGA> CHAIRMAN STEPS DOWN

Airgas Inc said chairman and chief executive officer C Perkins resigned but agreed to serve as a consultant on acquisitions and foreign sales.     The company said Perkins, under a five year consulting contract, will work closely with his designated successor, president Peter McCausland. McCausland is expected to be appointed chairman at a board meeting in May, it added.  Reuter &#3;
