WALL STREET STOCKS LOWER IN LATE TRADING

Wall Street stock prices were pounded lower late this afternoon by futures related sell programs and strong profit taking. A weak bond market ignited concern about rising interest rates and inflation, triggering the selloff, traders said.     The Dow Industrials fell 39 points to 2367.  Reuter &#3;
