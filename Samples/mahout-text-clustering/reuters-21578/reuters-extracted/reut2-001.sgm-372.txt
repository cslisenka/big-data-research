JEFFERIES AND CO MAKING MARKET FOR GM STOCK

Jefferies and Co Inc said it is making a market for General Motors Corp <GM> stock at 77-1/2 to 78.     GM said earlier today its directors authorized the repurchase of up to 20 pct of its common stock by the end of 1990 and that it would not repurchase more than ten mln shares this year.
