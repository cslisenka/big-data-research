BENETTON IN FINANCIAL SERVICES JOINT VENTURE

Benetton Group Spa <BTOM.MI> said it reached agreement with textiles concern GFT-Gruppo Finanziario Tessile Spa for a joint venture in the financial services sector.    A Benetton spokeswoman said details of the accord would be outlined at a news conference on Thursday in Milan. The Italian clothing group has targeted financial services as a sector in which to expand its activities.    In January, Benetton acquired a large minority stake in the Italian unit of the U.K. Financial services group Prudential Corp PLC [PRUL.L].  Reuter &#3;
