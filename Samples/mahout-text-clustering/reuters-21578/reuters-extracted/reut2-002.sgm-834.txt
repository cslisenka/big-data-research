EARTHWORM <WORM> ACCEPTS SOVIET INVITATION

Earthworm Tractor Co Inc said it accepted an invitation from the Ministry of Construction, Road Building and Municipal Machinery to visit the Soviet Union next week to explore trading construction and liquefied natural gas equipment.     The company said a representative of Prudential Bache Trade Corp will be part of the delegation.  Reuter &#3;
