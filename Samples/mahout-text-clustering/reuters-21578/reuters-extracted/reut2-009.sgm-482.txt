TEAM <TMI> TO UNVEIL NEW EQUIPMENT IN MAY

Team Inc said it will introduce a line of repair products for electrical distribution and transmission equipment for the utility industry in May.     The new products include a transformer repair service which will seal oil leaks in transformers. Other products include a new licensed repair method of electrical porcelain, and a newly developed concrete repair service.     Team said it expects to continue to sell equipment and attempt to reduce losses to generate positive cash flow. Earlier, Team reported third quarter net income of 91,000 dlrs on sales of 11.5 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
