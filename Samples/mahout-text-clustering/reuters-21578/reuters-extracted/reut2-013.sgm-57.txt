TEXAS AIR'S <TEX> CONTINENTAL MARCH TRAFFIC UP

Texas Air Corp'S Continental Airlines said its March 1987 load factor rose to 65.6 pct from 64.3 pct last year. Available seat miles rose to 5.1 mln from 2.5 mln and revenues passenger miles rose to 3.3 mln from 1.6 mln     For the year to date, load factor fell to 61.8 pct from 61.9 pct, available seat miles rose to 13.2 mln from 7.3 mln and revenue passenger miles rose to 8.2 mln from 4.5 mln  Reuter &#3;
