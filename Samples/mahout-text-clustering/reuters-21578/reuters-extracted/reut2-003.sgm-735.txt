ENGINEERING MEASUREMENTS (EMCO) SETS PARTNERSHIP

Engineering Measurements Co said it formed a general partnership with several external investment groups in which the company will have a 58.5 pct interest and be managing partner.     Called Measurement Auditors Co, the partnership provides flowmeter certification and testing services to the oil and gas industry, Engineering Measurements said.     The company also said marketing vice president Jerome J. Rusnak has been apointed president, succeeding Charles Miller who continues as chairman.  Reuter &#3;
