COMMAND AIRWAYS <COMD> FEBRUARY TRAFFIC ROSE

Command Airways said its February load factor rose to 40.4 pct from 39.0 pct, available seat miles rose to 9.6 mln from 7.1 mln last year and revenue passenger miles rose to 3.9 mln from 2.8 mln.     For the year to date, Command's load factor rose to 40.6 pct from 38.1 pct, revenue passenger miles rose to 37.0 mln from 27.8 mln and available seat miles rose to 91.2 mln from 72.7 mln.
