GENERAL NUTRITION <GNC> FILES FOR OFFERING

General Nutrition Inc filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a secondary offering of eight mln common shares.     All the shares are being sold by the estate of David Shakarian, the founder of the company. Kidder, Peabody and Co Inc will be the sole manager of the underwriting.     The Shakarian estate owns 25.6 mln shares of General Nutrition. There are 33.1 mln shares outstanding.  Reuter &#3;
