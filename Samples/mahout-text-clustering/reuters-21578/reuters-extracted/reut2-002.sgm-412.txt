EVANS/SUTHERLAND <ESCC> FILES FOR DEBT OFFERING

Evans and Sutherland Computer Corp said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering a 50 mln dlr issue of convertible subordinated debentures due 2012.     Proceeds will be used for working capital, Evans and Sutherland said.     The company named Hambrecht and Quist Inc as lead underwriter of the offering.  Reuter &#3;
