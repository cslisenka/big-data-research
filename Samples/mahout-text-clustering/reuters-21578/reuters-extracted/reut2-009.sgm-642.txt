FPL GROUP <FPL> UNIT FILES TO OFFER PREFERRED

FPL Group Inc's Florida Power and Light Co unit said it filed a shelf registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission for the future sale, through one or more offerings, of one mln shares of serial preferred stock.     It said that these shares, together with 150,000 shares of preferred stock that are unissued under a previous shelf registration, should cover the utility's preferred stock financing needs for the next two years.
