YORK FINANCIAL <YFED> SPLITS STOCK

York Financial Corp, parent company of York Federal Savings and Loan Association, said its board of directors declared a five-for-four stock split in the form of a 25 pct stock dividend.     The company said it will distribute the split on May 5, to shareholders of record April 20.  Reuter &#3;
