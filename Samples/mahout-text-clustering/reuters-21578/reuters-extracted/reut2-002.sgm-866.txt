BELL AND HOWELL <BHW> COMPLETES SALE OF UNIT

Bell and Howell Co said it completed the sale of its computer output microfilm business to COM Products Inc, a unit of privately-held <LeBow Industries Inc>.     The sum of the deal was not disclosed.     The unit makes a device that prints data directly from a computer onto microfilm.  Reuter &#3;
