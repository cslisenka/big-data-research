AMEX TRADING CHEMICAL WASTE <CHW> OPTIONS

The <American Stock Exchange> said it has started put and call option trading in Chemical Waste Management Inc stock as a replacement for options on Chesebrough-Ponds's Inc, which was recently acquired.     Initial expiration months are June and September and initial exercise prices 30 and 35, it said.  Position and exercise limits are 5,500 contracts on the same side of the market.  Reuter &#3;
