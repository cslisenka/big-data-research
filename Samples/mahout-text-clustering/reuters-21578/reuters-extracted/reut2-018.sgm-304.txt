DI INDUSTRIES FLORIDA UNIT GETS WATER CONTRACT

Drillers Inc of Florida, a unit of <DI Industries Inc>, formerly Drillers Inc <DRL>, said it has received two separate contracts for water injection projects valued at 408,500 dlrs and 850,000 dlrs respectively.     DI Industries said another unit, Drillers Inc of Texas completed a 560,000-dlr Department of Defense project which involved drilling a vertical firing shaft for the department's R and D Railgun.     DI said the projects represented efforts of the company to diversify from its traditional oil and gas drilling contracts.  Reuter &#3;
