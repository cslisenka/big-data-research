NANTUCKET <NAN> NAMES NEW HEAD FOR UNIT

Nantucket Industries Inc said James Adams has been named president of its hosiery division, replacing John Wineapple, and Terry Hampton has been named vice president of manufacturing for the division, replacing Howard Fromkin.     No reason was given for the changes.  Reuter &#3;
