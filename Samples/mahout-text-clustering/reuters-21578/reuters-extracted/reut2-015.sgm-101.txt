INVESTMENT TECHNOLOGIES <IVES> IN REBATE PACT

Investment Technologies Inc said it will make available its online investment advisory service to <Spear Securities Inc> customers.     Investment Technologies said that through a Spear rebate program, purchasers of the investment advisory service, VESTOR, can receive a cash rebate of up to the full subscription price of the investment advisory service from Spear Securities.     Spear's brokerage commission rebate program will allow money managers or individual investors who purchase VESTOR from Investment Technologies, or from other authorized distributors, to recive a rebate of the brokerage commissions, the company said.     Each month Spear will send participating customers a check equal up to 25 pct of monthly commissions generated by the client account, it added.  Reuter &#3;
