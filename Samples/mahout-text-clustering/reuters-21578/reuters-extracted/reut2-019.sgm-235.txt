THE HYDRAULIC CO <THC> FILES FOR OFFERING

The Hydraulic Co said it filed for a proposed public offering of 500,000 shares of common stock through an underwriting group co-managed by Salomon Brothers Inc and Edward D. Jones and Co.     The company said proceeds will be used to repay certain short-term debt.  Reuter &#3;
