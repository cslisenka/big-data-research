FINAL TERMS SET ON BELL GROUP CONVERTIBLE

The conversion price on the 175 mln Australian dlr convertible eurobond for Bell Group NV has been set at 13.37 Australian dlrs per share, lead manager Banque Paribas Capital Markets said.     The 10-year bond, which pays 10 pct and is priced at par, is guaranteed by the parent company Bell Group Ltd whose shares closed in Australia today at 10.70 Australian dlrs. The conversion premium is 24.95 pct.  REUTER &#3;
