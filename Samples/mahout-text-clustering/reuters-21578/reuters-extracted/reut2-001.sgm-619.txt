WEBCOR ELECTRONICS <WER> IN TECHNICAL DEFAULT

Webcor Electronics Inc said it remains in technical and payment default under its revolving credit agreement and in technical default under certain other obligations to its lender.     Although its lender has not enforced its right to demand payment of the debt in full, it continues to reserve its right to do so at any time, it said.     Earlier, Webcor reported a third quarter net loss of 1.8 mln dlrs and nine months net loss of 4.0 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
