TELEQUEST INC <TELQ.O> PRESIDENT RESIGNS

TeleQuest Inc said that Henry Marcheschi, president, chairman and ceo of the company, has resigned for health reasons.     The board of directors has elected Robert Lee, a co-founder of the company and former executive vice president of manufacturing and engineering, as president and chief executive officer, the company said.     Marcheschi will continue to work for the company as a consultant, Telequest added.  Reuter &#3;
