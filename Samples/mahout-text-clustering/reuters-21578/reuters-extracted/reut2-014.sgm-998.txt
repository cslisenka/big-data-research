EQUITABLE RESOURCES <EQT> FILES UNIT OFFERING

Equitable Resources Inc said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering a 75 mln dlr issue of units.     Each unit will consist of a 1,000 dlr face amount 25-year debenture with up to 21 five-year warrants to purchase the company's common stock. Each warrant will equal one share. The debentures will be non-refundable for 10 years.     Proceeds will be used to repay short-term loans incurred to finance part of Equitable's 1986 capital expenditure program and the redemption of 9-5/8 pct and 10-1/2 pct first mortgage bonds of 1995. First Boston will manage the issue.  Reuter &#3;
