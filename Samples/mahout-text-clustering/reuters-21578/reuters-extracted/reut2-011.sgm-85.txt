SERVICE RESOURCES <SRC> UNIT SETS PURCHASE

Service Resources Corp's Chas. P. Young Co subsidiary said it agreed to acquire Atwell Fleming Printing Ltd, a Canadian financial printer, for about 3.2 mln dlrs.     Young said the acquisition, which is expected to close in April, is subject to due dilligence review.  Reuter &#3;
