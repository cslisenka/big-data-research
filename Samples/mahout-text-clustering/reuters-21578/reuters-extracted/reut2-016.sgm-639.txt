MOODY'S MAY DOWNGRADE TRANSCANADA PIPELINE <TRP>

Moody's Investors Service Inc said it may downgrade 500 mln dlrs of Eurodebt of TransCanada Pipelines Ltd.     The agency cited TransCanada's 4.3 billion Can dlr offer for the assets of Dome Petroleum Ltd <DMP>. The offer entails a cash payment of 3.87 billion Can dlrs to Dome's creditors and the exchange of about 20 pct of the common stock of a new TransCanada subsidiary for Dome's common and preferred.     Moody's will examine the financing of the proposed transaction and resulting capital structure. TransCanada has A-3 Euro mortgage bonds and Baa-1 Eurodebt.  Reuter &#3;
