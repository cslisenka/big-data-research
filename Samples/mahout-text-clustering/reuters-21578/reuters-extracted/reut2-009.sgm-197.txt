NORWAY ISSUES 60 BILLION YEN EUROBOND

Norway is issuing a 60 billion yen eurobond due April 22, 1992 paying 4-1/4 pct and priced at 101-5/8 pct, lead manager Nomura International Ltd said.     The non-callable issue will be available in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in London.     The fees comprise 1-1/4 pct selling concession with 5/8 pct management and underwriting combined.  REUTER &#3;
