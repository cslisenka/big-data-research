U.S.-CHINA CIGARETTE FILTER JOINT VENTURE SET UP

Jiangsu Tobacco Corp and <Celanese Fibres Operations Ltd> yesterday established China's first cigarette filter joint venture, the Nantong Cellulose Fibres Co, the China Daily said.     The joint venture factory, due to start operations in 1989, will be able to produce up to 10,000 tonnes of cellulose fibre annually, it said. Cellulose fibre is the main component of cigarette filters, which China has always imported.     It said Celanese Fibres owns 30.68 pct of the Jiangsu province-based joint venture which has a total investment of 35.17 mln dlrs.  REUTER &#3;
