MITSUBISHI SEMICONDUCTOR PLANS U.S. EXPANSION

Mitsubishi Semiconductor America Inc, a subsdiary of <Mitsubishi Electric Corp>, said it plans to invest  36 mln dlrs to expand its Durham, N.C. semiconductor operation.     The company said it will build a building adjacent to the company's memory chip assembly plant and test center in N.C. The expansion will allow the subsidiary to fabricate the silicon wafers used in producing Application Specific Integrated Circuits (ASIC) chips, the company said.     Construction is expected to begin in July, with operations planned to start in early 1988, the company added.  Reuter &#3;
