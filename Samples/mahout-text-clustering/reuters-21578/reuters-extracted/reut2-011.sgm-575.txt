ANCHOR HOCKING <ARH> POSTPONES ANNUAL MEETING

Anchor Hocking Corp said it postponed its annual meeting of stockholders previously scheduled for May 6 pending completion of a review of proxy materials by the Securities and Exchange Commission.     The date and record date for the rescheduled meeting will be set later, the company said.     At the meeting, stockholders will be asked to approve a merger with Newell Co, to elect directors and decide related matters.  Reuter &#3;
