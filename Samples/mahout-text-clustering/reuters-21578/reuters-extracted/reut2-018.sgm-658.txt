GE <GE> GETS 250 MLN DLR ENGINE ORDER

General Electric Co said it has received contracts worth 250 mln dlrs, including options, to supply its CF6-80C2 engines for the 747-400 and MD-11 airliners ordered from Boeing Co <BA> and McDonnell Douglas Corp <MD> respectively by Thai Airways International.     The company said it also received a 16 mln dlr order from Thai Air for CF6-50 engines on a McDonnell Douglas DC-10 Series 30.  Reuter &#3;
