<HITECH ENGINEERING CO> WINS JUDGMENT

Hitech Engineering Co said it won litigation brought against it by Coldwell Banker Commercial Services Group, a unit of Sears, Roebuck and Co <S>.     In the lawsuit, tried in U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia, Coldwell had claimed that Hitech improperly canceled a lease for additional office space and sought damages of 350,000 dlrs.     The company said the plaintiff has the right to appeal but that no appeal has been filed to date. Hitech's legal counsel is confident that the verdict will not be overturned, it added.  Reuter &#3;
