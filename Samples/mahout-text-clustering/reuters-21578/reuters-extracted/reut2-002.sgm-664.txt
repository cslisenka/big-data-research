3COM <COMS> FILES FOR ONE MLN SHR OFFERING

3Com Corp said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission covering a proposed public offering of one mln common shares.     It said the offering will be managed by Goldman Sachs and Co and Montgomery Securities.     Proceeds from the proposed offering will be added to working capital to fund future growth, 3Com said.
