FIDELCOR <FICR> COMPLETES SALE OF UNIT

Fidelcor Inc said it has completed the sale of its Industrial Valley Title Insurance Co subsidiary to a group of investors including the unit's management for undisclosed terms.     Industrial Valley has assets of about 37.6 mln dlrs and was acquired last year along with IVB Financial Corp.  Reuter &#3;
