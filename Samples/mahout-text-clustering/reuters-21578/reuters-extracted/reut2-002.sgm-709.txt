NEW JERSEY APPROVES GPU <GPU> UNIT'S RATE CUT

General Public Utilities Corp said the New Jersey Board of Public Utilities has approved a 72 mln dlrs annual reduction in energy-adjustment charges for the company's Jersey Central Power and Light Co subsidiary.     The change in the utility's Levelized Energy Adjustment Charge represents a five pct decrease in overall electricity rates.     On January 28, Jersey Central filed for a 32 mln dlr a year decrease in the charge, asking to retain the difference to prevent an increase in these charges next year, a company spokesman said.
