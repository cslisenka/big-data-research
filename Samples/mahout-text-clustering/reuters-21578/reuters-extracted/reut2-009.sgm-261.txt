<TECHNIMED CORP> GETS FDA APPROVAL FOR DRUG

Technimed Corp said it has received U.S. Food and Drug Administration approval to market its proprietary whole blood glucose test.     The company said the test is the first home diagnostic glucose test to be approved by the FDA that uses simpler no-wipe technology. It is designed for use by diabetics in managing insulin therapy.     Technimed said it has entered into talks with "several major pharmaceutical companies" on the marketing of the test.
