PHONEMATE INC <PHMT.O> RELISTED ON NASDAQ

PhoneMate Inc said its common stock will be relisted on the NASDAQ National Market System on July 8.     NASDAQ said the company's shares were relisted in the NASDAQ system on June 4, following a delisting in September 1984 due to insufficient capital and surplus.     PhoneMate Inc designs, engineers and markets telecommunications products for the home and small business markets.  Reuter &#3;
