STERLING INC <STRL> FILES FOR OFFERING

Sterling Inc said it intends to file for an offering of 1,500,000 common shares, including 500,000 shares to be sold by shareholders.     It said proceedsd will be used to reduce bank debt.     The company said the sale is expected to be completed followiong a special shareholders' meeting planned for mid-April.  Reuter &#3;
