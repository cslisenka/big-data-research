FLETCHER TO BE FLETCHER CHALLENGE CHIEF EXECUTIVE

<Fletcher Challenge Ltd> (FCL) chairman Ron Trotter will step aside as chief executive in October on reaching 60 and will be replaced by group managing director Hugh Fletcher, FCL said in a statement.     Fletcher, 39, whose grandfather James Fletcher was the founder of <Fletcher Industries> from which FCL partly takes its name, joined the group in 1969.     He holds master of commerce, bachelor of science and MBA degrees from Harvard University and is also chairman of Air New Zealand. No new managing director has been named yet to replace him.
