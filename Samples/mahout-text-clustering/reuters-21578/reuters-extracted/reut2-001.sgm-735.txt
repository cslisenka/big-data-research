CHRYSLER <C> CANADA FEBRUARY CAR SALES FALL

Chrysler Canada Ltd, wholly owned by Chrysler Corp, said February car sales fell to 9,640 units from year-earlier 11,967 units.     Chrysler Canada said year-to-date car sales fell to 18,094 units from 22,073 units in the same period last year.  Reuter &#3;
