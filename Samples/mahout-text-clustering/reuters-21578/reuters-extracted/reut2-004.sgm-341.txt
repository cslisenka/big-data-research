U.S. MINERALS <USMX> AMENDS REGISTRATION

U.S. Minerals Exploration Co said it filed an amendment to its registration statement for a proposed public offering of a minimum of 500,000 shares and a maximum of 1.3 mln shares of common stock.     The amendment reflects an increase in the maximum number of shares to be offered from 800,000 to 1.3 mln.  Reuter &#3;
