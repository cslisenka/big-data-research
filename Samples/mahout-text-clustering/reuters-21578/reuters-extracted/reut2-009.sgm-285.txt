CORE-MARK INTERNATIONAL NAMES ACTING CHAIRMAN

Core-Mark International Inc said vice-chairman Edward Stanton will become acting chairman until the company's annual meeting following the previously announced resignation of David Gillespie as chairman and chief executive.     Board member Anthony Regensburg will will become acting chief executive and Daniel Gillespie will continue as president and chief operating officer.  Reuter &#3;
