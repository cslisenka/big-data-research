S/P UPGRADES PHELPS DODGE <PD> DEBT, PREFERRED

Standard and Poor's Corp said it upgraded Phelps Dodge Corp's senior debt to BB-plus from BB and preference stock to BB-minus from B-plus.     About 400 mln dlrs of debt and preference stock is outstanding.     Continuing cost cutting in its copper operations has significantly enhanced the firm's competitive position, S and P said. It added that the acquisition of Columbian Chemical Co has broadened Phelps Dodge's earnings base.  Reuter &#3;
