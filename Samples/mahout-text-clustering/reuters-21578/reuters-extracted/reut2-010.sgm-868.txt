WESTINGHOUSE <WX> TO MOVE POWER GENERATION UNIT

Westinghouse Electric Corp said it has relocated its power generation service division headquarters to locations of existing Westinghouse operations in Monroeville, Pa., and Orlando, Fla.     The company said about 25 of the 135 employees at the Broomall site will be moved to Monroeville and 45 to Orlando, with the remaining 65 having their employment terminated.     It said terminated employees will receive enhanced separation benefits.  Reuter &#3;
