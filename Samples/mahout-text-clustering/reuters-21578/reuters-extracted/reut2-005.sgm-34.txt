B.C. RESOURCES HAS NEW 360 MLN DLR CREDIT LINE

British Columbia Resources Investment Corp said it successfully concluded refinancing negotiations with bankers for a new 360 mln dlr restructured credit facility.     The credit line will be in place for four years to March 31, 1991, but is extendable up to 10 years under certain circumstances which were not specified by the company.     B.C. Resources said subsidiaries Westar Timber and Westar Petroleum have settled revised lending agreements, but debt discussions regarding subsidiary Westar Mining are continuing.   Reuter &#3;
