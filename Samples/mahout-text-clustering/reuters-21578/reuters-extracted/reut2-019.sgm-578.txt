MARINE <MCRP.O> GETS MARSHALL/ILSLEY PROPOSAL

Marine Corp said it received a proposal from Marshall and Ilsley Corp <MRIS.O> under which Marine shareholders would receive Marshall and Ilsley common stock or a combination of cash and stock equal to at least 62.50 dlrs a share of Marine common.     It said the proposal will be considered by the Marine board after analysis by its advisers.  Reuter &#3;
