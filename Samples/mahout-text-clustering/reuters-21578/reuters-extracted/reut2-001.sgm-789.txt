TANDY BRANDS <TAB> SELLS UNIT

Tandy Brands Inc said it sold its Grate Home and Fireplace division to an investor group that includes some members of Grate's management for 1,600,000 dlrs in cash and secured notes.     The company said the sale will not materially offset the 9,848,000 dlr non-recurring charge it took against the sale of the division.  Reuter &#3;
