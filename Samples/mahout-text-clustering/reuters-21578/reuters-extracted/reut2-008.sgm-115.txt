CHINA, PORTUGAL TO MAKE STATEMENT ON MACAO FUTURE

Talks between China and Portugal on the future of Macao have ended and the two countries will issue a joint statement at 5 P.M. Peking time (0900 GMT), Portuguese Ambassador Octavio Valerio said.     "We are very happy with the results," Valerio told reporters after four days of discussions on how and when the Portuguese-run peninsula in southern China will be handed back to Peking.
