HUGHES' CANADA RIG COUNT DOWN 19 THIS WEEK

Drilling activity slowed down substantially in Canada. The number of active rotary rigs fell by 19 in the week ended today, Hughes Tool Co reported.     A total of 66 rigs were working during the week, compared with 71 one year ago, it said.  Reuter &#3;
