WORK AT CHITTAGONG PORT HALTED BY STRIKE

Cargo handling remains halted at Bangladesh's Chittagong port since nearly 7,000 workers walked out on Monday following a pay dispute, the Port Workers Association said today.     Fourteen ships are stranded at the port.     Port officials said they would meet Association leaders today to try to resolve the dispute.  REUTER &#3;
