NATIONAL SEMI <NSM>, CANON IN DEVELOPMENT PACT

National Semiconductor Corp said it and Canon Inc <CANNY> plan to jointly develop integrated circuits and software products which will be manufactured by Canon.     The first product will be a laser-beam printer for National's Series 32000 family of 32-bit microprocessor products, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
