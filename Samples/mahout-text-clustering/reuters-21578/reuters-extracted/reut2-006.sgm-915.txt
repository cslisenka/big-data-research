ROCKWELL <ROK> UNIT, JAPAN FIRM FORM VENTURE

Allen-Bradley Co, a unit of Rockwell International Corp, said it has formed a joint venture with TDK Corp of Tokyo to produce, market and sell ferrite magnets.     Allen-Bradley will be responsible for production and TDK will handle sales and marketing. Products will be marketed through TDK Corp of America, a Chicago-based TDK unit that markets TDK's electronic components.     The venture, to be known as Allen-Bradley/TDK Magnetics, will be based in Shawnee, Okla, in the existing Allen-Bradley magnetics production facility.  Reuter &#3;
