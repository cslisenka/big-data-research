ATT AMERICAUS TRUST 2 <ATU> CLOSES EARLY

Americus Trust for American Telephone and Telegraph Co Shares, Series 2, said it has reached its limit of 10 mln ATT <T> shares and has closed.     The trust was scheduled to close April One.     Common stock tendered into an Americus trust is converted into trust units on a one share for one unit basis. Each unit may be divided into separately-traded PRIME and SCORE components.
