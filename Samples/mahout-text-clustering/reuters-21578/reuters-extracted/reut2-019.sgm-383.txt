LARISSA INDUSTRIES FILES FOR INITIAL OFFERING

Larissa Industries Inc said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an initial public offering of two mln shares of common.     All of the shares are being sold by Larissa, which makes and markets component assemblies used in automobiles, light trucks, vans, helicopters and other military vehicles and in nucelar reactors for the U.S. Navy submarines and other vessels.     The offering will be underwritten by Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and E.F. Hutton and Co Inc.  Reuter &#3;
