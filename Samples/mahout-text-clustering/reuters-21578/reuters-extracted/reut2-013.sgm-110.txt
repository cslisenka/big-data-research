AMERICAN HONDA U.S. LATE-MARCH CAR SALES UP

American Honda Motor Co said domestic car sales for the March 21-31 period rose to 16,635 from 6,358 a year ago.     It said sales for all of March advanced to 28,476 from 15,175. Year to date, the domestic car sales rose to 76,225 from 43,075.  Reuter &#3;
