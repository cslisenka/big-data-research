PULITZER<PLTZ.O> RAISES PRICE OF POST-DISPATCH

Pulitzer Publishing Co said it increased the retail price of the Sunday St. Louis Post-Dispatch to 1.00 dlrs a copy from 75 cts, effective June 14.     The Post-Dispatch, which last increased the price of its Sunday paper in 1980, said it will receive 19 cts as its share of the 25 cts increase.     For the quarter ended March 31, the Post had an average Sunday circulation of 556,620.  Reuter &#3;
