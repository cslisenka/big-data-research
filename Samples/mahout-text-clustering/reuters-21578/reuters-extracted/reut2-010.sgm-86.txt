UNITED SAVINGS INITIAL OFFERING UNDERWAY

<United Savings and Loan Association> said an initial public offering of 537,609 common shares is underway at 8.75 dlrs a share through underwriters led by Robinson-Humphrey Co Inc and Interstate Securities Corp.     Underwriters have been granted an option to buy up to 254,285 more shares to cover overallotments.     United Savings issued a total of 1,780,000 shares in converting from mutual form.  Reuter &#3;
