SHEARSON LEHMAN NAMES NEW MANAGING DIRECTOR

Shearson Lehman Brothers, a unit of American Express Co <AXP>, said Robert Stearns has joined the company as managing director of its merger and acquisition department.     Shearson said Stearns formerly was part of Merrill Lynch Pierce, Fenner and Smith Inc's <MER> merger and acquisitions department.  Reuter &#3;
