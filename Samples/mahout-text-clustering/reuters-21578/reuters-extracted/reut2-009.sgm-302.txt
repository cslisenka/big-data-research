VICORP <VRES> ENDS SALE/LEASEBACK DEAL

VICORP Restaurants Inc said by mutual agreement with United Trust Fund it has terminated the sale and leaseback of 29 Bakers Square restaurant properties.     The company completed a sale/leaseback of 31 Baker Street's with United Trust in December, raising 26.5 mln dlrs. VICORP said it does not feel it necessary to proceed with the remainder of the sale/leasebacks due to improvement in its financial condition.  Reuter &#3;
