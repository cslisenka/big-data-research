AMEX SEAT SELLS FOR RECORD 380,000 DLRS

The American Stock Exchange said a seat on the exchange sold for 380,000 dlrs, a record price that is 5,000 dlrs higher than the previous sale on Feb 17.     The current bid is 330,000 dlrs and the offer, 425,000 dlrs, the exchange said.  Reuter &#3;
