COCA-COLA ENTERPRISES <CCE> UNIT HEAD QUITS

Coca-Cola Enterprises Inc said Don Ulrich will be resigning as president of its Northeast Group to pursue other business interests.     The company said Jim Stevens, who is also a Coca-Cola Enterprises vice president and president of its Mid-Atlantic Coca-Cola Bottling Co affiliate, will succeed Ulrich.  Reuter &#3;
