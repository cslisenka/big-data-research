GM <GM> UNITS EXTEND INCENTIVES

General Motors Corp's Chevrolet and Oldsmobile divisions said they are extending cash rebates and discount financing on several car and truck lines through the summer.     Chevrolet said that through August 3 its customers could choose cash rebates of 300 dlrs to 1,000 dlrs depending on models or loan rates beginning at 3.9 pct through 9.9 pct, depending on length of contract. The program covers six car lines and S-10 Blazer and pickup trucks.     Oldsmobile said separately its cash rebates and discount financing packages with rates beginning at 3.9 pct for 24-month loans were continued retroactively to June 16 on its Delta 88 and 98 Regency models.     It gave no expiration for its incentive program.     Chevrolet said its program continues to cover its newly introduced Corsica and Beretta compact cars.     Through June 10, Chevrolet's car sales are 14.1 pct below last year's levels while Oldsmobile's retail deliveries of cars are off by 31.9 pct.  Reuter &#3;
