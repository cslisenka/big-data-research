CONSGOLD STERLING EUROBOND INCREASED, FIXED

The 15-year convertible eurobond for Consolidated Goldfields has been increased to 110 mln stg from the initial 100 mln stg and the final terms fixed, lead manager Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd said.     The coupon was set at 6-3/4 pct compared with the indicated 6-3/4 to seven pct while the conversion price was set 10.80 stg per share, a premium of some 16 pct.  REUTER &#3;
