GM <GM> CANADA MARCH CAR SALES UP 12.6 PCT

General Motors of Canada Ltd, wholly owned by General Motors Corp, said March car sales increased 12.6 pct to 38,520 units from 34,209 units a year earlier.     Year-to-date car sales fell 10.1 pct to 82,000 units from 91,243 units in the same period last year, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
