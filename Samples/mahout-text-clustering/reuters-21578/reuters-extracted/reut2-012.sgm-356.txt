FEDS OKAY WESTERN CAROLINA SAVINGS CONVERSION

Western Carolina Savings and Loan Association said the Federal Home Loan Bank Board approved the completion of its conversion to a state chartered stock savings and loan from a state chartered mutual bank.     Carolina said 575,000 shares were subscribed for at 10 dlrs per share during its initial offering.     It said trading will begin in its stock April eight on NASDAQ under the sumbol WCAR.
