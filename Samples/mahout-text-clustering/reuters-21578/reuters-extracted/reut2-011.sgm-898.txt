<ANCHOR SAVINGS BANK> TO TRADE ON NASDAQ

Anchor Savings Bank said it expects to start trading on the NASDAQ system on a when-issued basis on April Seven under the ticker symbol <ABKR>.     Anchor recently went public in a subscription offering of 17,595,000 shares that was oversubscribed and increased by 2,295,000 shares from the number originally planned.  Reuter &#3;
