BOEING <BA> UNIT GETS 31.3 MLN DLR NAVY CONTRACT

Boeing Co's Boeing Aerospace Co is being awarded 31.3 mln dlrs under a contract for spare and repair parts for the E-6A aircraft, the Defense Department said.     It said work on the contract is expected to be completed in December 1992.  Reuter &#3;
