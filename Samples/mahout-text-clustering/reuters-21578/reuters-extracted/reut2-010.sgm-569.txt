BANDO MCGLOCKLIN FILES INITIAL PUBLIC OFFERING

<Bando McGlocklin Capital Corp> said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission coverig a proposed initial public offering of 750,000 shares of common stock.     The closed end investment company estimated the offering's price at between seven dlr and eight dlrs a share.     The company said it is selling all the shares under the underwriting group management of The Milwaukee Co.     Bando said it will use the net proceeds to repay loans and reduce debt.  Reuter &#3;
