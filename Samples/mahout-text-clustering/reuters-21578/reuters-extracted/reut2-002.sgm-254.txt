TEKELEC <TKLC> SETS PACT WITH BRITISH TELECOM

Tekelec said it signed a license agreement with British Telecommunications PLC <BTY.L> to make and market a test instrument for digital microwave communications equipment.     Tekelec said the new product is expected to be priced at 25,000 dlrs and to be shipped in the second half of 1987.     The 20-year, non-exclusive agreement grants Tekelec the right to manufacture and market the instrument in North America and Japan.  Reuter &#3;
