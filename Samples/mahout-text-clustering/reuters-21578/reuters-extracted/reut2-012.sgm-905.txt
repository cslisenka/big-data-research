WESTON GETS U.S. AIR FORCE CONTRACT

<Roy F. Weston Inc> said it received a 9.9 mln dlr contract from the U.S. Air Force for assistance with site investigations and remedial studies at Air Force bases throughout the nation.     The contract covers four years and its Weston's fourth major contract with the Air Force's occupational environmental health laboratory.     The company said it will test air, soils, ground and surface waters site assessments and other areas.  Reuter &#3;
