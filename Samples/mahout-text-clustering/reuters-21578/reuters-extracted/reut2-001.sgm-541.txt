NATIONAL SEMI <NSM> UNIT STEPS UP DELIVERIES

National Semiconductor Corp's National Advanced Systems unit said it is accelerating the delivery schedule for the two most powerful models of its AS/XL series mainframe processors because of growing customer demand.     The company said the AS/XL 90 and AS/XL 100, which had been scheduled for general availability in the third quarter, will now be available in the second quarter.     National Advanced Systems also announced a new 7480 cartridge tape subsystem which uses 18 track compact tape cartridges.  Reuter &#3;
