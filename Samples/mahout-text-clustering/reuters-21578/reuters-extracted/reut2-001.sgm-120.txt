CHRYSLER <C> FEBRUARY U.S. CAR OUTPUT DOWN

Chrysler Corp said its February U.S. car production was 110,552 compared with 123,092 a year ago.     The number three automaker said U.S. truck production totalled 21,177 compared with nine last year. The figures exclude Canadian truck production, much of which is sold to U.S. dealers.     Year-to-date, Chrysler said car output declined to 216,987 from 257,941 and U.S. truck production totalled 39,834 compared with none a year ago.      Reuter &#3;
