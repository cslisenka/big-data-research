NORTHERN TELECOM <NT> IN PHONE PACT WITH CHINA

Northern Telecom Ltd said it signed a contract with the Peking Telecommunications Administration and the Peking Foreign Trade Corp to supply an SL-1 automatic call distributor system for Peking's central telephone office.     Financial terms were undisclosed.     It said Peking's telephone company would use the system to receive and distribute incoming information requests more effectively among operators.     The company said it and Peking's telephone administration were discussing cooperation on other telecommunications systems.  Reuter &#3;
