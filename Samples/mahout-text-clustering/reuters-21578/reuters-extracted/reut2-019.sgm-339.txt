ROCKWELL <ROK> BUYS SOFTECH <SOFT.O> PRODUCTS

Rockwell International purchased SofTech Inc's newest line of Ada-86 tools to use in building a digital autopilot for the anti-tank missle, Hellfire, SofTech said.     SofTech said the contract, which was awarded to Rockwell's Duluth Ga. division, will involve more than 10 man years of programming for each digital autopilot.  Reuter &#3;
