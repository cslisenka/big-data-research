MINNEAPOLIS HFCS TRADE PUT AT 109 CONTRACTS

An estimated 109 high fructose corn syrup futures contracts traded today in the contract's second day of trading at the Minneapolis Grain Exchange (MGE), an exchange spokesman said.     Trading was down from yesterday's 137 contracts. Open interest yesterday totaled 42, the spokesman said.     July HFCS futures closed at 12.70 dlrs per cwt, up 50 cents from its open of 12.20 dlrs. September closed at 12.35 dlrs, up 55, and December settled at 11.70 dlrs with no trades, up 20 cents from yesterday's close, the source said.   Reuter &#3;
