CHADIAN TROOPS REPORTEDLY RECAPTURE FAYA

Chadian troops have recaptured the northern oasis of Faya Largeau, which was the main base of Libyan troops in northern Chad, sources close to the government said.     There was no official confirmation, but Chadian radio played martial music and asked listeners to stay tuned.  Reuter &#3;
