FRANCE ANNOUNCES 10.5 BILLION FRANC T-BILL AUCTION

The Bank of France announced that it will publicly auction 10.5 billion francs of negotiable Treasury bills on March 16.     The auction will comprise 3.5 billion francs of 13-week bills, 3.5 billion francs of 26 week bills and 3.5 billion francs of 52-week bills, all fixed rate.  Reuter &#3;
