AMC <AMO> FEBRUARY CAR OUTPUT DECLINES

American Motors Corp said its February U.S. car production declined to 2,978 units from 3,808 a year ago.     AMC said its U.S. jeep production rose to 18,651 from 16,673 last year.     Year-to-date, AMC said its car output declined to 6,6069 from 6,631 and jeep production declined to 37,207 from 40,586 in the comparable 1986 period.  Reuter &#3;
