CNW <CNW> SELLS BONDS

CNW Corp said its Chicago and North Western Transportation Co sold 25 mln dlrs of consolidated mortgage 9.93 pct bonds, series C, due 1992, in a private placement.     Morgan Stanley and Co Inc was the placement agent. The bonds were sold yesterday.     Proceeds of the bond sale will be used for general corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
