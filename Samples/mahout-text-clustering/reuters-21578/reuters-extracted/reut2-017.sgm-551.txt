CIRCUS CIRCUS <CIR> TO HAVE 2ND QTR CHARGE

Circus Circus Enterprises Inc said it expects to report a charge of about six mln dlrs or 16 cts per share against results for the second quarter ending July 31 due to a tender for all 75 mln dlrs of its first mortgage bonds that expires June Two.     In last year's second quarter, the company took a 6,200,000 dlr or 16 ct per share charge from the redemption of 80 mln dlrs principal amount of subordinated notes.  Reuter &#3;
