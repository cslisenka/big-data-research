FED SETS TWO-DAY SYSTEM REPURCHASES

The Federal Reserve entered the government securities market to arrange two-day repurchase agreements for system account, a spokesman for the New York Fed said.     Federal funds were trading at 6-3/4 pct at the time of the direct injection of temporary reserves, in line with Friday's 6.72 pct average.  Reuter &#3;
