TIMES MIRROR <TMC> SELLS TIMBERLANDS

Times Mirror Co said it completed the sale of 26,000 acres of timberlands located in Washington and Oregon to <Fibre Co>.     Terms were not disclosed.     The company said the sale was part of its ongoing program to divest its timberland holdings in the Pacific Northwest.  Reuter &#3;
