TALMAN HOME FEDERAL PREFERRED OFFERING STARTS

Lead underwriters Morgan Stanley Group Inc <MS>, <Goldman, Sachs and Co> and <Salomon Inc> said an offering of 1,000 shares of market auction preferred stock of <Talman Home Federal Savings and Loan Association of Illinois'> Talman Finance Corp C unit is underway at 100,000 dlrs a share.     It said the initial dividend rate is 4.35 pct and the diovidend will be reset every 49 days in a Dutch auction process.  The first Dutch action date is May 12.  Reuter &#3;
