COLOROCS <CLRX> TO BE ISSUED U.S. PATENT

Colorocs Corp said it has been notified the company's first patent for "Improved Print Engine for Color Electrophotography" has been granted and will be issued tomorrow.     The company said the patent embodies discoveries made that enabled it to develop its proprietary full color print engine which will be applied to copiers, computer printers and facsimile equipment. The first product to embody the technology is a full color copier which will be manufactured for Colorocs by <Sharp Corp>.  Reuter &#3;
