IBM <IBM> TO SHIP NEW MODEL TWO MONTHS EARLIER

International Business Machines Corp said it will begin shipping its mid-range 9370 Information System up to two months earlier than previously planned.     IBM said model 20 and 60 processors with a selected set of features will be available starting July 1987. Other 9370 processor models will be available starting October 1987, it said.     In addition, the company said shipments of the 9370 Information System will begin this month to customers particiaping int he early support program.   Reuter &#3;
