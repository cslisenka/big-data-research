PURITAN-BENNETT <PBEN> MAKES ACQUISITION

Puritan-Bennedtt Corp said it has acquired a majority interest in Medicom Inc, which makes a heart monitor for use in diagnosing heart disorders, for undisclosed terms.     The company said the device will be sold under the name Companion Heart Monitor.  Reuter &#3;
