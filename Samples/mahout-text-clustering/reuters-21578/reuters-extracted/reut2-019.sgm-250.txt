FIRST WISCONSIN <FWB> DEBT AFFIRMED BY MOODY'S

Moody's Investors Service Inc said it affirmed First Wisconsin Corp's 50 mln dlrs of debt.     Affirmed were the firm's Baa-1 senior debt and Baa-2 preferred stock.     Moody's noted that First Wisconsin said it would add a special loan loss provision of 96 mln dlrs to reflect exposure to economically troubled international borrowers. The provision adjusts the firm's financial statement to more accurately reflect economic reality, the agency said.  Reuter &#3;
