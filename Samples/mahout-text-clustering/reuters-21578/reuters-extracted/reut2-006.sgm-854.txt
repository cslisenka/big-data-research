WICKES COMPANIES <WIX> COMPLETES SALE OF UNIT

Wickes Companies Inc said it completed the sale of its Sequoia Supply division to a new company created by the management of that division.     Paul Hylbert, president of Sequoia, has been named president and chief executive officer of the new company.  Reuter &#3;
