GM UNIT <GMH> NEGOTIATING FOR BRITISH CONTRACT

General Motors Corp's Hughes Aircraft Co subsidiary said it has been chosen by British Satellite Broadcasting to negotiate for a contract worth about 300 mln dlrs to deliver two satellites for direct broadcast television.     The company said the first of the satellites is scheduled to start up three channels of direct broadcast satellite television by late 1989.     The company said the contract should be signed by June 30.  Each satellite would be equipped with three 110-watt channels.  Reuter &#3;
