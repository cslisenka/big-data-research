WERNER ENTERPRISES <WERN> OFFERS TWO MLN SHARES

Werner Enterprises Inc said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission covering a public offering of two million shares of common stock.     The underwriters will be led by Alex, Brown and Sons Inc, along with the Robinson-Humphrey Co Inc, the company said.     The company said it will sell 800,000 shares, while 1,200,000 shares will be sold by five selling stockholders.  Reuter &#3;
