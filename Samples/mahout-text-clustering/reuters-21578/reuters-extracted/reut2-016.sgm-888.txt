NWA <NWA> NAMES NEW HEAD FOR UNIT

NWA Inc said Edward A. Neer Jr. has been named president and chief executive officer of its Mainline Travel Inc subsidiary, replacing Warren D. Phillips as chief executive and Robert L. Daniels asd president.     Neer had been western regional sales director of NWA's Northwest Airlines unit.     It said Daniels has been named president of Mainline's retail division.  Reuter &#3;
