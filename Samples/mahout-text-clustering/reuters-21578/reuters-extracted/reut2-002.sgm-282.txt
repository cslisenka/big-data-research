DIAMOND-BATHURST <DBH> SAYS FURNACE EXPLODES

Diamond-Bathurst Inc said an explosion yesterday during a furnace rebuild at its Antioch, Calif., facility completely destroyed the furnace.     The glassmaker said the rest of the facility was undamaged and there were no serious injuries.  It said it believes it is fully covered by insurance.     The company said it cannot yet determine the impact on the plant's operations.  Reuter &#3;
