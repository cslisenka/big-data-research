3M <MMM> CANNOT ACCOUNT FOR STOCK RISE

Minnesota Mining and Manufacturing Co knows of no reason for the rise in its stock, trading at 129, up 3-1/4 on the New York Stock Exchange, a company spokeswoman told Reuters.     She said the company had issued no news today that might account for the increase.  Reuter &#3;
