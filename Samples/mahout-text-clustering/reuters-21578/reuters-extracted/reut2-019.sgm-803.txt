CONTROL DATA <CDA> GETS 13.7 MLN DLR CONTRACT

Control Data Corp said the Aeronautical Systems Division of the U.S. Air Force awarded it a 13.7 mln dlr contract to provide a Central Datacomm System.     It said the system will act as the central interface between numerous user organizations and the division's Information Systems and Technology Center at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base in Ohio.     Terms of the five-year, two-phase contract call for Control Data to supply equipment, software, maintenance, training and support services.  Reuter &#3;
