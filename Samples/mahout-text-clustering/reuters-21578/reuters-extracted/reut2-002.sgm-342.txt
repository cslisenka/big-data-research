ENERGY OPTICS TO DEVELOP RANGE-FINDER FOR ROBOT

<Energy Optics Inc> said it has been selected by the U.S. Navy to develop a low cost, optical triangulation system.     Energy Optics said the Navy awareded a Phase I contract to investigate various analog and digital design alternatives and select the best for development and demonstration of a prototype.     The company's statement gave no value for this contract, but said this task is expected to take six months.  Reuter &#3;
