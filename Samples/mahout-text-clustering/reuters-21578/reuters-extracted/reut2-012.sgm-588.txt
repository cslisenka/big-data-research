IBM <IBM> DOWN ON LOWER MORGAN STANLEY OPINION

The stock of International Business Machine Corp declined slightly today and shares of Digital Equipment Corp <DEC> rose after brokerage house Morgan Stanley and Co reiterated a negative opinion on IBM and a buy on Digital, traders said.     IBM, which this morning introduced four new personal computers, the first major overhaul of its PC line since it entered the business in 1981, fell 1-3/4 to 149-3/8.     Digital Equipment rose 1-3/8 to 163-5/8.     Analyst Carol Muratore of Morgan Stanley was unavailable for comment. &#3;
