BRITISH CALEDONIAN SEEKS SAN DIEGO SERVICE

British Caledonian Airways said it has filed an application with the British Civil Aviation Authority for a license to operate between San Diego's Lindbergh Field and London's Gatwick Airport.     It said it would extend its existing Los Angeles/London nonstop service to San Diego and would initially offer three roundtrips weekly.  Reuter &#3;
