TRAVELERS REAL ESTATE <TRAT.O> PAYOUT CUT

Travelers Real Estate Investment Trust     Qtly div 17 cts vs 23 cts in prior qtr     Payable November 25     Record October 30     It said the lower dividend reflects the reduction in cash flow from a mortgage secured by a motel in Covington, La.     The trust said an appraisal is being made of the Covington property to determine whether an increase in loss reserve will be required at year end.         Travelers REIT said its investment adviser, Keystone Realty Advisers has committed to lend the trust up to 500,000 dlrs for a term of two years to cover past due payables and capital improvements on the Covington motel.     Keystone Realty is an affiliate of the Keystone Group, a Traverlers Corp <TIC> subsidiary.  Reuter &#3;
