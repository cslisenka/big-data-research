FIRST CHICAGO <FNB> TO BUY WOOD GUNDY STAKE

First Chicago Corp said its First National Bank of Chicago subsidiary agreed in principle to acquire a 35 pct interest in Wood Gundy Corp, an international investment banking firm headquartered in Toronto.     Under the agreement, the bank, through its Canadian bank subsidiary, First National Bank of Chicago (Canada) will invest 271 mln canadian dlrs in a combination of newly issued common equity and convertible debentures of Wood Gundy. First Chicago also agreed to invest additional funds jointly with Wood Gundy in venture capital and merchant banking businesses. Wood Gundy shareholders will retain 65 pct ownership.  Reuter &#3;
