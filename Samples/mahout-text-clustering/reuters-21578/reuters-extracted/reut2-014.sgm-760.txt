TCW CONVERTIBLE SECURITIES <CVT> SETS DIVIDEND

TCW Convertible Securities Fund Inc said its board declared an initial quarterly dividend of three cents per share, payable April 30 to shareholders of record April 17.     TCW said it anticipates paying a regular quarterly dividend. The company made its initial public stock offering March five.  Reuter &#3;
