FINANCIAL TIMES ADDS NEW INDICES

<Goldman, Sachs and Co> said <Pearson PLC's> Financial Times newspaper has started publishing the daily FT-Actuaries World Indices, which are jointly compiled with Goldman Sachs, broker <Wood Mackenzie and Co Ltd> and the British Institute of Actuaries.     The company said the indices include a main world share index and indices plotting share prices in different geographical regions and individual countries, as well as a number of international industrial indices.     Goldman said the indices are based on the price of about 2,400 issues from 23 stock markets.  Reuter &#3;
