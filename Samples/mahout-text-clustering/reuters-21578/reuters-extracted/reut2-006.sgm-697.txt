EAGLE ENTERTAINMENT <EEGL> ISSUES BONDS

Eagle Entertainment Inc said its subsidiary, Performance Guarantees Inc, issued completion bonds for two films with a production value of five mln dlrs.     The company said the it would receive 250,000 dlrs in fees for providing the completion bonds.     Both films, Green Monkey and Street Justice, are being produced by Sandy Howard.  Reuter &#3;
