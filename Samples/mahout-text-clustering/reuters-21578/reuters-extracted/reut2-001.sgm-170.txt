DATACORPY <DCPY> IN PACT WITH XEROX <XRX>

Datacopy Corp said it has signed a joint marketing agreement for its Model 730 office image scanner with Xerox Corp.     Under the pact, Xerox sales force will reference-sell the scanner as its choice for desktop publishing applications.    Datacopy will supply Xerox with technical support and respond to customer orders within 30 days of the purchase order.      Reuter &#3;
