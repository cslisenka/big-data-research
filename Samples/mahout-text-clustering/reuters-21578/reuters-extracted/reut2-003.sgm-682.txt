TENNECO <TGT> DECLINES COMMENT ON STOCK

The New York Stock Exchange said Tenneco Inc declined to comment on whether there were any corporate developments that might explain the unusual activity in its stock.     The exchange said it had requested a statement from the company in light of the activity. Tenneco was up 1-7/8 dlrs to 48-5/8.  Reuter &#3;
