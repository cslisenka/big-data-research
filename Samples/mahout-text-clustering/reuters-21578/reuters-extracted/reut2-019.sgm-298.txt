DUFF/PHELPS LOWERS INTERNATIONAL PAPER <IP> DEBT

Duff and Phelps said it lowered its ratings on senior debt and preferred stock to DP-7 (low A) from DP-6 (high A), affecting nearly 670 mln dlrs in debt securities.     The action reflects the increased financial leverage resulting from the company's 1986 acquisition of Hammmermill Paper, which raised the fixed obligations ratio to 34 pct, compared with 26 pct in 1985, Duff and Phelps said.
