TEXAS CAPITAL BANCSHARES UNIT FIRST TO FILE

<Texas Capital Bancshares> said its subsidiary, Texas Capital Bank-Westwood, was the first Texas bank to file for a statewide branch to be located in Austin.     The company said it filed with the Comptroller of the Currency under a 1986 state constitutional amendment allowing Texas banks to establish a limit of three branches within its home county.
