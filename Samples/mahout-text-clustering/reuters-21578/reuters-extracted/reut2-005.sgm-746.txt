FNMA <FNM> WILL NOT BUY HOME-EQUITY LOANS

The Federal National Mortgage Association said it will not purchase home-equity loans as part of its mortgage-purchase and swap activities in the secondary market.     "We don't believe that our provision of liquidity is needed in this segment of the market," David Maxwell, Fannie Mae's chairman, said.  Reuter &#3;
