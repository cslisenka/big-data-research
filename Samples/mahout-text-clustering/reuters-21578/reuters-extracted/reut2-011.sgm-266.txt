OXFORD ENERGY <OEN> TO OFFER SHARES

Oxford Energy Co said it expects to file later today for an offering of 1,200,000 common shares through underwriters led by Bear Stearns Cos Inc <BSC>.     The company said proceeds will be used for waste tire incineration projects and other corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
