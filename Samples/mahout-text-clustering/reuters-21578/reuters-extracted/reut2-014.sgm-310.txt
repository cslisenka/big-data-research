LONDON COFFEE CLOSES FIRMER IN DULL TRADING

Robusta futures closed firmer after a lacklustre session, ranging 19 to three stg per tonne higher. Second position July settled up 19 at 1,270 stg.     The rise mostly reflected a technical consolidation of recent losses, dealers said. Roaster interest appeared to be tapering off and origin offers were insignificant, providing little trading incentive.     Now that Brazil has closed its May registrations, the market is awaiting fresh fundamental news, they added.     Volume totalled 4,170 lots, including six options and 1,600 crosses.  REUTER &#3;
