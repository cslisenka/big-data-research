IBERDUERO ISSUES 150 MLN SWISS FRANC BOND

Hidroelectrica Iberica Iberduero S.A. (Iberduero) of Bilbao, Spain is issuing a 150 mln Swiss franc eight year bond with a five pct coupon and 100-1/4 issue price, lead manager Union Bank of Switzerland said.     The bond is on sale until June 26 with payment due on July 9.     It can be called at 101-1/2 in 1991 and for tax reasons only at 102 in 1988.  REUTER &#3;
