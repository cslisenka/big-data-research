SOCAL EDISON <SCE> SIGNS HOOVER DAM POWER PACT

Southern California Edison said it signed a 30-year contract with the federal government for hydroelectric power from Hoover Dam.     Southern California Edison said it would receive some 277.5 megawatts of electrical power under the contract, enough to serve about 160,000 homes.     Terms of the contract were not disclosed.     The original 50-year contracts for Hoover Dam hydroelectric power are scheduled to expire at the end of May, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
