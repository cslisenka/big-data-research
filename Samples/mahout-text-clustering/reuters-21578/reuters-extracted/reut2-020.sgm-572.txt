BORDEN <BN> SAYS PLANS TO ISSUE DEBT

Borden Inc said it plans to issue up to 250 mln dlrs of long-term debt securities in order to capitalize on sharply lower interest rates.     Proceeds will be used primarily to refinance existing commercial paper.     The company said the offering of long-term debt will be in addition to its previously announced offering of a master limited partnership interest relating to Borden's basic chemicals and plastics operations.
