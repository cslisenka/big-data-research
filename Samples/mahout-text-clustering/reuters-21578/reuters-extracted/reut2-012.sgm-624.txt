GENERAL AUTOMATION <GENA> COMPLETES PURCHASE

General Automation Inc said it completed its previously announced acquisition of Parallel Computers Inc, of Santa Clara, Calif.     The company said the purchase price consisted of 615,000 General Automation restricted shares together with 5-year warrants to buy an aggregate 445,000 additional shares at 5.25 dlrs each.
