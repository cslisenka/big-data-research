UNITED <UAL> TO BEGIN BURBANK-DENVER ROUTE

UAL Inc's United Airlines said it will offer scheduled service between Burbank, Calif., and Denver, Colo., starting June five.     United said it will operate two daily round-trip flights in the Burbank to Denver market using new Boeing Co <BA> 737-300 aircraft.     United said as a promotion for its new route it will offer a bonus of double mileage to all members of its frequent flyer prgram, Mileage Plus, who fly the Burbank-Denver route during the first 45 days of service.
