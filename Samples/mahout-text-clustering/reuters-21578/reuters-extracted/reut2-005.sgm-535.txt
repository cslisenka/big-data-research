GENRAD <GEN> SHIPS CHIP TEST SYSTEM

GenRad Inc said its Semiconductor Test Group shipped a GR125 VSLI test system to Marshall Industries <MI>.     GenRad said the new digital integrated circuit tester will be used in Marshall's Milpitas plant for retest and certification of programmable logic devices that Marshall distributes.  Reuter &#3;
