GREEN MOUNTAIN <GMP> TO SELL POWER TO UTILITY

Green Mountain Power Corp said it signed an agreement to provide <Bozrah Light and Power Co>, a private utility based in Gilman, Conn., with all its electric power requirements.     Under the agreement, which runs through 1996, Green Mountain said it will sell the utility roughly 30,000 megawatt-hours, generating about 1.5 mln dlrs in revenues.  Reuter &#3;
