SSMC <SSM> FORMS ELECTRICAL PRODUCTS DIVISION

SSMC Inc said it formed a separate division to consolidate the manufacture and marketing of its motors for the appliance, computer and automotive industries.     SSMC was spun-off from Singer Co <SMF> in July and operates the former sewing machines and furniture businesses of Singer.  Reuter &#3;
