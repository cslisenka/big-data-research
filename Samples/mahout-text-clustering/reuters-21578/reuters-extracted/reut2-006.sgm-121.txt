FEDERAL PAPER BOARD <FBO> TO SELL PREFERRED

Federal Paper Board Co said its board has approved the issuance of 140 mln dlrs of convertible preferred stock, with proceeds to be used to redeem on June 15 its 125 mln dlrs in 13 pct subordinated debentures.     The company also said it has completed the redemption of 2.3125 dlr convertible exchangeable preferred stock, most of which was converted into common instead of being redeemed.  Reuter &#3;
