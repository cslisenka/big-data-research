NISSAN U.S. CAR SALES RISE IN LATE MARCH

Nissan Motor Corp in USA, a unit of Nissan Motor Co Ltd, said its domestic car sales for the March 21-31 period rose 82.8 pct to 4,635 from 2,536 a year ago.     It said automobile sales for the month gained 37.6 pct to 10,130 from 7,363 and year to date they increased to 23,899 from 20,209.     Nissan said late-March U.S. truck sales increased 15.5 pct to 3,567 from 3,089. The month's truck sales declined 4.2 pct to 7,271 from 7,592, and year to date, sales increased to 20,398 from 18,477.
