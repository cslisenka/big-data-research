TEXAS UTILTIES <TXU> UNIT SEEKS RATE REFUND

Texas Utilities Co's TU Electric unit said it filed an application with the Texas Public Utilities Commission for authorization to refund about 69.7 mln dlrs to customers.     The refund, TU's fifth since May 1986, is made possible by lower costs for fuel to make electricity, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
