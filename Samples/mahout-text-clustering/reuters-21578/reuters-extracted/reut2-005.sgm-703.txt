ROYALE AIRLINES <RYAL> REPORTS LOAD FACTOR UP

Royale Airlines Inc said its load factor for February was 42.9 pct, up from 42.6 pct the same period last year.     Royale said it recorded 4,180,000 revenue passenger miles compared to 5,681,000 the same period the year before.     Royale said it cut its available capacity 26 pct in response to regional weaknesses, which resulted in a slightly higher load factor this past month.  Reuter &#3;
