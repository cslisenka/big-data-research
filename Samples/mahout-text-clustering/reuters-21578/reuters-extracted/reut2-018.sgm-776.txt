SECURITY PACIFIC <SPC> UNIT'S SHELF EFFECTIVE

Security Pacific National Bank, a unit of Security Pacific Corp, said the Securities and Exchange Commission declared effective its shelf registration statement covering 1.2 billion dlrs of mortgage related securities.     The bank said it filed the registration statement in April. This is the bank's second shelf registration filed in connection with Security Pacific Merchant Bank's efforts to securitize mortgages originated by Security Pacific National, it said.  Reuter &#3;
