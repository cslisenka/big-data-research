BELL SOUTH <BLS> UNIT TO BUY 600,000 LINES

Bell South Corp's South Central Bell operating company plans to purchase 600,000 lines of digital central office equipment by the end of the decade from a subsidiary of <Plessey Co Ltd>, Plessey said.     Plessey's subsidiary, Stromberg-Carlson, said the value of the purchase will be between 100 mln dlrs and 200 mln dlrs. It said it received the notification of the purchase following the conclusion of a digital switch study performed by BellSouth Services.  Reuter &#3;
