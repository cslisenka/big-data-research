XTRA <XTR> TO BUY FRUEHAUF <FTR> UNIT

XTRA Corp said it agreed to acquire all the stock of RentCo Trailer Corp, a wholly owned subsidiary of Fruehauf Corp for about 70 mln dlrs.     RentCo had revenues of about 70 mln dlrs.     The transaction is expected to be completed in April and is subject to regulatory approval.  Reuter &#3;
