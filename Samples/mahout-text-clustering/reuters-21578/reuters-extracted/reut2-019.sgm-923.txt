BALLY <BLY> TO REDEEM PREFERRED SHARES

Bally Manufacturing Corp said it called for optional redemption of its outstanding Series E increasing dividend preferred stock on August 1 at 50 dlrs a share.     The redemption price will be payable on August 1.     The regular quarterly dividend of 1.50 dlrs a share will be paid August 1, record July 15.  Reuter &#3;
