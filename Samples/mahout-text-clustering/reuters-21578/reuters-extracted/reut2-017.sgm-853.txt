HYCOR BIO <HYBD.O> FORMS UNIT TO SELL PRODUCT

Hycor Biomedical Inc said it was forming a unit, Hycor Instruments Inc, to distribute a line of chemistry analyzers made by <JEOL Ltd> of Japan.     The product, called a "clinalyzer," is an automated blood analyzer capable of providing diagnostic and other tests for hospitals and clinics, the company said.     The company also said it has discussed distributing other products made by JEOL.   Reuter &#3;
