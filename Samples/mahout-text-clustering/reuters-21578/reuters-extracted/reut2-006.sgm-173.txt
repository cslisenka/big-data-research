CENTRAL BANCORPORATION <CBAN> CHIEF TO RETIRE

Central Bancorporation Inc said Oliver W. Birckhead, 64, president and chief executive officer of the company and chairman and CEO of its Central Trust Co NA Cincinnati unit, will retire on December 31.     Central Bancorporation said Noble O. Carpenter will succeed Birckhead at the holding company on January one, 1988.     The company said Carpenter is currently executive vice president of the holding company and president and CEO of Central Trust Co of Northeastern Ohio NA.   Reuter &#3;
