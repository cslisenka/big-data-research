LOPAT INDUSTRIES <LPAT.O> HAS PROFITABLE MONTH

Lopat Industries Inc said it had its first profitable month of operations in May, earning 47,900 dlrs on revenues of 162,200 dlrs.     Lopat said "The May results signal the start of profitable operations and growing revenues over the balance of the year."  Reuter &#3;
