OLIN CORP <OLM> TO ELECT NEW CEO IN APRIL

Olin Corp said its board will elect in April John Johnstone Jr as its chief executive officer.     The company said he will succeed John M Henske, who is also chairman. It said Johnstone, 54, is now president and chief operating officer.     Henske, 53, has served as CEO since 1978 and chairman since 1980. He will continue as chairman until his retirement in June 1988.  Reuter &#3;
