CENTRONICS <CEN> CHAIRMAN RESIGNS

Centronics Corp said its chairman, Stephen Weinroth, resigned his post to devote more time to his duties as managing director of Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc.     The company said Thomas Kamp, formerly vice chairman, was named to replace Weinroth.  Reuter &#3;
