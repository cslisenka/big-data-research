PIZZA INN <PZA> TO MAKE ANNOUNCEMENT

Pizza Inn Inc said it will be making an announcement sometime this morning.     The company declined to discuss details of the announcement.     The American Stock Exchange delayed trading Pizza Inn shares this morning for news pending.     Pizza Inn recently received a buyout proposal from Concept Development Inc <CDII> for an exchange of stock and cash, subject to Concept's ability to get the necessary financing.  Reuter &#3;
