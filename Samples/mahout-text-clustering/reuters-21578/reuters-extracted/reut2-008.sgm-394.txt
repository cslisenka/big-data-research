VENTRA, MODULAR FORM JOINT VENTURE

<Ventra Management Inc> said it has formed a joint venture that it will manage to provide lease financing for <Modular Computer Systems Inc>     Ventra said it will fund leases with the proceeds of a 60 mln dlr leasing equipment receivable bond offering underwritten by Mabon, Nugent and Co.  It said the venture has already generated about 10 mln dlrs in computer leases.  Reuter &#3;
