LOGICON INC <LGN> GETS ADD ON TO CONTRACT

Logicon Inc said it received a three-mln-dlr modification to its contract to develop cruise missile simulation systems for the U.S. Air Force.     The company said the modification brings the contract to 12.6 mln dlrs.     In addition, a separate 500,000-dlr option may be exercised after the basic simulation systems and software simulation are delivered in September, 1988, it said.  Reuter &#3;
