COMPUTER FIRM SELLS 5.4 PCT XYVISION <XYV> STAKE

A group led by Leading Edge Hardware Products Inc, a Massachusetts computer firm, told the Securities and Exchange Commission it sold its entire 5.4 pct stake in Xyvision Inc.     The group, which includes Leading Edge and companies affiliated with it, said it sold the 324,200 Xyvision common shares between February 27 and April 1 for a total of 5.0 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
