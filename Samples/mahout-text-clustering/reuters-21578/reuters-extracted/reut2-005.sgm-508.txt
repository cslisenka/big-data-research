NYFE SEAT SELLS FOR 200 DLRS

The New York Stock Exchange said a seat sold on the New York Futures Exchange for 200 dlrs, unchanged from the previous sale on March 13.     It also said a second seat sold on the NYFE for 100 dlrs, down 100 dlrs from the previous sale.     The current bid is for 100 dlrs and the offer is for 300 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
