TOSHIBA TO SET UP MICROCHIP PARTNERSHIP IN U.S.

Toshiba Corp <TSBA.T> will set up a five-year technology partnership with California-based SDA Systems Inc with the intention of designing and producing computer microchips, a company spokesman said.     Toshiba's investment in the project is likely to total less than 10 mln dlrs over the five years, he said.  REUTER &#3;
