MCDONNELL DOUGLAS <MD> GETS ORDER

McDonnell Douglas Corp said it received its first order for an executive jet model of its MD-80 aircraft from Ginji Yasuda, owner of Aladdin Hotel and Casino in Las Vegas, NEvada.     A spokesman said the commercial model of the aircraft sells for about 26 mln dlrs, but would not disclose the value of the customized version of the MD-80.  Reuter &#3;
