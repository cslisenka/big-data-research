ZAYRE <ZY> MARCH SALES UP 12.7 PCT

Zayre Corp said sales for the five weeks ended April Four were up 12.7 pct to 522.3 mln dlrs from 463.5 mln dlrs a year before.     The company said sales for the first nine weeks of its fiscal year were up 17.4 pct to 849.3 mln dlrs from 723.7 mln dlrs a year before.  Reuter &#3;
