AMOSKEAG BANK <AMKG> TO ACQUIRE ENTREPO

Amoskeag Bank said it signed an agreement to acquire <Entrepo Financial Resources Inc>, a Philadelphia-based company which leases and remarkets high technology equipment.     Terms of the acquisition were not disclosed.     It said Entrepo has assets of 20 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
