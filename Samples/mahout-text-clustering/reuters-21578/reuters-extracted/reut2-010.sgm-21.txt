CIRCLE K <CKP> OFFICIAL INCREASES HOLDINGS

Circle K Corp said its chairman, Karl Eller, will buy 750,000 shares of Circle K common stock from the company's founder and Vice Chairman, Fred Hervey, in a private transaction.     After the sale, Hervey will directly or beneficially own 6,764,004 shares, or 13.61 pct of Circle K stock and Eller will hold 2,873,300 shares, of 5.78 pct of the stock.  Reuter &#3;
