MCRAE INDUSTRIES <MRI-A> INCREASES PAYOUT

McCrae Industries Inc said it raised its preferred dividend on its Class A common stock to 12 cts per share from 11 cts per share.     It said the dividend was payable April 20, 1987, to shareholders of record April 6.  Reuter &#3;
