BANGLADESH AUTHORIZED TO BUY PL 480 COTTON-USDA

Bangladesh has been authorized to purchase about 32,000 bales (480 lbs) of U.S. cotton under an existing PL 480 agreement, the U.S. Agriculture Department said.     It may buy the cotton, valued at 10.0 mln dlrs, between May 6 and August 15, 1987 and ship it by September 15, the department said.  Reuter &#3;
