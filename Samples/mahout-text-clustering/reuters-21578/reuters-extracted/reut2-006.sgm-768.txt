WESTERN WASTE <WWIN> DENIES CHARGES

Western Waste Industries said it believes charges alleging that the company illegally transported and disposed of hazardous waste are unfounded.     A complaint, filed by the Los Angeles County District Attorney's office, alleges that the company was involved in the disposal of materials at county landfills, Western Waste said.  Reuter &#3;
