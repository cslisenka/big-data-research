CONSUMERS <CMS> SELLS BONDS IN TWO TRANCHES

Consumers Power Co is raising 250 mln dlrs through a two-tranche offering of first mortgage bonds, said lead manager Morgan Stanley and Co Inc.     A 150 mln dlr offering due 1992 was given a nine pct coupon and par pricing to yield 112.5 basis points over comparable Treasuries. This tranche is non-callable for life.     A companion 100 mln dlr issue maturing in 1997 was assigned a 9-5/8 pct coupon and priced at 98.50 to yield 9.703 pct, or 143 basis points over Treasuries. These bonds are non-callable for five years.     The debt is rated Baa-3 by Moody's, BBB-minus by S and P.  Reuter &#3;
