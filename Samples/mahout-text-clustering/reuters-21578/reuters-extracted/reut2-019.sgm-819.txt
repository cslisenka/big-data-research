UNO RESTAURANT <UNO> STOCK TRADES ON AMEX

The American Stock Exchange said 4,250,000 shares of Uno Restaurant Corp, holding company for a chain of 37 pizza restaurants, began trading under the symbol "UNO."     The West Roxbury, Mass.-based company previously traded in the NASDAQ National System under the symbol "UNOS."  Reuter &#3;
