ALASKA AIR GROUP <ALK> OFFERS STOCK

Alaska Air Group Inc said it has registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer 1.8 mln shares of common stock, plus 180,000 shares to cover over-allotments.     The company said Merrill Lynch, Pierce, Fenner and Smith Inc and The First Boston Corp will lead the underwriting syndicate.     Proceeds will be used to reduce debt and for working capital and capital expenditures, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
