SAN/BAR <SBAR> APPOINTS NEW PRESIDENT

San/Bar Corp said it appointed Charles von Urff as president, chief operating officer and a director of the company to succeed Robert Johnson, who retired last year.     Prior to joining San/Bar von Urff had been president and chief executive of Microelectric Packaging Inc, the company also said.  Reuter &#3;
