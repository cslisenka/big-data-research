WHITEHALL <WHT> GETS NAVY CONTRACT

Whitehall Corp said the U.S. Navy has exercised contract options to lease two additional Quick Reaction Surveillance Systems for the year ending September 30 from Whitehall for 4,437,000 dlrs.     The company said the contract contains further options for the lease of those two systems and two others for two more years at abouyt 18 mln dlrs per year.  Reuter &#3;
