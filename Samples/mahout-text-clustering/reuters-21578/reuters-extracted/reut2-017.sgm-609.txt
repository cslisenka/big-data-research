EMERY <EAF> NAMES KILCULLEN HEAD OF PUROLATOR

Emery Air Freight Corp, which acquired controlling interest in Purolator Courier Corp <PCC>, said it has named John Kilcullen executive vice president and chief operating officer of Purolator.     Previously, Emery said Kilcullen was Purolator's executive vice president of operations.     Emery said Kilcullen now heads the Purolator executive management team and reports to Emery President Denis McCarthy.  Reuter &#3;
