ULTRASYSTEMS <ULS> UNIT GETS DOD CONTRACTS

Ultrasystems Inc said the U.S. Defense Department awarded the company's Ultrasystems Defense and Space Inc unit several new contracts and extensions to existing contracts having a total value of more than 15.1 mln dlrs.     The new awards involve software development and hardware integration for secure office automation systems, Ultrasystems said.  Reuter &#3;
