ENERGY VENTURES <ENGY> FORMS NEW ENERGY UNIT

Energy Ventures Inc said it formed a new subsidiary, EVI Inc, that will seek to fund growth of and offer financial services to oil service and equipment companies.     The new unit is majority owned by Energy Ventures. Simmons and Co Finance, an affiliate of Houston-based Simmons and Co International, is minority shareholder.     Initial capital of 25 mln dlrs was provided by Energy Ventures with an additional one mln dlrs contributed by Simmons.  Reuter &#3;
