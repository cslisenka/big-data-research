LTV <LTV> WINS 18.5 MLN DLR PAKISTAN ORDER

LTV Corp said Pakistan ordered 180 military trucks and spare parts worth 18.5 mln dlrs from AM General, a unit of LTV's Missles and Electronics Group.     The company said the order includes 140 M809 Series five-ton trucks and 40 M44A2 Series 2-1/2 ton trucks. They are scheduled to be delivered later this year.  Reuter &#3;
