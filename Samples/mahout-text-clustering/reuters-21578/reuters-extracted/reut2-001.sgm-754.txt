VENTURE FUNDING CORP EXTENDS WARRANT PERIOD

<Venture Funding Corp> said it has extended the exercise period on its outstanding class A and B warrants until April 15, 1988.     The original exercise date was April 15, 1987.     The company also said it has executed a non-binding letter of intent to acquire three affiliated California corporations that own and operate Mexican food restaurants.     It said it has placed "stop transfer" orders to prevent exercise of the warrants until adequate information is made available about the proposed business combination and the businesses to be acquired.
