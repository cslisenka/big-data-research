TITAN <TTN> EXTENDS ODD LOT TENDER OFFER

Titan Corp said it extended its odd-lot tender offer program to purchase shares of its preferred stock to June 10, 1987.     The offer is made to holders of 99 or fewer preferred shares.     The offer was originally scheduled to expire March 10.  Reuter &#3;
