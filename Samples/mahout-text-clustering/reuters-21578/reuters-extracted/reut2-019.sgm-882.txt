BELDEN <BBE> TO SELL UNITS INTERNATIONALLY

Belden and Blake Energy Co, a master limited partnership, said it entered into an agreement to sell 400,000 of its units for two mln dlrs in cash.     The company said <Great Pacific Capital S.A.> of Geneva, Switzerland, is acting as lead manager for the private placement of the units with international investors.     Under the agreement, it said an option has also been granted to buy up to 800,000 additional units at five dlrs per unit on or before July 13.  Reuter &#3;
