QMS <AQM> GETS ZIYAD <ZIAD> PRODUCT RIGHTS

QMS Inc said its Laser Connection subsidiary has been awarded exclusive rights to distribute the entire line of Ziyad laser printer sheet feeders in the U.S. and Canada.     The company said it expects the arrangement to add 15 to 20 mln dlrs to its annual sales.  Reuter &#3;
