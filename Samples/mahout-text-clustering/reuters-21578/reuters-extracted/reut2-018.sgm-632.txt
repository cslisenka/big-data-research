ENVIRONMENTAL POWER <POWR.O> TO OFFER SHARES

Environmental Power Corp said it has filed for an offering of two mln common shares through underwriters led by Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc.     The company said it will use 400,000 dlrs of the proceeds to pay part of the purchase price for Milesburg Energy Inc and the rest to repay up to one mln dlrs in short-term debt and for general corporate purposes.
