SUHARTO PARTY SET FOR EASY WIN IN INDONESIA POLLS

President Suharto's ruling Golkar party appears to have made substantial gains with over 75 pct of the votes counted in Indonesia's national elections.     Figures released by the election commission showed Golkar on target to take 70 pct of the vote.     Provisional figures indicate that with results of 68.9 mln ballots announced, Golkar had won 50.29 mln, the Moslem-based United Development Party 10.93 mln and the nationalist Democratic Party 7.69 mln.     The total electorate is 94 mln and officials said they thought about 90 pct of the votes had been counted. &#3;
