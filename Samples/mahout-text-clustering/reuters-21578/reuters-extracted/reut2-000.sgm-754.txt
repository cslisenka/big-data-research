EDO CORP <EDO> UNIT WILL BUILD AIRCRAFT PARTS

EDO Corp said McDonnell Aircraf Co has invoked its 1987 production option calling for ejection release units for the F-15E aircraft.     The company said the contract is not to exceed 10.1 mln dlrs and will be handled by its government systems division in College Point, N.Y.     The company said the contract is a follow up to the full scale development and 1986 contracts previously awarded to EDO.   Reuter &#3;
