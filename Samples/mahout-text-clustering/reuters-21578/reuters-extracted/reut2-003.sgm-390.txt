FLUOR CORP <FLR> WINS 197.6 MLN DLR ARMY CONTRACT

Fluor Corp said it won a 197.6 mln dlr contract from the U.S. Army Engineer District, with a follow-on option for an additional 450 mln dlrs.     Fluor's Fluor Constructors Inc unit will perform procurement and construction for Ground Based Free Electron Laser-Technology Integrated Experiment Project at the White Sands Missile Range in New Mexico.     Phase I Construction will begin this month and is scheduled for completion in September, 1990.     Based on its results, a Phase II follow-on option may be implemented for an additional two years, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
