HILLS DEPARTMENT STORES PLANS OFFERING CHANGES

Hills Department Stores Inc said it has decided to amend the registration on file with the Securities and Exchange Commission covering its planned initial offering.     The company said it expects to offer three mln common shares at a filing range of 11 to 12 dlrs a share along with a 40 mln dlr offering of convertible junior subordinated debentures instead of the 6.9 mln common shares proposed in the pending registration.     Hills said it expects to file a registration statement on the proposed changes within two weeks.  Reuter &#3;
