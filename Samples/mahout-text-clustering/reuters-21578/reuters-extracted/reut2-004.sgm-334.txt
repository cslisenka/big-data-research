BADGER <BMI> SEES NO REASON FOR TRADING ACTIVITY

Badger Meter Inc said it was not aware of any reason for the unusual trading activity in its common stock.     Badger was trading at 21-5/8, up 1-5/8.     The company made the statement in response to an inquiry from the American Stock Exchange regarding the unusual activity in its stock.  Reuter &#3;
