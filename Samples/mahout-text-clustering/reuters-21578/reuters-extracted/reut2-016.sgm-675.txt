INTERCO <ISS> COMPLETES LANE<LANE> ACQUISITION

Interco Inc said its shareholders and those of Lane Co approved a merger of the two companies at special meetings.     As previously announced, Lane's shareholders will receive 1.5 shares of Interco common stock for each share of Lane stock held.     Interco said the merger becomes effective April 14.  Reuter &#3;
