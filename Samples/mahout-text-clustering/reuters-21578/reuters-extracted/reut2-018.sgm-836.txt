MERCHANTSBANK <MCBKA> GETS DIVIDEND DISTRIBUTION

MerchantsBank of Boston said it has received an 8,400,000 dlr dividend from the Co-Operative Central Bank Share Insurance Fund, and the amount will be included in revenues for the second quarter.     The fund is liquidating since all cooperative banks in Massachusetts have obtained federal insurance.  Reuter &#3;
