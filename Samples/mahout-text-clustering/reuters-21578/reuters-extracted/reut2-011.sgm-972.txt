AMES <ADD> NAMES NEW CHIEF FINANCIAL OFFICER

Ames Department Stores Inc said Duane Wolter has been named executive vice president and chiedf financial officer, succeeding as chief financial officer Ralph M. Shulansky.     The company said Shulansky remains senior vice president-administration and investor relations.     Ames said yesterday that inventory shortages found at its Secaucus, N.J., distribution center would hurt results for the year ended January 31.     Wolter had been senior vice president, finance, and chief financial officer of <Rapid-American Corp's> McCrory Corp unit.  Reuter &#3;
