INVESTMENT FIRMS CUT CYCLOPS <CYL> STAKE

A group of affiliated New York investment firms said they lowered their stake in Cyclops Corp to 200,500 shares, or 5.0 pct of the total outstanding, from 260,500 shares, or 6.4 pct.     In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group, led by Mutual Shares Corp, said it sold 60,000 Cyclops common shares since February 26.  Reuter &#3;
