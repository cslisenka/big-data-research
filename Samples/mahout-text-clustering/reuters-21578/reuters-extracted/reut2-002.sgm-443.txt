VMS REALTY PARTNERS UNITS COMMIT TO LOANS

<VMS Realty Partners> said three of its investment subsidiaries will fund or extend loans totalling approximately 97 mln dlrs for various properties in California.     The company said its its VMS Short Term Trust <VST> unit agreed to fund or extend four loans totalling 70.9 mln dlrs.     It also said its VMS Hotel Investment Trust <VHT> unit will fund a 19.4 mln dlr secured loan for the Santa Barbara Hotel in California.     And its VMS Mortgage Investors II <VMTGZ> division approved a loan of 6,547,000 mln dlrs for a retail office building in downtown San Francisco, the company said.     VMS Realty Partners is a full service real estate firm focusing on acquiring, developing and managing hotels, office buildings, shopping centers, highrise apartment complexes, townhouses and garden apartments, it said.
