MAZDA MOTOR CORP REPORTS LOWER U.S. SALES

Mazda Motor Corp of Japan reported car sales in the U.S. during February of 10,049, down from 19,448 during February last year.     The company said year to date car sales in the U.S. totaled 21,092, down from 38,441 a year earlier.     Truck sales in the U.S. totaled 8,558 in February, down from 9,693 a year earlier. Year to date U.S. truck sales totaled 16,917, compared to 18,341 a year ago.  Reuter &#3;
