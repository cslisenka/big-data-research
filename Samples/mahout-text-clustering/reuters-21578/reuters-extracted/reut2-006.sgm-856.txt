GRUMMAN<GQ> UNIT GETS 303.9 MLN DLR SDI CONTRACT

The Grumman Space Systems division of Grumman Corp is being awarded a 303.9 mln dlr Air Force contract for work related to the Strategic Defense Initiative (SDI), the Defense Department said.     It said the contract, similar to one awarded to Lockheed Corp, is for a 34 month SDI Boost Surveillance and Tracking System demonstration/validation effort which is expected to be completed in January 1990.     The department said 34.3 mln dlrs of the contract funds will expire at the end of the current fiscal year. It added that 38 bids for the work were solicited and three proposals were received.
