VICON INDUSTRIES <VII> VOTES STAGGERED BOARD

Vicon Industries Inc said its shareholders approved an amendment to divide its nine-member board into three classes with staggered tenures.     It also said shareholders expanded the indemnification rights of the company's directors and officers.     Both actions were taken at the company's annual shareholders meeting, Vicon said.
