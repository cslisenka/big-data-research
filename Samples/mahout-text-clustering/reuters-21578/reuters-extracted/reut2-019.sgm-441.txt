MOSELEY HOLDING CORP <MOSE.O> SEES 1ST QTR LOSS

Moseley Holding Corp said it expects a loss for its first fiscal quarter ending June 30.     The company said the losses are related to its municipal bond, unit trust and corporate bond activities, all of which were affected by the difficult bond market.     The company said losses for those areas were close to four mln dlrs for April and May.     It said its mortgage-backed activities were profitable during those months.  Reuter &#3;
