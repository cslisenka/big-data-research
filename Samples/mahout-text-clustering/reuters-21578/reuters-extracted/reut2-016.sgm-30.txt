MARCH TRUCK SALES SAID UP 16.4 PCT

Retail sales of trucks in March rose 16.4 pct over the same month last year, said the Motor Vehicle Manufacturers Association.     The trade group said dealers sold 377,617 trucks in March, up from 324,327 last year.     Year-to-date truck sales were up 3.6 pct at 934,074 from 1986's 901,757.  Reuter &#3;
