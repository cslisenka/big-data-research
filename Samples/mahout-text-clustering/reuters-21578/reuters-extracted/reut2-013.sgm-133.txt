MITSUBISHI REPORTS HIGHER U.S. MARCH SALES

The Mitsubishi Motor Sales America Inc unit of Mitsubishi Motor Corp said domestic car sales during March rose to 6,403 from 4,089 a year earlier and year-to date car sales were up to 14,071 from 12,937 last year.     Truck sales rose to 3,800 during the month from 2,526 last year and year-to-date sales increased to 11,126 from 6,520, Mitsubishi said.  Reuter &#3;
