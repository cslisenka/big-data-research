PAKISTAN BUYS 6,000 TONNES RBD PALM OIL - TRADERS

Pakistan bought 6,000 tonnes of rbd palm oil at its import tender today for first half May shipment, traders said.     The price was believed to be in the region of 345.50 dlrs per tonne cost and freight, but confirmation is awaited, they said.     Pakistan sought to buy up to 12,000 tonnes for first half May and has not said when it is likely to tender for the balance.  REUTER &#3;
