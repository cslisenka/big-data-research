<SEMICON TOOLS INC> MAKING ACQUISITION

Semicon Tools Inc said it has signed a letter of intent to acquire a majority interest in privately held East Coast Sales, a distributor and fabricator of technical ceramic products and disposable clean room materials and supplies.     Terms were not disclosed.     It said it expects to acquisition to result in a substantial sales increase.  Reuter &#3;
