INTERCO <ISS> SHAREHOLDERS APPROVE MERGER

INTERCO Inc said its shareholders and shareholders of the Lane Co <LANE.O> approved the merger of the two companies.     In the merger, Lane's stockholders will receive 1.5 shares of INTERCO common stock for each outstanding share of Lane common stock, INTERCO said.  Reuter &#3;
