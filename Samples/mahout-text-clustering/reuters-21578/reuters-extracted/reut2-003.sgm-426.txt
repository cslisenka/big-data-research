AIRCAL <ACF> FEBRUARY LOAD FACTOR DOWN SLIGHTLY

AirCal said its February load factor averaged 53.4 pct, down slightly from 54.2 pct a year earlier.     The company said available seat miles, at 357.1 mln, were up 40.5 pct from a year ago, while revenue passenger miles, at 190.8 mln, were up 38.5 pct.     Year to date revenue passenger miles were 383.3 mln at the end of February, up 33.4 pct from a year earlier, while available seat miles, at 750.5 mln, were up 39.5 pct.     The load factor for the first two months of the year was 51.1 pct, down from 53.4 pct a year earlier.  Reuter &#3;
