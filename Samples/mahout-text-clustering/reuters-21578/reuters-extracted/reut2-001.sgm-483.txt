TANDY <TAN> FEBRUARY SALES UP 12 PCT

Tandy Corp said February sales and operating revenues were up 12 pct to 244.4 mln dlrs from 218.1 mln dlrs a year before.     It said sales and revenues of U.S. retail operations were up 15 pct to 210.7 mln dlrs from 183.6 mln dlrs a year before and sales of U.S. retail stores in existence for more than one year were up 13 pct.
