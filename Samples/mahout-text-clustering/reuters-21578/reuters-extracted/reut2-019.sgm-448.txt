MOSELY <MOSE.O> EXPECTS 1ST QTR LOSS

Mosely Holding Corp said it expects to report an unspecified loss in the first quarter ending June 30.     The company said the current quarter result will reflect losses associated with municipal bond, unit trust, and corporate bond activities.     It reported net income of 203,000 dlrs, or one cent per share, on revenues of 35.5 mln dlrs in the fiscal 1986 first quarter.  Reuter &#3;
