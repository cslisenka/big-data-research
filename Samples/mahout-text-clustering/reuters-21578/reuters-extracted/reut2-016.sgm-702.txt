FIRST FAMILY GROUP <FFAM> SALES INCREASE

First Family Group Inc said sales for March increased 27 pct over a year ago the same month to 4.4 mln dlrs from 3.5 mln dlrs.     The company also reported third quarter sales increased 35 pct to 14.5 mln dlrs from 10.6 mln dlrs.     First Family Group also reported sales for the first nine months of fiscal 1987. It said sales were 54.5 mln dlrs, compared to 52.7 mln dlrs a year ago.  Reuter &#3;
