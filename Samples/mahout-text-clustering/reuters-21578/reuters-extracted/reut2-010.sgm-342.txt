WALT DISNEY INCREASES EUROBOND TO 75 MLN AUST DLRS

The amount of a eurobond issued yesterday by Walt Disney Co Ltd has been raised to 75 mln Australian dlrs from an original 60 mln, lead manager Warburg Securities said.     The issue, which matures on May 7, 1990, has a 14-1/2 pct coupon and is priced at 101-3/8 pct.  REUTER &#3;
