<LINCOLN BANCORP> FILES FOR PUBLIC OFFERING

Lincoln Bancorp said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission covering a proposed public offering of one mln common shares at an estimated offering price of between eight and nine dlrs per share.     The company said First of Michigan Corp will manage the underwriting.     It said proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes and for expansion.   Reuter &#3;
