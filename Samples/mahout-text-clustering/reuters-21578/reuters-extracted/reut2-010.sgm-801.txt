WEDGESTONE REALTY <WDG> ISSUES WARRANTS

Wedgestone Realty Investors Trust said it has issued institutional investors 10-year warrants to buy 575,000 shares at 16.50 dlrs each in connection with the previously-announced sale of 10-year promissory notes to the investors.     It said the exercise price of the warrants may be reduced and the number of shares increased in certain circumstances.  Reuter &#3;
