RAYTHEON <RTN> WINS U.S. NAVY CONTRACT

Raytheon Co said the United States Navy awarded it a 215.3 mln dlr contract to produce 1,927 AIM/RIM-7M Sparrow Missile Guidance and Control Sections and associated hardware.     Under the contract, the company said it will provide missiles and associated hardware to the U.S. Navy and Air Force and to several U.S. allies.     Raytheon said the contract represents the major share of the fiscal 1987 competitive Sparrow missile procurement.  Reuter &#3;
