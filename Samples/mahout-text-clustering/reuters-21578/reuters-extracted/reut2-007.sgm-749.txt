FEDDERS <FJQ> OFFERS PREFERRED STOCK

Fedders Corp said an offering of 1,500,000 shares of 1.75 dlr convertible exchangeable preferred stock is under way at 25 dlrs per share through underwriters led by Bear Stearns Cos Inc <BSC>.     The preferred is convertible at any time into common stock at 9.50 dlrs a share and is exchangeable after two years at Fedders' option for seven pct convertible subordinated debentures due 2012.
