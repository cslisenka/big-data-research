SAPPORO BREWERIES PLANS 100 MLN SWISS FRANC NOTES

Sapporo Breweries Ltd is launching a 100 mln Swiss franc, 4-5/8 pct, five-year guaranteed notes issue priced at 100-1/4 pct, lead manager Swiss Bank Corp said.     The issue is guaranteed by Fuji Bank and payment date is March 17.  REUTER &#3;
