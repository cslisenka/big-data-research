LATIN AMERICAN, SPANISH BANK GOVERNORS TO MEET

Central bank governors from Spain, Latin America and the Caribbean will meet here this week for two separate conferences on finance and monetary problems, the Central Bank of Barbados said.     The 24th session of Central Bank governors of the American continent opens today for two days and the central bank governors of Latin America and Spain will hold a separate conference from April 29-30.     Representatives of the Inter-American Development Bank, the International Monetary Fund and other international financial organisations will attend both conferences.  REUTER &#3;
