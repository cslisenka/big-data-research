ATLANTIC FINANCIAL <ATLF.O> TO ACQUIRE S AND L

Atlantic Financial said it signed a definitive agreement to acquire <Centurion Savings and Loan Association>.     Atlantic did not disclose the purchase price.     Atlantic said it originally announced its intention to acquire Centurion, located in Los Angeles, on Feb 23, 1987.     Centurion has assets of 105 mln dlrs, Atlantic said.  Reuter &#3;
