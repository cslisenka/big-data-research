BERYLLIUM INT'L SIGNS JOINT VENTURE PACT

<Beryllium International Corp> said it has signed a joint venture agreement with Cominco American Inc, a unit of Cominco Ltd <CLT>, to develop a beryllium mine and processing plant on Beryllium International's property in the Topaz Mountains southwest of Salt Lake City.     Beryllium said as a 49 pct owner of the venture iot would be contributing the mine while Cominco, as operator and 51 pct owner, would conduct drilling, metallurgical studies, process testing and other preliminary work for a feasibility study.     Beryllium said the cost of the preliminary work in 1987, which will start immediately, should be about 250,000 dlrs to 300,000 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
