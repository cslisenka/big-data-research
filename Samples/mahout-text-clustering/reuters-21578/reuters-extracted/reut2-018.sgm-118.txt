WHARF RESOURCES <WFRA.O> SETS DEAL WITH NEVARD

Wharf Resources Ltd said it has reached a verbal agreement with <Nevada Packard> for a joint venture to explore, evaluate and produce the Nevada precious metal property near Lovelock, Nev.     Wharf said it wil receive an exclusive option to update an existing feasibility study and evaluate a production decision.     Subject to completion of necessary documentation, Wharf said it expects work on the Nevada property to begin immediately.  Reuter &#3;
