SAAB-SCANIA WINS 15 MLN DLR BUS CONTRACT

<Saab-Scania AB> of Sweden said its Saab-Scania of America Inc unit was awarded a 15 mln dlr contract to supply transit busses to the transport services department of Honolulu, Hawaii.     The company said it will supply 94 CN-112 busses under the contract.  Reuter &#3;
