CHURCHILL TO MARKET WESTERN UNION <WU> SERVICES

<Churchill Communications Corp> said it signed an agreement under which it will sell and service Western Union Corp's telex, electronic mail and long distance services to customers not directly assigned to Western Union representatives.     Churchill, a privately held firm that processes electronic mail services, said the agreement extends for two years, with options for renewal.  Reuter &#3;
