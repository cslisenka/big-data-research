ATT <T> OFFERS USA DIRECT SERVICE IN DENMARK

American Telephone and Telegraph Co said it is offering its USA Direct service in Denmark.     The service allows callers in Denmark to reach an ATT operator in the United States by dialing a single telephone number, 0430-0010, ATT said.     USA Direct is now available in 26 countries, including the United Kingdom, France and Australia, the company added.  Reuter &#3;
