SEVENTEEN MISSING COPPER MINERS FOUND ALIVE

Seventeen miners who were missing in a copper mine fire were found today and efforts were underway to rescue them and 29 others who were trapped but safe in an underground lunch room, officials said.     The mine's owner, Noranda Inc., said one miner died in the fire, six miners escaped unharmed, 29 made it to an underground lunch room, where they had air and water, and 17 were later found safe in other lunch rooms.
