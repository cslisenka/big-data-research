BRAZIL BUYS FRENCH WHEAT AT TENDER

Brazil bought 75,000 tonnes of French wheat at tonight's tender, a Brazilian Wheat Board spokesman said.     For May shipment, it accepted offers from J. Souflet for 50,000 tonnes of wheat at 83.49 dlrs Fob per tonne.     For June shipment, Brazil bought 25,000 tonnes from J. Souflet also at 83.49 dlrs Fob per tonne.     The next tender is set for April 9, for May, June and July shipment, the spokesman said.  Reuter &#3;
