SWISS BANK NET FOREIGN ASSESTS UP IN 4TH QUARTER

Net foreign assets of Swiss banks rose by 3.5 billion Swiss francs to 71.2 billion francs in the fourth quarter of 1986, due to increased interbank business and purchases of foreign securities, the Swiss National Bank said.     Assets rose 5.3 billion to 205.0 billion while liabilities climbed 1.8 billion to 133.8 billion. For 1986 as a whole, assets rose by 11.9 billion and liabilities by 3.0 billion.     The level of fiduciary funds invested abroad rose by 3.2 billion to 223.0 billion, the first rise since the third quarter of 1985. Funds invested in such accounts from abroad were up by 3.2 billion to 180.7 billion.  REUTER &#3;
