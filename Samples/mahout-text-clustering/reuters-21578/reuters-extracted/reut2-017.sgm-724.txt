ELECTROSPACE <ELE> GETS DEFENSE CONTRACT

Electrospace Systems Inc said it has received the first 1,623,000 dlr increment of a possible five mln dlr contract award from the U.S. Space and Naval Warfare Systems Command.     It said the contract covers production and systems integration of seven AN/WSC-6 SHF SATCOM systems for use on combatant and survey ships.  Reuter &#3;
