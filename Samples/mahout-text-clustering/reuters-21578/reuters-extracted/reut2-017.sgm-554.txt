FOOD LION <FDLNB.O> MAY SALES RISE

Food Lion Inc said sales for the four weeks ended May 23 were up 21.9 pct to 222.6 mln dlrs from 182.6 mln dlrs a year earlier.     The company said sales for the year to date were up 23.4 pct to 1.06 billion dlrs from 858.8 mln dlrs a year before.  Reuter &#3;
