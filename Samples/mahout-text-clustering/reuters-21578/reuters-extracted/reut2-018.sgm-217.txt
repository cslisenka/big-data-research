CHICAGO PACIFIC <CPA> TO REPURCHASE NOTES

Chicago Pacific Corp chairman Harvey Kapnick told analysts that he expects whithin the next year to repurchase about 25 mln dlrs of the company's 200 mln dlr issue of 14 pct subordinated notes.     To date the company has repurchased about 60 mln of these notes.
