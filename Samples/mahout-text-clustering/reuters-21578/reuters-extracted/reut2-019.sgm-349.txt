TRANSAMERICAN <TA> GETS APPROVAL ON CHAPTER 11

TransAmerican Natural Gas said it received approval from a U.S. District Court in Texas allowing the company to solicit creditor acceptance of its negotiated Chapter 11 plan of reorganization.     The company said the approval authorized the company to distribute the disclosure statement to its creditors and solicit creditors' acceptance of the plan.     TransAmerican said the confirmation hearing is scheduled for August 17 in U.S. Southern District of Texas court.      Reuter &#3;
