STANLEY WORKS <SWK> SEES RECORD 1987 NET

Stanley Works said it expects record sales and earnings in 1987 against earnings of 78.7 mln dlrs on sales of 1.37 billion dlrs in 1986.     "We feel confident that, with our present momentum, the Stanley Works is positioned for strong performance with record sales and earnings expected for 1987," chairman Donald W. Davis said.     The company said its first quarter earnings from operations rose to 19.2 mln dlrs from 14.8 mln dlrs a year ago.  Reuter &#3;
