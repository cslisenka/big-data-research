GERMAN APRIL WHOLESALE TURNOVER FALLS FOUR PCT

West German wholesale turnover provisionally totalled about 66 billion marks in April, a real decline of four pct compared with the same month last year, the Federal Statistics Office said.     In March wholesale turnover had risen by a real seven pct on the year-ago period.     The Statistics Office said that in the first four months of this year wholesale turnover of about 244 billion marks was a real one pct down from last year.  REUTER &#3;
