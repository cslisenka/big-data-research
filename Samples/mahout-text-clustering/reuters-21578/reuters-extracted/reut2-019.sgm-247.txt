VIACOM <VIA> SETS EX-DIVIDEND DATE FOR PREFERRED

Viacom Inc said the ex-dividend date for its preferred stock is Friday, June 19.     The company, which halted activity on the stock this morning, said trading will resume shortly. The last price for Viacom Inc preferred was 24-1/2.  Reuter &#3;
