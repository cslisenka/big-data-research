ALZA CORP <AZA> SETS CONTAMINATION PLAN

ALZA Corp said it has notified the State of California Regional Water Quality Control Board of a plan to remedy groundwater and soil contamination it detected at a site in Stanford Research Park.     A preliminary water sample showed water apparently contaminated some years ago with the industrial solvent chloroform. The company said the contamination does not affect drinking water or water used its operations on the site.     The plan is expected to cost a maximum of 750,000 dlrs, the company said.
