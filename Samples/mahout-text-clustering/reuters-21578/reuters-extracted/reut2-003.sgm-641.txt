<MESA AIRLINES INC> INITIAL OFFERING UNDERWAY

Lead underwriters Josephthal and Co Inc, Emmett A. Larkin Co Inc and Hays Securities Corp said an initial public offering of 865,000 common shares of Mesa Airlines Inc is underway at 7.50 dlrs per share.     Underwriters have been granted an overallotment option to purchase up to another 129,750 shares.  Reuter &#3;
