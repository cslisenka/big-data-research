PREMARK <PMI> EXTENDS BUY-BACK DEADLINE

Premark International Inc said it is extending to April 17 the offer to buy its common stock from holders of fewer than 100 shares.     Owners of record Jan 27, 1987 are eligible for the offer, which enables shareholders to sell their holdings to Premark without paying brokerage fees or commission.     The offer is being made on a "first-come first-served" basis with a maximum of 1.7 mln shares to be accepted.  Reuter &#3;
