JACOR <JCOR> TO BUY TWO DENVER RADIO STATIONS

Jacor Communications Inc said it agreed to buy two Denver radio stations from A.H. Belo Corp <BLC> for 24 mln dlrs in cash and notes.     Jacor said the two stations are KOA-AM and KOAQ-FM.     The acquisitions must be approved by the Federal Communications Commission, Jacor added.  Reuter &#3;
