FORD <F> HAS 12 PCT MARKET SHARE IN EUROPE

Ford Motor Co said that through May its European dealers Europe sold a record 643,000 cars and increased their market share to 12 pct from 11.7 pct a year earlier.     The company said industry sales ran at a seasonally adjusted annual rate of 11.6 mln cars during the first five months of 1987 compared with 10.9 mln cars in the same period last year.  Reuter &#3;
