FIRST CAPITAL <FCH> ESOP TO BUY MORE STOCK

First Capital Holding Corp said its board authorized management to up to two mln shares of the company's common stock for its Employee Stock Ownership Plan.     The company said its board had previously authorized purchases of up to one mln shares and to date, the plan has only purchased 100,000 shares.  Reuter &#3;
