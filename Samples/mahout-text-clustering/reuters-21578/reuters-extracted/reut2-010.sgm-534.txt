SVENSKA FINANS GETS 100 MLN DLR EURO-CP PROGRAM

A 100 mln dlr euro-commercial paper program has been arranged for Svenska Finans International BV, Manufacturers Hanover Ltd said as one of two arranger/dealers.     The other arranger/dealer is Svenska Handelsbanken Plc.     The borrower is the wholly owned international holding company subsidiary of Svenska Finans AB, a subsidiary of Svenska Handelsbanken, Sweden.     In connection with the program, the parent has issued a letter of intent, which is not a guarantee but states the parent's intention to make sure a subsidiary meets its financial obligations.
