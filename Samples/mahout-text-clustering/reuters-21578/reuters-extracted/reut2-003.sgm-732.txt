PRAGMA BIO-TECH <PRAG> IN NITROGLYCERIN TESTING

Pragma Bio-Tech Inc, a clinical testing company, said it has started performing comparative studies on differnet varieties of nitroglycerin for use in the treatment of angina pectoris episodes.     The company would not disclose details of the testing.  Reuter &#3;
