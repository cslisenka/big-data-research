CHEMFIX <CFIX> ACQUIRES BALANCE OF AFFILIATE

Chemfix Technologies Inc said it acquired the remaining 50 pct of its California affiliate, VenVirotek, previously owned by a Ventura, Calif. businessman.     Terms were not disclosed.     VenVirotek proved the Chemfix Process could be successfully applied to convert spent drilling fluids into a reusable clay-like product that is suitable for use as landfill cover.  Reuter &#3;
