BP PLANS CAPITALISATION SHARE ISSUE

British Petroleum Co Plc <BP.L> said it is proposing a capitalisation share issue of two new ordinary shares for each ordinary share held at the close of business on April 15.     The move is subject to the approval of shareholders at an extraordinary general meeting to be held on April 30, it said in a statement.     The new shares will not participate in the proposed final dividend for 1986, expected to be paid on May 7.  REUTER &#3;
