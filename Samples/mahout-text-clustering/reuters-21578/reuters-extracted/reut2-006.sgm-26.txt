DEVELOPMENT CORP OF AMERICA <DCA> MERGED

Development Corp of America said its merger with Lennar Corp <LEN> was completed and its stock no longer existed.     Development Corp of America, whose board approved the acquisition last November for 90 mln dlrs, said the merger was effective today and its stock now represents the right to receive 15 dlrs a share.     The American Stock Exchange said it would provide further details later.  Reuter &#3;
