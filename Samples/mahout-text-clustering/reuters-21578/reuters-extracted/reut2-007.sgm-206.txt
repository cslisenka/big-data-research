FED ADDS RESERVES VIA CUSTOMER REPURCHASES

The Federal Reserve entered the U.S. Government securities market to arrange 1.5 billion dlrs of customer repurchase agreements, a Fed spokesman said.     Dealers said Federal funds were trading at six pct when the Fed began its temporary and indirect supply of reserves to the banking system.  Reuter &#3;
