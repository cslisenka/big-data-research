COLGATE-PALMOLIVE <CL> PLANS TO FILE DEBT

Colgate-Palmolive Co said it plans to file with the Securities and Exchange Commission a shelf registration covering up to 300 mln dlrs of unsecured debt securities.     Reuben Mark, chairman and president of the company, told this to a meeting of the New York Society of Security Analysts, Colgate-Palmolive said in a release.     Proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
