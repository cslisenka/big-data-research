REPUBLIC NEW YORK <RNB> ADDS TO LOAN-LOSS

Republic New York Corp said it would add 100 mln dlrs to its allowances for loan losses specifically related to its loan exposure to Latin American customers.     Republic New York estimated its second quarter net loss would total about 80 mln dlrs as a result.  REUTER &#3;
