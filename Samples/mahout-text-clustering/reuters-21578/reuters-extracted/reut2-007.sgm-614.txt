PACIFICORP <PPW> SELLS SERIAL PREFERRED STOCK

PacifiCorp said an offering of 50 mln dlrs of 7.12 pct serial preferred stock is underway at 100 dlrs per share through underwriters led by Salomon Inc <SB>, Merrill Lynch and Co Inc <MER>, L.F. Rothschild, Unterberg, Towbin Inc <R> and Printon, Kane and Co.     The company said its proceeds from the offering were 99.20 dlrs per share.  A sinking fund starting in 1993 will start retiring the issue, with retirement to be completed in 2027.     PacifCorp also said James P. Best, senior vice president of its telecommunications unit, has temporarily switched jobs with parent company vice president and controller Stan M. Marks to broaden management experience within the company.  Reuter &#3;
