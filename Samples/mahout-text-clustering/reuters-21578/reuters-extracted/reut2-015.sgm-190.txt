LANESBOROUGH'S SUBORDINATED NOTES LOWERED BY S/P

Standard and Poor's Corp said it cut to CCC-plus from B-minus Lanesborough Corp's 50 mln dlrs of senior subordinated notes.     S and P said the downgrade reflected Lanesborough's planned purchase of Ampex Corp from Allied-Signal Inc <ALD> for 479 mln dlrs.     "The acquisition represents a dramatic departure from management's stated financial policies," S and P said. It also said that Lanesborough is making a major investment in a venture with extremely high financial risk.     The company's implied senior debt rating is B.  Reuter &#3;
