NATRIONAL HEALTHCORP <NHC> EXPANDING CENTERS

National HealthCorp LP said it has started adding 46 beds for its Greenwood Health Care Center in Greenwood, S.C., and plans to add 10 beds to its Springfield Health Care Center in Springfield, Tenn.     The company said it has opened Colonial Hill Retirement Apartments adjacent to its Colonial Hill Health Care Center in Johnson City, Tenn., and has completely remodeled National Health Care Center in Lawrenceburg, Tenn.     It said it is acquiring the title to that center, which had been owned by Lawrence County Lions Club and called Lions Nursing Home.     National said the projects will cost a total of about 3,500,000 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
