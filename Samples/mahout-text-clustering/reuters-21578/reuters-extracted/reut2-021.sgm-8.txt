LANE TELECOMMUNICATIONS PRESIDENT RESIGNS

Lane Telecommunications Inc <LNTL.O> said Richard Lane, its president and chief operating officer, resigned effective Oct 23.     Lane founded the company in 1976 and has been its president since its inception, the company said.     He said he resigned to pursue other business interests.     Kirk Weaver, chairman and chief executive officer, said Lane's resignation was amicable.     No replacement has been named.  Reuter &#3;
