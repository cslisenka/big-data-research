ALLEGHENY INT'L <AG> SELLS THREE OVERSEAS UNITS

Allegheny International Inc said it sold three overseas subsidiaries to Reil Corp Ltd, a North Sydney, Australia, investment group.     Terms were not disclosed.     The units sold were Sunbeam Corp Ltd Australia, Sunbeam New Zealand Ltd and Victa (U.K.) Ltd. The units make and distribute various products, including lawn mowers, small appliances and sheep shearing equipment. They employ a total of about 1,750.  Reuter &#3;
