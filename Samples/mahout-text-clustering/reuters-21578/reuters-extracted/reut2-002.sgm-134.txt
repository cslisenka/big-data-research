SEARS <S> FEBRUARY SALES UP 4.9 PCT

Sears, Roebuck and Co said revenues of its Merchandise Group in February rose 4.9 pct to 1.85 billion dlrs from 1.76 billion dlrs a year ago.     It said domestic sales were led by better than average increases in apparel, home fashions and hardware.  Reuter &#3;
