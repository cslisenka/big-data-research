CANADA BUDGET DEFICIT FELL IN DECEMBER

Canada's federal budget deficit fell to 2.01 billion dlrs in December from 2.27 billion dlrs a year earlier, the finance department said.     The government said revenues in December totaled 8.17 billion dlrs, up from 7.26 billion dlrs in 1985.     Expenditures were 10.18 billion dlrs, up from 9.53 billion dlrs in the previous year, the government said.  Reuter &#3;
