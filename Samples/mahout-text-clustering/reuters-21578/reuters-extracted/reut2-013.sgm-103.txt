ANALYSIS AND TECHNOLOGY <AATI> WINS CONTRACTS

Analysis and Technology Inc said it won 7.6 mln dlrs worth of Navy contracts, subcontracts and increases to existing contracts.     The company said its backlog of contracts is approximately 107 mln dlrs, compared to 62 mln dlrs of backlog contracts a year ago this time.  Reuter &#3;
