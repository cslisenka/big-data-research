ALLEGHENY INTERNATIONAL <AG> SELLS THREE UNITS

Allegheny International Inc said it has sold its Chemetron Railway Products Inc, True Temper Railway Appliances Inc and Allegheny Axle Co units to newly-formed Chemetron Railway Products Inc for undisclosed terms.     It said the new company was formed by senior management of the three railway product units and Kleinwort Benson Group of London.  Reuter &#3;
