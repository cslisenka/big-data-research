Enviropact Inc said it purchased two facilities in Miami and Tampa, Fla.     In Miami, its said it bought a 1.9 acre lot with a 12,500 square foot facility for 545,000 dlrs. In Tampa, it said it bought a 13,000 square foot facility for 440,000 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
