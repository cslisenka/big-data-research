ROTO-ROOTER <ROTO.O> SEES IMPROVED 4TH QTR NET

Roto-Rooter Inc said it expects fourth quarter profits to exceed the 22 cts a share reported for the final quarter of 1986 and the 23 cts earned in 1987's third quarter.     It reported the third quarter profit was up  pct from the 20 cts a share earned during the quarter in 1986. Nine month profits were up 23 pct to 64 cts a share from 52 cts last year.  Reuter &#3;
