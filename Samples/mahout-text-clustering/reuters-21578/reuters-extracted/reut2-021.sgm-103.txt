NOMURA SAYS IT SEEKS LINK WITH KIDDER PEABODY

Nomura Securities Co Ltd is seeking a possible business relationship with Kidder Peabody and Co Inc, said Setsuya Tabuchi, the chairman of the board of Nomura.     Tabuchi told an investment conference "at present the company's staff (of Nomura) has been studying the possiblity of a joint buiness," he said.     Tabuchi said he has not been informed how far the study has progressed. He declined to elaborate further.     Kidder Peabody is a wholly-owned subsidiary of General Electric <GE>. Kidder officials were not immediately available for comment.  Reuter &#3;
