SAFEGUARD <SFGD.O> TO BUY BACK MORE SHARES

Safeguard Health Enterprises Inc said its board authorized management to step up its stock repurchase program by doubling the repurchase ceiling to 1.6 mln shares.     The company also said it has already purchased 691,000 shares through September 30 under the previous authorization to buy 800,000 shares, or 10 pct of the stock then outstanding.  Reuter &#3;
