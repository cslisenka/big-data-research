CANADA OKAYS VIPONT LABS <VLAB> TOOTHPASTE

Vipont Laboratories Inc said the Canadian Bureau of Non-Prescription Drugs approved its Viadent toothpaste and oral rinse for marketing.     The company also said it finalized an agreement with a Canadian marketing company to introduce the product in the country. It said Viadent helps prevent plaque build-up and inflamed gums.     The products should be available in Canada in three to four months, the company added.  Reuter &#3;
