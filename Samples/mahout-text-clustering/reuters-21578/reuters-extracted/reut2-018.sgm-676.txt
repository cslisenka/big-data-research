COUPON FIXED AS INDICATED ON WACOAL WARRANT BOND

The coupon on the 80 mln ECU, five year equity warrant eurobond for Japan's Wacoal Corp has been fixed at the indicated 1-1/2 pct, bookrunner Banque Paribas Capital Markets said.     The exercise price was set at 1,323 yen per share, representing a premium of 2.56 pct over today's closing price of 1,290 yen. The foreign exchange rate was set at 167.11 yen to the ECU.
