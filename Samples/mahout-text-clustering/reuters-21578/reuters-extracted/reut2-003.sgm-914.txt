HOME SHOPPING <HSN> FILES 400 MLN DEBT OFFERING

Home Shopping Network Inc filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an offering of 400 mln dlrs of convertible subordinated debentures due April 1, 2002.     Proceeds from the offering will be used to build and equip enlarged broadcast and order processing facilities, to redeem some of the company's 11-3/4 pct senior notes due Oct 15, 1996 and for general corporate purposes, the company said.     Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc will underwrite the sale.  Reuter &#3;
