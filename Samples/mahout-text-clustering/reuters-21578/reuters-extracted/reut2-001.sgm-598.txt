CHRYSLER <C> LATE-FEBRUARY SALES RISE 11 PCT

Chrysler Corp said sales of its U.S.-built cars rose 11 pct in the February 20-28 period to 30,634 units compared to 27,526 in the period a year ago.     There were seven selling days in both periods.     For the month of February sales fell two pct to 78,508 cars from 80,322 a year ago.     Year-to-date sales were not immediately available.
