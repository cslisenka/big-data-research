H.B. FULLER <FULL.O> HOLDERS APPROVE SHARE RISE

H.B. Fuller Co said shareholders at the annual meeting approved an increase in authorized common shares to 40 mln from 15 mln and a requirement that certain accumulations of its voting securities by subject to prior shareholder approval.     In addition, it said shareholders approved the elimination of the personal liability of directors in legal proceedings alleging a breach of fiduciary duty.  Reuter &#3;
