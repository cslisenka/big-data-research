AMOCO <AN> TO BUILD TAIWAN CHEMICAL PLANT

Amoco Corp said a Taiwan petrochemical venture in which it is part owner plans to build a chemical plant in Taiwan.     The company said the plant, to be built by China American Petrochemical Co Ltd which is 50 pct owned by Amoco Chemical Co, will make purified terephthalic acid (PTA), a raw material used to make polyester fibers.     It said the facility, China American's third PTA plant, will have a capacity of 550 mln pounds of PTA annually.     China American's other two owners are Chinese Petroleum Corp, 25 pct, and Central Investment Holding Co, 25 pct.  Reuter &#3;
