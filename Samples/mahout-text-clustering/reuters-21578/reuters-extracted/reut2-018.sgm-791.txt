ZENITH <ZEN>, SQUIBB <SQB> IN SUPPLY TALKS

Zenith Laboratories Inc said it is discusssing with Squibb Corp's SquibbMark division a three-year agreement to supply some products to Squibb to sell under a SquibbMark label.     Zenith said the deal includes products it already markets and some that are pending marketing approval from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration.     Zenith also said the proposed agreement is still in negotiation and subject to approval by senior managements of Squibb and Zenith.  Reuter &#3;
