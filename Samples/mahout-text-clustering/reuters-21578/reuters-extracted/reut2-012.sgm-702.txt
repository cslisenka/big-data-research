ASSETS OF U.S. MONEY FUNDS FELL IN WEEK

Assets of money market mutual funds decreased 833 mln dlrs in the week ended yesterday to 235.40 billion dlrs, the Investment Company Institute said.     Assets of 197 general purpose funds rose 1.10 billion dlrs to 63.21 billion dlrs, 92 broker-dealer funds fell 1.59 billion dlrs to 107.02 billion dlrs and 91 institutional funds fell 341 mln dlrs to 65.17 billion dlrs.
