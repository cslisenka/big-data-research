ATKINSON'S <ATKN> CANADA UNIT GETS CONTRACT

Guy F. Atkinson Co of California said its Commonwealth Construction Co unit received a 110 mln dlr contract covering construction of a paper machine for St. Mary's Paper Inc of Ontario, Canada.     The company said project startup is scheduled for April 1987 with completion expected in December 1988.  Reuter &#3;
