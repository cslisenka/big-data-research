TEXACO CANADA <TXC> LOWERS CRUDE POSTINGS

Texaco Canada said it lowered the contract price it will pay for crude oil 64 Canadian cts a barrel, effective today.     The decrease brings the company's posted price for the benchmark grade, Edmonton/Swann Hills Light Sweet, to 22.26 Canadian dlrs a bbl.     Texaco Canada last changed its crude oil postings on Feb 19.  Reuter &#3;
