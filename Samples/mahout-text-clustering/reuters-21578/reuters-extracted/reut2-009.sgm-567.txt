HELM <H> AFFILIATE SELLS SYSTEM

Teletrak Advanced Technology Systems Inc, 35 pct-owned by Helm Resources Inc, said it received a  contract to provide its Laser Base 400 System to a national commercial bank.     The system is an optical disk storage and retrieval system that utilizes commercially accepted computer hardware, it said.     Teletrak also said a New York law firm has retained it to develop the laser base software for the firm's word processing system.   Reuter &#3;
