LANDMARK BANCSHARES <LBC> TO BE LISTED ON NYSE

Landmark Bancshares Corp said it expects its stock to begin trading on March 26 on the New York Stock Exchange.     The company, whose stock has traded on the American Stock Exchange since November 1984, said it will retain its symbol, LBC, when trading begins on the Big Board.  Reuter &#3;
