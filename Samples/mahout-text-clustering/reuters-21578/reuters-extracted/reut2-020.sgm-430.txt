ACETO <ACET.O> BOOSTS SHARE REPURCHASE

Aceto Corp said its board increased its authorized share repurchase program to 500,000 shares from 200,000. The shares will be bought in the open market.     It has bought back 185,422 shares under the previously authorized 200,000 share program.  Reuter &#3;
