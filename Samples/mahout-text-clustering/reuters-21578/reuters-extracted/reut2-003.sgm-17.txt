NEC <NIPNY> UNIT INTRODUCES NEW COMPUTERS

NEC Corp's NEC Informational Systems Inc said it introduced three advanced personal computers that are fully compatible with the International Business Machines Corp <IBM> PC AT.     The computers, the PowerMate 1, PowerMate 2 and BusinessMate, are based on a chip made by Intel Corp <INTC>.     The PowerMate 1 and 2 are available immediately and sell for 1,995 dlrs and 2,595 dlrs, respectively, the company said.     The BusinessMate, scheduled for April availability, will sell for less than 6,000 dlrs, it said.  Reuter &#3;
