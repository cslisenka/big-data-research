FINAL TERMS SET ON EAGLE INDUSTRY WARRANT BOND

The coupon on the 17 mln dlr, five year, equity warrant eurobond for Eagle Industry Co Ltd has been set at the indicated level of 1-7/8 pct, lead manager Nomura International Ltd said.     The exercise price was set at 729 yen a share, representing a premium of 2.53 pct over today's closing price of 711 yen. The foreign exchange rate was set at 146.20 yen to the dollar.  REUTER &#3;
