UNICORP <UAC> POSTS GAIN ON PROPERTY SALE

Unicorp American Corp <UAC.A> said it will take an after-tax gain in the second quarter of 1.5 mln dlrs from the sale of three properties in the Boston area.     It said the properties were sold for aggregate proceeds of about 6.3 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
