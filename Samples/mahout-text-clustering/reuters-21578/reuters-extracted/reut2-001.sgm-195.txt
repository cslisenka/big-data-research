STEELHEAD RESOURCES <SHZ> LEASES FELDSPAR MINE

Steelhead Resources Ltd said it has leased some feldspar deposits and will begin feldspar mining operations near Wickenburg, Arizona.     The company said the deposits contain in excess of 500,000 tons of feldspar.  Reuter &#3;
