BDM <BDM> GETSW ARMY CONTRACT

BDM International Inc said it has received a U.S. Army contract with a base year value of 1,400,000 dlrs to support the plans and operations staff of the U.S. Army Joint Readiness Training Center, based at Little Rock Air Force Base.     The company said with three option years, total value would be about 7,200,000 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
