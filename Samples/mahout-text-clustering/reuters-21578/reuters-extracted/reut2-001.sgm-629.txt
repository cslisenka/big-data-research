HOME INTENSIVE <KDNY> EXTENDS CONVERSION RIGHTS

Home Intensive Care Inc said it extended by 11 days the period of time during which its callable March cumulative convertible preferred stock may be converted at the current conversion ratio of 2.2 shares of common stock for every preferred share.     The conversion ratio is now in effect until the close of business on March 16, after which the ratio drops to two shares of common stock for every preferred share until March 5, 1988.  Reuter &#3;
