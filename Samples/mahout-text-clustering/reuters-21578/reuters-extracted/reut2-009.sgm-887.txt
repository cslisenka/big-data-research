OAKWOOD HOMES <OH> SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBENTURES

Oakwood Homes Corp is raising 25 mln dlrs via an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due 2012 with a 6-1/2 pct coupon and par pricing, said lead manager Donaldson, Lufkin and Jenrette Securities Corp.     The debentures are convertible into the company's common stock at 21.25 dlrs per share, representing a premium of 23.19 pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set.     Non-callable for three years, the issue is rated B-2 by Moody's Investors and B by Standard and Poor's. J.C. Bradford and Legg Mason co-managed the deal.  Reuter &#3;
