BODDIE-NOELL FILES FOR INITIAL PUBLIC OFFERING

<Boddie-Noell Restaurant Properties Inc> said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission covering its initial public offering of 3,100,000 shares of common stock.     The company said it plans to qualify as a real estate investment trust and buy 53 existing Hardee's restaurant properties located in Virginia and North Carolina.  Reuter &#3;
