FIRST OF AMERICA <FABK>, MICHIGAN BANK AFFILIATE

First of America Bank Corp said it completed its affiliation with Lewiston State Bank of Lewiston, Mich.     The Lewiston bank, which has been renamed First of America Bank-Lewiston, has assets of 54.9 mln dlrs. First of America has assets of more than 7.9 billion dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
