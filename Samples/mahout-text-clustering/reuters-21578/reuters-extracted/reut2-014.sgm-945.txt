ALLIED SIGNAL <ALD> FIBERS UNIT SETS RESEARCH

Hauser Chemical Research Inc said it has been contracted by Allied Signal Inc's Allied Fibers division for research consulting on further development of improved adhesion of polyolefin fiber through photografting.     Hauser said it previously completed the successful first phase of this research effort.  Reuter &#3;
