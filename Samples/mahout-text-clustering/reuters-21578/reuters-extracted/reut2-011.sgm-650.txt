DUCOMMUN <DCO> PRESIDENT RESIGNS

Ducommun Inc said W. Donald Bell resigned as president and a director of the company and president of Kierulff, Ducommun's largest division.     Chairman Wallace Booth will re-assume the duties of president, a role he relinquished in 1985, the company also said.  Reuter &#3;
