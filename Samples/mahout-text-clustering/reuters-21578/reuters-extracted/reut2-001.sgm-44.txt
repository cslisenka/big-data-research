VWR CORP <VWRX> FORMS NEW UNIT

VWR Corp said it has formed a new subsidiary, Momentun Textiles, to handle the distribution of upholstery fabrics, leathers and naugahyde to contract and consumer furniture manufacturers.     It said its VWR Textiles and Supplies unit, which had handled that business, will continue to distribute non-woven textiles, construction fabrics and manufacturing supplies.  Reuter &#3;
