GM CANADA FEBRUARY CAR SALES OFF 17.8 PCT

General Motors of Canada Ltd, wholly owned by General Motors Corp, said February car sales fell 17.8 pct to 25,779 units from 31,361 units the previous year.     Year-to-date car sales fell 23.8 pct from last year, the company said. It did not immediately disclose actual year-to-date figures.  Reuter &#3;
