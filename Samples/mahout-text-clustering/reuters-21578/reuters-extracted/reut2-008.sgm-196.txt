IRANIAN TANKER ATTACKED OVER WEEKEND - LLOYDS

The Iranian steam tanker Avaj, 316,379 tonnes dw, was attacked and hit at 1715 hours on March 21, Lloyds Shipping Intelligence service reported.     One person was killed. The tanker is owned by the National Iranian Tanker Co.  REUTER &#3;
