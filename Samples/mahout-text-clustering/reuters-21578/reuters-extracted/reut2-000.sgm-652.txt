VALLEY FEDERAL <VFED> NAMES NEW OFFICERS

Valley Federal Savings and Loan Association said it appointed Joseph Biafora to the post of chairman and the company's president, Donald Headlund, was named to the additional post of chief executive.     The new appointments follow the death of former chairman and chief executive Robert Gibson, the company said.     It said Biafora had been vice chairman of the board.  Reuter &#3;
