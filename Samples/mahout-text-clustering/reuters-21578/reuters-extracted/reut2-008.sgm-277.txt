WAITE KIDNAPPED AS SPY - TEHRAN RADIO

Tehran radio said British church envoy Terry Waite, missing in Beirut since January 20, had been kidnapped as a spy by a Lebanese group.     "Terry Waite was taken hostage by an armed Lebanese group, calling itself the Revolutionary Justice Organisation. He has been accused of espionage activities," said the radio, monitored by the British Broadcasting Corporation.  Reuter &#3;
