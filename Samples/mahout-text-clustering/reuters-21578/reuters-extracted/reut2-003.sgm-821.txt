TRANSCO ENERGY <E> CALLS 3.875 DLR PREFERRED

Transco Energy Co said on April 10 it will redeem all 707,905 outstanding shares of its 3,875 dlr series cumulative convertible preferred stock at 51.17 dlrs per share plus accrued dividends of 74 cts.     Transco said each share of the preferred is convertible into 1,263 common shares.  Reuter &#3;
