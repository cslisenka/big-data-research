TENNECO <TGT> TO REDEEM PREFERRED STOCK

Tenneco Inc said it will redeem on April eight all 2,515,400 outstanding shares of its 11 dlr cumulative preferred stock.     The company said it would pay 102 dlrs a share for the preferred stock, plus accrued and unpaid dividends of 24 cts a share.     Tenneco said it will pay the regular quarterly dividend on the preferred on March 31 to shareholders of record February 27.  Reuter &#3;
