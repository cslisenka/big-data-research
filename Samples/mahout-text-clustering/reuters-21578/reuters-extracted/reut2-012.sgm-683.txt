U.S SENATE OVERRIDES REAGAN'S HIGHWAY BILL VETO

The Senate overrode President Reagan's veto of an 88 billion dlr highway funding bill, 67-33.     The measure now becomes law. It was a major personal loss for Reagan, who came to the Capitol earlier in the day to campaign for support.  Reuter &#3;
