PAN AM <PN> AGREES TO EXTEND UNION PACT

The Air Line Pilots Association said Pan Am Corp agreed that pilots of its non-union subsidiary, Pan Am Express, will be placed on the pilot seniority list of its unionized subsidiary, Pan American World Airways, and will be covered by the current ALPA contract.  Reuter &#3;
