MUSTO <MUSMF> NEGOTIATES PRIVATE PLACEMENT

Musto Explorations Ltd said it negotiated the private placement of up to 300,000 shares at 1.65 dlrs each.     It said the investors would receive non-transferable share purchase warrants allowing them to acquire up to another 300,000 shares at 1.90 dlrs each over one year.     The placement is subject to regulatory approval, it said.  Reuter &#3;
