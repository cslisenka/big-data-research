KIDNAPPED BUSINESSMAN RELEASED

Kidnapped Japanese businessman Nobuyuki Wakaoji has been released after four months in captivity, Presidential spokesman Teodoro Benigno said.     Benigno told reporters the 53-year-old Mitsui and Co Ltd executive was freed yesterday and is being held in an undisclosed hospital.     He gave no further details on the release, which was confirmed minutes earlier by a Mitsui spokesman in Tokyo.  Reuter &#3;
