NOSTALGIA <NNET>, ACTS SATELLITE IN DEAL

Nostalgia Network Inc said it signed an agreement to air two minute and five minute commercial announcements of its home shopping service called Collectible Showcase on the ACTS Satellite Network.     ACTS Network, with over six mln subscribers, will air the spots about 28 times per week for 13 weeks.      Reuter &#3;
