3COM CORP <COMS> INTRODUCES NEW WORKSTATION

3Com Corp said it introduced a new network workstation for business applications.     The product, called 3Station, is the first in a family of network workstations, which will later include a Token Ring version, the company said.     It said the 3Station is an IBM-compatible 80286-based Ethernet product which can be integrated with personal computers on a network.     The company also said it plans to begin shipments on May 15.
