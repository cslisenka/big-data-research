GULF/WESTERN <GW> UNIT, DISNEY <DIS> SET PACT

Gulf and Western Inc's Simon and Schuster Inc unit said it will become the exclusive worldwide distributor for Walt Disney Co's educational films, videos and filmstrips.     The company said the agreement covers more than 300 films and videos and 500 filmstrips for schools, colleges and other institutions.  Reuter &#3;
