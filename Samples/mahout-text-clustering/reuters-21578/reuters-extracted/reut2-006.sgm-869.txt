WESTINGHOUSE <WX> GETS 114 MLN DLRS IN CONTRACTS

Westinghouse Electric Corp's Defense and Electronic Systems Center is being awarded two Air Force contract increases totalling 114.0 mln dlrs for work related to the F-16 radar, the Defense Department said.     It said both contracts, which combine purchases for the U.S. Air Force and Turkey under the Foreign Military Sales program, are expected to be completed in December 1988.  Reuter &#3;
