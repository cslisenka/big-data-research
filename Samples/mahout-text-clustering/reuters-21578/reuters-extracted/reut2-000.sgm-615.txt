ALBERTSON'S <ABS> TO INCREASE NEW STORE OPENINGS

Albertson's said it plans to open 33 to 35 new stores during 1987 and remodel about 20-25 stores.     This compares to 21 new store openings in 1986 and 17 remodelings.     Albertson's, a grocery chain, said total capital expenditures for these projects will be in excess of 200 mln dlrs in 1987.  Reuter &#3;
