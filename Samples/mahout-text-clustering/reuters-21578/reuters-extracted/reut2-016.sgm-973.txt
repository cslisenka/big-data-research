SMALL QUANTITY OF U.K. WHEAT SOLD TO HOME MARKET

A total of 2,769 tonnes of British intervention feed wheat, out of an available 57,300 tonnes, was sold at today's tender for the home market, the Home Grown Cereals Authority, HGCA, said.     Price details were not reported.  Reuter &#3;
