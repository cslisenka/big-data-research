LTV <QLTV> UNIT WINS AIR FORCE CONTRACT

LTV Corp's LTV Missiles and Electronics Group said it received a 28.3 mln dlr contract from the U.S. Air Force for continued design and development of an anti-satellite weapon system.     The company said the contract work will be performed at its facilities in Grand Prairie, Texas, and is expected to be completed by September.  Reuter &#3;
