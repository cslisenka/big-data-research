OPTO MECHANIK <OPTO.O> GETS 7.2 MLN IN CONTRACTS

Opto Mechanik Inc, a designer and manufacturer of military optical and electro-optical systems, said it received 7.2 mln in contracts from domestic and foreign customers.     Opto Mechanik said the series of contracts included a 3.8 mln dlr contract from the U.S. Army Missle Command to produce the sight used to guide the TOW anti-tank missle.  Reuter &#3;
