SAVINGS INSTITUTION TO HELP AILING THRIFTS

The U.S. League of Savings Institutions said it has launched a program to bolster the liquidity of some savings and loan institutions and reduce their dependency on high interest rate deposits.     A spokesman for the league said 48 institutions have committed 340 mln dlrs to the program and estimated that the program would begin within a week.  Reuter &#3;
