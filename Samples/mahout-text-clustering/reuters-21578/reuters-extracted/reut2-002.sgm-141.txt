BEST PRODUCTS <BES> FEBRUARY SALES OFF 12.7 PCT

Best Products Inc said sales for the four weeks ended February 28 were off 12.7 pct to 111.3 mln dlrs from 127.6 mln dlrs a year before, excluding sales from catalog stores and Ashby's Ltd outlets that were closed or sold last year.     The company said in an effort to improve gross margins, it significantly cut promotional activity in February from a year earlier.  In addition, it said it distributed its spring catalog nationwide in February last year and will distribute it in March this year.  Reuter &#3;
