PIEDMONT <PIE> ACCEPTS RESERVATIONS FOR BAHAMAS

Piedmont Aviation Inc said it is accepting reservations for nonstop service to Nassau, Bahamas from Charlotte, N.C. beginning November 15.     The new service will connect more than 30 cities on the Piedmont system to Nassau, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
