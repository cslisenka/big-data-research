DUTCH DU PONT UNIT TO CUT WORKFORCE BY 140

Du Pont De Nemours Nederland BV, a unit of U.S. Chemical firm E.I. Du Pont De Nemours and Co <DD.N>, plans to dismiss 140 of its workforce, Dutch labour federation FNV spokesman Frank Koolen said.     A company spokesman confirmed the dismissal plans and added the firm, which produces plugs and contact systems for the electronics industry, needs to be restructured to cut losses.     He declined to give financial details regarding the losses.     The FNV spokesman also could not give exact sums, but said from 1984 the unit had lost some tens of millions of guilders.
