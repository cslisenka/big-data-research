CANDLEWOOD BANK PLANS INITIAL OFFERING

<Candlewood Bank and Trust Co> said it plans an initial public offering of 500,000 common shares at an expected price of 10 dlrs each through underwriter Moseley Holdings Corp <MOSE>, which has been granted an option to buy up to 75,000 more shares to cover overallotments.     Candlewood is being formed to serve the area around Candlewood Lake in Connecitcut,  Proceeds from the offering would support its operations.  Reuter &#3;
