HONG KONG BANKS LEAVE INTEREST RATES UNCHANGED

The Hong Kong Association of Banks said it decided to leave interest rates unchanged at today's regular weekly meeting.     Current rates are savings accounts and 24-hours two pct, seven-day call, one and two-weeks 2-1/4 pct, one and two months 2-3/4 pct, three and six months 3-1/4 pct, nine months 3-1/2 pct and 12 months four pct.     Prime rate is 6-1/2 pct.  REUTER &#3;
