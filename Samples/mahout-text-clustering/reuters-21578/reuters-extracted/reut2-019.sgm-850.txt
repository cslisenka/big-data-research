GATEWAY <GWAY.O> SEES LOWER 2ND QTR RESULTS

Gateway Communications Inc said it expects its second quarter earnings to be lower than last year's income of seven cts per share, but its revenue for the period should increase by 20 pct over the 2.7 mln dlrs reported a year ago.     The decline in second quarter earnings is attributable to higher operating costs resulting from increased sales, development and expansion, Gateway said.  Reuter &#3;
