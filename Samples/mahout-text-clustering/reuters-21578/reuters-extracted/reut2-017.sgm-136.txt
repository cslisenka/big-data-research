ASCS SEEKS 12.5 MLN POUNDS VEG OIL FOR EXPORT

The Agricultural Stabilization and Conservation Service (ASCS) is seeking 12.5 mln pounds of vegetable oil for export June 6-20 and June 21/July 5, an ASCS official said.     Offers must be received at the Kansas City Commodity Office by 1430 CDT May 4, and successful offerors will be notified May 5.  reuter &#3;
