PHELPS DODGE <PD> FILES FOR OFFERING

Phelps Dodge Corp said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an offering of four mln depositary shares.     Each depositary share represents one quarter share of a convertible exchange preference share. The liquidation preference of each depositary share is 50 dlrs.     Quarterly dividends on the preference shares are cumulative and are payable beginning July 1, 1987.     Proceeds will be used to redeem outstanding five dlr convertible exchangeable preference shares, to repay bank debt and for general corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
