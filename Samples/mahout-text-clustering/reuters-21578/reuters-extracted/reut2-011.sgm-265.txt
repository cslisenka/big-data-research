CITICORP <CCI> STARTS MONEY TRANSFER SERVICE

Citicorp's Citicorp Global Payment Products subsidiary said it has started nationwide operation of its new rapid public money transfer service, Citicorp Express Money Service.     It said consumers can now send up to 10,000 dlrs across the country within 15 minutes, 24 hours a day.  Reuter &#3;
