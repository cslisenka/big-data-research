FINANCIAL PERFORMANCE <FPCC> PURSUES EXPANSION

Financial Performance Corp said it continued its rapid expansion with a signing of a partnership agreement with Gold Sierra Financial Advisors, founded by former Bank of America officers in capital markets.     It said the agreement, under which it owns 50 pct of the partnership, enables it to move into the mergers and acquisitions business.  Reuter &#3;
