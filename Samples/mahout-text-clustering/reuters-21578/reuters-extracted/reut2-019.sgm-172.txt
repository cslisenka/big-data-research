HEALTHCARE SERVICES <HSAI.O> NAMES EXECUTIVES

Healthcare Services of America Inc said its chairman and chief executive officer, Charles Speir, resigned and its president and chief operating officer, Thomas Rodgaers Jr, was relieved of his duties.     Michael Cronin will take over as chairman and Michael Murphy as acting president and chief executive officer, the company said.     Cronin has been a director of the company since 1983 and Murphy has been a consultant to the compay, Healthcare Services said.  Reuter &#3;
