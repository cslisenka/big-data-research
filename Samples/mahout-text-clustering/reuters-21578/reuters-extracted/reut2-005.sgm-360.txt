NO FEDERATION BANK SEEN INVOLVED IN VW AFFAIR

Hanns Christian Schroeder-Hohenwarth, President of the West German Federation of Banks, said that as far as he knew no member bank of the Federation was involved in activities which led to currency losses of some 480 mln marks for Volkswagen AG <VOWG.F>, VW.     VW has said fraudulent currency manipulation possibly led to the losses.     The Federation of Banks includes all the major West German commercial banks. Schroeder-Hohenwarth was speaking at a news conference after a Federation meeting.  REUTER &#3;
