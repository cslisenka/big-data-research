RCM TECHNOLOGIES <RCMT> ORDER INCREASED

RCM Technologies Inc said its contract to provide engineering personnel to the Sikorsky Aircraft division of United Technologies Corp <UTX> has been increased by 4,300,000 dlrs to 11.0 mln dlrs.     It said the contract runs through the end of 1987.     Reuter &#3;
