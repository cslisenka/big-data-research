JAPAN JOINING INTERNATIONAL COCOA AGREEMENT

Japan is in the process of joining the International Cocoa Organisation (ICCO), which will bring the number of members in the body to 36, ICCO officials said.     Japan is completing constitutional procedures necessary to its accession to the ICCO and is expected to become an importing member in three to four months, they said.  REUTER &#3;
