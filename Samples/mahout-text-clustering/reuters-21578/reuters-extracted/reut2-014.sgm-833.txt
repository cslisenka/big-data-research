JAPANESE CONSORTIUM WINS NEW YORK SUBWAY CAR ORDER

<Nissho Iwai Corp> and Kawasaki Heavy Industries Ltd <KAWH.T> have jointly won an order to build 200 subway cars worth about 200 mln dlrs for the Metropolitan Transportation Authority of New York, a Nissho spokesman said.     The cars, to be produced with 51 pct U.S. Content, will be delivered between early 1988 and August 1989, he said.     Ten will be built in Japan, but the others will be made at a Yonkers, New York, plant equally owned by Nissho and Kawasaki.  REUTER &#3;
