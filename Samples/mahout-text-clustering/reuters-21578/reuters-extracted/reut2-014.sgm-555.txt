CRYODYNAMICS <CRYD> WARRANTS CONVERTED

Cryodynamics Inc said it received 1,575,000 dlrs through a warrant conversion program that ended March 31.     The company said 85.5 pct of its one mln outstanding warrants were converted to common stock. It said it now has 7,500,000 common shares outstanding.     The money generated by the warrant conversion has been added to working capital, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
