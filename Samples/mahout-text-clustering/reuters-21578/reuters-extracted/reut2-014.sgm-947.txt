<ASIX SYSTEMS> GETS MORE FINANCING

ASIX Systems, a developer of test systems for ASIC devices, said it has received 4,800,000 dlrs in a second round of equity financing.     The company said investors include first round participants Paragon Partners, Citicorp <CCI>, Vista Ventures, Rust Ventures and Orien Ventures, plus new investors North Carolina Bank Venture Co LP, RepublicBankCorp <RPT> and ECI Ventures.     It said it has now raised total capitalization of 8,600,000 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
