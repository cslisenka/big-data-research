CARSON PIRIE SCOTT AND CO <CRN> MARCH SALES UP

Carson Pirie Scott and Co said its March retail sales increased 8.2 pct to 70.6 mln dlrs from 65.3 mln dlrs a year ago.     It said cumulative sales gained 13.2 pct to 125.0 mln dlrs from 110.4 mln dlrs in the same 1986 period.  Reuter &#3;
