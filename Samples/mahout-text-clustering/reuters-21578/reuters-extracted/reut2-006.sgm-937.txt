EQUATORIAL COMMUNICATIONS <EQUA> SETS PACT

Equatorial Communications Co said it set an agreement with FIserv Inc <FISV> to supply and operate a private satellite-based data transaction network.     Under the terms of the agreement, Equatorial will provide FIserv with C-200 series transmit/receive micro earth stations to be located at client financial institutions throughout the U.S.  Reuter &#3;
