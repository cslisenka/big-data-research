RAPIDTECH SYSTEMS INC BUYS PATENT RIGHTS

<Rapidtech Systems Inc> said it acquired all rights to the patent for the "pocket modem", which eliminates the need for stand-alone modems that are attached between telephones and computer terminals, as well as modems built into computers.     Rapidtech said the patent includes all rights to license and build the modems, as well as a patent application for an optical isolator.     The company did not disclose a price for the rights.
