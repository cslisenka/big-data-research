FIRST FINANCIAL <FFMC> BUYS CONFIDATA

First Financial Management Corp said it acquired Confidata Corp, a unit of <North Ridge Bank>, paying 500,000 dlrs in cash and pledging a guarantee on a 500,000 dlr Confidata note held by North Ridge.     First Financial, which provides data processing services to community banks and other financial institutions, said the acquisition will expand its customer base.  Reuter &#3;
