FEDERAL INDUSTRIES SETS COMMERCIAL PAPER ISSUE

<Federal Industries Ltd> said it introduced a commercial paper program with an authorization of 440 mln dlrs through agents <Bank of Montreal>, <Dominion Securities Inc> and Wood Gundy Inc.     Net proceeds from the sale of notes will be used for general corporate purposes and will replace existing outstanding debt, the company said.     It did not elaborate on financial terms of the issue.  Reuter &#3;
