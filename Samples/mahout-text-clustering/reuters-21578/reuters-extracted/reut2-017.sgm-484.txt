NORWAY'S RS BANK ISSUES 40 MLN SWISS FRANC BOND

RS Bank Sparebanken Rogaland of Stavanger, Norway is launching a 40 mln Swiss franc seven year bond with a 4-7/8 pct coupon and par issue price, lead manager Banque Gutzwiller, Kurz, Bungener Ltd said.     Subscription is from June 11 until June 17 with closing and pay-out on July 7.     It can be called at 101 in 1992, and at 100-1/2 in 1993.  REUTER &#3;
