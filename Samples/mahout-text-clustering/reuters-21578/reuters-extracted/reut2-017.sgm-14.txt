ALTEX <AII> TO SELL OILFIELD SERVICES ASSETS

Altex Industries Inc said it agreed to sell the assets of its wholly-owned oilfield service subsidiary, Parrish Oil Tools Inc.     The price and buyer were not disclosed.     Altex said Parrish had a loss from operations of 428,000 dlrs on revenues of 881,000 dlrs in fiscal 1986 and a loss from operations of 48,000 dlrs on revenues of two mln dlrs in fiscal 1985.
