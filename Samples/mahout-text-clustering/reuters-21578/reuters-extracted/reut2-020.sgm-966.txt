AMETEK <AME> TO BUY STOCK, REVIEW DIVIDEND

Ametek Inc said its board approved a program to purchase the company's stock.     It also announced directors will review increasing the quarterly dividend at their regular November meeting, considering a payment of 15 cts a share on the recently split stock. Ametek said this would be equal to 30 cts a share on the present shares, up from the 25 cts quarterly paid previously.     The company provided no details on its stock repurchase program. It noted such purchases have been made from time-to-time in the past.  Reuter &#3;
