WESTERN UNION <WU> IN PACT WITH ELECTRONIC DATA

Western Union said it signed an agreement with <Electronic Data Systems Corp>.     Under the agreement, EDS will provide Western Union's easylink electronic mail service to the 35,000 EDS diamond communications users of General Motors Corp <GM> internal electronic mail network. Diamond is an interface linking eight incompatible electronic mail systems at GM's various divisions.     Diamond users will now have a communications path to 160,000 easylink users and 1.5 mln telex users worldwide, Western Union said.  Reuter &#3;
