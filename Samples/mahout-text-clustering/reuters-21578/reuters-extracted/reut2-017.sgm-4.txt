FED ADDS RESERVES VIA TWO-DAY REPURCHASES

The Federal Reserve entered the U.S. Government securities market to arrange two-day System repurchase agreements, a Fed spokesman said.     Dealers said that Federal funds were trading at 6-7/16 pct when the Fed began its temporary and direct supply of reserves to the banking system.  Reuter &#3;
