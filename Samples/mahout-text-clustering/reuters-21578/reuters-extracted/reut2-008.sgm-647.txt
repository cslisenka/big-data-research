COPENHAGEN TELEPHONE 600 MLN FRENCH FRANC ISSUE

Copenhagen Telephone Co Ltd is issuing a 600 mln French franc 15-year retractable adjustable bond, lead manager Credit Commercial de France (CCF) said.     The bond will have an issue price of 101-1/8 pct and for the first five years it will carry a nine pct coupon.     The final due date will be April 28, 2002 and the issue will be both puttable and callable on April 28, 1992 and 1997 at par.     It will be denominated in 10,000 franc units and be listed in Luxembourg. Co-lead will be Privatbanken.  REUTER &#3;
