COMMUNITY BANK SYSTEM <CBSI> BUYS NICHOLS BANK

Community Bank Systems Inc and the <Nichols National Bank> said they have signed a definitive agreement for Nichols to become a member of the CBSI Group of banks for an exchange of stock worth 2.8 mln dlrs.     CBSI said it expects to complete the deal, pending Nichols' shareholder and regulatory approval, later this year.  Reuter &#3;
