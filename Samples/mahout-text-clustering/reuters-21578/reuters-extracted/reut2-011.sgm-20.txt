HARTMARX <HMX> UNIT PRESIDENT RESIGNS

Hartmarx Corp said Geoffrey Bloom resigned as president and chief executive officer of its Jaymar-Ruby Inc subsidiary to accept the position of president and chief operating officer of Wolverine World Wide Inc <WWW>.     It said Elbert Hand, president and chief operating officer of Hartmarx, and president and chief executive officer of the Hartmarx Men's Apparel Group, was named chief executive officer of Jaymar-Ruby Inc. Burton Ruby, son of the Jaymar-Ruby founder, remains as chairman.             Reuter &#3;
