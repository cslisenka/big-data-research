A.H. BELO <BLC> SEEKS TO REINCORPORATE

A.H. Belo Corp said it will ask shareholders at the May Six annual meeting to approve a change of state of incorporation to Delaware from Texas.     It said it wants to take advantage of Delaware's more flexible corporation laws and laws allowing the limitation of directors' liability.     Belo said two thirds approval is required.  Reuter &#3;
