IONE COMPLETES INITIAL PUBLIC OFFERING

<Ione Inc>, which buys, sells and manages retail gift, sundries and smokeshops located in hotels, said it successfully completed a 1.8 mln share common stock initial public offering at 25 cts a share.     Rudy Steury, Ione president, said Ione will use the proceeds to further develop its retail shop network, as well as to purchase and resell more shops.  Reuter &#3;
