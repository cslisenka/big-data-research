DOMTAR <DTC> WITHDRAWS STOCK OFFERING

Domtar Inc said it is withdrawing a previously-announced issue of five mln common shares, "due to unfavourable conditions in the financial markets."     Domtar said its capital expenditure program will not be affected by the issue's withdrawal as other sources of financing are currently available.     Underwriters were Nesbitt Thomson Deacon Ltd, Levesque, Beaubien Inc and McLeod Young Weir Ltd.  Reuter &#3;
