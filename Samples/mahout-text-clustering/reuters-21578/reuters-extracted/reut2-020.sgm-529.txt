UNITED BRANDS <UB> SEES 3RD QTR NET UP

United Brands Co said it expects to report substantially improved earnings for the third quarter over the year-ago 4,300,000 dlrs or 26 cts per share.     The company also said it has repurchased 695,000 common shares for about 28 mln dlrs from affiliate <Great American Communications Co>.     The company said it has authorized the repurchase of an additional 750,000 common shares in market or private transactions from time to time.  Reuter &#3;
