REPUBLIC BANK <RNB> DEBT AFFIRMED BY S/P

Standard and Poor's Corp said it affirmed about 600 mln dlrs of debt of Republic Bank NY Corp.     Affirmed were Republic's AA senior debt, AA-minus subordinated debt, A-plus preferred stock, A-1-plus commercial paper and A/A-1-plus certificates of deposit.     S and P cited the bank's decision to join the growing number of U.S. banks that have set aside reserves for developing country credits.     Republic strengthed its reserves by 100 mln dlrs.     Despite an expected second quarter loss, the firm remains one of the best capitalized banks in the U.S., S and P said.  Reuter &#3;
