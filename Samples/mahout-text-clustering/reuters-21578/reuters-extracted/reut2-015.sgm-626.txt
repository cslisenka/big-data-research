GANDALF <GANDF> IN PACT WITH APPAREL COMPUTER

Gandalf Technologies Inc said its Redifacts division and Apparel Computer Systems Inc of Concord, Calif., started a joint marketing effort in industrial data collection and dissemination.     Financial terms of the agreement were not disclosed.     Gandalf said the companies will link software products to develop an on-line, real-time payroll information system for apparel production factories.  Reuter &#3;
