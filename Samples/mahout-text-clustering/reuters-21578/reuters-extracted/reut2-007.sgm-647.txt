WYMAN-GORDON <WYMN> TO INVEST 11.7 MLN DLRS

Wyman-Gordon Inc said it plans to invest 11.7 mln dlrs in two projects to improve the company's metals technology capabilities.     The projects include the installation of a plasma cold hearth refining system in its Millbury facility for the production of nickel-base alloy power metal and titanium alloy electrodes, the company said.     The other involves the modification of its 35,000-ton hydraulic forging press, Wyman-Gordon said.     Both projects are scheduled for completion in 1989, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
