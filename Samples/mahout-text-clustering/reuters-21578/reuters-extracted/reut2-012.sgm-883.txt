BELGIAN STATE LOAN RAISES 168.8 BILLION FRANCS

The latest Belgian state loan, subscriptions for which closed today, raised a total of 168.8 billion francs, a finance ministry spokesman said.     The eight-year, eight pct domestic loan which was priced at par was originally expected by the ministry to raise 150 billion francs.  REUTER &#3;
