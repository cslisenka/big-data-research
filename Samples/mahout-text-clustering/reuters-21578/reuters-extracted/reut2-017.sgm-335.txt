NCR <NCR> SLASHES PRICES ON PERSONAL COMPUTERS

NCR Corp said it cut prices on its personal computers, the 80286-based PC8 and 8088-based PC6 models, by 10 pct to 21 pct effective immediately, to maintain a competitive position on pricing.     NCR said prices of its high-end PC8 models have been cut by 10 pct to 13 pct and by 10 pct in its PC8 model designed to compete with 286-based XT-level PCs. It also reduced the list price of its PC6 model configured with 256 RAM and 360 K flex drive, along with other products in the line.     NCR also said it reduced the price of its 64MB fixed disk drive to 2,995 dlrs from 3,595 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
