ARUS <ARSCC> GETS 5.2 MLN DLR ORDER

Arus Corp said it has received a letter of intent from Winston Financial Corp for the purchase of over 5,200,000 dlrs in telephone diagnostic testing equipment.     It said it expects to negotiate a firm order in May.     The company said the total value of the order is at least 5,200,000 dlrs and could be more, depending on circuit configurations required.
