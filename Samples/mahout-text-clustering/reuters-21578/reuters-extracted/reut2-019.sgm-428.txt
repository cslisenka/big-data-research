VENEZUELA WANTS 2.4 BILLION DLRS EXPORT FINANCE

venezuela will seek 2.4 billion dollars in export financing during 1987, double the amount it received last year, public finance director jorge marcano said.     Marcano told reporters at the finance ministry yesterday that the 2.4 billion is part of a total package of 3.2 billion dlrs in new credits, which venezuela will try to obtain.     "we've begun efforts to secure that financing," he said.  Reuter &#3;
