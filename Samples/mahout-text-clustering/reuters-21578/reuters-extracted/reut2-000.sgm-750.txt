NATIONAL EARLY WARNING SEEKS OMEGA BALLISTICS

<National Early Warning Systems Inc> said it has offered to purchase <Omega Ballistics Systems Inc> for an undisclosed amount of cash, stock and notes.     Valley Cottage, N.Y., based Omega is a developer and designer of lightweight, custom-crafted ballistic, assault, flame and fire resistant paroducts.  Reuter &#3;
