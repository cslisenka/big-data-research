INTERAND <IRND> NAMES NEW VIDEO PHONE MARKETER

Interand Corp said Harris/3M Document Products Inc is the new national sales representative for the Discon Imagephone, Interand's advanced interactive video telephone system.     It said the portable telephone can display detailed black and white pictures using the ordinary voice-grade telephone network.  Reuter &#3;
