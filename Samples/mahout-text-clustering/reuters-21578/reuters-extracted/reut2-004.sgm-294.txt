NL INDUSTRIES INC <NL> CHAIRMAN AND CEO QUITS

NL Industries Inc said its chairman and chief executive officer Theodore Rogers resigned this morning.     Rogers, who also resigned his posts as chairman and ceo of the company's subsidiary, NL Chemicals, was immediately replaced by Harold Simmons, the company said.     Simmons is president and chief executive officer of <Valhi Inc>, which owns approximately 51 pct of NL Industries, according to the company.     Fred Montanari will remain executive vice president of NL Industries and president of NL Chemicals, the company said.     Rogers was the company chief executive officer since 1983 and its chairman since 1984, according to the company. He retained both posts after a takeover in 1986 led by Simmons, th company said.     Rogers will remain as a consultant in certain company matters, NL said.  Reuter &#3;
