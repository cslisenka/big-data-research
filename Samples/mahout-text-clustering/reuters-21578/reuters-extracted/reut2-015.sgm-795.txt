DENPAC <DNPC> PLACES PROCESSOR ASSEMBLY ORDER

Denpac Corp said it placed an order for the assembly of the first 100 production units of its Sinterloy processor, which will begin delivery later this year.     The company said two pre-production units have been placed for clinical and laboratory evaluations, with an additional six pre-production units to be placed shortly.     The company said the Sinterloy processor is used by dental laboratories to fabricate dental appliances such as bridges and crowns.  Reuter &#3;
