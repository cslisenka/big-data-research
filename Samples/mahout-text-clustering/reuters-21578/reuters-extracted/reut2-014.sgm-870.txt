QUEBEC ISSUES 30 BILLION YEN EUROBOND

The Province of Quebec is issuing a 30 billion yen eurobond due May 7, 1997 paying five pct and priced at 102-1/2 pct, lead manager Bank of Tokyo International Ltd said.     The non-callable bond is available in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in Luxembourg. The selling concession is 1-1/4 pct, while management and underwriting combined pays 3/4 pct.     The payment date is May 7.  REUTER &#3;
