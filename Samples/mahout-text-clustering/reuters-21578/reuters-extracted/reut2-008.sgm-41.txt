LOUISIANA LAND <LLX> ASSUMES INEXCO'S DEBT

Louisiana Land and Exploration Co said it assumed the obligation to pay the principal and interest on the 8-1/2 pct convertible subordinated debentures due September 1, 2000 of Inexco Oil Co, a unit of Louisiana Land.     Effective March 23, the debentures will be listed as the debentures of Louisiana Land, LLXOO.  Reuter &#3;
