NORANDA WINS AID TO PARE ACID RAIN EMISSIONS

<Noranda Inc> has agreed to cut acid rain emissions by 50 pct at its Quebec copper smelter by 1990 with the federal and Quebec governments each providing 41.6 mln dlrs towards the cost, the federal industry department said.     Under an agreement, Noranda will also be required to  41.6 mln Canadian dlrs and cover operating costs of the proposed 125 mln dlr sulphuric acid plant.     The department said the agreement will allow Noranda to meet polutions standards in Quebec's new environmental regulations passed in 1985.  Reuter &#3;
