CERTIFICATED COTTON STOCKS

Certificated cotton stocks deliverable on the New York Cotton Exchange No 2 cotton futures contract as of April 6 were reported at 35,082 bales, down 336 bales from the previous day's figure. There were no bales awaiting review and 169 bales awaiting decertification.  Reuter &#3;
