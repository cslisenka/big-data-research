WORLD BANK ISSUES 100 MLN ECU EUROBOND

The World Bank is issuing a 100 mln ECU eurobond due July 21, 1994 paying 7-1/2 pct and priced at 101-1/2 pct, lead manager Morgan Guaranty Ltd said.     The non-callable bond is available in denominations of 1,000 and 50,000 ECUs and will be listed in Luxembourg. The selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while, management and underwriting combined pays 1/2 pct.     The payment date is July 21.  REUTER &#3;
