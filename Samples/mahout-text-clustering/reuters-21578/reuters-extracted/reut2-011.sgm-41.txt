OXOCO CONCLUDES PRIVATE SALE OF NEW COMMON

Oxoco Inc said it concluded the private placement of 3,906,250 shares of its new common stock at an average purchase price of 1.28 dlrs per share for a cash payment of five mln dlrs.     Oxoco said the sale was to a group of venture capital investors led by Hambrecht and Quist of San Francisco.     It said the venture capitalists now own 71.6 pct of the outstanding stock of the company.     In addition, Andrew Loomis and Michael McGovern were elected chairman and co-chairman, respectively, of the company, it said.  Reuter &#3;
