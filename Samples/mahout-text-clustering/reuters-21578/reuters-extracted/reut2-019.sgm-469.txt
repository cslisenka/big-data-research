WPP GROUP PLC EXTENDS ITS OFFER FOR JWT <JWT>

<WPP Group Plc> said it is extending its 50.50 dlrs a share offer for JWT Group Inc until midday June 22.     The company temporarily extended the offer on June 17 but did not give an expiration date.     Its 45 dlrs a share offer for JWT, which has been rejected by JWT, expires on July 10.  Reuter &#3;
