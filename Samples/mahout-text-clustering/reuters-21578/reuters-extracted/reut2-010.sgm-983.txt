<TEXSCAN CORP> IN AGREEMENT WITH LENDERS

Texscan Corp said it has reached agreement in principle with bank lenders United Bancorp of Arizona <UBAZ> and Security Pacific Corp <SPC> on a plan of reorganization from Chapter 11 bankruptcy.     It said a plan is expected to be filed within 10 days.  Reuter &#3;
