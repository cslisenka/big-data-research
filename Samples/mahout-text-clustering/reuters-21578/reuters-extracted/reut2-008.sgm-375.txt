LOPAT <LPAT> NAMES WEST COAST DISTRIBUTOR

Lopat Industries Inc said <BKK Inc>'s Falcon Disposal Service Inc has agreed to become the exclusive distributor of Lopat's K-20 toxic waste clean up products in 12 western states.     Lopat said Falcon's sales specified under the agreement are targeted at 43 mln dlrs over the next 54 months.  Reuter &#3;
