GM <GM> CLOSING TWO PLANTS TEMPORARILY

General Motors Corp said it is closing two plants for brief periods in March, placing 5,500 on temporary layoff for up to two weeks.     The company said its Hamtramck Buick-Oldsmobile-Cadillac assembly plant in Detroit will be down from March 9 to March 16 due to excess inventory of several models, placing 2,000 workers on temporary layoff.     Its Chevrolet-Pontiac-GM of Canada assembly facility in Norwood, Ohio, will be closed two weeks from March 9 to 23 due to material shortages, affecting 3,500. There is no change in the 37,000 hourly workers out of work indefinitely.  Reuter &#3;
