BUTTES GAS AND OIL <BGO.P> FILES BANKRUPTCY PLAN

Buttes Gas and Oil Co said it filed a plan of reorganization in a U.S. Bankruptcy court handling its Chapter 11 bankruptcy case.     The company said the plan wll provide for distribution of cash, promissory notes and stock among 12 classes of creditors and equity interests.  Reuter &#3;
