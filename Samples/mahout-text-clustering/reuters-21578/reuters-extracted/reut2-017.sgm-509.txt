CANADA SOUTHERN <CSW.TO> TO OFFER SHARES

Canada Southern Petroleum Ltd said its board has authorized an offering of about three mln shares of limited voting stock to shareholders of record on June 23.     The company said the rights offering will expire August Three.  The rights are nontransferable.     It said shareholders will be entitled to buy one new share for every three held at one dlr U.S. or 1.34 dlrs Canadian. Shareholders subscribing for their entire allotments will have the option of subscribing for shares not bought by others on a contingent allotment basis in any amount up to 100 pct of their original allotments.
