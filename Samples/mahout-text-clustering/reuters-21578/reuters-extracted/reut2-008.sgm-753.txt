HOECHST UNIT ISSUING 130 MLN SWISS FRANC BOND

Hoechst Invest NV, a Dutch finance subsidiary of Hoechst AG of West Germany, is issuing a 130 mln Swiss franc five pct bond, lead manager Credit Suisse said.     The par-priced, 14-year is guaranteed by Hoechst AG.   REUTER &#3;
