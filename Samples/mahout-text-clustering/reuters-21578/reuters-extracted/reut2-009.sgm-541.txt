MERCK <MRK> GETS FDA HEARTWORM MEDICINE APPROVAL

Merck and Co Inc said its veterinary drug Heartgard-30 for the prevention of heartworm disease in dogs has been approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration.     The company said the drug will be availiable only through licensed veterinarians.  Reuter &#3;
