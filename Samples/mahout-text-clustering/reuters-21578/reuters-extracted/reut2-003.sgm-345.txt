EMERY AIR <EAF> FEBRUARY SHIPMENTS UP 12 PCT

Emergy Air Freight Corp said its shipments rose 12 pct to 1,190,000 last month from 1,067,000 in February 1986.     The company said the weight of these shipments was up 20 pct to 52.7 mln pounds from 44.0 mln a year earlier.  Reuter &#3;
