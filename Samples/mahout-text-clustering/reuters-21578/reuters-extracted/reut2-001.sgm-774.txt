E.F. HUTTON GROUP INC <EFH> NAMES EXECUTIVE

E.F. Hutton Group Inc said it named Jerry Welsh to the newly-created post of senior executive vice president for marketing and strategic development.     The company said Welsh was previously executive vice president of worldwide marketing and communications for American Express'<AXP> Travel Related Services Co Inc.  Reuter &#3;
