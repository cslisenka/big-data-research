VANGUARD TECHNOLOGIES <VTI> WINS CONTRACT

Vanguard Technologies International Inc said it was awarded a 2.2 mln dlr contract from the Office of the Secretary of Defense to provide facilities management services.     Under the one-year contract, which also has two one-year optional performance periods, the company said it will provide local area network operations, coordination of hardware installation and maintenance, user support services and systems and applications programming.
