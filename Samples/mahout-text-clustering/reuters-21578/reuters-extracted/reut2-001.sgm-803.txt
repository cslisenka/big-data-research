SAVIN <SVB> NOTE CONVERSION ENDS

Savin Corp said the first period for the exercise of the special conversion right for its zero coupon convertible senior notes due February one, 1996, has expired.     Savin said about 1,767,000 shares of its common stock will be issued as a result of the conversion.     The share certificates issued through the conversion will be distributed to noteholders beginning March six, Savin added.  Reuter &#3;
