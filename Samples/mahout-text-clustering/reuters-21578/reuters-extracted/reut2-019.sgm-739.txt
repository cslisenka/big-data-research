WORLD BANK LOANS TURKEY 325 MLN DLRS

The World Bank said it has loaned Turkey 325 mln dlrs to help support an energy sector reform program.     The loan-supported program hopes to reduce the extensive public sector involvement in the country's energy production and supply, the bank said.     The loan is for 17 years, including four years of grace, with a variable interest rate, currently 7.92 pct, the bank said.  Reuter &#3;
