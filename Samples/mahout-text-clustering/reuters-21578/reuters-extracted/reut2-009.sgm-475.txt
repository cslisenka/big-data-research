<VISTA MANAGEMENT INC> GETS BOND GUARANTEES

Vista Managemement Inc said it has obtained notice of the availability of insurance guarantees on 60 mln dlrs of leasing receivable bonds of its CBS Leasing subsidiary from Developers Insurance Co, allowing the bonds to be rated by leading credit rating agencies.     The company said the guarantees are subject to the placement of over one mln dlrs of reinsurance and to a pending change of control of Developers Insurance.  Reuter &#3;
