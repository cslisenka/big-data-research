STERLING DRUG <STY> SELLS X-RAY PRODUCTS UNIT

Sterling Drug Inc said it sold the assets of its Greene Dental Products Inc subsidiary to Rinn Corp, an Elgin, Ill., maker of dental x-ray film, supplies and equipment.     The company did not disclose the cash purchase price.     Greene Dental makes dental x-ray tabs, mount products and record systems. No indication of its size was given.  Reuter &#3;
