TECHNIMED CORP SAID COMPANY CANCELLED CONTRACT

<Technimed Corp> said Garid Inc of Eden Praries, Minn., has cancelled its requirements contract wih Technimed for the supply of a dry chemistry device for the Med-Pen system. Technimed said further action maybe required to protect its proprietary technology.  Reuter &#3;
