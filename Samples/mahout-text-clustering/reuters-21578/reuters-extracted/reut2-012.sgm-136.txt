MICHAELS STORES <MKE> MARCH SALES RISE

Michaels Stores Inc said March sales were up 19.2 pct to 7,823,000 dlrs from 6,564,000 dlrs a year earlier, with same-store sales up 2.7 pct.     The company noted that Easter sales fell in March last year and are falling in April this year.  Reuter &#3;
