MELVILLE <MES> NAMES CHIEF FINANCIAL OFFICER

Melville Corp said it elected Robert Huth as executive vice president and chief financial officer, succeeding Kenneth Berland on April six.     Berland will continue as vice chairman and chief administrative officer.     Huth is a partner in the accounting firm of Peat, Marwick, Mitchell and Co.  Reuter &#3;
