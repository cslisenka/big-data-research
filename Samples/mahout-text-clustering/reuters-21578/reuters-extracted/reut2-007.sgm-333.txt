LOMAS MORTGAGE <LMC> FILES TO OFFER SHARES

Lomas Mortgage Corp said it has filed for an offering of four mln common shares through underwriters led by Merrill Lynch and Co Inc <MER>.     The company said it has granted underwriters an option to buy up to another 600,000 shares to cover overallotments.  Reuter &#3;
