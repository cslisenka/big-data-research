U.S. AND SOVIETS DRAFT EUROMISSILE TREATY

U.S. And Soviet negotiators have completed the text of a draft treaty calling for the elimination of medium-range missiles in Europe, a Soviet negotiator said.     "We must say that as a result of the work done at the current round the sides have drafted the first joint draft text of the treaty on medium-range missiles," Alexei Obukhov, deputy leader of the Soviet negotiating team, told reporters.     He said there was still much work to be done and several areas of disagreement remained to be resolved.           Reuter &#3;
