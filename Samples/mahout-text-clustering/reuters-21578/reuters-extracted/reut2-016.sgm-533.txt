AIRSHIP <AIRSY> GETS JAPANESE CONTRACT

Airship Industries Ltd said it has received a six mln stg contract from a Japanese advertising agency it did not name for a 12-month lease of one of its 600 Class airships equipped with a nightsign, with a 12-month renewal option.     The company said the contract will start by October One and contribute significantly to profits.  Reuter &#3;
