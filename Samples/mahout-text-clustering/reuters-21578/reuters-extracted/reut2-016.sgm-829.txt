WAINOCO OIL <WOL> TO REDEEM SOME RIGHTS

Wainoco Oil Corp said its board has voted to redeem its shareholder value rioghts for 10 cts each, payable June One to holders of record on April 27.     The rights have been attached on a one-for-one basis to  Wainoco common shares.  As previously announced, the company is redeeming the rights to have sufficient authorized and unreserved common shares to proceed with a planned unit offering of common stock and warrants.     It said its shareholder purchase rights distributed in June 1986 are unaffected.  Reuter &#3;
