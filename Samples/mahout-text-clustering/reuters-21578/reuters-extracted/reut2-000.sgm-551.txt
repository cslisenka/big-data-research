MERCURY ENTERTAINMENT <MCRY> SEES BETTER RESULTS

Mercury Entertainment Corp said it expects improved results in 1987.     The company today reported a loss for the year ended November 30 of 413,021 dlrs on revenues of 600,971 dlrs, compared with a loss of 163,932 dlrs on revenues of 665,800 dlrs a year before.  Reuter &#3;
