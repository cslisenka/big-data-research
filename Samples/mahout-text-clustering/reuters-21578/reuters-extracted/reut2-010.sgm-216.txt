CECO CORP REPURCHASES 20 MLN DLRS IN NOTES

<The Ceco Corp> said it repurchased the 20 mln dlr 13-1/4 pct senior subordinate notes due November 30, 1996 issued last December nine as part of the financing for its leveraged buyout.     Ceco said it financed the purchase through an increase in its existing credit line.     Ceco said Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc acted as Ceco's agent in this transaction.  Reuter &#3;
