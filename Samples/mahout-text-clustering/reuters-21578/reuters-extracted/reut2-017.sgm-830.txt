GM <GM> MAY CAR OUTPUT FALLS 26.3 PCT

General Motors Corp said its May North American car production fell 26.3 pct to 328,221 units from 445,440 a year ago.     The company said its May production of commercial vehicles (trucks and buses) rose 5.1 pct to 158,444 from 150,752 a year ago.  Reuter &#3;
