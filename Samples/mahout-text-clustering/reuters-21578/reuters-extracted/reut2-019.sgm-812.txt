AMERICAN CENTURY <ACT> COMPLETES FORECLOSURE

American Century Corp said its Commerce Savings subsidiary received 42.8 mln dlrs from the foreclosure of certain properties in Aspen, Colo.     It said the sum represents the amount Commerce bid for the properties in the April foreclosure sale plus interest. The properties include a hotel and condominium development site, the 60-acre Aspen Meadows development and a residence.  Reuter &#3;
