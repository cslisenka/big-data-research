ELECTRONIC MAIL <EMCA> FINANCING ATTEMPT FAILS

Electronic Mail Corp of America said its efforts to secure additional financing for expansion have fallen through.     The company said that there are no immediate prospects for financing through other sources, though efforts will continue. The company said operations wil continue while further efforts are made.     Negotiations with an undisclosed company had been onging for four months, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
