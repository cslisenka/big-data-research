USX <X> GETS GEORGE WASHINGTON BRIDGE WORK

The Port Authority of New York and New Jersey said it has awarded USX Corp a 3,400,000 dlr contract to replace the expansion joints and gratings on the upper level of the George Washington Bridge between New York and Fort Lee, N.J.     It said most of the joints and gratings have been in place for over 50 years.  The bridge opened in 1931     Work on the project should start this spring and be completed in the fall of 1988, it said.  Work affecting traffic will be performed on weeknights and at least one lane in each direction will remain open at all times.  Reuter &#3;
