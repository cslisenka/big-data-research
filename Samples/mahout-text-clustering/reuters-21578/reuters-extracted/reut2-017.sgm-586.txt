AMERICAN SAVINGS <AAA> NAMES NEW CHIEF EXECUTIVE

American Savings and Loan Association of Florida said its board has named president and chief operating officer Edward P. Mahoney to the added post of chief executive officer, succeeding Morris N. Broad, who remains chairman.     The company said Broad will focus on merchant banking investments, real estate developments and mortgage lending activities.  Reuter &#3;
