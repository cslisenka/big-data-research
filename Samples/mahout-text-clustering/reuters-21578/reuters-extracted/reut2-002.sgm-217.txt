ERICSSON'S U.S. UNIT WINS 1.4 MLN DLR CONTRACT

Ericsson Corp, the U.S. unit of <LM Ericsson Telephone Co> of Sweden, said it won a 1.4 mln dlr contract from California's Somona State University for computer communications equipment.     The contract includes Ericsson's MD110 digital private branch exchange and a local area network, supplying voice services to 1500 campus computer users and greater computer access, the company said.
