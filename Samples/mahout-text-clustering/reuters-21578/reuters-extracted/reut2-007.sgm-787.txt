YUGOSLAVIA FREEZING PRICES AFTER STRIKES

The government said it will freeze prices at end-December levels within five days.     The announcement followed a wave of strikes that swept the country in protest against a wages freeze.     The government, in a statement through the official Tanjug news agency, said the freeze would affect food, consumer goods, tools, textiles and furniture and would last 90 days.  REUTER &#3;
