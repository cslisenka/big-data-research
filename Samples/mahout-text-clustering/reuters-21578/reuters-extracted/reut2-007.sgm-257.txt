LITTON <LIT> WINS 164.2-MLN-DLR NAVY CONTRACT

Litton Industries said its Applied Technology division won a 164.2 mln dlr contract from the U.S. Navy to produce AN/ALR-67 (V) and AN/ALR-45F airborne threat warning systems.     The company said the systems will be used on a variety of Navy attack and reconnaissance aircraft.     Deliveries are set from August this year to February, 1990.     The Navy also has an option in June to increase the contract value to 226.6 mln dlrs, Litton said.  Reuter &#3;
