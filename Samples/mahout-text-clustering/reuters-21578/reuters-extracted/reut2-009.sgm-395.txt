<Accugraph Corp> said it named Trio-Tech International Pte Ltd, of Singapore, as exclusive distributor of Accugraph's computer-aided design and manufacturing software for Singapore, the Philippines, Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia and Brunei.     Financial terms were undisclosed.  Reuter &#3;
