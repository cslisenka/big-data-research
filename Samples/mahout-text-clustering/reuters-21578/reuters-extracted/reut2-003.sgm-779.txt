REICHOLD CHEMICALS <RCI> INCREASES PRICES

Reichhold Chemicals Inc said it is raising the prices of its Emulsion Polymers Division's styrene-based latexes for nowovens, textiles, adhesives and other specialty end uses, effective April nine.     The increases range between three and nine cts per dry pound.  Reuter &#3;
