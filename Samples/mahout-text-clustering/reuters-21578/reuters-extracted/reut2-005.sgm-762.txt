LOS ANGELES SECURITIES <LSGA> SETS MANAGMENT

Los Angeles Securities Group said it has concluded negotiations with Ronald Vannuki under which he will become its chief executive officer and buy about 710,000 shares of its common stock.     The company said Vannuki will buy about 210,000 shares from current shareholders and about 500,000 shares from the company over three years.     Vannuki, 46, has been Vice President for Kayne, Anderson and Co Inc, a Los Angeles-based securities broker-dealer.  Reuter &#3;
