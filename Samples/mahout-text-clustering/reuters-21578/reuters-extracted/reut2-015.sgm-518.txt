PAYLESS CASHWAYS <PCI> MARCH SALES UP

Payless Cashways Inc said its sales for the five weeks ended April Four rose to 167.4 mln dlrs from 147.5 mln dlrs a year ago, or one pct on a comparable store basis.     Year to date, it said sales gained to 499.6 mln dlrs from 422.0 mln dlrs, or four pct on a comparable store basis.  Reuter &#3;
