MONY FUNDING ISSUES 125 MLN DLR EUROBOND

Mony Funding Inc is issuing a 125 mln dlr eurobond due April 7, 1997 paying 8-1/8 pct and priced at 101-1/2 pct, sole lead manager Citicorp Investment Bank Ltd said.     The non-callable bond is guaranteed by Mutual Life Insurance Co of the U.S. And is available in denominations of 5,000 dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg. The selling concession is 1-3/8 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct.     The payment date is April 7.  REUTER &#3;
