TANDEM <TNDM> HAS HOME SHOPPING DEAL

Tandem Computers Inc said the Telaction Corp unit of J.C. Penney Co Inc <JCP> selected the Tandem NonStop computer system for a new interactive home shopping system scheduled to go into operation during the summer.     Tandem said the new system will be the first consumer controlled home shopping and information service delivered by cable television.     The Telaction service will be initially available to more than 125,000 households in the Chicago test area, Tandem also said.  Reuter &#3;
