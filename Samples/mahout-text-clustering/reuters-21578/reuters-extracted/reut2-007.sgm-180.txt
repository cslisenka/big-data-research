ADVANCED MARKETING INITIAL OFFER UNDER WAY

Lead underwriter Muller and Co Inc said an initial public offering of 500,000 units of <Advanced Marketing Technology Corp> is under way at eight dlrs a unit.     Each unit consists of four common shares and one Class A warrant allowing the purchase of one common share and one Class B warrant from March 25, 1987 until March 18, 1992.     Each Class B warrant will allow the holder to buy one common share at 2.80 dlrs during the same period.  Reuter &#3;
