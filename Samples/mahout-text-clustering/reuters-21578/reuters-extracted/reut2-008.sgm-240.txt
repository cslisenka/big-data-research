HOG AND CATTLE SLAUGHTER GUESSTIMATES

Chicago Mercantile Exchange floor traders and commission house representatives are guesstimating today's hog slaughter at about 295,000 to 305,000 head versus 295,000 week ago and 305,000 a year ago.     Cattle slaughter is guesstimated at about 120,000 to 125,000 head versus 114,000 week ago and 119,000 a year ago.  Reuter &#3;
