SIMMONS <SIMM.O> MARCH LOAD FACTOR DECLINES

Simmons Airlines Inc said its load factor for March declined to 42.2 pct from 46.1 pct a year earlier.     It said revenue passenger miles increased to 16.5 mln from 12.7 mln, and available seat miles rose to 39.1 mln from 27.5 mln    Year to date, Simmons said its load factor fell to 41.5 pct from 45 pct. It said revenue passenger miles rose to 45.8 mln from 36.3 mln, and available seat miles gained to 110.3 mln from 80.6 mln.  Reuter &#3;
