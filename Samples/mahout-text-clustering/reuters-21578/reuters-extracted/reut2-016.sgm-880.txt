GOODMARK FOODS <GDMK.O> NAMES NEW CHAIRMAN

GoodMark Foods Inc said president and chief executive officer Ron E. Doggett will assume the added post of chairman effective June One, when H. Hawkins Bradley retires.     It said Bradley will remain on the board and as a company consultant.  Reuter &#3;
