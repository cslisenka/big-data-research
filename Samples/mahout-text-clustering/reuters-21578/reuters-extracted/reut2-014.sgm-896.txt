HOKURIKU ELECTRIC ISSUES 35 MLN DLR WARRANT BOND

Hokuriku Electric Industry is issuing a 35 mln dlr equity warrant bond due May 7, 1992 paying an indicated coupon of 2-1/8 pct and priced at par, lead manager Nomura International Ltd said.     The issue is guaranteed by Hokuriku Bank Ltd. The selling concession is 1-1/2 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 3/4 pct. Final terms are due April 16.     The payment date is May 7 and the issue is available in denominations of 5,000 dlrs. Listing will be in Luxembourg.  REUTER &#3;
