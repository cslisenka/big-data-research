ROYCE <RLAB> TO REDEEM WARRANTS

Royce Laboratories Inc said it is calling for redemption its redeemable common purchase warrants on April 30 at 2.5 cts per warrant.     It said warrant holders retain the right to purchase one share of its common at an exercise price of three dlrs per share until 1700 EST April 29.  Reuter &#3;
