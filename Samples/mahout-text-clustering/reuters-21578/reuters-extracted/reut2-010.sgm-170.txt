VIACOM <VIA> SETS RECORD DATE FOR MERGER VOTE

Viacom International Inc said it set April 6 as the record date for shareholders entitled to vote at a special meeting to be held to vote on the proposed merger of Arsenal Acquiring Corp, a wholly-owned subsidiary of <Arsenal Holdings Inc> into Viacom.     It said the date of the special meeting has not yet been determined.  Reuter &#3;
