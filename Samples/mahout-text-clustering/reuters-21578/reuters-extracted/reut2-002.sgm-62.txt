F.W. WOOLWORTH <Z> FEBRUARY SALES INCREASE

F.W. Woolworth Co said total sales for the four weeks ended February 28 rose 11.3 pct to 416 mln, from 374 mln in the comparable 1986 period.     Woolworth said domestic sales increased 7.1 pct, to 249 mln from 233 mln, while foreign sales rose 18.4 pct to 167 mln, from 141 mln.  Reuter &#3;
