CENTERIOR ENERGY <CX> SEES LOWER SPENDING

Centerior Energy Corp said its construction spending "peaked" in 1986 at 1.1 billion dlrs, and annual expenses are expected to be substantially lower over the next several years.     Last year, the company placed in operation Perry Nuclear Unit One. In December, its Davis-Beebe Nuclear Power Station resumed operations after an 18-month outage.  Reuter &#3;
