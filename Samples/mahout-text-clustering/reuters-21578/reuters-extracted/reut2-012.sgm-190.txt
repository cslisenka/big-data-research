DYNALECTRON <DYN> BUYS AVIATION FACILITY

Dynalectron Corp said it purchased certain assets of Standard Aero Inc including leasehold rights to its aircraft modification and maintenance facility in Phoenix, Ariz.     Terms were not released.     The facility consists of 285,000 square feet of hangar and shop space. Dynalectron will operate the plan as Dynair Tech Inc and is offering continued employment to about 460 former standard Aero employees.  Reuter &#3;
