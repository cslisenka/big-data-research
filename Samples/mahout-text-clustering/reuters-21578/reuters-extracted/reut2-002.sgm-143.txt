DAYTON HUDSON <DH> FEBRUARY SALES RISE

Dayton Hudson Corp said sales for the four weeks ended February 28 increased 10.8 pct on a comparable-store basis to 601.8 mln dlrs from 503.8 mln dlrs a year ago.     It called the pace of February's sales "encouraging, with strength evident in all companies."  Reuter &#3;
