AMERICAN CAN <AC> FILES FOR DEBT OFFERING

American Can Co filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a shelf offering of up to 500 mln dlrs of debt securities on terms to be determined at the time of the sale.     Proceeds from the offering will be used for general corporate purposes, including repayments of outstanding debt, business acquisitions or investments, American Can said.     The company did not name an underwriter for the sale.  Reuter &#3;
