DPCE SEEKS 14.6 MLN STG IN RIGHTS ISSUE

<DPCE Holdings Plc> said it planned to raise 14.6 mln stg net through an underwritten rights issue of 4.7 mln shares at 320p issued on a one-for-six basis.     The funds would be used to expand the computer maintenance company's presence in major centres where computer technology is developed. The company planned to make acquisitions, extend the range of services it offered and buy capital equipment to support major new contracts.     DPCE shares were unchanged at 365p.  REUTER &#3;
