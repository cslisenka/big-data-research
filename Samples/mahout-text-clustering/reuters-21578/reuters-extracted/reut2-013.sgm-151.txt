GM<GM> PONTIAC DIVISION SETS SUMMER LEASE PLAN

General Motors Corp's Pontiac Division said it is introducing a summer lease merchandising program that will run from April Six through August Three.     It said the plan features a special GMAC 50-month retail schedule with low lease payments on selected Pontiacs.     Participating dealers will offer monthly payments starting at 179 dlrs for a Pontiac Sunbird, it said.  Reuter &#3;
