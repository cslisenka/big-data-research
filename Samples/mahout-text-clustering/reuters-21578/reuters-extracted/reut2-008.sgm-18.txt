MORTON THIOKOL <MTI> GETS 61.4 MLN DLR CONTRACT

Morton Thiokol Inc's Wasatch Operations is being awarded a 61.4 mln dlr contract modification finalizing a previously awarded contract for missile rocket motors for Terrier, Tarter and Aegis ships, the Defense Department said.     It said the work is expected to be completed in October  1987.  Reuter &#3;
