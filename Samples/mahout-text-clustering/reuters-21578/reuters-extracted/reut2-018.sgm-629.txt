MULTI-LOCAL MEDIA <MLMC.O> INITIAL OFFER STARTS

Lead underwriters Kidder, Peabody and Co Inc and Shearson Lehman Brothers Holdings Inc <SHE> said an initial public offering of two mln common shares of Multi-Local Media Corp is underway at 10.50 dlrs a share.     Underwriters have been granted an overallotment option to purchase up to 300,000 more shares, it said.  Reuter &#3;
