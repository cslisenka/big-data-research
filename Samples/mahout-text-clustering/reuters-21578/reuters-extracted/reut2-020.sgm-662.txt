UNITED TECH <UTX> SETS COMMON SHARE BUYBACK

United Technologies Corp said its board authorized management at its discretion to buy up to six mln shares of the company's common stock in the open market.     "We are taking this action because the price of the stock represents an excellent buying opportunity," the company said in a statement.     The military, commercial and industrial products company had about 131.6 mln shares outstanding at Sept 30.     Its shares were trading off 1-3/4 at 37-1/4 late in the day.  Reuter &#3;
