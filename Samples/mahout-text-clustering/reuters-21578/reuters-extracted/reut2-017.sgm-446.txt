KOREA ELECTRIC POWER PLANS FIRST EUROYEN BOND

Korea Electric Power Corp plans to issue its first euroyen bond, a 7.5 billion yen issue with six-year maturity carrying a coupon of less than five pct, company officials said.     They said the bond would be launched in London later this month with Daiwa Securities Co Ltd as lead manager.     The officials gave no further details.  REUTER &#3;
