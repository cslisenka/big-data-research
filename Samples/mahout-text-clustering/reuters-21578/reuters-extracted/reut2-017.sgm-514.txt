OCCIDENTAL <OXY> SETS DEBT RETIREMENT DATE

Occidental Petroleum Corp said the previously reported retirement of nine series of Natural Gas Pipeline Co of America debt will occur on July two.     Retirement of the subsidiary's was announced by Occidental last week.     It said notices of redeemptions and pre-payments will be mailed shortly to all registered holders.  Reuter &#3;
