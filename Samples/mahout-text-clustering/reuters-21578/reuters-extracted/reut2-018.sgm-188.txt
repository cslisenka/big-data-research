K-TRON <KTII.O> ELECTS NEW PRESIDENT

K-Tron International Inc said its board elected John C. Tucker president, filling a position that had been vacant since July 30, 1985.     Tucker joined K-Tron in 1981 as vice president, chief financial officer and treasurer. In 1986 he was promoted to executive vice president and chief operating officer.  Reuter &#3;
