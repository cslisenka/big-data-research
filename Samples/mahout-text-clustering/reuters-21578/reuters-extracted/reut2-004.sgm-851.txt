VOLKSWAGEN OF AMERICA EARLY MARCH SALES RISE

Volkswagen of America said its U.S. car sales for the March 1-10 period rose 4.9 pct to 849 from 809 a year earlier.     It said there were eight selling days in each period.     Year to date, Volkswagen said domestic sales declined 42.5 pct to 7,476 from 13,003.  Reuter &#3;
