MASON BEST FORMS ENERGY HOLDING COMPANY

<Mason Best Co> said it has formed <Meridian Energy Corp>, a privately-owned holding company structured to acquire operating companies ans assets in the oil and gas industry.     Mason Best, a Texas-based merchant banking firm, said it is sponsor and largest shareholder of Meridian. The new company's chairman and chief executive officer is  Ralph E. Bailey, who retired as vice-chairman of Du Pont Co <DD> and chairman of its Conoco Inc subsidiary on March 31.     Meridian is headquartered in Dallas with an administrative office in Stamford, Conn.  Reuter &#3;
