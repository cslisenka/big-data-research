DAI-ICHI HOTEL SWISS FRANC NOTES COUPON CUT

The coupon on Dai-Ichi Hotel Ltd's 50 mln Swiss franc issue of five-year notes with equity warrants has been cut to 1-5/8 pct from the indicated 1-7/8 pct, lead manager Swiss Volksbank said.     The warrants have an exercise price of 1,507 yen per share, compared with the last traded price of 1,470 yen, it said.     The notes are guarantees by Long-Term Credit Bank of Japan Ltd. Payment is due on March 25.  REUTER &#3;
