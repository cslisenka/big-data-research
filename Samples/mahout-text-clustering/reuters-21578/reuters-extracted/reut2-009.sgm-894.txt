CRAY <CYR> INSTALLS 22 MLN DLR COMPUTER

Cray Research Inc said Los Alamos National Laboratory in Los Alamos, N.M. installed a Cray X-MP/416 supercomputer valued at about 22 mln dlrs.     It said the system was installed in the fourth quarter of 1986 as a lease and converted to a purchase in the first quarter of 1987.  Reuter &#3;
