SEAGRAM <VO> UNIT REALIGNING OPERATIONS

Seagram Co Ltd's Joseph E. Seagram and Sons Inc said it is realigning its Fine Wine operations following last week's sale of its medium-priced table wines business.     Seagram Vintners, the entity under which all fine wine operations were group, is being dissolved, the company said. The Seagram Classic Wine Co will move its operations to the West Coast, the company said.     Seagram Chateau and Estate Wines Co will continue to be headquartered in New York, the company said.
