TUNISIA SETS SOFT WHEAT BUYING TENDER ON JUNE 16

Tunisia has launched a fresh buying tender for 200,000 tonnes of French soft wheat for tomorrow for shipping between July and September, trade sources said.     Tunisia has in recent weeks cancelled several tenders, which are covered by the French government's COFACE export credits.  Reuter &#3;
