MINE SAFETY APPLIANCES <MNES> TO SELL UNIT

Mine Safety Appliances Co said it agreed to sell its filter products division to Donaldson Co Inc <DCI> for undisclosed terms.     It said the filter products unit will be relocated from its plant in Pennsylvania to a Donaldson facility in Illinois. It said it expects a number of the unit's 100 employees will be offered positions with Donaldson.  Reuter &#3;
