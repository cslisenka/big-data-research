BANK OF NOVA SCOTIA ISSUES NEW ZEALAND DLR BOND

The Bank of Nova Scotia, Nassau branch, is issuing a 50 mln New Zealand dlr eurobond due April 14, 1989 carrying a 18-1/4 pct coupon and priced at 101 pct, lead manager Morgan Stanley International said.     The issue, which will take the form of deposit notes, will be co-lead by Dominion Securities and Wood Gundy Inc.     The bond is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 dlrs and payment date is April 14.     Fees comprise 3/4 pct selling concession and 1/2 pct for management and underwriting.  REUTER &#3;
