PERRY DRUG <PDS> MARCH SALES UP 9.3 PCT

Perry Drug Stores Inc said its March sales rose 9.3 pct to 60 mln dlrs from 55 mln dlrs in the same month a year ago.     Fiscal year-to-date (November through March) sales increased 15 pct to 300 mln dlrs from 261 mln dlrs in the comparable period of fiscal 1986, it said.  Reuter &#3;
