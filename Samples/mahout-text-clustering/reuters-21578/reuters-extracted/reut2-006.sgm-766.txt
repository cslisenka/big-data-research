UNION <UCO> TO SELL UNION FLONETICS UNIT

Union Corp said it agreed in principle to sell its Union Flonetics Corp subsidiary to Irvin Kaplan, a Houston investor.     The unit makes valves and marine specialty equipment for  the military. Kaplan is also controlling shareholder of <Hunt Valve Co Inc>.     The amount of the cash transaction, expected to close in April, was not disclosed.   Reuter &#3;
