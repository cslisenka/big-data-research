PARADYNE <PDN> LAUNCHES MODEMS, NETWORK SYSTEM

Paradyne Corp said it introduced a new 3400 series of high-speed network diagnostic modems that provide an open-ended, modular approach to telecommunications requirements.     It said one modem, the 3450, costs 8,900 dlrs and the other, the 3470, costs 11,500 dlrs.     It also said it introduced the ANALYSIS 6510, a new generation of advanced and automated network management and control systems. It said the price of an entry level configuration is 21,250 dls.
