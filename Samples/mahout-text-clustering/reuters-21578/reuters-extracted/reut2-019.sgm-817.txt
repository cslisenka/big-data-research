ALLIS-CHALMERS <AH> DEBENTURES CUT BY S/P

Standard and Poor's Corp said it downgraded 95.9 mln dlrs of debt of Allis-Chalmers Corp.     Cut to D from B-minus were the 6.1 pct sinking fund debentures due 1990 and to D from C were the cumulative convertible preferred stock.     S and P cited the company's filing for reorganization under Chapter 11 of the federal bankruptcy code.     The issues were removed from review where they were placed on March nine due to the firm's failed recapitalization plan.  Reuter &#3;
