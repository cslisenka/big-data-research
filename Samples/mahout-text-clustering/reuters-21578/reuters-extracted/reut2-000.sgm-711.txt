NORTH AMERICAN GROUP <NAMG> BUYS GEORGIA FIRM

North American Group Ltd's North American Acquisition Corp said it has a definitive agreement to buy 100 pct of Pioneer Business Group Inc of Atlanta.     Terms of the acquisition were not disclosed. Closing of the acquisition is scheduled for April.     North American Acquisition said the agreement is subject to due diligence and a satisfactory review of Pioneer's operation. Pioneer makes business forms.  Reuter &#3;
