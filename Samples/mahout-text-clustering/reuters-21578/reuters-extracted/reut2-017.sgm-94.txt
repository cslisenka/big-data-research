BETHLEHEM STEEL CORP <BS> INCREASES PRICES

Bethlehem Steel Corp said it was raising the prices by 30 dlrs per ton on section extras on certain wide planned structural shapes.     The increase will affect seven wide planned section groups and will increase the price to 500 dlrs per ton from 470 dlrs per ton, effective May 3, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
