AMR <AMR> AMERICAN AIR OFFERS TO SHIFT FLIGHTS

AMR Corp's American Airlines said it is prepared to shift a total of 1,010 flights and add more than 33 hours a day in flying time to assist the process of reducing flight delays in the nation's air transport system.     American said it proposes to make the changes on June 15 at its two major conecting hubs, Dallas/Fort Worth and Chicago, which have been the focal points for wide-ranging schedule experiments by the airline over the past several weeks.
