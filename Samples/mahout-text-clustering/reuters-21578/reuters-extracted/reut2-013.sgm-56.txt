FRANCE HLM LAUNCHES 750 MLN FRANC BOND

Housing group France HLM is launching a 750 mln French franc 8.40 pct 15-year bond with an issue price of 95.36 pct and denominated in 5,000 franc units, lead manager Banque Paribas said.     The issue is being co-led by Credit Lyonnais.     Payment date is April 20 and redemption will be in 12 equal annual instalments starting after the first three years of the issue's life. It will be listed in the Official Bulletin (BALO) on April 6.  REUTER &#3;
