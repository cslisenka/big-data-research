FERRO <FOE> INCREASES SHARES

Ferro Corp said shareholders approved increasing the number of authorized common shares to 50 mln from 20 mln.     Shareholders also approved amending the indemnification provisions of the Code of Regulations of the company and re-elected three directors to three-year terms on the board of directors.
