PENGUIN GROUP <PGC> REPORTS ORDERS

Penguin Group Inc said it told a group of analysts that it has started to book orders for its L'Ectronique line of costume jewelery and The Simplifier, a device which employs artificial intelligence to make computers easy to use.     Penguin officials were not available to comment on the potential market of these products.  Reuter &#3;
