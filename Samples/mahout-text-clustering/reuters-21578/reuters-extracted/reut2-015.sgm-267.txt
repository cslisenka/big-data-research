GENERAL AUTOMATION <GENA> SETS JOINT VENTURE

General Automation Inc said it signed a joint venture with Madrid, Spain-based Ingenieria De Sistemas Electronics S.A.     The pact establishes General Automation Iberica S.A. as a local manufacturer and master distributor of General Automation's ZEBRA multi-user business systems in Spain and Portugal.     General Automation Iberica will be equally financed by General Automation and Ingenieria.  Reuter &#3;
