CONSUMERS POWER <CMS> TO RESTART NUCLEAR PLANT

Consumers Power Co said it intends to restart the the Palisades Nuclear Plant within the next several days.     Consumers Power said it shut the plant down last May 19 because of problems with a regulating steam valve.     The utility said the problem has been corrected and Region III of the Nuclear Regulatory Commission gave it the go-ahead to restart the plant.  Reuter &#3;
