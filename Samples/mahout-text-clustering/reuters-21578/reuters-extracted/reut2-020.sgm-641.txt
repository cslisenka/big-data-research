PACIFIC STOCK EXCHANGE RESUMES SOME OPTIONS

The Pacific Stock Exchange said it has resumed trading on 17 of the equities options that were halted earlier because of conditions in the underlying securities.     At 1044, pdt the exchange announced it had halted trading on 30 such options.     The exchange did not indicate when it might resume trading on the remaining options.  Reuter &#3;
