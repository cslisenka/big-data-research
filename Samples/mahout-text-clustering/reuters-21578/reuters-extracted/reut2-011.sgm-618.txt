BUTLER <BTLR> TO PURCHASE NATURALITE

Butler Manufacturing Co said it signed a letter of intent to purchase <Naturalite Inc>, a designer of skylights.     Terms were not disclosed.     Naturalite had sales of about 20 mln dlrs in 1986. The transaction is expected to be completed in May and subject to both boards' approvals.  Reuter &#3;
