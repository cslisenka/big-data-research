ASHTON-TATE <TATE> NAMES SENIOR SCIENTIST

Ashton-Tate Corp said it appointed Harry Wong to the newly created position of senior scientist.     Wong, formerly of the University of California's Lawrence Berkeley Laboratory, will provide technical direction in advanced database architectures and structured query language, SQL, a computer language.     The company also said it is acquiring technology to develop a future database product that incorporates SQL.  Reuter &#3;
