CALIFORNIA ENERGY <CECI> NEGOTIATES FINANCING

California Energy Co Inc said it negotiated a 160 mln dlr private financing with Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc.     As part of the financing, it received a commitment letter, subject to standard conditions, for 133 mln dlrs of project financing from Credit Suisse.     Proceeds from the financing will be used for equipment procurement and construction of an 80-megawatt power plant by its China Lake Joint Venture, the company said. It said the plant will be built on a geothermal site at China Lake, Calif., on property leased from the federal government.  Reuter &#3;
