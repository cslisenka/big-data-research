AIR WIS <ARWS> FEBRUARY LOAD FACTOR FALLS

Air Wis Services Inc said February load factor fell to 42.9 pct from 44.2 pct a year before.     The company said revenue passenger miles rose 2.1 pct to 22.2 mln from 21.7 mln a year before, with available seat miles up 5.3 pct to 51.8 mln from 49.2 mln.  Reuter &#3;
