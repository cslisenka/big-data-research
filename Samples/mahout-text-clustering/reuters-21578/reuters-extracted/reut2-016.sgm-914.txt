GRUMMAN CORP <GQ> GETS 77 MLN DLR NAVY CONTRACT

Grumman Aerospace Corp is being awarded a 77 mln dlr increment to a Navy contract related to fiscal 1988 A-6F aircraft production, the Defense Department said.     It said work on the contract is expected to be completed in October 1990.  Reuter &#3;
