FISERVE <FISV> GETS BUSINESS WORTH ONE MLN DLRS

FIserve Inc said 14 savings and loans with 1.5 billion dlrs in cumulative assets will transfer their data processing to FIserve from Midwest Commerce Data Corp, a unit of Midwest Commerce Corp.     About one mln dlrs a year in new recurring revenues will be generated for FIserve, it said.  Reuter &#3;
