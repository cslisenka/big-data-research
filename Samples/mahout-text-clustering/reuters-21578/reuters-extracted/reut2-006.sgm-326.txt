AVATAR HOLDINGS <AVTR> OFFERING RIGHTS TO NOTES

Avatar Holdings Inc said it is offering rights to buy 31.2 mln dlrs principle amount of 5-1/4 pct convertible subordinated debentures due 2007.     The company said shareholders of record on March 17 have 30 days to exercise rights for the notes.  Reuter &#3;
