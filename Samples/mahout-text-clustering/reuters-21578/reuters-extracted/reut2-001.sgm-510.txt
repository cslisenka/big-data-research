GENERAL DATACOMM <GDC> IN SALE/LEASEBACK DEAL

General DataComm Industries Inc said it has agreed to sell for 40 mln dlrs its Network Services facility and its corporate facility and lease back the facilities.     It said it has an option to buy the buildings back at a later date.  Proceeds from the financing will be used to reduce debt, it said.  Reuter &#3;
