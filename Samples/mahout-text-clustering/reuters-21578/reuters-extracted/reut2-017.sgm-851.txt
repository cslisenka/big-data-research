UNITED PARK <RPK> HAS FULL SUBSCRIPTION

United Park City Mines Co said it had a full and complete subscription to all of the 5,400,731 shares ot its common stock offered in connection with its recent rights offering.     It said it will issue 4,892,153 shares of its common under the basic subscription right and 508,578 shares of its common under the oversubscription privilege. It said it will also receive 2.7 mln dlrs in proceeds from the offering.  Reuter &#3;
