ENVIRONMENTAL POWER <POWR> UNIT GETS CONTRACT

Environmental Power Corp said its subsidiary, Environmental Protection Resources Inc, of Houston, has been hired to build a 20 mln dlr resource recovery plant for Lubbock, Texas.     Environmental said its subsidiary also will own and operate the plant, which will convert solid waste into electricity.     The plant is scheduled for operation by early 1989, the company said.
