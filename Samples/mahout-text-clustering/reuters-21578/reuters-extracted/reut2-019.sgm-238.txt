TRIBUNE <TRB> TO ISSUE 100 MLN DLRS IN NOTES

Tribune Co said it will offer up to 100 mln dlrs of medium-term notes.     The notes will be offered on a continuing basis through Salomon Brothers Inc and Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.     The notes may have maturities of nine months to 15 years. Specific terms of each transaction will be established at the time of sale, the company said.     Proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
