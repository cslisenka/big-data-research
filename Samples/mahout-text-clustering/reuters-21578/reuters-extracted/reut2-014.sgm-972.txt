GREAT LAKES CHEMICAL <GLK> TO BUILD NEW PLANT

Great Lakes Chemical Corp said it will build a new plant in El Dorado, Ark., to produce several fluorine-based specialty chemicals.     It said total cost of the project, which would employ up to 50 workers, is estimated in excess of 20 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
