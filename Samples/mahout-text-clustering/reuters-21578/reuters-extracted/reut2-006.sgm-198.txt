PENTRON CORP <PEN> UNIT TO BUY ICE CREATIONS

Pentron Corp said its Rotational Molding Inc unit has purchased privately held Ice Creations Unlimited for an undisclosed amount of cash and other considerations.     The company said Ice Creations had sales of 1.2 mln dlrs in the year ended May 31, 1986.     Ice creations is a molder of plastics.  Reuter &#3;
