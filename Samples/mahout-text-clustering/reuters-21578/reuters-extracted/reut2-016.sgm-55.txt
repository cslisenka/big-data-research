VALEX PETROLEUM <VALP> RECLASSIFIES DEBT

Valex Petroleum Inc said its 1.3 mln dlrs in long-term debt with a commercial bank was reclassified as a current liability, which resulted in its auditors giving it a qualified opinion.     Earlier, Valex reported a net loss 219,632 for 1986.  Reuter &#3;
