KAYABA INDUSTRY ISSUES 50 MLN SWISS FRANC NOTES

Kayaba Industry Co Ltd is privately placing 50 mln Swiss francs of five year straight notes with a 4-3/4 pct coupon and 100.25 issue price, lead manager Credit Suisse said.     Payment is due March 31.     The issue is guaranteed by Fuji Bank Ltd.  REUTER &#3;
