COMMUNICATION PROBLEMS AFFECTING CBOE QUOTES

The Chicago Board Options Exchange, CBOE, said that due to telecommunications problems, last sale prices, market quotes and index values are updating intermittently.     The exchange said the problem affected prices from 1456 CST (2056 GMT). Trading in options at the CBOE closes at 1515 CST (2115 GMT).  Reuter &#3;
