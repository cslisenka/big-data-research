CBT WHEAT FUTURES EXPECTED TO OPEN STEADY

CBT wheat futures were expected to open steady, supported by followthrough speculative buying after yesterday's strong close when new contract highs were set in new crop, traders said.     However, rumors that several countries have passed on their tenders for U.S. wheat or taken wheat from other exporting countries may keep nearbys on the defensive.  ----  The USDA will issue this week a swap catalogue of government-owned grain that storage-surplus elevators will be able to swap with storage-deficit locations, a senior USDA official said...Wheat and rapeseed crops in east China suffered considerable damage due to frost during a spell of unusually cold weather in late March, the China daily said.
