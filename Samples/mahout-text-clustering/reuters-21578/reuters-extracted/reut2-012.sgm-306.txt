TREASURY BALANCES AT FED ROSE ON MARCH 31

Treasury balances at the Federal Reserve rose on March 31 to 3.576 billion dlrs from 3.254 billion dlrs on the previous business day, the Treasury said in its latest budget statement.     Balances in tax and loan note accounts fell to 5.394 billion dlrs from 7.291 billion dlrs on the same respective days.     The Treasury's operating cash balance totaled 8.969 billion dlrs on March 31 compared with 10.544 billion dlrs on March 30.  Reuter &#3;
