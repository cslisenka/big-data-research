UNION NATIONAL <UNBC> SIGNS DEFINITIVE PACT

Union National Corp said it signed a definitive agreement under which its First National Bank and Trust Co of Washington unit will merge with <Second National Bank of Masontown>.     Under a previously announced merger agreement, each share of Second National's common stock will be converted into 25 shares of Union National common.  Reuter &#3;
