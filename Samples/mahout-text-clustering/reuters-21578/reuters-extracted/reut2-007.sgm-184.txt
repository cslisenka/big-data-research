RYAN'S FAMILY STEAK HOUSE <RYAN> SETS SPLIT

Ryan's Family Steak Houses Inc said its board declared a three-for-one stock split, payable May 20 to holders of record May 6.     The company said the split is subject to shareholder approval of an increase in authorized shares to 100 mln from 20 mln at the April 22 annual meeting.  Reuter &#3;
