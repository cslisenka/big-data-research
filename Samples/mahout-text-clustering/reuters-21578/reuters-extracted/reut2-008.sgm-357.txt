SUMMAGRAPHICS CONTENDS PATENT SUIT INVALID

<Summagraphics Corp> said the patent infringement suit filed by Lockheed Corp's <LK> Sanders Rogers Associates unit is invalid.     Summagraphics said it will seek an injunction to stop Sanders from continuing with its suit.     The company said prior to Sanders filing a patent for an electromagnetic digitizing apparatus, it was working with technology that was patented and subsequently reflected in the Rogers patent.
