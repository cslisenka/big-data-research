BOISE CASCADE <BCC> TO REDEEM PREFERRED STOCK

Boise Cascade Corp said on April 30 it will redeem all 62,000 shares of its Series A three dlr cumulative convertible preferred stock at 65 dlrs per share.     The company said the shares are convertible through April 29 into common stock at the rate of 0.84896 common share per preferred share.  Reuter &#3;
