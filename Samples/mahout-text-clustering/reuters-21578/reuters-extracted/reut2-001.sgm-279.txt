INTERNATIONAL TECHNOLOGY <ITX> BUYS FIRM

International Technology Corp said it has purchased privately-held Western Emergency Service Inc in a stock transaction.     Western Emergency, an environmental services firm, has annual sales of between one and two mln dlrs, compared to International Technology's roughly 240-mln-dlr annual sales, a spokesman for International Technology said.
