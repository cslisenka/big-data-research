LITTON <LIT> RAISES SHARE BUYBACK LIMIT

Litton Industries Inc said its board authorized an increase in the maximum number of shares to be repurchased under its stock buyback program to 4.5 mln from 2.5 mln.     The company said the board initially authorized the stock buyback in October 1985 and since then about two mln common shares have been repurchased.  Reuter &#3;
