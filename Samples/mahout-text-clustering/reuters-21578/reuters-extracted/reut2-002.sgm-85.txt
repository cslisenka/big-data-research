CARSON PIRIE SCOTT <CRN> FEBRUARY SALES UP

Carson Pirie Scott and Co said its February sales increased 18.2 pct to 104.3 mln dlrs from 88.2 mln dlrs a year ago.     It said each of its business groups - retail foodservice and lodging and distribution - contributed to the sales gain.     The Food Service and Lodging Group's sales were up 8.9 pct after eliminating sales of the Steak 'n Egg Kitchen Restaurant chain sold Aug 15, 1986.  Reuter &#3;
