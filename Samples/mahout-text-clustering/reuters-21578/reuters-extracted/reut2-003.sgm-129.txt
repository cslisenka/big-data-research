BURTON GROUP ADRS START TRADING TODAY

The Burton Group Plc <BRTO.L> said trading will commence in the U.S. In Burton Group American Depositary Receipts (ADRs).     Burton said it expected the ADRs to increase significantly the North American demand for Burton's shares. A programme to promote the ADRs with presentations in the U.S. Is due soon. Burton shares here were 6p firmer at 306p.  Reuter &#3;
