GKN REORGANISES JOINT VENTURE

<GKN Plc> said it and <Zahnradfabrik Friedrichshafen AG> would reorganise the ownership structure of their <Viscodrive GmbH> joint venture.     Under the agreement, GKN's Uni-Cardan AG unit would increase its stake in Viscodrive to 75 pct from 50 pct, while ZF's stake would drop to 25 pct.      At the same time ZF would take an 80 pct stake in a new company, <Steertec Lenkungen GmbH> leaving Uni-Cardan with 20 pct.     Viscodrive manufactures viscous control units while Steertec will develop power steering systems.  REUTER &#3;
