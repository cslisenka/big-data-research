ADMAR GROUP <ADMR> OPENING NEW OFFICE

Admar Group Inc said it will open a new Eastern Operations Center for its HealthWatch Medical Review System in Dedham, Mass., by April One.     The company said it will also make its Med Network multistate preferred provide organisation available in the Boston area during the second quarter.  Reuter &#3;
