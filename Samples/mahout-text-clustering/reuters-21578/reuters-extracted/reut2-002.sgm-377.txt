EASTERN AIRLINES <EAL> FEBRUARY LOAD FACTOR UP

Eastern Airlines Inc said its February load factor rose 6.73 percentage points to 64.91 pct from 58.18 pct a year ago.     The company said February revenue passenger miles rose 21.8 pct to 3.02 billion from 2.48 billion a year ago. Available seat miles in the month of February rose 9.2 pct to 4.66 billion from 4.27 billion a year ago.     For the year-to-date period, load factor was up 4.64 percentage points to 61.96 pct from 57.32 pct a year ago, the company said. Revenue passenger miles rose 14.5 pct to 6.01 billion, and available seat miles rose 5.9 pct to 9.70 billion.  Reuter &#3;
