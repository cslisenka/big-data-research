DEROSE <DRI> CHAIRMAN STEPS DOWN

DeRose Industries Inc said Robert A. DeRose has retired as chairman and chief executive officer but will remain on the board through 1987 in an advisory capacity.     It said president and chief operating officer Victor A. DeRose, his son, has been named chairman and CEO.     The company said director Tom Johnson, who is also president of High Chaparral Inc, has been named president and chief operating officer.  Reuter &#3;
