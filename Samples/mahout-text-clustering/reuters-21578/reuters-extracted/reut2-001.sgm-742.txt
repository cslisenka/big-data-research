AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO FEBRUARY SALES RISE

Honda Motor Co Ltd of Japan's American Honda Motor Co Inc unit said its February sales rose to 56,704 from 48,443 a year ago.     The sales figures include sales of 7,056 cars from its new Acura division, which was not in place a year ago. Year to date sales totaled 102,751 at the end of February, up from 98,724.  This included sales of 12,723 from the Acura division.     In the company's Honda division, sales of the Accord model led the monthly and year-to-date totals, followed by Civic sales, then Prelude sales. In the Acura division, Intera sales outpaced Legend sales.  Reuter &#3;
