INTERNATIONAL MINERALS <IGL> OFFERS PREFERRED

International Minerals and Chemical Corp said it is offering 1,500,000 shares of 3.25 dlr Convertible Exchangeable Preferred Stock, Series B.     It said each share of Series B Preferred will be convertible at any time at the holder's option into the 1.266 shares of the company's common and will be exchangeable at the company's option on any dividend payment date beginning April 15, 1990 for 6-1/2 pct convertible subordinated debentures due 2017.     The offering is through Merrill Lynch Capital Markets, Lazard Freres and Co and Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc.       Reuter &#3;
