ARGOSYSTEMS <ARGI> WINS GOVERNMENT CONTRACT

ARGOSystems Inc said it won a 15-mln-dlr contract with the U.S. Government to deliver 25 digital signal processing systems with spares over the next three years.     The company said it how has a record backlog of over 155 mln dlrs, up 30 pct from a year ago.  Reuter &#3;
