INTERNATIONAL AMERICAN <HOME> NEWS TO COME

International American Homes Inc said it would not be able to release any statements concerning its stock halt on Nasdaq for about 45 minutes to an hour.     American Homes, whose stock halted on a last sale of 7-3/4, said the only executive who could release the news would not be available until then.  Reuter &#3;
