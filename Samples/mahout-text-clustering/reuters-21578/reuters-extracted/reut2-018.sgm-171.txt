AMERICAN STORES <ASC> UNIT ELECTS NEW CHAIRMAN 

American Superstores Inc, a unit of American Stores Co <ASC>, said it elected Jonathan Scott as chairman of the board and chief executive officer effective immediately.     Scott replaces Thomas Sunday, former chairman, who passed away May 25, the compnay said.     The food retailer chain said previously Scott served as vice chairman and chief executive officer.
