FINNISH STATE LAUNCHES BONDS FOR BILLION MARKKA

The Finnish State launched two bonds totalling one billion markka with loan periods of 10 years and five years and annual interest rates of 7.75 and 7.25 pct respectively.     The bonds sell concurrently until the total is reached and nominal values are 25,000 markka, 10,000 markka, 5,000 markka and 1,000 markka, the Finance Ministry said.     It was decided with the banks the rates should be 0.25 pct less than the eight and 7.5 pct rates respectively of 10-year and five-year bonds launched by the State on August 17, a Ministry statement said.
