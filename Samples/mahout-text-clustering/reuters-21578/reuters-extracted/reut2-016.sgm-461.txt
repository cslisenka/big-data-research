ROYAL PALM SAVINGS <RPAL.O> NAMES PRESIDENT

Royal Palm Savings said it named Joseph Burgoon as president, a position which has been vacant since Jay Rosen left it in December 1985 to pursue other business ventures.     Burgoon most recently served as executive vice president and chief operating officer of Cardinal Federal Savings Bank of Cleveland.  Reuter &#3;
