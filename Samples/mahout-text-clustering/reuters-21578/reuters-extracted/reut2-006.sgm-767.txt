KEYCORP <KEY> SELLS SUBORDINATED CAPITAL NOTES

KeyCorp is offering 75 mln dlrs of subordinated capital notes due 1999 with an 8.40 pct coupon and par pricing, said sole manager First Boston Corp.     That is 122 basis points more than the yield of comparable Treasury securities.     Non-callable for life, the issue is rated A-3 by Moody's Investors Service Inc and A-minus by Standard and Poor's Corp.  Reuter &#3;
