ITT'S <ITT>SHERATON TO ADD 34 PROPERTIES IN YEAR

ITT Corp's Sheraton Corp subsidiary said during 1987 it plans to add 34 properties, down from 40 in 1986.     It said revenues of owned, leased, managed and franchised properties in 1987 were 3.6 billion dlrs, up from 3.3 billion dlrs in 1985.  Reuter &#3;
