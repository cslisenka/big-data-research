COOPER <COO> FILES FOR 300 MLN DLR NOTE OFFERING

Cooper Companies Inc filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a proposed offering of up to 300 mln dlrs of senior extendible notes set to mature on January 1, 1993.     Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc will act as an underwriter for the proposed offering, Cooper said.     Proceeds from the offering will be used to repay debt, for general corporate purposes, and to support future growth.  Reuter &#3;
