TEXACO <TX> HOLDERS SEEK PREFERRED DIVIDEND

Texaco Inc said it has been asked by a committee of shareholders about the possibility of the company paying a preferred stock dividend to shareholders.     The company said the inquiry, from the Equity Security Holders Committee, outlines the committee's ideas on how such a dividend might be paid.     Texaco said it would explore the committee's outline, as well as other options that would support shareholders' interests and assets.  Reuter &#3;
