TUNISIA TO BREAK TIES WITH IRAN

Tunisia has decided to break diplomatic relations with Iran, the foreign ministry said.     A communique published by the official TAP news agency said the Iranian embassy in Tunis had been engaged in activities liable to disturb public order.     These included acts aimed at sowing ideological confusion and anarchy, the communique said.  Reuter &#3;
