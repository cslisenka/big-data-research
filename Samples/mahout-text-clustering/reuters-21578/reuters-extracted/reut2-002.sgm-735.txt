HADSON <HADS> PLANS 3,750,000 SHARE OFFERING

Hadson Corp said it intends to file a registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission within one week covering an offering of 3,750,000 common shares.     The company had 14,260,000 shares outstanding on December 31 and 75.0 mln shares authorized.     It said proceeds of the offering are intended to be used to finance expansion of existing operations, for acquisitions and for working capital.  Reuter &#3;
