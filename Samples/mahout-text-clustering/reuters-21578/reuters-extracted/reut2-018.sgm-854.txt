CONT'L ILLINOIS <CIL> CANNOT EXPLAIN STOCK MOVE

Continental Illinois Corp said it knows of know reason why its stock was trading higher in heavy volume.     The stock was up 1/2 to 5-3/8 on turnover of 1,386,000 shares, making it one of the most actively traded NYSE issues.     Traders earlier said Goldman Sachs and Co was trading on the stock. However, Goldman Sachs banking analyst Robert Albertson had not placed the stock on his recommended list, his office told Reuters.  Reuter &#3;
