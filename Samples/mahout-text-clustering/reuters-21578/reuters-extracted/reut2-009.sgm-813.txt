PHILIPS ELECTRICAL SELLS STAKE IN UNIDARE

<Philips Electrical (Ireland) Ltd> has arranged the sale of the one mln ordinary shares it holds in its subsidiary <Unidare Aluminium Ltd>, Unidare said.     The placing has been arranged through <Allied Irish Investment Bank Plc> at an ex-dividend price of 371 Irish pence per share.  REUTER &#3;
