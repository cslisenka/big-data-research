ONE CHRYSLER <C> PLANT TO WORK OVERTIME

Chrysler Corp said one of its U.S. car and truck assembly plants will work overtime next week.     Six of its seven U.S. facilities will work during the week. The plant on overtime, both during the week and on Saturday, April 11, is the company's Sterling Heights, Mich., factory.     The company's U.S. plants and offices will be closed Friday, April 17, and Monday, April 20, for the Good Friday and Martin Luther King Jr. holidays.  Reuter &#3;
