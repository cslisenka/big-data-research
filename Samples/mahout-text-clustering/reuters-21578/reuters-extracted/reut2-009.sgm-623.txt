MINNESOTA POWER <MPL> SELLS SEVEN-YEAR BONDS

Minnesota Power and Light Co is raising 55 mln dlrs through an offering of first and refunding mortgage bonds due 1994 with a 7-3/4 pct coupon and par pricing, said sole manager PaineWebber Inc.     That is 68 basis points more than the yield of comparable Treasury securities.     Non-callable for five years, the issue is rated A-2 by Moody's Investors Service Inc and A-plus by Standard and Poor's Corp.  Reuter &#3;
