ALLURE COSMETICS <ALUR> SIGNS PACT WITH MOTOWN

Allure Cosmetics Inc said it reached an agreement with <Motown Industries> of Los Angeles to be the exclusive licensee of the Motown name for skin care, cosmetics and hair-care products.     Allure said the medium-priced products are scheduled to be introduced at a trade show in Philadelphia in early April. The company said the products will be sold in professional salons, major drug chains and department stores.     Allure also said it entered into a merchandising agreement with the U.S. Olympic Committee to use the Olympic designation on  suntan, sun treatment, lip care and other facial skin-care products.     Alllure said it also introduced a new face lift masque, intended to reduce wrinkles and other signs of aging.  Reuter &#3;
