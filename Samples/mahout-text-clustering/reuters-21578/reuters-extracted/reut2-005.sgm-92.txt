HEALTHCARE INTERNATIONAL <HII> GETS FUNDING

Healthcare International Inc said it completed two financial transactions with HealthVest <HVT>, a health care real estate investment trust.     The company said the first entailed receiveing approximately 11 mln dlrs through a sale and leaseback arrangement on the property of Austin Diagnostic Clinic, which it acquired in December 1986.     The company also said it received 33 mln dlrs in financing on its Healthcare Medical Center of Tustin in Orange County, Calif. The funds will be used to repay floating indebtedness, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
