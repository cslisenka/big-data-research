TRELLEBORG TAKE LARGER SLICE OF BOLIDEN

Swedish tyres, process equipment and components firm <Trelleborg AB> said it was taking a larger stake in mining and metals concern Boliden AB <BLDS.ST> than it had originally intended.     Trelleborg had previously announced it was exercising an option to acquire up to 65 pct of Boliden's voting rights.     In a statement, the company said they had received offers of up to 68 pct of the rights, and that they would accept all.  Reuter &#3;
