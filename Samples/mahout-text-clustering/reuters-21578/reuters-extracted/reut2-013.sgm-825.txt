PHOTON TECHNOLOGY <PHON> FINALIZES AGREEMENT

Photon Technology International said it signed a research and development agreement with <M-L Technology Ventures L.P.>, a research and development limited partnership sponsored by Merrill Lynch Capital Markets    The agreement calls for MLTV to pay Photon more than 3.1 mln dlrs over a three-year period to develop new electro-optical technologies with applications in the medical field, the company said.     In 1988, MLTV may, at its option, have the right to acquire warrants to purchase, during the next six years, 475,000 shares of the company's common stock at 6.50 dlrs per share, adjusted for future earnings, Photon said.
