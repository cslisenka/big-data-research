INDIUM CORP RAISES PRODUCER LIST PRICE

Indium Corp of America said it has raised its list price for indium ingots, minimum 99.97 pct, to 4.50 dlrs per troy ounce from 3.95 dlrs, effective immediately.     Traders said the rise was based on continuing strong consumer demand in the United States and Japan.     Free market price ideas rose this morning to a nominal range of 135 to 145 dlrs per kilo, the highest level for about five years, compared with 125/132 dlrs on Friday, dealers said.  REUTER &#3;
