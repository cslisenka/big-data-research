WALT DISNEY ISSUES 60 MLN AUS DLR EUROBOND

Walt Disney Co Ltd is issuing a 60 mln Australian dlr eurobond due May 7, 1990 with a 14-1/2 pct coupon and priced at 101-3/8 pct, Warburg Securities said as lead manager.     The non-callable bonds will be issued in denominations of 1,000 Australian dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg.     Gross fees of 1-1/2 pct comprise one pct for selling and 1/2 pct for management and underwriting combined.  REUTER &#3;
