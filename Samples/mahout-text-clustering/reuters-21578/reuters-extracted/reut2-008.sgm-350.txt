AMERICAN TELEVISION <ATCMA> BUYS TIME <TL> UNIT

American Television and Communications Corp said it has completed the acquisition of Manhattan Cable Television Inc from Time Inc for about 9,400,000 Class B common shares.     American Television was spun off from Time in August 1986. The company said following this transaction, Time now owns 82 pct of American Television.  Reuter &#3;
