MEXICO PUTS FOREIGN INVESTMENT AT 17.8 BILLION DLRS

Foreign investment in Mexico stood at end August at 17.8 billion dlrs, following an increase of 900 mln dlrs in the first eight months, the Trade and Industrial Development Ministry said.     It said authorized investments rose 30 pct last year, equivalent to 2.45 billion dlrs, and expected a similar increase for 1987 as a whole.  Reuter &#3;
