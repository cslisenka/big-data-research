FORD <F> UNIT TO BUILD PLANT FOR TOKICO

Ford Motor Land Development Corp, a wholly owned real estate subsidiary of Ford Motor Co, said it will build a 48,000 square foot facility for (Tokico America Inc) in return for a long-term lease.     Terms were not disclosed.     Tokico, an automotive parts and robotics supplier, is a subsidiary of (Tokico Ltd) of Japan.     Ford said it is Tokico's principal customer in the U.S.  Reuter &#3;
