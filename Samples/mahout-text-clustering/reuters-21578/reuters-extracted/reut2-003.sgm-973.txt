RELIANCE ELECTRIC UNIT TO REDEEM DEBENTURES

Reliance Electric Co said its Reliance Industrial Co unit will redeem on April 10 all outstanding 7-1/4 pct debentures due 1996 and 9-5/8 pct debentures due 1994.     Reliance Industrial will buy back the 7-1/4s at 101 plus accrued interest and the 9-5/8s at 102.344 plus accrued interest.     Reliance Electric was a unit of Exxon Corp <XON> until December 30, 1986, when a group of investors took the company private in a leveraged buyout, a Reliance spokesman said.  Reuter &#3;
