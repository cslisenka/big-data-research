OLIN <OLN> NAMES SUCCESSOR FOR CHIEF EXECUTIVE

Olin Corp said John W. Johnstone Jr, 54, president and chief operating officer, will succeed John M. Henske as chief executive officer on April 30.     Henske, 63, will continue as chairman until he retires in June 1988, Olin said.  Reuter &#3;
