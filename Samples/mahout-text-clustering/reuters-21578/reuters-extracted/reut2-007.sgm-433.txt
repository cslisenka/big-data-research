MMR HOLDING CORP INITIAL OFFERING PRICED

The initial public offering of 1.1 mln shares of MMR Holding Corp common stock was priced at 13.50 dlrs a share, said William Blair and Co and Howard, Weil, Labouisse, Friedrichs Inc, co-managers of the underwriting group.     All shares will be sold by the company.     Based in Baton Rouge, La., MMR is an electrical and mechanical specialty contractor. Its fiscal 1986 contract revenues were 135.2 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
