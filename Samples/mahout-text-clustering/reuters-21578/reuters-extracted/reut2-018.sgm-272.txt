U.S. TO SELL 13.2 BILLION DLRS OF BILLS

The U.S. Treasury said it will sell 13.2 billion dlrs of three and six-month bills at its regular auction next week.     The June 8 sale, to be evenly divided between the three and six month issues, would result in a paydown of 1.3 billion dlrs as maturing bills total 14.512 billion dlrs.     The bills are to be issued June 11.  Reuter &#3;
