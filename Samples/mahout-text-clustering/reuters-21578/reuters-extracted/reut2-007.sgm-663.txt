SWANK INC <SNK> CAN'T EXPLAIN STOCK ACTION

Swank Inc said it knows of no corporate development to account for the volume of activity in its stock yesterday and can only assume institutional trading was the cause.     In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Swank stock rose 7/8 to 15-3/4 on volume of 104,700 shares.  Reuter &#3;
