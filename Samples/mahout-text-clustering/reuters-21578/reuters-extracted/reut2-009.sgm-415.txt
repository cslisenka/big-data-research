K MART <KM> UNIT BUYS TEXAS LAND

K Mart Apparel Corp, a unit of K Mart Corp, said it purchased 35 acres 15 miles east of downtown Dallas from Baker Associates No. 2 Joint Venture.     Financial details were not disclosed.     K Mart said it will construct a 456,000-square-foot distribution center and 15,000 square feet of office space on the site.  Reuter &#3;
