THYSSEN UNIT TO COOPERATE WITH CANADIAN FIRM

Thyssen AG <THYH.F> unit Thyssen Guss AG, a metals group, said it had agreed to cooperate with Canadian firm <Cercast Inc> to manufacture fine casting products.     Both companies would merge plants in the West German towns of Soest and Moers under a new company in which the two groups would have equal shares in a capital of three mln marks.     A spokeswoman for Thyssen Industrie AG, which holds 100 pct of Thyssen Guss, said the joint-venture should produce turnover of an annual 40 mln marks. Thyssen Industrie is held 90 pct by Thyssen AG.
