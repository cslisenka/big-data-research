OKLAHOMA THRIFT PLACED UNDER RECEIVERSHIP

The Federal Home Loan Bank Board (FHLBB) today placed <Investors Federal Bank> of El Reno, Oklahoma under receivership and transferred its 97.8 mln dlrs in assets to the <Investors Savings and Loan Association>.     An FHLBB statement said the thrift was insolvent and "had substantially dissipated its assets," mainly by participating in large commercial real estate developments. It said it violated federal laws and regulations on loan documentation, loans to directors and conflict of interest. The sucessor organisation is a federal savings and loan to be managed under contract by Sunwood Management Corp of Parker, Colorado.  REUTER &#3;
