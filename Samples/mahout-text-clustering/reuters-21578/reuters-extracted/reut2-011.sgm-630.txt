MECHTON INT'L <MCHT> SETS STOCK BUYBACK

Mechton International Corp said its board authorized the company to buy its outstanding stock in the open market at prices deemed appropriate by management.     No amount of shares were specified.     The company also said it expects 1987 results to exceed 1986 net income of 46,000 dlrs or three cts a share. It cited recent acquisitions and establishment of operations in France.  Reuter &#3;
