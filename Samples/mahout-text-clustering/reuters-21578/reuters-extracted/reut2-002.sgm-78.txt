AMERICAN SOFTWARE <AMSWA> SETS STOCK SPLIT

American Software Inc said its board declared a three-for-two stock split on Class A and Class B common shares, payable March 31, record March 16.     The company said it expects to increase its semiannual dividend 12.5 pct to six cts per share post-split from eight cts pre-split.  Reuter &#3;
