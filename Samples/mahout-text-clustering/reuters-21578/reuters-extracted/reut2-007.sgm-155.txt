PETROLANE PARTNERS <LPG> UNITS OFFERED

Texas Eastern Corp <TET> said an initial public offering of 12.5 mln units of its master limited partnership Petrolane Partners LP is under way at 20.50 dlrs each.     Petrolane will own and operate Texas Eastern's domestic liquefied petroleum gas distribution business.  Texas Eastern said it will retain a 45 pct interest in Petrolane.     The company said closing of the sale of the assets is expected March 26.  Reuter &#3;
