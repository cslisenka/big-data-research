XICOR <XICO> SEES END OF INTEL <INTC> PROGRAM

Xicor Inc said based on recent talks with Intel Corp, it expects its joint research program with Intel on the development of advanced memory devices to be ending shortly.     The company said it will reallocate personnel presently assigned to the program to its one megabit CMOS E2 PROM program and other development programs.     Reasons for the end of the Intel venture were not given.  Reuter &#3;
