SOVIET MINISTER IN GERMAN JOINT VENTURE TALKS

Soviet deputy prime minister Alexei Antonov begins two days of economic talks in Bonn that will include discussions on Soviet-West German joint ventures.     Antonov and West German Economics Minister Martin Bangemann are heading their respective delegations to the joint commission on economic, scientific and technical cooperation.     "The main point of the round will be Soviet economic reforms, new forms of economic cooperation, joint ventures, and cooperation in construction," government spokesman Friedhelm Ost said.
