HITECH ENGINEERING <THEX> COMPLETES STOCK SALE

HiTech Engineering Co said it completed the private sale of 200,000 shares of its common stock to its chairman and president, Francine Prokoski, for 37,500 dlrs.     The company said the proceeds will be used to develop products under a contract with a private company.  Reuter &#3;
