LTV <QLTV> GETS 500 MLN DLR BOEING <BA> CONTRACT

LTV Corp said it has received a contract worth more than 500 mln dlrs from Boeing Co to produce major sections for Boeing 767, 757 and 747 airliners, including the new 747-400.     The company said the contract covers continuing production through mid-1990.  LTV makes aft body sections and horizontal and vertical stabilizers for 747 and 757's and horizontal stabilizers for 767's.     It said as part of the contract it will build a new type of horizontal stabilizer for the 747-400 containing tanks capable of holding 3,300 gallons of fuel.     The added fuel capacity will allow the 747-400 to fly about 1,000 miles further than the 747-300, LTV said.     The company said it will start making the 747-400 shipsets in June and the first aircraft is scheduled for completion in early 1988.  It is to be delivered to NWA Inc <NWA> in December 1988.
