PRESIDENT SAYS SHULTZ TO HEAD FOR MOSCOW TALKS

President Reagan announced he is sending Secretary of State George Shultz to Moscow for talks with Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze next month.     Arms control is expected to be the major topic.     The announcement said the talks will take place April 13-16 and would review "the entirety of our relationship."  Reuter &#3;
