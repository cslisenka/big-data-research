EDWARD HINES LUMBER NET REALIZABLE VALUE SET

Edward Hines Lumber Co, which is in the process of complete liquidation, said the estimated net realizable value of its assets as of Dec 31, 1986 was four dlrs a share.     It said this is the same estimated per share value announced earlier in 1986.     The company said total liquidation distributions so far are slightly in excess of 40 dlrs a share.  Reuter &#3;
