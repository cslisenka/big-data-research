ASARCO <AR> COMPLETES SHARE OFFERING

Asarco Inc said it completed the sale of four mln shares of Asarco common stock to an underwriting group led by First Boston Corp.     The underwriters sold the shares to the public at a price of 22.50 dlrs a share, the minerals and energy company said.     Proceeds of about 86 mln dlrs will be used to reduce outstanding debt, it said.     The offering included 3.5 mln shares announced April 1 and 500,000 to cover overallotments.  Reuter &#3;
