UAE CENTRAL BANK CD YIELDS MOSTLY LOWER

Yields on certificates of deposit offered by the United Arab Emirates central bank were mostly lower than last Monday's offering, the bank said.     The one-month issue remained at 6-1/8 pct but the two, three and six-month yields fell 1/16 point to 6-1/16 pct from 6-1/8 pct.  REUTER &#3;
