FERRANTI UNIT LANDS MULTI MLN DLR CONRTACT

Ferranti Plc <FNTI.L> said its Edinburgh based electro-optics department of Ferranti Defence Systems Ltd has secured a multi mln dlr contract to supply laser rangefinders for the Canadian Forces Low Level Air Defense System.     The first production order for 38 of the type 629G lasers has been placed by <Martin Marietta> of the U.S., A subcontractor to <Orelikon Aerospace Incorporated> of Canada, the prime contractor for the system. Some 300 further orders are anticipated as the programme proceeds.     Ferranti shares were 2p firmer at 134 after the news.  REUTER &#3;
