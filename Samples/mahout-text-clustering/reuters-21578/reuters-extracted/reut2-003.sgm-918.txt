ROSE'S STORES INC <RSTO> REPORTS HIGHER SALES

Rose's Stores Inc reported February sales of 92.7 mln dlrs, an increase of 18.1 mln dlrs, or 24.2 pct from sales of 74.6 mln dlrs in the comparable period the year prior.     The company said identical stores sales increased 15 pct for the month of February.     Rose's operates 224 discount stores as of February 1987 compared to 208 stores a year ago in 13 southeastern states.  Reuter &#3;
