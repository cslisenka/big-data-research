FORD CANADA FEBRUARY CAR SALES OFF SIX PCT

Ford Motor Co of Canada, 90 pct-owned by Ford Motor Co, said February car sales fell six pct to 13,734 units from year-earlier 14,589 units.     Year-to-date car sales fell one pct to 25,534 units from 25,851 units the previous year, it said.  Reuter &#3;
