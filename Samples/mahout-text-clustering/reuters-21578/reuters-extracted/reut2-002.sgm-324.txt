BISHOP GRAPHICS <BGPH> COMPLETES STORE SALE

Bishop Graphics Inc said it completed the sale of its Newport Beach Art Supply Center to Standard Brands Paint Co's <SBP> Art Store unit.     Terms were not disclosed.     Bishop also said it has opened a new sales and service office in Irvine, Calif.      Reuter &#3;
