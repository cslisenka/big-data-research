SOVIET NEGOTIATOR REPORTS ARMS TALKS BREAKTHROUGH

Chief Soviet arms negotiator Yuli Vorontsov said Soviet-American talks on cutting medium-range nuclear missiles had made a breakthrough.     Vorontsov said he expected a treaty would be ready for signing within three to four months following the latest talks in Geneva.     He told a news conference here "all the elements point to optimism" and only technical work on treaty language remained outstanding.  REUTER &#3;
