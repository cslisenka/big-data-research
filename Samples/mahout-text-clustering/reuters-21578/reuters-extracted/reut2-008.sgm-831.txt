CONTROL RESOURCE <CRIX> NAMES NEW PRESIDENT

Control Resource Industries Inc said it named R. Joseph Clancy as president.     R. Steven Lutterbach, who previously held the president's title, continues as chairman and chief executive officer.     Clancy was a vice president and member of the board of directors.     The company also named Cody Geddes as vice president and chief financial officer, succeeding John Wojcik, who remains as executive vice president.  Reuter &#3;
