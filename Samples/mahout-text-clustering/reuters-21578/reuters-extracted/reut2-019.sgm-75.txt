WORLD BANK ISSUES 100 MLN AUSTRALIAN DLR BOND

The World Bank is issuing a 100 mln Australian dlr bond due July 20, 1992 paying 13-3/8 pct and priced at 101-7/8 pct, lead manager Hambros Bank Ltd said.     The non-callable bond is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 Australian dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg.     The selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct.     The payment date is July 20.  REUTER &#3;
