DUFF/PHELPS DOWNGRADES MELLON BANK CORP <MEL>

Duff and Phelps today lowered the senior debt rating of Mellon Bank Corp. to D&P-8 (High BBB) from D&P-6 (middle A) and lowered the preferred stock rating to D&P-9 (middle BBB) from D&P-7 (low A).     These rating changes affect approximately 607 mln dlrs of securities.     In addition, the certificate of deposit rating of Mellon Bank has been lowered to Duff one minus from Duff one. The rating affects approximately 2.7 bln dlrs on CDs.     "A sharp deterioration in asset quality," resulting in a "significant" first-quarter loss were cited in the change.  Reuter &#3;
