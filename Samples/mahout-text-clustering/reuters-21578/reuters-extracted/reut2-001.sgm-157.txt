CANADA INDUSTRIAL PRODUCTION UP 2.53 PCT

Canadian industrial production rose 2.53 pct in December after falling 0.51 pct in November, Statistics Canada said.     The federal agency said year-over-year production was off 0.65 pct in December, compared with a decline of 1.65 pct in November.  Reuter &#3;
