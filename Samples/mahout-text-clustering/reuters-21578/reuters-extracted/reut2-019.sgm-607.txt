AMEV HOLDINGS TO SELL UNIT TO USA FINANCIAL

AMEV Holdings Inc, the New York-based operating arm of NV AMEV <AMEV.AS> in The Netherlands and parent company of Security Mutual Finance Corp, said it reached a definitive agreement to sell Security Mutual to USA Financial Services Inc.     Security Mutual, based in Decatur, Ala., operates 33 loan offices in fourh Southeastern states and has 90 mln dlrs in receivables outstanding.     USA Financial is a subsidiary of Chicago-based United Savings of America.  Reuter &#3;
