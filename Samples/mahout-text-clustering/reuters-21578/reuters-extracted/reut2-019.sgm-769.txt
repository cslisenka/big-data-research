PEPSI <PEP> NAMES NEW AD AGENCY FOR BLACK MARKET

PepsiCo Inc's Pepsi-Cola USA said it named privately owned Lockhart and Pettus Inc of New York to handle its advertising and promotion to Black consumers.     Pepsi-Cola USA said it is the second major new account for Lochart and Pettus in as many weeks. Earlier this month, Chrysler Motors Corp named the agency to handle its minority marketing.
