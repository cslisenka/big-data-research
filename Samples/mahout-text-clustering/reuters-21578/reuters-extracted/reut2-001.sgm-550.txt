CEREALS MCAS TO BE UNCHANGED NEXT WEEK

Cereals Monetary Compensatory Amounts, MCAs, will be unchanged for the week starting March 9, an EC Commission spokesman said.     They will be plus 2.4 points for the Netherlands and West Germany, minus two for Denmark, minus eight for France, minus nine for Ireland, minus 5.7 for Italy, minus 30.2 for Britain, minus 44.1 for Greece and minus 10.5 for Spain.
