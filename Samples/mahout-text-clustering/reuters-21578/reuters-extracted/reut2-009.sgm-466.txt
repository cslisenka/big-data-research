EMERSON RADIO <EME> SELLS DEBT PRIVATELY

Emerson Radio Corp said it has sold 33 mln dlrs of 8-3/8 pct senior notes due 1997 to institutional investors.     The company said prepayment provisions will make the average life about seven years.  It said funds will be used to help finance growth.  Reuter &#3;
