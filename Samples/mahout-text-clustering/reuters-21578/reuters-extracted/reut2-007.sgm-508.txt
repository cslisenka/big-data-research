TRANS-CANADA RESOURCES GETS CREDITOR EXTENSION

<Trans-Canada Resources Ltd> said an Alberta court on March 6 granted it an extension to April 6 from March 9 on Trans-Canada's filing of a plan of arrangement with the court and extended to May 22 a stay of legal proceedings against the company.     The court also ruled that shareholders' and creditors's meetings must occur by May 4, Trans-Canada said.  Reuter &#3;
