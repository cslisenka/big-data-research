NO MEXICO REACTION TO PERU SILVER TALKS PROPOSAL

mexico's minister of energy and mines, alfredo del mazo, has yet to reply to a peruvian invitation for ministerial-level talks on bilateral cooperation in silver marketing, a ministry spokesman said.     Peruvian officials said they extended the invitation earlier this week and that it was possible the talks could be held within the next 15 days.     Meanwhile, a banco de mexico spokesman confirmed that mexican central bank head miguel mancera aguayo held private talks here yesterday with the president of the peruvian central bank leonel figueroa.
