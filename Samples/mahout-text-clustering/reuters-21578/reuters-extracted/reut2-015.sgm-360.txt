INVESTOR CHARGES WITH STOCK MANUPULATION

William Rodman, a 43-year-old New York investor was indicted for allegedly plotting to manipulate the price of three over-the-counter stocks to drive up their prices and create interest in the companies.     Rodman was also charged with securities and wire fraud stemming from the alleged manipulation of the stocks of Memory Metals Inc <MRMT>, Memory Protection Devices Inc <MPDI> and <Intravision Inc>, beginning in January 1984.     The indictment charged that Rodman and other unnamed co-conspirators bought and sold the stocks through 100 accounts in at least 35 broker-dealer firms.      Reuter &#3;
