U.S. HEALTHCARE <USHC.O> IN BLUE CROSS VENTURE

U.S. Healthcare Inc said it has signed an agreement with Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Missouri to jointly develop and operate a new health maintenance organization for the St. Louis area.     The company said Blue Cross will wholly own HMO Missouri, which U.S. Healthcare will manage and market. It said it hopes to start offering the HMO by January.  Reuter &#3;
