WARWICK INSURANCE <WIMI.O> BUYS STOCK

Warwick Insurance Managers Inc, the holding company for Warwick Insurance Co and its subsidiaries, said it purchased 55,000 shares of its own common stock.     The company said it may establish an employee stock ownership plan, and that the shares purchased would be made available to the plan if it is adopted.  Reuter &#3;
