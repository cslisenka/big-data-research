FLOODS AND HEAVY RAIN HIT IRAN

Fifty people were killed or injured in flooding in southeastern Iran and three died after heavy rain elsewhere, Tehran Radio reported today.     The radio, monitored by the British Broadcasting Corp, said flooding around Zahedan, near the Pakistan-Afghanistan border, caused heavy damage.     Rain in central Iran damaged houses, bridges, crops and irrigation systems.  Reuter &#3;
