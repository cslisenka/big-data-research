BIOSENSOR <BSNR> SIGNS MARKETING AGREEMENT

Biosensor Corp said it signed an agreement with Del Mar Avionics, Irvine, Calif., to market the Biosensor Multiday system.     Multiday monitoring products can analyze patient heart signals for up to five days in or out of the hospital. Patients can transmit complex cardiac data over the telephone to their medical center, allowing physicians to review the information as necessary.  Reuter &#3;
