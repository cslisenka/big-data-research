STERLING <STY> SEEKS FDA APPROVAL FOR MILRINONE

Sterling Drug Inc said it submitted a new drug application to the Food and Drug Administration for permission to market an oral form of Corotrope (milrinone), a drug for treating chronic congestive heart failure.     Sterling said the application includes a series of studies of 952 patients and results of three multicenter studies involving another 571 patients, to demonstrate the efficacy and safety of the drug as an alternative to digitalis.
