CANADIAN MANUFACTURING SHIPMENTS FALL IN MONTH

Shipments of products by Canadian manufacturers declined by 1.5 pct in January to 21.08 billion dlrs, seasonally adjusted, led by a slowdown in transportation equipment, Statistics Canada said.     In December orders rose 2.8 pct.     The federal agency said new orders fell 4.0 pct following a 4.5 pct increase in December.     Inventories fell to 33.71 billion dlrs from 34.00 billion dlrs the previous month.              Reuter &#3;
