JEFFERIES MAKING MARKET IN ALLEGIS <AEG>

Jefferies and Co said it is making a market in Allegis Corp at 67 to 68.     The New York Stock Exchange halted the stock before the close at 75 for an order imbalance.     A Jefferies official said the firm, which specializes in third market trading, is staying open tonight "to accomodate our customers."   Reuter &#3;
