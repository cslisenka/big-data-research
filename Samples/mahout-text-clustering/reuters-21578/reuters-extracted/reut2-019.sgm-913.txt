INFINITE GRAPHICS <INFG.O> IN JOINT VEENTURE

Infinite Graphics Inc said it signed a letter of intent to create a joint venture with <Computer Design Equipment Co> to expand sales of its computer-aided design equipment in the Midwest.     Infinite Graphics said it will manage and hold the controlling interest in the venture, National CADD-Pro Upper Midwest.     Computer Design has headquarters in Syracuse, Ind.  Reuter &#3;
