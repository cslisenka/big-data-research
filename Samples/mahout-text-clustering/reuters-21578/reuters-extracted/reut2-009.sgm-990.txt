UNITED WATER <UWR>, BASE TEN <BASE> SET VENTURE

United Water Resources Inc said it formed a venture with Base Ten Systems Inc's Base Ten Telecom Inc to sell a telephone-based automatic meter reading system to the utility industry.     United Water Resources, parent of Hackensack and Spring Valley Water Cos, said it will provide marketing and customer support for Base Ten's automatic meter reading system.     The company said it will install the system for 168,000 commercial and residential customers this spring.  Reuter &#3;
