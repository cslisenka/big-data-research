SOUTHWEST AIRLINES <LUV> TO REPURCHASE SHARES

Southwest Airlines Co said it intends to repurchase up to 500,000 common shares in the open market from time to time.     The company also said it is expanding its air freight service to Dallas, Amarillo, Houston, Austin, Lubbock, San Antonio and Midland/Odessa, Texas and San Francisco.  Reuter &#3;
