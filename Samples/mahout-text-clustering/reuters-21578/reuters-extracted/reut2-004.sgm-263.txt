SHOWBOAT <SBO> UNIT SELLS MORTGAGE-BACKED BONDS

Ocean Showboat Finance Corp, a unit of Showboat Inc, is raising 180 mln dlrs through an offering of mortgage-backed bonds due 2002 with an 11-3/8 pct coupon and par pricing, said sole manager Donaldson, Lufkin and Jenrette Securities Corp.     Moody's Investors Service Inc rates the bonds B-1 and Standard and Poor's Corp rates them B-plus.  Reuter &#3;
