NORTHERN TELECOM <NT> SENDS SHIPMENT TO NIPPON

Northern Telecom said it shipped the first digital central office switching system it sold to Nippon Telegraph and Telephone under the terms of a 250 mln dlrs supply agreement.     The agreement began with the signing of a letter of understanding in December 1985. THe formal seven-year agreement was signed in May 1986.     The Northern Telecom systems is the first U.S.-manufactured digital central office switching systems to be installed in the Japanese public telephone network, it said.     Reuter &#3;
