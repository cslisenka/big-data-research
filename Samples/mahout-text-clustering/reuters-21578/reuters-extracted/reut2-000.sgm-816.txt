MFS MUNICIPAL INCOME TRUST <MFM> SETS PAYOUT

MFS Municipal Income Trust said it declared a monthly income distribution of 5.7 cts a share compared with 5.5 cts a share paid in the previous month.     It said the distribution is payable March 27 to shareholders of record March 13.  Reuter &#3;
