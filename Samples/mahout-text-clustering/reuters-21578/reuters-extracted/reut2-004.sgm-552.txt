ROCHESTER GAS <RGS> OFFERED PRD SHARES

Rochester Gas and Electric Corp said 300,000 shares of 8.25 pct preferred stock, series R, will be offered at 100 dlrs per share plus accrued dividends, if any, from the date of the original issue.     It said The First Boston Corp <FBC> and <E.F. Hutton and Co Inc> are co-underwriters of the issue.     It said net proceeds of the offering will be used to redeem its 10.84 pct preferred stock, series G, which is currently callable at 107 dlrs per share and for other corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
