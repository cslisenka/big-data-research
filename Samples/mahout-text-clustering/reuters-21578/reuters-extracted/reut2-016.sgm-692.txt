TEXTRON <TXT> VICE-CHAIRMAN RESIGNS

Textron Inc said E. Paul Casey resigned as vice-chairman and a director to pursue other interests.     Casey assumed his duties at Textron in October, 1986, following acquisition of Ex-Cell-O Corp by Textron. Casey was formerly chairman, president and chief executive of Ex-Cell-O.  Reuter &#3;
