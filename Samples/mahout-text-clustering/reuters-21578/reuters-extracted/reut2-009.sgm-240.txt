SINGER <SMF> GETS TEXAS AIR <TEX> CONTRACT

Singer Co said it has signed a 12-year contract with Texas Air Corp's Continental Airlines Inc unit to lease Continental an aircraft simulator for flight crew training.     Singer said it will also market training on the simulator to other airlines.     The company said initially it will provide a simulator for McDonnell Douglas Corp's MD-80 but will replace that simulator in 1988 with one for the MD-82.  Reuter &#3;
