INLAND STEEL <IAD> TO BUILD NEW PLANT IN INDIANA

Inland Steel Industries and Governor Robert Orr of Indiana said the new joint venture cold- rolled steel plant between Inland Steel and Nippon Steel Corp will be built on a site in St. Joseph County Indiana.     Inland Steel said yesterday that the joint venture, to be named I/N Tek, will cost more than 400 mln dlrs and will employ over 200 people by the time the companies complete the project in 1990.
