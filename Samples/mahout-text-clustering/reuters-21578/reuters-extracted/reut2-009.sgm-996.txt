EMHART <EMH> WINS FOUR COMMUNICATIONS CONTRACTS

Emhart Corp said it received four contracts valued at a total of seven mln dlrs to design, develop and install data communications and automated dispatching systems.     The company said its Planning Research Corp unit, which received the contracts, will do the work for the city of Grand Rapids, Mich., the New Jersey Transit Authority, the Los Angeles County Sheriff's office and the city of Calgary, Alberta, Canada.
