LIFE TECHNOLOGIES <LTEK> IN LICENSING AGREEMENT

Life Technologies Inc said it signed a two mln dlr technology transfer and licensing agreement with Toray Industries Inc, a Tokyo-based maker of  synthetic fibers and industrial, consumer and medical products.     Under terms of the agreement, Life said Toray purchased exclusive rights to market Life's hepatitus B detection technology in Japan, Korea and Taiwan.     Moreover, Life said the companies agreed to begin a joint research and development program to develop other human diagnostic products.  Reuter &#3;
