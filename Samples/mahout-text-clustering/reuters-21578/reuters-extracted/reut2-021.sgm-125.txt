<GLOBAL MOTORS> TO MARKET INDONESIAN VEHICLES

Global Motors Inc said it has agreed to become become the exclusive North American importer of the Lincan Gama, a four-wheel-drive sport vehicle manufactured by <P.T. Indauda> of Indonesia.     In a letter of intent it signed with P.T. Indauda, Global said it expects to begin marketing the first Lincan Gamas in mid-1989, with 20,000 units projected to be marketed in the first year.     Global is the parent company of Yugo America Inc, importer of the Yugoslavian Yugo vehicle.  Reuter &#3;
