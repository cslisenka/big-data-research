AUSTRIA INCREASES BOND TO 75 MLN AUSTRALIAN DLRS

The Australian dollar eurobond launched yesterday for the Republic of Austria has been increased to 75 mln dlrs from the original 50 mln, Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd said as lead manager.     The five year transaction has a 14-1/4 pct coupon and was priced at 101-3/4 pct.  REUTER &#3;
