<MICROTEL INC> MAKES ACQUISITION

Microtel Inc said it has completed the acquisition of <American Teledata Corp> and its US Dial subsidiary, which provide long distance telephone service in northeast Florida. Terms were not disclosed.     Microtel's shareholders include Norfolk Southern Corp <NSC>, M/A-Com Inc <MAI>, Centel Corp <CNT>, Alltel Corp <AT> and E.F. Hutton Group Inc <EFH>.  Reuter &#3;
