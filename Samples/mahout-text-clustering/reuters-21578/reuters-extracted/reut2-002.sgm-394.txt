NORFOLK SOUTHERN <NSC> COMPLETES COAL PURCHASE

Norfolk Southern Corp said it completed the purchase of 112 mln tons of recoverable coal reserves in Pike County, Ky., from Rouge Steel, a unit of Ford Motor Co <F>.     Norfolk Southern said the deal involves about 30,000 acres of coal bearing properties that will be administered by the company's Pocahontas Land Corp unit.  Reuter &#3;
