SOLAR SYSTEMS SUN DANCE <SSDN> EXTENDS WARRANTS

Solar Systems by Sun Dance said its board has extended the date until which its 1986 warrants and redeemable warrants may be exercised at 50 cts each to April 30 from March 31.     It said it plans no further extension.  Reuter &#3;
