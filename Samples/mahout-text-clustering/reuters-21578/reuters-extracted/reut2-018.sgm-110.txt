STEWART <STEW.O> SELLS PLANT TO SARA LEE <SLE>

Stewart Sandwiches Inc said it has sold its coffee roasting plant to Sara Lee Corp's Superior Coffee and Foods subsidiary for undisclosed terms.     The company said Superior will become the exclusive packer of Squire labeled coffee products, which are marketed by Stewart, and Stewart will provide equipment, service and distribution suppoort for some Superior coffee accounts.
