LIFESTYLE RESTAURANTS <LIF> ADJUSTS REVENUES

Lifestyle Restaurants Inc said revenues for the first quarter ended January 24, 1985, were 17.5 mln dlrs and not the 17.8 mln dlrs it had reported earlier.     The company also said a note attached to its earnings concerning a gain in 1986 on certain sales was incorrect and should be disregarded.  Reuter &#3;
