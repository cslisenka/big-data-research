SOUTHWEST <LUV> PLANS TO BEGIN DETROIT SERVICE

Southwest Airlines said it will begin service to Detroit, Michigan on June 4, 1987.     It said that with the new service, it will serve 27 cities in 13 states across the U.S.     It also said that delivery of six new 737-300 aircraft will enable it to expand its route system in June.
