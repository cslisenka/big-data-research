H.B. SHAINE CEASES BROKER-DEALER OPERATIONS

<H.B. Shaine and Co Inc> of Grand Rapids, Mich., ceas its broker-dealer operations, at the close of Oct 19, the New York Stock Exchange said.     The firm reported that, under such financial difficulty, it could not continue to do business as a member firm of the NYSE, according to the NYSE.     Officials of the company could not be reached for further comment.      Reuter &#3;
