GOULD <GLD> SELLS FRENCH BATTERY UNIT

Gould Inc said it sold its French Battery unit, Cie Francaise d'Electro Chimie, to a group of investors including the unit's employees and <GNB Inc> of Minnesota.     Terms of the sale were not disclosed. The unit had 1986 sales of 65 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
