ADVANCED TOBACCO <ATPI> TO MAKE ANNOUCEMENT

Advanced Tobacco Products Inc said it will make an announcement regarding its stock halt this morning on NASDAQ.     The company said lawyers are preparing the release with company officials and should be making the announcement within the hour.     The stock's last bid was 3/8 and last ask 1/2.  Reuter &#3;
