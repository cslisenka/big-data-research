SYNERGETICS <SYNG> GETS CHINESE ORDER

Synergetics International Inc said it has received its first order from China, a 250,000 dlr order for a flood management system to be used in the Daning and Yuzixi river basins of the Yangtse River.     The company also said it has received Japanese government certification for its satellite transmitter products, allowing users of Synergetics' remote computer systems to transmit a variety of hydrological and meteorological data via the Japan Space Agency's GMS satellite.     In addition, it said it has appointed Vaisala KK of Helsinki as exclkusive distributor of its products throughout Japan and has awarded Sierra/Misco Inc expanded distribution rights, in the Philippines, Malaysia, Sumatra, Nepal, India, Bengal, Thailand, Singapore and Indonesia.
