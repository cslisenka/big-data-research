WILDERNESS EXPERIENCE MAY FILE FOR BANKRUPTCY

<Wilderness Experience Inc> said it may be forced to file for bankruptcy court protection, or seek a liquidation of the company, if it is unsuccessful in obtaining the financing necessary to meet its bank debt commitments.     The company said it has been unable to renew a short-term borrowing arrangement with its bank or to make scheduled debt payments.     It also said the bank has said it will foreclose their security interest in the company if it does not present an acceptable payment program within the next several weeks.  Reuter &#3;
