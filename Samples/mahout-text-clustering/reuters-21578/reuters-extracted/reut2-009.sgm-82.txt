NASDAQ SHORT INTEREST IN MARCH FALLS

NASDAQ said short interest as of mid-march totaled 184.9 mln shares compared to 215.7 mln shares for the month of February.     NASDAQ said the mid-March figures are based on 3,983 securities and the February figures are based on 3,842 securities.       Reuter &#3;
