NABISCO EXPECTED TO ISSUE DOLLAR STRAIGHT

Nabisco Brands Inc, or one of its units, is expected to issue shortly a dollar straight eurobond, probably totalling 100 mln dlrs, bond market sources said.     They said they expect the issue will have a seven year maturity, but to have an investor put option after four years at par. The coupon is expected to be 8-3/4 pct while pricing will be at 101-3/8 pct.  REUTER &#3;
