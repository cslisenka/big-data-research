RED LION INNS L.P. BEGINS OFFERING

Red Lion Inns L.P. said it began an initial public offering of 4.9 mln units priced at 20 dlrs a unit.     Proceeds and a 105.87 mln dlrs mortgage loan will be used to finance the acquisition of 10 Red Lion hotels, it said.       Reuter &#3;
