NOSTALGIA NETWORK <NNET> ADDS SUBSCRIBERS

Nostalgia Network Inc said it signed an agreement with the ACTS Satellite Network to produce a series of two- and five-minute commercials of its home shopping service, "Collectible Showcase."     It said the ACTS Network, with more than six mln cable television subscribers, will air the commercials approximately 28 times a week for 13 weeks, and as a result Nostalgia Netowork said it expects a significant increase in sales volume in April.     Nostalgia is 29 pct owned by Futuresat Industries Inc.  Reuter &#3;
