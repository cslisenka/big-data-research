BAYER <BAYRY> MAKES U.S. ACQUISITION

Bayer AG of West Germany said it has acquired Wyrough and Loser Inc of Trenton, a maker of rubbr processing chemicals, for undisclosed terms.     It said its Mobay Corp subsidiary will transfer its entire U.S. Rhein-Chemie Rheinau GmbH specialty chemicals business for the rubber, plastics and lube oil industries to Wyrough and Loser later this year.
