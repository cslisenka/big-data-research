APPLIED MAGNETICS <APM> NAMES OFFICERS

Applied Magnetics Corp said its board named Ben Newitt to the new position of vice chairman and as chief executive officer.     Newitt, who had been president and chief executive officer, is succeeded as president by William Anderson, who had been executive vice president and chief operating officer.  Reuter &#3;
