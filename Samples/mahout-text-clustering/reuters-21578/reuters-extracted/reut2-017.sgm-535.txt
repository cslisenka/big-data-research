MERCEDES-BENZ CREDIT ISSUES 100 MLN DLR EUROBOND

Mercedes-Benz Credit Corp is issuing a 100 mln dlr eurobond due June 25, 1992 paying 8-1/4 pct and priced at 100-1/2 pct, lead manager Deutsche Bank Capital Markets said.     The non-callable bond is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg. The selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct.     The payment date is June 25.  REUTER &#3;
