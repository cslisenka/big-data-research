SOUND WAREHOUSE <SWHI> SAME-STORE SALES SLIP

Sound Warehouse Inc said revenues for the five weeks ended March One were up 27 pct to 15.0 mln dlrs from 11.9 mln dlrs a year before, with same-store revenues off one pct.     It said revenues for the third quarter were up 24 pct to 48.0 mln dlrs from 38.7 mln dlrs a year before, with same-store revenues off one pct, and nine month revenues were up 21 pct to 116.8 mln dlrs from 96.4 mln dlrs, with same-store revenues up three pct.
