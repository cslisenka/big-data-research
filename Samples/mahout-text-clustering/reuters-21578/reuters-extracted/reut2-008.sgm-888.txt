EPITOPE <EPTO> SETS ANTIBODIES FOR AIDS RESEARCH

Epitope Inc said it has developed a complete panel of monoclonal antibodies to the AIDS virus that can be used for AIDS research.     The company said it will begin marketing the panel immediately.     It said the monoclonals recognize different protein portions of the AIDS viurs and will be used in research for  AIDS diagnosis and therapy.  Reuter &#3;
