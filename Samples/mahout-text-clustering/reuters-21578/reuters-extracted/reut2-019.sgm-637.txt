CORRECTED-FIDELCOR <FICR.O> TO ADD TO LOAN LOSSES

Fidelcor Inc said it will take a one-time 30 mln dlr special provision in the second quarter for possible losses in loans to developing countries.     The corporation said it will be profitable in the quarter despite the action that will reduce quarterly net income by 23 mln dlrs, or 37 cts a share on a fully diluted basis.     For the year-ago second quarter, Fidelcor reported earnings of 24.8 mln dlrs, or 95 cts a share. (Corrects spelling on company name)  Reuter &#3;
