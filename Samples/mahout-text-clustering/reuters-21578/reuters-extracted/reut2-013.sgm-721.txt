NEW YORK COCOA FUTURES EXPECTED TO OPEN LOWER

Cocoa futures are expected to open 10 to 15 dlrs lower following London cocoa prices.     ----     Buffer stock purchases could reach 75,000 tonnes in a matter of weeks without lifting prices significantly, London cocoa traders said....Cocoa Producers Alliance heard address from Cameroon minister urging coordination of sales policies among members, who account for 80 pct of world output....CPA meeting to discuss a backup withholding scheme if the International Cocoa Agreement fails to defend prices.... Norway said it would check imported cocoa beans and most chocolate brands on sale after an outbreak of salmonella poisoning, while Ghana news agency scorned report....Sterling opened at 1.6195/05 dlrs in New York from 1.6175/85 yesterday.
