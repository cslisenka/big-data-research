HYUNDAI MOTOR AMERICA SAYS CAR SALES STRONG

Hyundai Motor America said February sales of its Excel subcompact car totaled 18,656.     Hyundai began selling the car in the U.S. on February 20, 1986. Sales that month totaled 588.     The company said sales in the first 12 months totaled 201,328.  Reuter &#3;
