NATIONAL CITY <NCTY.O> UNIT BEGINS NEW SERVICE

National City Corp's National City Bank unit said it has begun a personal computer-based financial management service for small businesses.     The service, the InTouch Financial Manager, is based on a personal computer system develoed by <Harbinger Comput Services> of Atlanta, and is licensed in Ohio by <Money Station Inc>, an electronic funds transfer network.     National City said the service will allow businesses to use a personal computer and a local telephone call to communicate with their bank to initiate transactions and to receive data anmessages.
