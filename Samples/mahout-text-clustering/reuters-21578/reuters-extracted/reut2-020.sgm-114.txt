PHOENIX AMERICAN <PHXA.O> ENDS SELF-TENDER

Phoenix American Inc said it has terminated its tender offer for up to 600,000 of its common shares at five dlrs each due to the dramatic decline in stock prices.     Phoenix said it is concerned that the current volatility in the financial markets could hurt its financial condition and prospects.  Reuter &#3;
