LOUISIANA-PACIFIC <LPX> TO SELL SAWMILL

Louisiana-Pacific Corp said it plans to sell its sawmill in Lakeview, Oregon and 18,000 acres of timberland to Ostrander Construction Co.     The company said the transaciton shoould be finalized in early April. Terms were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
