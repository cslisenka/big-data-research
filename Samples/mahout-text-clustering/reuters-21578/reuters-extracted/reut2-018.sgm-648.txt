CEDAR FAIR LP <FUN> SETS INITIAL DIVIDEND

Cedar Fair LP said its board declared an initial prorated quarterly dividend of 17 cts per share, payable August 14 to holders of record June 30.     The partnership said it expects distributions for the third and fourth quarters to meet the rate of 26-1/4 cts it had predicted previously.  Reuter &#3;
