BALTEK <BTEK.O> BUYS ECUADORIAN PLANT

Baltek Corp said it has acquired a shrimp packing plant in Ecuador for undisclosed terms.     The company said the acquisition will result in a tripling of its Ecuadorian shrimp sales to about three mln pounds annually by 1988.  Reuter &#3;
