EG AND G <EGG> UNIT WINS GOVERNMENT CONTRACT

EG and G said its EG and G Services unit was awarded a contract to provide facilities operations, maintenance and related services for the U.S. government's chemical decontamination training facility at Fort McClellan, Ala.     Terms were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
