UNILEVER CAPITAL CORP ISSUES NZ DLR EUROBOND

Unilever Capital Corp NV is issuing a 65 mln New Zealand dlr eurobond due July 7, 1989 with an 18-1/4 pct coupon and priced at 101-1/4, bookrunner Hambros Bank Ltd said.     Joint lead managers are EBC Amro Bank Ltd and Hambros.  The bonds, guaranteed by Unilever Plc, will be issued in denominations of 1,000 and 5,000 dlrs and listed in Luxembourg.     Fees comprise 1/2 pct for management and underwriting combined and 7/8 pct for selling.  REUTER &#3;
