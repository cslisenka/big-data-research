KAYPRO <KPRO.O> CUTS PRICES ON COMPUTERS

Kaypro Corp said it has cut prices on its Kaypro 386 Models A and E supermicrocomputers and Extra! Extra! desktop pulbishing system by 500 dlrs.     The Kaypro 386 Model A will retail for 4,495 dlrs, and the Kaypro 386 Models E-40 and E-130 will sell for 5,795 dlrs and 8,095 dlrs, respectively, the company said.     The Kaypro 386, Model E is available with eithe a 40 or 130 MB hard disk, the company said.     In addition, the Kaypro Extra! Extra! will be reduced to 7,995 dlrs, and the Kaypro 16E, a transportable, IBM PC-compatible computer, now retails for 1,595 dlrs, and the dual floppy Kaypro 162E is 1,395 dlrs, Kaypro said.
