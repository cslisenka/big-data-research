CONSOLIDATED STORES <CNS> BUYS WAREHOUSE SPACE

Consolidated Store Corp said it purchased two mln square feet of warehouse space on 146 acres of land adjacent to its present Columbus distribution center of 430,000 square feet on 121 acres.     The company said the building and lands were acquired from White Consolidated Industries Inc, an <Electrolux AB> subsidiary, for 27 mln dlrs through a sale and leaseback.  Reuter &#3;
