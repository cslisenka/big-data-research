GLAXO SEEKS TOKYO LISTING

Glaxo Holdings Plc <GLXO.L> said it has filed an application to obtain a listing of its ordinary shares on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.     It said in a statement it expects to obtain the listing by by the end of the company's financial year on June 30.     No new shares will be issued in connection with the listing on the Tokyo exchange, Glaxo said.     On February 18 Glaxo said it was seeking to list its American Depositary receipts, now traded on the NASDAQ system, on the New York Stock Exchange. It also expected this listing to be in place by June 30.  Reuter &#3;
