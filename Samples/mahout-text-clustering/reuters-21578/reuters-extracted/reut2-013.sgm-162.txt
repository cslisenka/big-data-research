TEXAS AMERICAN <TXA> BANKS TO BE MERGED

Texas American Bancshares Inc said three of its Texas American banks will be merged into Texas American Bank/Galleria.     The corporation said the Spring Branch, Fonderen and Gulfway Texas American banks will become separate banking offices of TAB/Galleria.  Reuter &#3;
