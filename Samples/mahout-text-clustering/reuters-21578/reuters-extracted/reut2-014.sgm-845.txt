FINAL TERMS SET ON ATARI CORP CONVERTIBLE EUROBOND

The coupon on the 75 mln dlr, 15-year, convertible eurobond for Atari Corp has been set at 5-1/4 pct compared with indicated range of five to 5-1/4 pct, lead manager Paine Webber International said.     The conversion price was set at 32-5/8 dlrs, representing a premium of 20.38 pct over yesterday's Atari share close of 27 dlrs.  REUTER &#3;
