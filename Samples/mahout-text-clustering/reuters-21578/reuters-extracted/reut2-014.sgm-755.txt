TRANS-CANADA RESOURCES WINS FILING EXTENSION

<Trans-Canada Resources Ltd> said it received an Alberta court order extending to April 20 from April six the filing of a plan of arrangement with the court under the Companies' Creditors Arrangement Act.     It said the order also extended the stay of legal proceedings against the company to May 20 and provided for shareholders' and creditors' meetings by May 15.  Reuter &#3;
