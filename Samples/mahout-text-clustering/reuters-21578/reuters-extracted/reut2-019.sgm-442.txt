PHILADELPHIA ELECTRIC CO <PE> SELLS SHARES

Philadelphia Electric Co said it completed the sale of 1,500,000 shares of common stock by a continuous offering plan through Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc.     The company said proceeds from the sale, which amount to 32.4 mln, will be used for its construction program.  Reuter &#3;
