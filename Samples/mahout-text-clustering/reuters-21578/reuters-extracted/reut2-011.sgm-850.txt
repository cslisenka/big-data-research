EIB ISSUES 300 MLN DANISH CROWN BOND

The Luxembourg-based European Investment Bank issued a 300 mln Danish crown bullet Eurobond, an official at lead manager Den Danske Bank af 1871 A/S told Reuters.     The seven-year bond pays 11 pct and is being issued at 101 of par, payment date is May 20.     Denominations are 20,000 crowns, the bond is registered in Luxembourg and total fees are 1 7/8 pct.
