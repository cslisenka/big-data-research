MCCARTHY AND STONE GETS 100 MLN STG FACILITY

McCarthy and Stone Plc, a public U.K. Company which owns and operates retirement homes, has signed a 100 mln stg multi-option facility, National Westminster Bank Plc said as arranger.     The facility incorporate a 70 mln stg committed element from a group of international banks.     The facility will provide the group with working capital to meet its planned expansion in the U.K. Over the next three years.  REUTER &#3;
