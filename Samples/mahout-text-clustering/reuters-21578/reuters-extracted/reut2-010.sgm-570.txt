CORRECTED - C.O.M.B <CMCO> TO HAVE OPTIONS ON AMEX

C.O.M.B Inc commenced trading put and call options on the American Stock Exchange, according to the Exchange.     The company's options will trade with a ticker symbol of <COQ> with initial expiration months of April, May, July and October, with position and exercise limits at 5,500 contracts on the same side of the market, according to the Exchange.
