MOSELEY SECURITIES <MOSE.O> CHAIRMAN RESIGNS

Moseley Securities Corp said Howard Berg resigned as chairman and chief executive officer.     It said Frederick S. Moseley III assumed the position of chairman. In other management changes, Omar Kassem was named vice chairman and chief executive officer, and James Wolitarsky, executive vice president and chief financial officer, was appointed president and chief operating officer.     The company also said it named a Management Committee consisting of Wolitarsky and six executive vice presidents.  Reuter &#3;
