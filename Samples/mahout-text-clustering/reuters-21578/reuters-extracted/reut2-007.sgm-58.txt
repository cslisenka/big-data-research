COCA-COLA <KO> IN FILM COLORING VENTURE

Color Systems Technology Inc <CLST> said it has completed formation of a joint venture called Screen Gems Classicolor with Coca-Cola Co to color and distribute feature films and television programs.     Color Systems said it and Coca-Cola will share profits from worldwide television, basic cable, pay television and home video distribution, and Color Systems will also be paid by the venture to convert to color the black and white material involved.     The company said Coca-Cola has initially contributed a number of its Screen Gems television series and Color Systems its library of more than 100 feature films and television series. It said the venture expects to acquire more black and white material for color conversion and will start generating revenues by distributing the combined library initially as-is.  Reuter &#3;
