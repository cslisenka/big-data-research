ATT <T> AND TELERATE <TLR> IN JOINT PACT

American Telephone and Telegraph Co and Telerate Inc said they will form a joint partnership to develop and market electronic transaction services for global financial markets.     The companies said the partnership will provide a system that gives international money traders the ability to manage transactions instantaneously.      Reuter &#3;
