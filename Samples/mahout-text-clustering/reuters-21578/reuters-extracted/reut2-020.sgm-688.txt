QUAKER OATS <OAT> TO RESUME BUYBACK PROGRAM

Quaker Oats Co said it will resume a share repurchase program initially authorized in September 1985.     At the time, a one mln share buyback was approved. Quaker Oats said it bought back about 78 pct of the shares when the program was suspended, with 450,000 shares remaining to be purchased.     As of the Sept 30 first quarter, the company had 79.8 mln shares outstanding.  Reuter &#3;
