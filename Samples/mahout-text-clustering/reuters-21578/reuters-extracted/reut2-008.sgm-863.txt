RESORTS INT'L <RT.A> TO MAKE STATEMENT

A Resorts International Inc spokesman said the company will make a statement later today regarding its class A and class B <RT.B> common stock.     The American Stock Exchange halted trading in the shares this morning because of the pending announcement. The A shares last traded at 58-3/4 and the B shares at 130-1/2, the exchange said.
