WEDGESTONE REALTY <WDG> FILES FOR OFFERING

Wedgestone Realty Investors Trust said it has filed for an offering of 1,250,000 common shares through underwriters led by Ladenburg, Thalmann and Co and Moseley Holding Corp <MOSE>.     It said the offering is expected to be made in mid-April.  Reuter &#3;
