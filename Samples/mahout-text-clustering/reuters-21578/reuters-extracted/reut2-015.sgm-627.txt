K MART <KM> OPENS BANK BRANCHES IN STORES

K Mart Corp said it was opening branch offices of <First Nationwide Bank> in 21 of its stores in Florida.     The company said this brings to 57 the number of bank branch, which are part of the Nationwide Network, an alliance of 40 independent locally managed financial institutions, locations in Florida.
