<COFAB INC> BUYS GULFEX FOR UNDISCLOSED AMOUNT

CoFAB Inc said it acquired <Gulfex Inc>, a Houston-based fabricator of custom high-pressure process vessels for the energy and petrochemical industries.     CoFAB said its group of companies manufacture specialized cooling and lubricating systems for the oil and gas, petrochemical, utility, pulp and paper and marine industries.  Reuter &#3;
