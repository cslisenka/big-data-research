RAYTHEON <RTN> UNIT GETS 337 MLN DLR CONTRACT

Raytheon Co said its Beech Aricraft unit has received a five-year 337 mln dlr incrementally-funded contract from the U.S. government to support C-12 and U-21 type aircraft.     It said the first year of the contract has been funded to a level of 35 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
