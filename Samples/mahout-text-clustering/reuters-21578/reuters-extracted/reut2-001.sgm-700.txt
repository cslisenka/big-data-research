FRANCE SETS 7.5 BILLION FRANC T-BILL TENDER

The Bank of France said it will offer 7.5 billion francs' worth of negotiable Treasury bills at its next public weekly tender on March 9.     The total includes two billion francs of 13-week bills, 2.5 billion francs of two-year bills and three billion francs' worth of five year bills.     No maximum or minimum price has been set.
