SOUTHWEST REALTY <SWL> IN RIGHTS OFFERING

Southwest Realty Ltd said it has filed for a rights offering.     It said for each three shares owned, a shareholder will receive a transferable right to purchase one additional share at 2.50 dlrs.     The company said the offering is expected to be made on June 15.  Reuter &#3;
