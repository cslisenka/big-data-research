BUNDESBANK SETS NEW REPURCHASE TENDER

The Bundesbank set a new tender for a 28-day securities repurchase agreement, offering banks liquidity aid at a fixed bid rate of 3.80 pct, a central bank spokesman said.     Banks must make their bids by 1400 GMT tomorrow and funds allocated will be credited to accounts tomorrow, April 22. Banks must repurchase securities pledged on May 20.  REUTER &#3;
