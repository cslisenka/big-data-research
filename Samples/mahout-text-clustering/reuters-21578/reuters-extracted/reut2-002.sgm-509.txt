TCW CONVERTIBLE SECURITIES <CVT> SOLD STOCK

TCW Convertible Securities Fund Inc said it has sold 20 mln shares of its common stock to a group of underwriters led by Bear Stearns and Co Inc, E.F. Hutton and Co, Advest Inc, Blunt Ellis and Loewi Inc, Piper, Jaffray and Hopwook Inc and Sutro and Co Inc.     The shares were sold by the underwriters at ten dlrs per share in a public offering.     The offering produced net proceeds of 186 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
