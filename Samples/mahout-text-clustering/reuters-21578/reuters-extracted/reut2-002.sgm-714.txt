AMERICAN AIRLINES <AMR> LOAD FACTOR DECLINES

American Airlines Inc, owned by AMR Corp, said its load factor for February dropped to 60.2 pct from 64.0 pct a year ago.     It said revenue passenger miles for the month increased 5.9 pct to 3.66 billion from 3.45 billion a year ago. Available seat miles rose 12.7 pct ot 6.08 billion from 5.40 billion and passenger boardings rose 8.6 pct to 3.5 mln from 3.2 mln a year earlier.  More &#3;
