KNIGHT-RIDDER <KRI> JANUARY REVENUES RISE

Knight-Ridder Inc said its newspaper advertising revenues for the month of January rose 9.1 pct to 105.1 mln dlrs from 96.3 mln dlrs a year earlier.     It said January broadcasting revenues rose 48.8 pct to 6.8 mln dlrs from a year ago, but include the results of three stations absent in the prior year period. Business Information Services revenues rose 5.9 pct to 7.2 mln dlrs, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
