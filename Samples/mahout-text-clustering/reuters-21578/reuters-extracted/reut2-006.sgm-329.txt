TRANS MOUNTAIN ALLOWED 10.6 PCT TOLL RISE

The National Energy Board said Trans Mountain Pipe Line Co Ltd will be allowed to raise its tolls 10.6 pct, effective Jan 1, 1987.     Last September, the board approved an average rate of return on equity of 14 pct effective Jan 1, 1987. This rate of return is unchanged by today's decision.         Reuter &#3;
