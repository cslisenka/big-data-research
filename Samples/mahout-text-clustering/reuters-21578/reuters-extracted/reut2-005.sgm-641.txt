MORGAN <MS> LICENSES OVERSEAS INDEX FOR TRADING

Morgan Stanley Group Inc said its Capital International Europe, Australia and Far East, or EAFE, stock index will be a used as a basis for trading futures and listed options.     The company said it granted licenses to the Chicago Mercantile Exchange and the Chicago Board of Options for the EAFE index.     The index includes 900 companies representing 16 national stock markets in Europe, Australia and the Far East. It is used as a benchmark of international stock market activity, the company said.
