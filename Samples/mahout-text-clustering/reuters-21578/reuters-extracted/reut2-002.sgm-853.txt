LIPOSOME CO <LIPO> AND UNIT TO SPONSOR SEMINAR

The Liposome Co Inc said it and its wholly-owned subsidiary, Liposome Japan Co Ltd, will sponsor a major seminar on liposome at Tokyo University on March  12, 1987.     Liposomes are microscopic man-made spheres, composed of lipids, that can be engineered to entrap drugs or other biologically active molecules within the lipid membranes of the spheres or in the aqueous space between the membranes, the company explained.     Leading academic, research and government institutions in Japan are expected to attend, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
