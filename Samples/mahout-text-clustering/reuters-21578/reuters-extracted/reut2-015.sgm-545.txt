SEARS, ROEBUCK <S> MARCH RETAIL SALES UP

Sears, Roebuck and Co said retail sales of its Merchandise Group in March rose 4.2 pct to 2.62 billion dlrs from 2.51 billion dlrs a year ago.     For the nine weeks ended April Four, Sears Merchandise Group sales rose 4.5 pct to 4.46 billion dlrs from 4.27 billion dlrs in the same 1986 period.  Reuter &#3;
