ITT <ITT>  UNIT FILES FOR PREFERRED STOCK SALE

The Hartford Fire Insurance Co, a unit of ITT Corp, filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for the shelf sale of 210 mln dlrs in class c and d preferred stock.     It said proceeds would be used to redeem class a stock.     The First Boston Corp and Merrill Lynch Capital Markets were named underwriters.  Reuter &#3;
