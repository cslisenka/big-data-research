AUXTON COMPUTER <AUXT> IN ARRANGEMENTS

Auxton Computer Enterprises Inc said it has entered into an agreement to provide cellular business office services to a major non-wireline cellular carrier in 12 major cellular markets throughout the U.S.     It said it has also reached agreement to determine the process needed to install its SOCRATES and LUCAS software packages for a national communications carrier outside the U.S.     In addition it said it has agreed to start evaluating the feasibility of installing its SOCRATES software to provide a major national satellite paging company with billing services to subscribers traveling beyond the signal capacities of their home systems.
