EIB PLANS 300 MLN GUILDER BOND ISSUE DUE 1995

The European Investment Bank is planning a 300 mln guilder 6.25 pct bullet bond due 1995, lead manager Amsterdam-Rotterdam Bank NV said.     The issue will be priced April 7 and subscriptions close April 9. The payment date is May 14 and the coupon date May 15, Amro Bank said.  REUTER &#3;
