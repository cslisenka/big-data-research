EASTMAN KODAK <EK> SETS CHEMICAL VENTURE

<Wilson and George Meyer and Co> and the Eastman Chemicals division of Eastman Kodak Co said they formed 50-50 joint venture called WGM/Eastman Inc.     According to the agreement, Eastman will buy Meyer and Co's interest in the unit by the end of 1991.     The unit will supply Eastman Kodak chemicals to companies in the western region of the U.S., they said.   Reuter &#3;
