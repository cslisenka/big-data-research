MORGAN, OLMSTEAD, KENNEDY <MOKG> NAMES PRES

Morgan, Olmstead, Kennedy and Gardner Capital Corp said Michael Fusco has been named president of its securities brokerage and investment banking firm, Morgan, Olmstead, Kennedy and Gardner Inc.     Fusco formerly was executive vice president of PaineWebber Inc.     He replaces G. Bryan Herrmann, who will retain his title of president of the parent group, and assume the title of vice chairman of the parent, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
