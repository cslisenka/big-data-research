GUINNESS PLC NON-EXEC CHAIRMAN TO REMAIN

Sir Norman Macfarlane, non-executive chairman of <Guinness PLC>, has agreed to remain in his post for two more years, the company said.     The company said its board asked Macfarlane to continue in his capacity. Macfarlane has been in his post since January.  Reuter &#3;
