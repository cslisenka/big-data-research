CONSECO <CNSC.O> EMERGES FROM BANKRUPTCY

Conseco Industries Ltd said an order of confirmation of its reorganization plan has been signed in its Chapter 11 bankruptcy proceedings, terminating its status as a debtor in possession.     Conseco said under the plan, general creditors and debenture holders will receive 27 pct of their claims over a two-year period and president Charles J. Trainor will pay 250,000 dlrs for 2,500,000 new shares.     The company said debenture holders will have 30 days to convert their debentures into common shares.     It said it has arranged for two mln dlrs of new bank financing.  Reuter &#3;
