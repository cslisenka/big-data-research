BOWER CHANGES NAME, RESTRUCTURES LOAN

<Bower industries Inc> said it changed its name to Metalclad Corp and has restructured a loan agreement with its bank.     Under the pact, a 3.5-mln-dlr revolving credit line will be converted to a 1.8-mln-dlr six year note and a 1.7-mln-dlr revolving line of credit.     The company said it is also restructuring a 4,011,000-dlr debt to another creditor, whereby two mln dlrs will be forgiven, 1,950,000 dlrs will be converted into two long-term notes due April, 1989 and 61,000 dlrs has been included in a line of credit.
