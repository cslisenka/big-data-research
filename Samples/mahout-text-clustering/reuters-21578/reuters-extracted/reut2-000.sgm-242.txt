 1-MAR-1987 05:34:32.47

IRAN CLAIMS NEW VICTORIES NEAR BASRA

Iran said its forces had captured one of Iraq's strongest fortifications east of Basra on the Gulf War southern front in a major battle overnight.     The Iranian National News Agency, received here, said Iranian forces smashed four Iraqi brigades, killed or wounded 1,500 Iraqi soldiers and destroyed 45 enemy tanks and personnel carriers.     IRNA said the Iranian troops seized one of the strongest Iraqi fortifications and breached Iraqi defence lines southwest of Fish Lake, 10 kilometres (six miles) east of Iraq's second largest city of Basra.
