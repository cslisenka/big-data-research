TWO RESIGN FROM SCOTT INSTRUMENTS <SCTI> BOARD

Scott Instruments Inc said two members of its board resigned.     The company did not give a reason for the departure of the directors, David M. Jacobs and Frederick Brodsky.     Scott said it does not plan to fill the vacancies left by their resignations.  Reuter &#3;
