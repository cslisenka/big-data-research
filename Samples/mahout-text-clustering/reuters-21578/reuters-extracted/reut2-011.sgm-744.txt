GULF STATES <GSU> SUBMITS FINANCING PLAN

Gulf States Utilities Co said it filed a 266 mln dlr financing plan with Texas regulators and asked for quick approval so that it can implement a previously approved 39.9 mln dlr interim rate increase.     In the filing with the Texas Public Utilities Commission, Gulf States said it listed six sources of funding that it believes meet the commission's requirement that it obtain 250 mln dlrs in credit and other financing before it can institute the interim rate hike.
