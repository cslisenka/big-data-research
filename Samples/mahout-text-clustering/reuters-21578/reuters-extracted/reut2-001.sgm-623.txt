MORRISON PETROLEUMS HAS GAIN FROM PROPERTY SALE

<Morrison Petroleums Ltd> said it will have a net gain after taxes of 1,580,000 dlrs or 29 cts a share from the 2,750,000 dlr sale of 200 acres of real estate in Oshawa, Ontario.     Proceeds increased working capital to 12.0 mln dlrs and were invested in short term deposits, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
