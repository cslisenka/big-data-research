LIBERTY FINANCIAL <LFG> PRESIDENT TO RESIGN

Harold H. Kline, 48, will resign on May one as president of Liberty Financial Group Inc and its Liberty Savings Bank unit to pursue other business opportunties, the company said.     The company said Kline's post will be filled by Charles G. Cheleden, chairman and chief executive officer.     Kline will continue to serve on the boards of Liberty Financial and Liberty Savings, the company added.  Reuter &#3;
