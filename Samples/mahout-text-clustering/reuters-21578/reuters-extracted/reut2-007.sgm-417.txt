CHRYSLER <C> TO RUN FOUR PLANTS ON OVERTIME

Chrysler Corp said it scheduled six of its seven U.S. car and truck assembly plants to operate the week of March 23, with four on overtime.     The company also said it scheduled two of its U.S. assembly plans to operate Saturday, March 21.     As previously reported, the Belvidere (Ill.) assembly plant is not scheduled to operate due to model changeover.  Reuter &#3;
