SEVERE DROUGHT AFFECTS EAST, NORTHEAST THAILAND

A severe drought has affected 170,560 hectares of farmlands in six eastern and northeastern provinces, Interior Permanent Secretary Pisarn Mulasartsathorn said.     He told reporters some 178,790 farming families in 45 districts in Chaiyaphum, Korat, Phrae, Chainat, Rayong and Trat provinces were seeking relief water from the government.     The official did not say what crops have been damaged.     Senior Interior Ministry officials said this week the worse than average drought this year has affected 14 provinces and may last until early May.  REUTER &#3;
