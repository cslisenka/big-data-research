BALLY <BLY> FILES SHELF FOR PREFERRED STOCK

Bally Manufacturing Corp said it filed a shelf registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a secondary public offering of two mln shares of Series D convertible exchangeable preferred stock.     The stock is exchangeable at Bally's option into the company's convertible debentures due February one 2007 and is convertible at the holder's option into shares of Bally common. The preferred stock carry a liquidation value of 50 dlrs a share.     All of the shares are being offered by holders of Bally who bought the preferred stock in a private placement in February 1987. Bally will not receive any proceeds of the offering, it said.  Reuter &#3;
