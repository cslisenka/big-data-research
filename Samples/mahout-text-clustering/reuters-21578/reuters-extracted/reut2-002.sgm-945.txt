BROKER'S CHOICE TO BUY RUBBERTECH

Broker's Choice Capital Inc said it signed a letter of intent to buy Rubbertech Inc.     If approved by shareholders, Broker's Choice will issue about 71 mln shares of authorized, but unissued shares of restricted common stock to Rubbertech Inc.  Reuter &#3;
