OAKWOOD HOMES <OH> BEGINS STOCK REPURCHASE

Oakwood Homes Corp said it was beginning a stock repurchase program under which it may purchase up to 400,000 shares, or about seven pct, of its common stock.     Oakwood said purchase will be made from time to time as conditions permit on the open market and in privately negotiated transactions.  Reuter &#3;
