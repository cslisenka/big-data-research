ELXSI <ELXSF> WINS MCDONNELL DOUGLAS <MD> ORDER

Elxsi Ltd said it sold three computer systems worth four mln dlrs to Douglas Aircraft Co, a unit of McDonnell Douglas Corp.     The company said the systems will be used in the C-17 military transport program. One was shipped last month and two others will be shipped in June, it said.  Reuter &#3;
