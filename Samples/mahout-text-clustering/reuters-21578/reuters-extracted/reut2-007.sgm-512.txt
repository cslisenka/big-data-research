THE GAP INC <GPS> NAMES PRESIDENT

The Gap Inc said it named Millard Drexler as its president.     Drexler, 42, had been executive vice preisdent for merchandising and president of the Gap Stores Division.     Previously, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Donald Fisher also held the position of president as well.     Drexler retains his position as president and chief executive officer of the Gap Stores Division.  Reuter &#3;
