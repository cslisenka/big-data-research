FIRST FEDERAL <FARK> BUYS BACK CAPITAL NOTES

First Federal Savings of Arkansas FA said it bought back 4.75 mln dlrs of its 15-3/8 pct subordinated capital notes.     There are now 14.25 mln dlrs of these notes outstanding, compared to an original issue of 25 mln dlrs sold in September 1985, First Federal said.            Reuter &#3;
