CARTER HAWLEY HALE <CHH> FEBRUARY SALES RISE

Carter Hawley Hale Stores Inc said sales for the four weeks ended February 28 were up 7.6 pct to 240.8 mln dlrs from 223.8 mln dlrs a year before, with same-store sales up 5.6 pct.     Sales for the prior year exclude John Wanamaker stores, sold at the end of 1986.  Reuter &#3;
