AMAX <AMX> ACCEPTS OFFER FOR AUSTRALIAN INTEREST

AMAX Inc said it has accepted offers to buy its share and option interests in <Australian Consolidated Minerals Ltd>.     AMAX said the sale is part of its ongoing program to increase shareholder value.     The company did not identify who made the offer, but said the interests involved include 35.2 mln Australian Consolidated shares to be sold for 6.32 Australian dlrs per share and 5.4 mln options to be sold for 4.82 Australian dlrs per option.
