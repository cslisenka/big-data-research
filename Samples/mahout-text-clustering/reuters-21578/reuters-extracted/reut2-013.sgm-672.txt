SIOUX CITY CATTLE SEEN UP 0.50-1.00 DLR-PRIVATE

Cattle prices expected 0.50 to 1.00 dlr higher on estimated receipts of 900 head, private sources said. Steer top seen at 67.00 to 67.50 and Heifers at 66.00 dlrs.     Sources expect support from good buyer demand influenced by higher trending wholesale carcass beef.  Reuter &#3;
