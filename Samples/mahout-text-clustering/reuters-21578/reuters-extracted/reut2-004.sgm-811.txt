PACKAGING SYSTEMS <PAKS> TO BUY LABEL FIRM

Packaging Systems Corp said it agreed to acquire <Walter-Richter Labels Inc>, a privately held maker of woven labels based in Paterson, N.J.     Terms of the acquisition, which is expected to be completed within 60 days, were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
