ARVIN <ARV> RAISES NUMBER OF AUTHORIZED SHARES

Arvin Industries Inc said its shareholders at the annual meeting voted to increase authorized common shares to 50 mln from 30 mln.     The company told shareholders that the offering of 1.7 mln common shares in January raised 50 mln dlrs, and the 500 shares of variable rated preferred stock offered in February also raised 50 mln dlrs. The company said it also placed 150 mln dlrs in long-term debt in March.  Reuter &#3;
