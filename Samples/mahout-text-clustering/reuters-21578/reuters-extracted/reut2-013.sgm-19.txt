COPLEY PROPERTIES <COP> TO INVEST IN JOINT PACT

Copley Properties Inc said the company will invest 9,500,000 dlrs in a joint venture to acquire and develop 33.4 acres of industrial land in Hayward, Calif.     Copley said it will own 60 pct of the project and be entitled to a 10 pct preferential return on its investment plus 60 pct of all excess cash from operations, refinancings and other capital transactions.
