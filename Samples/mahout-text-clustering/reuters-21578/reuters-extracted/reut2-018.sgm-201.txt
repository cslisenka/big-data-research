DESIGNS INC <DESI.O> INITIAL OFFERING UNDERWAY

Lead underwriter Kidder, Peabody and Co Inc sand an initial public offering of two mln common shares of Designs Inc is underway at 13 dlrs per share.     The company is selling 1,125,000 shares and shareholders the rest, and underwriters have been granted an option to buy up to 300,000 more shares to cover overallotments.  Reuter &#3;
