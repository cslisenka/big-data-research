THERMWOOD <THM> EXTENDS DATE ON WARRANTS

Thermwood Corp said it is extending the expiration date of its 620,000 common stock warrants through Dec 31, 1987.     It said the warrants to purchase one share of common stock, which have an exercise price of 3.00 dlrs, were to expire April 11.     The company noted that its stock price has more than doubled since late last year and "is now much closer to the exercise price of the warrants."     Thermwood is trading at 1-7/8, off 1/8.
