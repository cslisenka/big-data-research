NISSAN STARTS TO MARKET REMODELLED 4WD VEHICLES

Nissan Motor Co Ltd <NSAN.T> said it has started to market a remodelled version of its four wheel drive (4WD) Safari vehicle in Japan.     Nissan said in a statement it hopes to sell 250 vehicles a month in Japan.     It also plans soon to start exporting 3,400 vehicles a month to the Australian, Middle East and Asian markets under the name Patrol, a spokesman said.     It will sell the vehicle in Europe sometime in the future, with shipments from <Motor Iberica S.A.>, its Spanish unit. The volume for Europe will be set later.  REUTER &#3;
