NYNEX <NYN> UNIT WINS ARMY CONTRACT

Nynex Business Information Systems Co said it won a contract valued at nine mln dlrs from the U.S. Army Corp of Engineers to provide an advanced communications system for a facility in upstate New York.     The company, a unit of Nynex Corp, said the contract was for initial work on the project at the new Fort Drum Light Infantry Post near Watertown, about 65 miles north of Syracuse.  Reuter &#3;
