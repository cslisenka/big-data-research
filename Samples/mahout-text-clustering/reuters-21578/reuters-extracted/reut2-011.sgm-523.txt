DIGITAL <DEC> SETTLES SUITS WITH COMPUTER FIRM

Digital Equipment Corp and Systems Industries Inc <SYSM> said they settled all pending litigation between the two companies.     They said Digital's 1980 patent infringement suit against Systems Industries was dismissed, as was Systems Industries' 1986 antitrust suit against Digital.     The companies said Systems Industries will be licensed to manufacture computer mass storage devices that use Digital technology.     They also said the company will pay Digital royalties in settlement of Digital's patent infringement claims.  Reuter &#3;
