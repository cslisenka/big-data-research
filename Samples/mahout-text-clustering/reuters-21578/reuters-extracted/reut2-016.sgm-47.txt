REAGAN HITS HOUSE-PASSED BUDGET

President Reagan criticized the House-passed budget by saying the Democratic budget plan was just business as usual.     The budget cut defense by eight billion dlrs and called for 18 billion dlrs in new taxes.     He said the defense cut potentially theratened national security and was asking the American taxpayers to pay for the Democrats' excesses.  Reuter &#3;
