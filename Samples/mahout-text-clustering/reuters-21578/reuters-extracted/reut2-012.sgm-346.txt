PEOPLES TELEPHONE SIGNS PACT WITH SOUTHLAND <SLC>

Peoples Telephone Co, the third largest private pay phone firm in the U.S., said it signed an exclusive five-year, 25 mln dlr contract with the Southland Corp.     Under the contract, Peoples said it will provide telephone service to Southland's 7-Eleven Stores in Florida, and install and maintain telephone circuitry for 7-Eleven's MovieQuick and computerized lottery systems.
