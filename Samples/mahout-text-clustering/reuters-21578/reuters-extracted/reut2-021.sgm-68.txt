<THERMASCAN INC> RECEIVES FDA APPROVAL ON DRUG

Thermascan Inc said the U.S. Food and Drug Administration has approved clinical trials of a new, advanced AIDS confirmation test developed by the company.     Thermascan said that about 10,000 trials will be taken on the the new test, called Fluorognost in the next three months through blood banks, hospitals and health centers as well as by individual physicians.     The trials will be conducted in New York at the Beth Israel Medical Centre, the Sacremento Medical foundation Blood Center, the Karolinska Institute, Stockholm, and the Institute of Hygiene at the University of Innspruck.
