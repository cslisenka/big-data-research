ARMCO <AS> CHANGES EUROPEAN MARKETING UNIT

Armco Inc said its former European Steel Mill Merchandising department has become a unit of the parent's specialty steels division based in Butler.     The unit, newly named Specialty Steels-Europe is based in Cologne, West Germany. It will market and sell in Europe the division's U.S.-made products and specialty products made in Europe.
