BROWNING-FERRIS <BFI>WASTE DISPOSAL SITE CLEARED

Browning-Ferris Industries Inc said the Colorado Department of Health and the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency have awarded the company permits to build and operate a hazardous waste disposal site near Last Chance, Colo.     The company said construction will start this year and take about 12 months, costing about 14 to 16 mln dlrs.  It said it has already spent 10 mln dlrs on development costs.     The site will not accept materials that react rapidly with air or water, explosives, shock sensitive materials or radioactive wastes, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
