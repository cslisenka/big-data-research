NORTHROP <NOC>/EATON <ETN> IN HELICOPTER PROGRAM

Northrop Corp said it and Eaton Corp have been selected by the McDonnell Douglas Corp/Textron Inc team to codevelop the aircraft survivability equipment for the U.S. Army's light helicopter experimental program.     The Army is expected to pick contractors to produce the helicopters in 1992.  Reuter &#3;
