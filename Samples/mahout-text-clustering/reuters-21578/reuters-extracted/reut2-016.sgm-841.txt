CHRYSLER <C> PAYS 77 MLN DLRS IN BONUSES

Chrysler Corp said its board made cash bonus awards of 77 mln dlrs to 1,914 executives in 1986 based on 1985 earnings, including a 975,000 dlr bonus that brought chairman Lee Iacocca's cash compensation to 1.7 mln dlrs.     The number three U.S. automaker also disclosed in its shareholder proxy statement that Iacocca exercised stock options last year that netted him nearly 9.6 mln dlrs and received 337,500 shares of Chrysler stock last November three that at current market prices would be worth more than 18 mln dlrs.
