GM <GM> MID-MARCH CAR SALES OFF 15.5 PCT

General Motors Corp said car sales for March 11 through 20 were off 15.5 pct to 109,174 from 129,242 a year earlier.     The company said truck sales were off 8.8 pct to 45,015 from 49,365 a year before.     For the year to date, GM said car sales were off 25.1 pct to 738,262 from 986,211 a year before and truck sales were off 9.6 pct to 292,387 from 323,401 a year before.  Reuter &#3;
