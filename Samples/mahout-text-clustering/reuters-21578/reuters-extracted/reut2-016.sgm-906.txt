WHITE HOUSE LISTS TARIFFS ON JAPANESE GOODS

The White House issued a list of Japanese exports to covered by the 100 pct tariffs imposed by President Reagan.     - Automatic data processing machines (1986 imports worth 180 mln dlrs), including certain desk and lap models with microprocessor-based calculating mechanism capable of handling words of at least 16-bits off the microprocessor;     - Complete color television sets, with 18, 19 or 20 inch screens (1986 imports 90 mln dlrs);     - Power tools, including certain drills, percussion hammers, sanders, polishers, grinders.
