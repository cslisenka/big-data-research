BCI HOLDINGS UNIT PRESIDENT NAMED

BCI Holdings Corp said it named Stephen Rowley president of Day-Timers Inc, a subsidiary of the company's Beatrice Consumer Durables, effective immediately.     It said he replaces Robert Dorney, founder and former president, who will remain as senior vice president, new product development of the company, which produces time management systems.
