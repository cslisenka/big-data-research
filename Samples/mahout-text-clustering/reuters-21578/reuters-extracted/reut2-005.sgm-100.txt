HYTEK MICROSYSTEMS <HTEK> PRESIDENT RESIGNS

Hytek Microsystems Inc said its board accepted the resignation of President James Phalan at a special meeting held today.     Phalan, who had been president since the company was founded in 1974, resigned for personal reasons, Hytek said.     It also said Thomas Bay, Hytek's vice president for marketing, was appointed to the additional role of president and chief executive.  Reuter &#3;
