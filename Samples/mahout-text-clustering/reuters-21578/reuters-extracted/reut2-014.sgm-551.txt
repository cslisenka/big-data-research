HOG/CORN RATIO UP IN WEEK - USDA

The hog/corn ratio for the week ended April 4 was 32.7 versus 32.1 the previous week and 17.1 in the corresponding week a year ago, the U.S. Agriculture Department said.     The steer/corn ratio in the same week was 42.2, versus 41.2 the previous week and 22.9 in the corresponding week a year ago.     The ratio represents the number of bushels of corn at Omaha that can be bought for 100 lbs of hog or steer.  Reuter &#3;
