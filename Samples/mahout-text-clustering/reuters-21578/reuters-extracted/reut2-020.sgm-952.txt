PAR PHARMACEUTICAL <PRX> GETS APPROVAL FOR DRUG

Par Pharmaceutical Inc said it received approval from the Food and Drug Administration on two applications to manufacture and market the generic drug Leucovorin Calcium in tablet form.     Par said it would begin to market the drug in dosage strengths of five mg and 25 mg.     Leucovorin calcium is the generic name for Wellcovorin, a product of <Burroughs Wellcome Co> used as an adjunct to cancer therapy, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
