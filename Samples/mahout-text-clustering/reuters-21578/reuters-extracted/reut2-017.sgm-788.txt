PEPSICO <PEP> CHICKEN UNIT COMMITS 40 MLN DLRS

Pepsico Inc's Kentucky Fried Chicken said it has committed 40 mln dlrs over the next 90 days to introduce a major new product category.     The company said it is introducing Chicken Littles, a chicken patty sandwich served on a soft dinner roll, which will sell for 39 cts.  Reuter &#3;
