CHILDREN'S DISCOVERY <CDCRA> GETS NEW CHAIRMAN

Children's Discovery Centers of America Inc said Richard Niglio has been appointed chairman and chief executive officer effective April 15.     Niglio, 44, is currently president and chief executive officer of Victoria Station Inc, a restaurant chain based in San Francisco.     The company said Niglio replaces James DeSanctis.  Reuter &#3;
