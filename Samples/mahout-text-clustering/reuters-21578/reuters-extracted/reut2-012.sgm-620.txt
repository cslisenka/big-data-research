BETHLEHEM STEEL <BS> RAISES H-PILE PRICES

Bethlehem Steel Corp said effective with shipments of May 3 transaction prices for all H-piles will be increased by 30 dlrs per short ton.     The company said a new base price, coupled with revised size extras, will result in a published price of 446 dlrs per ton HP14 and 420 dlrs for all other H-pile sizes.  Reuter &#3;
