FORD TO INVEST 23 MLN STG IN U.K. ENGINE PLANT

Ford Motor Co <F> is to invest 23 mln stg in high technology equipment at its Bridgend engine plant in South Wales, the Press Association said.     The move includes a new computerised engine test facility which the company says will make the factory one of the most efficient of its kind in the world.     Bridgend currently supplies Escort, Orion and some Fiesta engines to Ford plants in Britain, West Germany and Spain.  Reuter &#3;
