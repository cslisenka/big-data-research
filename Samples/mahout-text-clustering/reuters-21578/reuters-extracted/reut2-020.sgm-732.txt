CERPROBE CORP <CRPB.O> PRESIDENT TO RESIGN IN EARLY 1988

CerProbe Corp said its chairman, John Tarzell, will give up the positions of president and chief executive in early 1988 to concentrate on long-range planning for the company in the semiconductor testing field.     CerProbe said a successor to Tarzell has not been selected.             Reuter &#3;
