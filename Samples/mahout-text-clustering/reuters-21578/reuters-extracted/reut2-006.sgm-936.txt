SAYAD TO BE BRAZIL'S AMBASSADOR AT EC

Joao Sayad, who resigned yesterday from his post as Planning Minister, accepted President Jose Sarney's invitation to fill the vacant post as Brazil's ambassador at the European Community (EC), a Presidential spokesman said.     The spokesman said Sayad refused Sarney's request to remain as Planning Minister for another 30 days.  Reuter &#3;
