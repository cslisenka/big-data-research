CAROLINA POWER <CPL> TO REDEEM PREFERRED STOCK

Carolina Power and Light Co said it will redeem on April 22 all two mln shares of its 2.675 dlr preferred stock, series A.     The company said it will pay 25.75 dlrs a share plus accrued dividends of 15.6 cts.     The redemption, Carolina Power said, will substantially reduce its operating costs.  Reuter &#3;
