S/P AFFIRMS RATINGS ON SECURITY PACIFIC <SPC>

Standard and Poor's Corp said it affirmed Security Pacific Corp's 4.5 billion dlrs of debt.     S and P said that although the bank's decision today to increase its reserve against developing country debt by 500 mln dlrs will result in a loss in the second quarter, the firm still expects to report a profit for the year.     Affirmed were Security Pacific's AA senior debt, AA-minus subordinated debt and preferred stock and A-1-plus commercial paper. Also, uninsured certificates of deposit of Security Pacific National Bank and Arizona Bank of Phoenix were affirmed at AA/A-1-plus.  Reuter &#3;
