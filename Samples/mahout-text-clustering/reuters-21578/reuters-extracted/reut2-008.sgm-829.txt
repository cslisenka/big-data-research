GNB JOINS IN LEVERAGED BUYOUT OF FRENCH UNIT

<GNB Inc> said it joined with the management of the French company Compagnie Francaise d'Electro-Chimie to purchase the company for an undisclosed amount.     The French company, which produces lead-acid batteries, had sales in 1986 of 75 mln dlrs, GNB said.  Reuter &#3;
