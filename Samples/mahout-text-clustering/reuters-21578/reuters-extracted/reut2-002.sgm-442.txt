EDISON BROTHERS STORES <EBS> FEBRUARY SALES UP

Edison Brothers Stores Inc said its February sales rose 5.6 pct to 56.7 mln dlrs from 53.7 mln dlrs a year ago.     For the eight weeks ended February 28, it said sales increased to 109.4 mln dlrs from 109.3 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
