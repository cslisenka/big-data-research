FIRSTCORP <FCR> INCREASES REPURCHASE PROGRAM

Firstcorp Inc said its board has increased the maximum number of Class A shares in its repurchase program to 250,000 from 200,000 shares.     The program was first adopted in June 1986 and provided for a maximum of 100,000 shares to be repurchased through June 1987.     In June 1986, the company increased the shares to 200,000 and extended the period to end September 1987.  Reuter &#3;
