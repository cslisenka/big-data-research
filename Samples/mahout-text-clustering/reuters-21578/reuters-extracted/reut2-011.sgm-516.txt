TCW CONVERTIBLE SECURITIES FUND SELLS SHARES

<TCW Convertible Securities Fund Inc> said it sold one mln common shares to a group of underwriters to cover over-allotments following a March five public offering of 10 mln shares at 10 dlrs each.     The underwriting group is led by led by Bear Stearns and Co Inc, E.F. Hutton and Co Inc, Advest Inc, Blunt Ellus and Loewi Inc, Piper Jaffray and Hopwood Inc and Sutro and Co Inc, the company said.     It said total proceeds to the fund from the 21 mln shares was195.3 mn dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
