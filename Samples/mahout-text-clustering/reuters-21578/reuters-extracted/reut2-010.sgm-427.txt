MORGAN GUARANTY JOINS DEALERS FOR BSAF PROGRAM

Morgan Guaranty Ltd said it has been added to the group of dealers for the euro-commercial paper program for BASF Corp that was launched last year.     The program is for an unspecified amount and is guaranteed by the parent BASF Ag.     The other dealers are Union Bank of Switzerland and Morgan Stanley International.  REUTER &#3;
