EASCO OFFERS INITIAL SALE OF SECURITIES

Easco Hand Tools Inc of Hunt Valley, Md., filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for the sale of 67,275,000 dlrs in common stock, its first public stock offering.     It said proceeds would be used to reduce indebtedness.     PaineWebber Inc was named underwriter.  Reuter &#3;
