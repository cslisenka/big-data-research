CLARK EQUIPMENT <CKL> STAKE ACQUIRED

Clark Equipment Co said it was informed by Arthur M. Goldberg acting on behalf of a group of investors that the group had accumulated 1,262,200 shares, or about 6.7 pct of Clark's outstanding common stock.     It said Goldberg recently approached Clark to repurchase the shares. However, negotiations for the block repurchase were unsuccessful and have been terminated.  Reuter &#3;
