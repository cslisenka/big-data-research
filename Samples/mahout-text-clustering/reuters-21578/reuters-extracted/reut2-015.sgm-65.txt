CMS <ACMS> UNVEILS FIRST IBM SYSTEM/2 ADD ONS

CMS Enhancements Inc said it has introduced the first add-on mass storage products for the new International Business Machines Corp <IBM> IBM Personal System/2 Model 30 personal computers.     CMS said the products are available immediately and include hard disk drives, tape backup subsystems and a hard disk drive on an expansion card.     It added it plans to introduce more products for higher- end models of the IBM personal computers within the next two months.     Prices for the new products range from 795 dlrs for a tape backup subsystem, to 5,695 dlrs for a mass storage subsystem.  Reuter &#3;
