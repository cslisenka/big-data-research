CENTRONICS <CEN> NAMES SALOMON <SB> AS ADVISOR

Centronics Corp said it has named Salomon Inc financial advisor to help it identify acquisition candidates.     Centronics sold its computer business in February and substantially all of its assets now consist of cash.  It said it intends to acquire at least one business.  Reuter &#3;
