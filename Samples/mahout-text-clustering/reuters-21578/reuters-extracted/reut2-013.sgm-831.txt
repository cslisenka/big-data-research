LIFFE VOLUMES HIGHER IN MARCH

Turnover on the London International Financial Futures Exchange (LIFFE) rose to 1.16 mln contracts in March, up from 517,746 a year earlier, figures issued by the International Commodities Clearing House (ICCH) show.     Turnover for the whole of the first quarter was 2.87 mln contracts against 1.52 mln in the same period last year.     The biggest increases in March were seen for gilt options, long gilt futures and short sterling, the ICCH figures show.
