SEA GOLD OIL ANNOUNCES PRIVATE PLACEMENT

<Sea Gold Oil Corp>  said it has agreed to a private-placement financing of 400,000 common shares of its unissued capital stock with Gulf International Minerals Ltd <GIM.V> at 50 cts per share to raise 200,000 dlrs Canadian.     The company said one of the conditions of the placement was the granting of a first right of refusal to Gulf in the event that the company should choose to enter into a joint-venture agreement on any of its mineral claims currently held in the Skyline area.         If Gulf should sell any of the 400,000 shares, the first right of refusal will be null and void, the company said.     Proceeds will be used for the continued exploration and development of the Sea Gold's mining properties.  Reuter &#3;
