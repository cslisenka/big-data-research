EMERSON <EMR> TO ISSUE NEW ZEALAND NOTES

Emerson Electric Co said it will issue about 56 mln dlrs (U.S.) 18.55 pct notes due 1989.     It said interest and principal are payable in either New Zealand or U.S. dollars.     The notes will be offered through an underwriting syndicate managed by Prudential-Bache Capital Funding at an offering price of 100 pct. Proceeds will be used to retire commercial paper and for general corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
