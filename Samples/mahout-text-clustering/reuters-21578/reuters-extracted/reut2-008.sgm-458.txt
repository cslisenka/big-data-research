TRINOVA <TNV> SAYS MOST PREFERRED CONVERTED

TRINOVA Corp said about 813,000 shares or 95 pct of its 855,255 Series A 4.75 dlr cumulative convertible preferred shares have been converted to common stock in response to its call for redemption of the issue at 100 dlrs per share.     Each preferred share was convertible into 2.25 common shares.  Reuter &#3;
