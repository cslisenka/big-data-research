BP SHARES SLIDE ON U.K. GOVERNMENT SALE NEWS

British Petroleum Co Plc <BP.L> shares fell sharply in early trading, in response to last night's news that the U.K. Government will sell its remaining 31.7 pct BP holding during the financial year starting next month, dealers said.     By about 0840 GMT, BP shares were down to 802p, 26p below last night's close of 828p and 14p lower than the opening quote. The long-rumoured sale was announced by the government at the end of the second day of the budget debate in Parliament.     At last night's closing price, unofficial estimates put the value of the government shares in BP at around 4.8 billion stg.  REUTER &#3;
