FRANKLIN RESOURCES <BEN> FORMS THREE FUNDS

Franklin Resources Inc said it formed three mutual funds that free Pennsylvania residents of taxes levied by the state's counties on stocks, bonds and mutual fund shares.     The funds include the Franklin Pennsylvania Investors U.S. Government Securities Fund, which invests in Ginnie Mae securities, and the Franklin Pennsylvania Investors High Income Fund, investing in high-yield corporate bonds. Both funds pay monthly dividends.     The Franklin Pennsylvania Investors Equity Fund invests in stocks of U.S. companies and pays semi-annual dividends.   Reuter &#3;
