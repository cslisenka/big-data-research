BLUEFIELD SUPPLY <BFLD> IN LIQUIDATING PAYOUT

Bluefield Supply Co said its board declared its second liquidating dividend of 1.71 dlrs per share, payable March 16 to shareholders of record March 13.     The company paid an initial liquidating dividend of 15.75 dlrs per share on January Eight.  Reuter &#3;
