NIPPON SHINPAN TO ISSUE CREDIT CARD WITH MASTERCARD

Nippon Shinpan Co Ltd <NSHT.T> has agreed with <Mastercard International> to issue a new credit card called Nippon Shinpan-Master Joint Card for use in Japan and abroad, a Nippon Shinpan spokesman said.     The card, to be issued within the year, will be honoured at Mastercard-affiliated outlets in 160 countries and at locations accepting the Nippon Shinpan card, he said.     Nippon Shinpan reached a similar agreement with <Visa International> on September 29.  REUTER &#3;
