NASA PICKS BOEING <BA> FOR SYSTEM CONTRACT

Boeing Co said the National Aeronautics and Space Administration selected it for negotiations leading to a contract for the Technical and Management System project in support of the Space Station program.     NASA's initial commitment is about 40 mln dlrs.     Under terms of the contract, Boeing will design and operate an information exchange network to support development of the proposed space station.
