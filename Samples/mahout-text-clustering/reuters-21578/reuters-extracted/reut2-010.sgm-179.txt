VALLEY FEDERAL <VFED> SPLITS STOCK TWO-FOR-ONE

Valley Federal Savings and Loan Association said its board declared a two-for-one stock split for its common stock.     The split will be effected in the form of a 100 pct stock dividend, to be issued April 30 to stockholders of record March 31.  Reuter &#3;
