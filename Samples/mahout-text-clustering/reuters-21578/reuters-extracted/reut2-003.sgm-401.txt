INVESTORS GROUP SHARE OFFERING APPROVED

Investors Group Inc, a unit of <Power Financial Corp>, said filing a final prospectus for the previously announced new issue of one mln common shares of Investors Group and secondary offering by Power Financial of three mln Investors Group common received regulatory approval.     The issue will be priced at 23.50 dlrs a share and 1,650,000 shares of the total issue will be offered outside Canada. The Canadian offering will be purchased by investment dealers <Dominion Securities Inc> and Gordon Capital Corp, and the international offering will be acquired by Dominion Securities, Gordon and S.G. Warburg Securities, Investors said.  Reuter &#3;
