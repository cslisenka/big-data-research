COCA-COLA BOTTLING <COKE> NAMES NEW PRESIDENT

Coca-Cola Bottling Co Consolidated said James Moore Jr. will assume the position of president and chief executive office, replacing Marvin W. Griffin Jr., who resigned effective today.     The company said Moore previously served as president of <Atlantic Soft Drink Co>.  Reuter &#3;
