NORTH HILLS <NOHL> GETS CONSTRUCTION FUNDING

North Hills Electronics Inc said its North Hills Israel Ltd unit received a commitment for 1,500,000 dlrs in financing from the <Overseas Private Placement Corp>.     The company said the money will be used to construct and equip a 14,000-square-foot manufacturing plant in Israel.     North Hills said construction of the plant, which will make electronic power supplies and interconnect devices for computer local area networks, will be completed by the second quarter of its current fiscal year.
