IRVINE SENSORS <IRSN> COMPLETES UNIT OFFERING

Irvine Sensors Corp said it completed a public offering of 1.25 mln units, consisting of two shares of common stock and one warrant to purchase an additional share.     The units were sold at a price of 1.60 dlrs each, Irvine Sensors said, adding, the units will trade under the NASDAQ symbol "IRSNU" and the new warrants under the symbol "IRSNW."  Reuter &#3;
