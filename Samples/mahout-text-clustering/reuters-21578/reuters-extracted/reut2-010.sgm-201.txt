HAWKEYE ENTERTAINMENT <SBIZ> COMPLETES OFFERING

Hawkeye Entertainment Inc said it completed its initial public offering at the maximum subscription level of 14 mln shares at 25 cts per share, for proceeds of 3.5 mln dlrs.     The company said the proceeds will be used to create, acquire, develop, produce and package entertainment properties, primarily in film and television and secondarily in music.  Reuter &#3;
