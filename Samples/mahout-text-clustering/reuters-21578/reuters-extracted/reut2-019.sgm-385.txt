NEW ENGLAND CRITICAL CARE <NECC.O> BUYS NPO

New England Critical Care Inc said it completed its purchase of NPO Therapies Inc of Salt Lake City.     The company said that NPO Therapies' president and founder, Kelly Mutchie, has joined New England Critical Care as regional vice president covering the western United States.     NPO Therapies provides home infusion therapy services.  Reuter &#3;
