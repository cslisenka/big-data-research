CHEFS <CHEF.O> COMPLETES PRIVATE SALE

Chefs International Inc said it completed a private sale of nine mln units of its securities for 20 cts per unit for a total price of 1,800,000 dlrs.     The company said each unit consisted of one share of Chef's common stock and one three-year warrant exerciseable to purchase one share of Chef's stock at 25 cents.     The company also said Robert E. Brennan purchased 8,250,000 of the units.      Reuter &#3;
