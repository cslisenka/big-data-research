BANCTEC <BTEC> GETS LARGEST ORDER EVER

BancTec Inc said it received an order for image processing equipment valued at six mln dlrs, its largest order ever.     BancTec said the order, expected to be shipped during the current fiscal year, was placed by a major international company, which it did not name.  Reuter &#3;
