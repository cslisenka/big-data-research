API OIL INVENTORY REPORT TO BE ISSUED TONIGHT

The American Petroleum Institute, API, said its weekly U.S. petroleum inventory report will be issued tonight, despite many company closures on Friday of last week for the Easter holiday.     The API report is usually released around 1700 EDT on Tuesday nights.     The Energy Information Administration said it also expects its weekly oil statistic report to be released as usual, on Wednesday night at about 1700 EDT.  Reuter &#3;
