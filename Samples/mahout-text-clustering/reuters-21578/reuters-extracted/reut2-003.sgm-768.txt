NYSE SAYS GENERAL REFRACTORIES <GRX> NO COMMENT

The New York Stock Exchange said General Refractories Co had no comment on the activity in its stock.     The company's shares were trading off 1-1/2 at 15-1/4.     The exchange said it contacted the company and asked if there were any corporate developments to explain the unusual activity. The company declined to issue a statement, the NYSE said.  Reuter &#3;
