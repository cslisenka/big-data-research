COLONIAL MUNICIPAL INITIAL OFFERING STARTS

Colonial Municipal Income Trust said an initial public offering of 26 mln shares is under way at 10 dlrs each through underwriters led by Morgan Keegan Inc <MOR>, Bateman Eichler, Hill Richards Inc and Piper, Jaffray and Hopwood Inc.     Underwriters have been granted an option to purchase up to 3,900,000 more shares to cover overallotments.  Reuter &#3;
