SANTA ANITA <SAR> TO BUILD IN COMMERCIAL PARK

Baldwin INdustrial Properties Ltd said five new buildings valued at 9.5 mln dlrs will be built as Phase IV of the Baldwin Commerce Center in baldwin Park, calif.     Baldwin INdustrial Properties is a joint venture of Santa Anita Realty Enterprises Inc, Grant General Contractors Inc, Baldwin Associates and Wm. P. Willman and Assocates.  Reuter &#3;
