TRIBUNE <TRB> TO BUY HOLLYWOOD PROPERTY

Tribune Co said it agreed to buy from Gene Autry and the Autry Foundation certain properties located in Hollywood, California. Terms were not disclosed.     The properties include broadcast and studio facilities on 10.1 acres of land located at Sunset Boulevard and Van Ness Avenue in Hollywood.     Also included in the agreement are three additional parcels of land on Sunset Boulevard totaling 2.3 acres, it said.      Reuter &#3;
