COMEX COPPER FUTURES ONLY SLIGHTLY HIGHER EARLY

Comex copper futures posted smaller than expected gains early because of commission house resistance beginning at 62.90 cents basis May, brokers said.     Commission house selling has been entering between 62.90 and 63.10 cents because many speculators remain nervous after recent sharp declines, brokers said.     Locals were buyers in sympathy with firmer precious metals and trade houses bought scale down, brokers said.     May delivery was up 0.05 cent at 62.70 cents a lb in a 62.85 to 62.55 cents range.
