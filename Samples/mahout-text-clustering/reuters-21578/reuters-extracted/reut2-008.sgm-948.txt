LVI GROUP INC <LVI> FILES PREFERRED OFFERING

The LVI Group Inc said it will file  with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering 30 mln dlrs of a new issue of a convertible exchangeable preferred stock of the company.     The company said the offering will only be made by means of a perspectus to be filed as part of the registration statement.  Reuter &#3;
