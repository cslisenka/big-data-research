U.S TREASURY SELLING 24.25 BILLION DLRS OF NOTES

The U.S. Treasury said it will auction 24.25 billion dlrs of two, four and seven year notes next week to raise a total of 8.325 billion dlrs of new cash.     The financing will begin with the monthly auction on Tuesday, June 23 of 9.75 billion dlrs of two year notes.     That will be followed on Wednesday and Thursday by respective quarterly sales of 7.5 billion dlrs of four year notes and 7.0 billion dlrs of seven year notes to refund 15.93 billion dlrs of two year and four year notes maturing June 30. The two year notes mature June 30, 1989. The four years mature June 30, 1991 with the seven years due July 15, 1994.  Reuter &#3;
