C.O.M.B. <CMCO> SELLS THREE RETAIL STORES

C.O.M.B. Co said it sold three of its retail stores in Omaha to Bob Cummins Enterprises Inc, a retail closeout merchandiser.     It said the sale is consistent with its previously announced Retail Division restructuring plans.   Reuter &#3;
