SPECTRUM DIGITAL <SPDG> INSTALLS FIRST SYSTEM

Spectrum Digital Corp said it installed its first multi-node ISDX T-1 multiplexer.     The company said the T-1 system was installed at the University of North Carolina's Education Computing Service at Research Triangle Park, N.C.  Reuter &#3;
