U.S. TO SELL 12.8 BILLION DLRS IN BILLS

The U.S. Treasury said it will sell 12.8 billion dlrs of three and six-month bills at its regular auction next week.     The March 30 sale, to be evenly divided between the three and six month issues, will result in a paydown of 1.2 billion dlrs as maturing bills total 13.99 billion dlrs.     The bills will be issued April 2.  Reuter &#3;
