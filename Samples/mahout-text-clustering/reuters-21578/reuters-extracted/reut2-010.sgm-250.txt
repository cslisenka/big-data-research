WESTWORLD <WCHI> EXTENDS EXCHANGE OFFER

Westworld Community Healthcare Inc said it is extending its exchange offer to April 8 at 1700 EST.     The offer is for Westworld 30 mln dlr principal amount of 14-1/2 pct subordinated notes due 2000 and its 35 mln dlrs principal amount 14-3/8 pct subordinated debt due 1995.     It said to date, 7,380,000 dlrs principal amount of securities have been tendered under the offers.  Reuter &#3;
