GREENWICH PHARMACEUTICALS COMPLETES PLACEMENT

Greenwhich Pharmaceuticals Inc <GRPI.O> said it has completed a private placement of its common stock, generating more than eight mln dlrs in net proceeds.     These funds, together with the two mln dlrs in cash currently held by the company, will be used to complete its Therafectin rheumatoid arthritis drug development program and to develop other drugs, the company said.
