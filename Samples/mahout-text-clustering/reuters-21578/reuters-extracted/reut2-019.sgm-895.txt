PEPSI <PEP> GETS SUPPLIER CONTRACT

Pepsi-Cola USA said received an exclusive five-year contract to supply soft drinks to all of Harcourt Brace Jovanovich Inc's <HBJ> Sea World theme parks in the United States, effective Jan 1, 1988.     Pepsi said Sea World has had an account with Coca-Cola for the past 16 years. Pepsi said Sea World operates five major theme parks in Orlando, San Diego, Cleveland, Winter Haven, Fla., and Baseball City, Fla.     Pepsi said the new agreement also calls for a major promotional partnerships between the companies.  Reuter &#3;
