SCOTT'S HOSPITALITY ACQUIRES CAPITAL FOOD

<Scott's Hospitality Inc> said it acquired all issued shares of Capital Food Services Ltd, of Ottawa. Terms were not disclosed.     Scott's said Capital Food had 1986 sales of more than 20 mln dlrs and will continue to operate under its present name with existing management.     Capital Food provides food services to several Ottawa institutions, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
