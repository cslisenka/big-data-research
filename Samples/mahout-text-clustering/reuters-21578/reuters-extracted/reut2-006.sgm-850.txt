FORMER WHITE HOUSE AIDE DEAVER INDICTED

Former White House aide Michael Deaver, a close friend of President Reagan, was indicted by a grand jury on criminal charges that he lied about his Washington lobbying business.     Deaver, long a confidant of Reagan and his wife Nancy, faces a maximum 25 years in prison if convicted on five counts that he lied to Congress and the grand jury investigating his business affairs.
