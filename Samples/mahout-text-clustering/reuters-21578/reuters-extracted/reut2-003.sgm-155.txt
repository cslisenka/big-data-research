HIGHLAND SUPERSTORES <HIGH> FEBRUARY SALES RISE

Highland Superstores Inc said its February sales rose 14 pct to 46.5 mln dlrs from 40.9 mln dlrs a year ago.     The company said on a comparable store basis, considering stores open for at least 24 months, sales declined 10 pct. It said it currently operates 53 stores.   Reuter &#3;
