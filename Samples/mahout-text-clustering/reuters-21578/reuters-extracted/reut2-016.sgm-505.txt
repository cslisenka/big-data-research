FISCHER-WATTS, HORIZON GOLD <HRIZ.O> TEST MINE

<Fischer-Watts Gold Co Inc> said it  and Horizon Gold Shares Inc have begun sampling their jointlyl-held Rich Hill Placer Prospect mine in Yavapai County, Arizona.
