FLETCHER CHALLENGE SETS PRICE FOR BONUS SHARES

Fletcher Challenge Ltd <FLTC.WE> (FCL) announced a striking price of 5.89 N.Z. Dlrs a share for shareholders who have elected to receive bonus shares under its dividend reinvestment plan.     It said in a statement the price was determined by taking 90 pct of the weighted average prices of ordinary shares sold on the New Zealand Stock Exchange between October 12 and 19.     FCL shares were quoted from Monday as ex-dividend and closed at 6.10 dlrs.   REUTER &#3;
