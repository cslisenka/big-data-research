NECO <NPT> POSTPONES ANNUAL MEETING

NECO Enterprises Inc said it has postponed its annual meeting to May 21 from the previously scheduled April 28 and the record date for the meeting to March 25 from March 11.     A spokesman said the changes were made because of the pending tender offer for the company's stock.  Reuter &#3;
