HITECH ENGINEERING <THEX> TO MAKE PROJECTOR

Hitech Engineering Co said it agreed to make a new version of (Electrohome Ltd's) large-screen video projector and to obtain certification for the projector on the Defense Department's preferred products list.     Under the agreement, Kitchener, Ontario-based Electrohome will pay an undisclosed amount to Hitech for initial research and development and to buy some of the units, the Tempest version of the ECP2000.     The Tempest program is a classified U.S. Government program dealing with data processing equipment.  Reuter &#3;
