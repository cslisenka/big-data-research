DST SYSTEMS <DSTS> PRESIDENT DIES

DST Systems Inc said its president and director, Robert L. Gould, died of an apparent heart attack.     It said his duties will be assumed by Thomas McDonnell, the company's chief executive officer.     Mr. Gould had served as president and chief operating officer since October, 1984.  Reuter &#3;
