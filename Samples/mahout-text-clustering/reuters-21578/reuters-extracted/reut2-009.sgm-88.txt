CANADA ENERGY MINISTER TO MAKE STATEMENT

Canadian Energy Minister Marcel Masse will hold a news conference at noon est Wednesday, following a meeting with his Alberta counterpart, Neil Webber, his office announced.     Published reports in Canada, quoting government sources, said Masse will announce a major aid package for the province's struggling energy industry.     An unidentified Alberta member of Parliament said the plan will include drilling incentives and tax measures.  Reuter &#3;
