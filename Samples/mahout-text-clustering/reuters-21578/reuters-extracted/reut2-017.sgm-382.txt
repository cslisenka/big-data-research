U.K. TO SUPPLY WHEAT TO CHINA UNDER AID PROGRAM

The U.K. Will ship 39,250 tonnes of feed wheat to China this month as food aid at a cost to the British aid program of 3.4 mln stg, the Overseas Development Administration (ODA) said.     The wheat will be supplied by grain exporter Ceres UK Ltd.     As part of the European Community's obligation under the Food Aid Convention, the U.K. Is pledged to supply 110,700 tonnes of cereals each year. From this commitment the U.K. Makes allocations to the World Food Programme.  REUTER &#3;
