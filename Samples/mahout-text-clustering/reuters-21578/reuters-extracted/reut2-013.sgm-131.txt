US WEST <USW> UNIT NAMES NEW PRESIDENT

US West Inc said it named A. Gary Ames to succeed Robert Blanz as president and chief executive officer of its Mountain Bell subsidiary.     Blanz, who announced his intention to retire effective June 1, was elected chairman of the Mountain Bell board of directors.     Ames currently serves Mountain Bell as executive vice president and chief operating officer.  Reuter &#3;
