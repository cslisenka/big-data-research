WEEKLY ELECTRIC OUTPUT UP 2.4 PCT FROM 1986

U.S. power companies generated a net 47.47 billion kilowatt-hours of electrical energy in the week ended March 21, up 2.4 pct from 46.36 billion a year earlier, the Edison Electric Institute (EEI) said.     In its weekly report on electric output, the electric utility trade association said electric output in the week ended March 14 was 48.31 billion kilowatt-hours.     The EEI said power production in the 52 weeks ended March 21 was 2,557.44 billion kilowatt hours, up 2.1 pct from the year-ago period.     Electric output so far this year was 602.26 billion kilowatt hours, up 2.2 pct from 589.51 billion last year, the EEI said.  Reuter &#3;
