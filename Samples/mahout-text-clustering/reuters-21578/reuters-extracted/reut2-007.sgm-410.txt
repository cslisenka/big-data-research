L. LURIA <LUR> TO SUPPLY NETWORK PROGRAM

L. Luria and Son INc said it reached an agreement in principle to be the exclusive supplier of merchandise to be sold through a new game and home shopping television show being considered by a major television network.     The company declined to elaborate beyond this statement. It said the arrangement is subject to a formal agreement and board approval of the network and Luria.  Reuter &#3;
