STOLTENBERG AGREES WITH POEHL ON INFLATION OUTLOOK

West German Finance Minister Gerhard Stoltenberg said that there were absolutely no signs of current dangers to price stability in West Germany.     He was speaking at a news conference shortly after Bundesbank president Karl Otto Poehl told a conference in Frankfurt that inflationary fears, which have been responsible for recent interest rate increases, were unjustified and exaggerated.
