SWISS MORTGAGE INSTITUTE ISSUES 160 MLN SFR BOND

The Swiss Mortgage Institute (Schweizerischer Hypothekarinstitute) is issuing a 160 mln Swiss franc five pct 12-year bond priced at 100.5 pct, lead manager Credit Suisse said.     Subscriptions run until Oct. 29.  Reuter &#3;
