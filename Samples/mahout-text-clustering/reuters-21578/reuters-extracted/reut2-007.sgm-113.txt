PITNEY BOWES <PBI> TESTS NEW MAIL SERVICE

Pitney Bowes Inc said Mountain Bell, a unit of US West Inc <USW>, began a test of a new Pitney Bowes computerized mail processing system.     The test has been approved by the U.S. Postal Service, the company said.     The processing and postage payment system allows faster and less costly mail preparation, automated funds transfer to cover postage charges and a production and funds tracking system, it said.     Mountain Bell processes more than 19 mln pieces of mail annually.  Reuter &#3;
