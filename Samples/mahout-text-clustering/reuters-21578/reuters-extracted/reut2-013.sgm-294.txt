OKI ELECTRIC STUDIES IMPORT OF U.S. CAR PHONES

<Oki Electric Industry Co Ltd> may import car telephones made by its 100 pct U.S. Subsidiary, <Oki America Inc>, for sale in Japan, an Oki spokesman said.     The firm would like to import the car phones, which are made according to a system developed jointly by Motorola Inc <MOT> of the U.S. And Oki Electric, the spokesman said.     A final decision will hinge on obtaining approval from Japan's Post and Telecommunications Ministry and whether or not it is feasible to convert the models to meet Japanese specifications, he said.
