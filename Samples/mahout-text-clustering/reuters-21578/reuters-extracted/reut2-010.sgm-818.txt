TEXSCAN HAS TENTATIVE REORGANIZATION PLAN

Texscan Corp said it has agreed in principle with its lending banks -- United Bank of Arizona and Security Pacific Bank -- on a joint plan of reorganization.     Subject to documentation, the plan will be filed within the next 10 days, Texscan said without providing further details.  Reuter &#3;
