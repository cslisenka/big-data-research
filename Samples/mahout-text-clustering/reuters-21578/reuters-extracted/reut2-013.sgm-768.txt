SURGIDYNE INC FILES FOR INITIAL OFFERING

Surgidyne Inc said it filed a registration statement for a proposed initial public offering of its common stock.     The company proposes to offer 1,125,000 common shares at about 2.00 dlrs a share.     The offering would be underwritten by Westonka Investments Inc.     Surgidyne develops and markets specialty medical and surgical products.  Reuter &#3;
