UAE CENTRAL BANK CD YIELDS HIGHER

Yields on certificates of deposit offered by the United Arab Emirates central bank were higher than last Monday's offering, the bank said.     One-month maturity rose 1/16 point to 6-3/16 pct, two-month 1/8 point to the same level, and three- and six-month issues 3/16 point to 6-1/4 pct each.  REUTER &#3;
