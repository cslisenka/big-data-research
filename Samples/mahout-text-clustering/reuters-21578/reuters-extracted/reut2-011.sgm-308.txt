NORD RESOURCES <NRD> SETS PRIVATE SALE OF STOCK

Nord Resources Corp said Kleinwort Grieveson and Co, a U.K. investment banking firm, has agreed to privately place 600,000 Nord common shares with institutional investors outside of the U.S., primarily in the U.K.     Nord said the placement was priced at 17.50 dlrs a share, 90 pct of the stock's closing price yesterday, and is scheduled to close April 10.     The company said it will raise about 10 mln dlrs which will be used for general corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
