PERSONAL COMPUTER PRODUCTS <PCPI.O> PLACES DEBT

Personal Computer Products Inc said it completed a 4.2-mln-dlr, five pct convertible preferred stock issue to private investors.     Net proceeds of about 3.8 mln dlrs will be used to expand marketing, sales and research and development efforts, to supplement working capital, and to repay the company's entire bank debt of about 650,000 dlrs.
