BURROUGHS-WELLCOME SETS RESEARCH SPENDING

(Burroughs-Wellcome Inc) said it plans to invest 10 mln dlrs in research and development-related activities over the next five years if changes planned to Canada's patent act become law.     The company said it would increase its commitment to clinical research if the patent act is changed to provide further protection for patent rights, as planned.  Reuter &#3;
