NORSTAR <NOR> TO COMBINE NEW YORK AREA BANKS

Norstar Bancorp said it is combining its banks on Long Island and New York City into one institution to be called Norstar Bank.     The combined bank, with headquarters on Long Island, will have assets of two billion dlrs and 80 branches, Norstar said.     It added that the combination will provide important administrative and operating efficiencies to its customers.     Norstar said the combination will also make it easier to manage its bank network as it prepares to merge with Fleet Financial Group <FLT> through a 1.3 billion dlr stock transaction.
