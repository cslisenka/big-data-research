INVESTOR HAS 5.1 PCT OF SIERRA HEALTH <SIE>

Peter Lin, a Montebello, Calif., physician, told the Securities and Exchange Commission he has acquired 285,000 shares of Sierra Health Services Inc, or 5.1 pct of the total outstanding common stock.     Lin said he bought the stock for about 1.7 mln dlrs solely for investment purposes.     Although he said he may buy more stock in Sierra Health Services, Lin said he has no intention of seeking control of the company.  Reuter &#3;
