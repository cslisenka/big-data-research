REXNORD <REX> SEES MAY MERGER WITH BANNER <BNR>

Rexnord Inc said it expects to merge with a wholly-owned subsidiary of Banner Industries Inc in early May.     Late last month, Banner said it completed a tender offer for and held 96 pct of Rexnord's common shares.  Reuter &#3;
