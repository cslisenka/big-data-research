GENERAL SIGNAL <GSX> LOW BIDDER ON RADIO JOB

General Signal Corp said its General Railway Signal Co unit is the apparent low bidder at 32.5 mln dlrs on a transit radio system contract for the Southern California Rapid Transit District in Los Angeles.     The company also said its General Farebox Inc subsidiary received a 3.1 mln dlr farebox system contract from the Greater Cleveland Regional Transit Authority.  Reuter &#3;
