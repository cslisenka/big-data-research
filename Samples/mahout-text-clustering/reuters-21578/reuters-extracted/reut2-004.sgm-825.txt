BIOASSY <BSCC> HAS CLOSED WOBURN FACILITY

Bioassay Systems Corp said its toxicology facility in Woburn was closed February 28.     The company said it is exploring opportuniites to dispose of the operation's assets and assign its lease or sublease the space.     Bioassay said its Decatur, Ill., facility remains fully operational.  Reuter &#3;
