WTD INDUSTRIES <WTDI> TO SELL DEBENTURES

WTD Industries Inc said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering a 30 mln dlr issue of senior subordinated debentures due 1997.     Proceeds will be used mainly for the acquisition of additional woods products manufacturing facilities and related properties, WTD said.     The company named Kidder, Peabody and Co Inc as lead underwriter of the offering.  Reuter &#3;
