HYPONEX <HYPX> FILES FOR DEBT OFFER

Hyponex Corp said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a public offering of 100 mln dlrs of senior subordinated debentures due 1999, with an over-allotment option of 15 mln dlrs.     Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc is sole manager of the offering, the lawn products company said.  Reuter &#3;
