PACIFIC SOUTHWEST AIRLINES <PSWA> LOAD FACTOR

Pacific Southwest Airlines said its average load factor during February was 54.9 pct, down from 56.1 pct a year earlier.     In the first two months of the year the load factor totaled 51.5 pct, down from 54.0 pct a year ago.     Revenue passenger miles in February totaled 327.6 mln, compared to 295.5 mln. So far this year, revenue passenger miles totaled 640.2 mln, compared to 600.5 mln.     Available seat miles in February totaled 596.6 mln, up from 526.8 mln a year ago. Year to date available seat miles totaled 1.24 billion, compared to 1.11 billion a year ago.  Reuter &#3;
