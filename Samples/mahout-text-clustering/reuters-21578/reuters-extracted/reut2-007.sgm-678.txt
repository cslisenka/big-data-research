ILLINOIS TOOL WORKS <ITW> SELLS TWO DIVISIONS

Illinois Tool Works Inc said it completed the sale of its Drill and End Mill division, Pine Bluff, Arkansas and Eclipse Counterbore division, Detroit, to newly-formed Eclipse Industrial Pruducts Inc, based in St. Louis.  Terms were not disclosed.     All employees at both locations will be retained, it added.  Reuter &#3;
