ERBAMONT <ERB> ACQUIRES ANTIBIOTICOS S.A.

Erbamont N.V. said its major shareholder <Montedison SpA>, which owns 85 pct of its common, and Farmitalia Carlo Erba, its 75 pct owned subsidiary, have acquired <Antibioticos S.A.> and related subsidiaries.     Terms of the acquisition were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
