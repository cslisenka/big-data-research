GENERAL ELECTRIC <GE> UNIT OFFERS NEW SERVICE

General Electric Co's RCA Global Communications said it now offers RCA THRUFAX, a service that delivers telex messages to facsimile terminals.     No special equipment or extra cost is required to receive telex messages, but the facsimile terminal must be registered - without charge - with RCA, the company said.     The company said through the service, every telex terminal in the world will be able to reach RCA-registered facsimile terminals in the U.S., of which there are approximately 500,000.  Reuter &#3;
