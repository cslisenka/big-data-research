OLD DOMINION SYSTEMS <ODSI> GETS CONTRACT

Old Dominion Systems Inc said it has received a contract to provide multi-line voice messaging systems to the U.S. Immigration and Naturalization Service.     It said the contract is to provide 280,000 dlrs of equipment immediately and contains options for the purchase of another 800,000 dlrs worth through May 30.  Reuter &#3;
