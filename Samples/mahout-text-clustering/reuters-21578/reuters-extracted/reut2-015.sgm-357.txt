ALBERTA ENERGY PLANS DEBENTURE ISSUE

<Alberta Energy Co Ltd> said it filed a preliminary prospectus for an issue of convertible debentures, which may total 100 mln dlrs depending on market conditions.     It said it had not yet determined the interest rate, conversion terms, maturity date or size of the issue.  Reuter &#3;
