GREAT AMERICAN FIRST SAVINGS <GTA> OPENS UNITS

Great American First Savings Bank Arizona said it has opened four new branches located in Fry's Food Stores.     The branches are all in the Tucson area.     Great American opened nine branches in Fry's Food Stores in 1986 and opened two earlier this year.     The company said two more will open next week.  Reuter &#3;
