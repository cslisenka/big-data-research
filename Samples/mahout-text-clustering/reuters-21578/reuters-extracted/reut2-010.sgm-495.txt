RENT-A-CENTER <RENT> FILES DEBENTURE OFFERING

Rent-A-Center Inc said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission covering a 35 mln dlr offering of 25-year convertible subordinated debentures.     Proceeds will be used to finance a business expansion over the next one or two years by opening or acquiring additional stores, Rent-A-Center said.     The company named Morgan Stanley and Co Inc as lead underwriter of the offering.  Reuter &#3;
