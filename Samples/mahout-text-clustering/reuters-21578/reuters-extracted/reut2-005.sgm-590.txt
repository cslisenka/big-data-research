<KENT TOYS INC> IN PACT WITH LORIMAR <LT>

Kent Toys Inc said it signed an agreement with Lorimar-Telepictures Corp to produce a television game show called Lottery.     Under terms of the agreement, Kent said it will receive a share of the above the line production, a percentage of net profits and a percentage of first-run syndication fees.  Reuter &#3;
