IC INDUSTRIES <ICX> NAMES KARL BAYS AS CHAIRMAN

IC industries Inc said Karl Bays has been elected chairman and chief executive officer effective July one.     William Johnson, who is recovering from a recent stroke, has been named chairman emeritus and will continue as a member of the board.     Bays is currently chairman of Baxter Travenol Laboratories Inc <BAX>.  Reuter &#3;
