TNP ENTERPRISES <TNP> UNIT TO SELL BONDS

Texas-New Mexico Power Co, a unit of TNP Enterprises Inc, said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering 65 mln dlr issue of first mortgage bonds.     Proceeds will be used to repay short-term debt and for other general purposes, the company said.     It said the bonds would probably be offered through Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc and Salomon Brothers Inc.  REUTER &#3;
