BOWNE AND CO <BNE> TO REPURCHASE ONE MLN SHARES

Bowne and Co Inc said its board authorized the repurchase of up to 1,000,000 shares of the company's common stock, from time to time on the open market or otherwise at prices deemed satisfactory by the company.     The company said the repurchased shares wil be used for Bowne's employee stock option and purchase plans.  Reuter &#3;
