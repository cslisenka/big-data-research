OCCIDENTAL <OXY> UNIT NAMES NEW PRESIDENT

MidCon Corp, a unit of Occidental Petroleum Corp, said that effective May 1, Dan Grubb will be the unit's president and chief operating officer, taking over the title of president from O.C. Davis, who will remain chairman and chief executive officer.     MidCon said Robert Hodge, Midcon senior vice president for planning and administration, will take over for Grubb as president of Midcon Services when he assumes his new duties.     Also, Midcon said that Philip Gasparac, Midcon senior vice president and chief financial officer, will take on the additional responsibilities of planning, rates and joint ventures.  Reuter &#3;
