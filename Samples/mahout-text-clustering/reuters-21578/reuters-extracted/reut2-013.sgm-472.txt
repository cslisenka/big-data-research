SHOWA ALUMINIUM ISSUES 100 MLN DLR WARRANT BOND

Showa Aluminium Corp is issuing a 100 mln dlr equity warrant eurobond due April 23, 1992 paying an indicated coupon of 2-1/8 pct and priced at par, lead manager Nomura International Ltd said.     Final terms will be fixed on April 15. The selling concession is 1-1/2 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 3/4 pct. The non-callable bond is available in denominations of 5,000 dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg.     The payment date is April 23.  REUTER &#3;
