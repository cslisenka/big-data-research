CREDITANSTALT ARRANGES 100 MLN STG CD PROGRAM

Creditanstalt-Bankverein <CABV.VI>, the largest commercial bank in Austria, has arranged a 100 mln stg certificate of deposit program, banking sources said.     The dealers for the program will be S.G. Warburg and Co Ltd and County NatWest Capital Markets Ltd.  REUTER &#3;
