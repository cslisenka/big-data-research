COMPUTER PRODUCTS <CPRD> SELLS CONVERTIBLE DEBT

Computer Products Inc is raising 35 mln dlrs through an offering of convertible subordinated debentures due 1997 with a 9-1/2 pct coupon and par pricing, said sole manager Robinson-Humphrey Co Inc.     The debentures are convertible into the company's common stock at 4.625 dlrs per share, representing a premium of 23.33 pct over the stock price when terms on the debt were set.     Non-callable for three years, the issue is rated B-3 by Moody's and CCC-minus by Standard and Poor's. The gross spread is 38.75 dlrs, the selling concession is 20 dlrs and the reallowance is 10 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
