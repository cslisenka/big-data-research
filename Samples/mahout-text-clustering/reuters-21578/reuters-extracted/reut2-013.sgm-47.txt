LTV <QLTV> UNIT WINS TWO ARMY CONTRACTS

LTV Corp said its AM General division received two contracts valued at 11.7 mln dlrs from the U.S. Army to make special equipment kits for Hummer troop and cargo vehicles.     Deliveries are set for May 1987 through November 1988, the company said. AM General is part of LTV Missiles and Electronics Group.  Reuter &#3;
