PENTLAND INDUSTRIES PLC HOLDERS APPROVE SPLIT

Pentland Industries PLC said shareholders at the annual meeting approved an increase in authorized share capital to 36 mln stg to 12 mln stg by creation of another 240 mln ordinary shares, allowing for a three-for-one stock split.     It said dealings in the new shares are expected to start on June 15.  Reuter &#3;
