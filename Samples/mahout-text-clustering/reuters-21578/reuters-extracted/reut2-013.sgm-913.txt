<KINGSTON SYSTEMS INC> INITIAL OFFERING UNDERWAY

Lead underwriter Woolcott and Co Inc said an initial public offering of 1,100,000 shares of Kingston Systems Inc is underway at eight dlrs per share.     Underwriters have been granted an option to buy up to 165,000 more shares to cover overallotments.  Reuter &#3;
