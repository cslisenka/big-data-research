C. ITOH UNIT SETS BUYER FINANCING PROGRAM

CIE Systems Inc, a unit of C. Itoh Electronics Inc, said it established a program to provide lease and lease-purchase financing for buyers of its business computer systems and electronic products.     The program will be administered by CFC Leasing, an independent company, and more than 100 mln dlrs in funds are expected to be available over the next four years from a consortium of six financial institutions, the company said.     C. Itoh Electronics Inc is a U.S. unit of (C. Itoh and Co Ltd) of Japan.  Reuter &#3;
