NEWFOUNDLAND CAPITAL TO ISSUE DEBENTURES

<Newfoundland Capital Corp Ltd> said it will issue 25 mln dlrs principal amount of eight pct convertible subordinated debentures.     Underwriter is Loewen, Ondaatje, McCutcheon and Co Ltd. Net proceeds will be used for working capital, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
