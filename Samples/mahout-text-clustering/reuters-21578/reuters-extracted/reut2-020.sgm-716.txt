SYNTECH INTERNATIONAL <SYNE.O> SUES SOUTHLAND

Syntech International Inc said it initiated a suit in Nevada District Court against Southland Corp <SM> for alleged breach of contract.     Syntech said its complaint alleges that Southland failed to make payments in connection with the purchase of 208 Syntech ticketing terminals.     The company said some 3.8 mln dlrs is past due on the contract.  Reuter &#3;
