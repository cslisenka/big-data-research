COCA-COLA ENTERPRISES <CCE> OPTIONS ON CBOE

The Chicago Board Options Exchange said it will begin trading call and put options March 16 on Coca-Cola Enterprises Inc.     It said options will trade on the February quarterly expiration cycle with initial exercise prices set at 15, 17-1/2 and 20 and position limits of 8,000 contracts on the same side of the market.     It said the new listing augments the exchange's trading of Coca-Cola Co <KO>.  Reuter &#3;
