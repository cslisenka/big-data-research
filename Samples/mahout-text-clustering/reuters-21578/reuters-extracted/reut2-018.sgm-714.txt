WELLS-GARDNER <WGA> AWARDED CONTRACT

Wells-Gardner Electronics Corp said it was awarded a five-year contract to supply touch panel monitors to Matrox Electronics Systems Ltd, Dorval, Quebec, Canada.     The company said the contract is expected to generate total revenues of about five mln dlrs.     It said the monitors will be part of a videodisc system for the U.S. Army's Electronic Information Delivery System.  Reuter &#3;
