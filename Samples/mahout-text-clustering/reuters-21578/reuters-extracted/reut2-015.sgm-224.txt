ELECTROSPACE <ELE> WINS U.S. DEFENSE CONTRACTS

Electrospace Systems Inc said it won two follow up contracts from the U.S. Defense Communications Agency worth 10.7 mln dlrs.     The company said the three year contracts are for scientific and technical support at the Center for Command and Control Communications systems. The company has provided the services since 1972.
