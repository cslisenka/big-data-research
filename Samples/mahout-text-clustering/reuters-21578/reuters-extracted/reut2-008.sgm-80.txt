FRANCE TO SIGN DISNEYLAND CONTRACT ON TUESDAY

The French government said it would sign a contract with <Walt Disney Co> on Tuesday for the construction of Europe's first Disneyland amusement park.     A statement from the office of Prime Minister Jacques Chirac said the project, which will cost some 12 billion francs, would create up to 30,000 jobs and be built outside Paris.     Funding will be provided by a combination of bank loans, preferential financing by the French state and private investment. France has also agreed to invest two billion francs to extend rail and road links.  REUTER &#3;
