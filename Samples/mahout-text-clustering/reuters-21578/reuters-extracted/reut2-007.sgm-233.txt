CORROON AND BLACK <CBL> COMPLETES ACQUISITION

Carroon and Black Corp said it completed the acquisition of <Poggi-Harrison Agency Inc> and <Risk Control Inc>.     Terms of the acquisitions were not disclosed.     Separately, Carroon said it also completed the acquisition of <Rosskopf, Rapp and Schmidt Insurance Agency>, based in El Monte, Calif.  Reuter &#3;
