COMPUGRAPHIC CORP <CPU> IN JOINT VENTURE

Compugraphic Corp said it entered into an exclusive distribution and technology exchange pact with <Autographix Inc>.     Compugraphic said the pact calls for it to distribute Autographix products to corporate and government markets in North America. It said Autographix will continue to sell its products to the commerical slide production market.     The technology exchange includes joint development projects intergrating Compugraphic's type and text technology with Autographix' presentation graphics workstations.   Reuter &#3;
