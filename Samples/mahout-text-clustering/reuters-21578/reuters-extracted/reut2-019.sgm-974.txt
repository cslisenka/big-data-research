ALLIED-SIGNAL <ALD> SELLS TO SCHLUMBERGER <SLB>

Allied-Signal Inc and Schlumberger Ltd jointly announced that Schlumberger had acquired Allied-Signal's Neptune International unit. Terms weren't disclosed.    Atlanta, Ga.-based Neptune, which makes water meters and flow measurement equipment, had sales last year of 80 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
