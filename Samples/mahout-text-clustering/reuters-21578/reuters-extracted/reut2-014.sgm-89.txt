MELRIDGE <BULB> UNIT IN REAL ESTATE AGREEMENT

Melridge Inc said its 50 pct owned Conroy's Inc subsidiarey, which franchises flower shops, has entered into an agreement for an affiliate of Public Storage Inc to acquire and develop real estate for the expansion of Conroy's franchise operations.     The company said under the agreement, Public Storage will obtain an undisclosed equity interest in Conroy's and Melridge and undisclosed equity interest in the Public Storage affiliate.
