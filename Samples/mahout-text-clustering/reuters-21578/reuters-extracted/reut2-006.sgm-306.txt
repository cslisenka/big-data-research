PRIDE REFINING GETS 75 MLN DLR DEFENSE CONTRACT

Pride Refining Co of Abilene, Texas, is being awarded a 75 mln dlr contract for jet fuel, the Defense Department said.     It said work on the contract, which was awarded as part of a multi-contract procurement program, is expected to be completed by April 30, 1988.  Reuter &#3;
