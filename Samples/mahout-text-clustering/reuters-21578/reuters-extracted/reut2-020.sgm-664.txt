UNION CAMP <UCC> TO BUY BACK SHARES

Union Camp Corp said its board approved repurchases of up to two mln shares of its common in the market or in privately negotiated transactions.     Union Camp said that no borrowings will be required to make the purchases and there is no plan to reissue the common stock purchased.     Union Camp has about 73.7 mln shares outstanding.
