ORBIT <ORBT.O> UNIT INCREASES BACKLOG

Orbit Instrument Corp said its unit, Orbit Semiconductor Inc, has received orders totalling about 2.5 mln dlrs which puts its backlog at a record level of about 4.7 mln dlrs.     Additionally, the company said it will now offer two Micron CMOS processes that can withstand Megarad total dose radiation.     Applications for the processes include products manufactured for military, medical and power industry market, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
