LME SILVER REACHES FIVE MONTHS HIGH BY MIDSESSION

LME silver ended the morning with the three months large contract trading at a new five month high of 420 pence per troy ounce, compared with 415/418 pence indicated on yesterday's afternoon kerb close.     Traders said the market is continuing to be largely marked-up in sympathy with bullion movements but investors have been generally reluctant to participate in LME silver trading ever since the traumatic period some seven years ago when the market was subjected to massive speculative manipulation.
