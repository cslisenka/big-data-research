MERCANTILE STORES <MST> SETS 2-1/2-FOR-1 SPLIT

Mercantile Stores Co Inc said its stockholders approved a 2-1/2-for-1 common stock split.     Mercantile said the record date for the split was June one and that it expects to mail the additional shares resulting from the split on June 15.  Reuter &#3;
