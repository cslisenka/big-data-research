WORLD BANK TO SEND ECONOMIC MISSION TO BRAZIL

World Bank President Barber Conable said his agency plans to send a mission to Brazil next week to explore economic conditions.     Speaking to reporters at the International Monetary Fund and World Bank annual meeting, Conable said that he felt positive and optimistic that something between Brazil and its creditors will be worked out.     Conable met last night with Brazilian officials and told reporters that "We agreed to send a mission to Brazil next week to explore further the state of progress and to get clarifications."
