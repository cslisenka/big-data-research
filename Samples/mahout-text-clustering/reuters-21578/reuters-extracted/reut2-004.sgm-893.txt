FRANKLIN RESOURCES <BEN> SAYS NET MAY DOUBLE

Franklin Resources Inc said it believes earnings could double this year as compared to a year ago when the company reported income of 32 mln dlrs on 143 mln dlrs in revenues.     Franklin Resources is a financial services company. Its fiscal year ends September 30.  Reuter &#3;
