DELTA AIR <DAL> FEBRUARY LOAD FACTOR FALLS

Delta Air Lines Inc said February load factor fell to 54.1 pct from 59.9 pct a year before.     The company said revenue passenger miles rose 4.1 pct to 2.48 billion from 2.39 billion a year before and available seat miles rose 15.3 pct to 4.60 billion from 3.99 billion.     For the first two months of calendar 1986, Delta said load factor fell to 52.1 pct from 56.7 pct a year before, as revenue passenger miles rose 4.7 pct to 4.97 billion from 4.75 billion and available seat miles rose 14.0 pct to 9.55 billion from 8.37 billion.
