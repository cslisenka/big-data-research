SHULTZ SAYS "SHADOW" CAST OVER HIS USSR VISIT

U.S. Secretary of State George Shultz said the Marines spy case had cast a "heavy shadow" over his scheduled talks in Moscow next week.     "Unfortunately, as is so often the case when we are in the midst of potentially promising discussions with the Soviets, the discussions take place in a difficult environment generated by their actions, and these things cast a heavy shadow on our discussions," he told reporters.
