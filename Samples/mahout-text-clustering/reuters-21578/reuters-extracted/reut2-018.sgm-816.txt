DOSKOCIL <DOSKD.O> BUYS BACK FIVE PCT OF STOCK

Doskocil Companies Inc said it bought back 310,000 shares or about five pct of its common stock and its board authorized the repurchase of an additional 200,000 common shares.     If completed, the total purchases would represent 8.5 pct of Doskocil's outstanding common stock.  Reuter &#3;
