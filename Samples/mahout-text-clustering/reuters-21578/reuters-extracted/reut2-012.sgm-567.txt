STATUS GAME <STGM> TO SELL CONDOMS

Status Game Corp said it will immediately start selling condoms through the 500 cigarette machines it owns and operates in Las Vegas.     The company said it is negotiating a contract with one of the world's largest condom maker, which it did not identify, for the production of specially-packaged condoms for the cigarette machine sales.
