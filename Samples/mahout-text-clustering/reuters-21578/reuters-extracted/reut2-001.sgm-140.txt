DEUTSCHE BANK UNIT ISSUES 75 MLN STG EUROBOND

Deutsche Bank Finance NV Curacao is issuing a 75 mln stg eurobond due April 2, 1997 paying 9-7/8 pct and priced at 101-3/4 pct, joint-lead manager Kleinwort, Benson Ltd said. Deutsche Bank Capital Markets is the other joint-lead manager.     The issue is guaranteed by Deutsche Bank AG and is callable at 100-3/8 pct after seven years, declining by 1/8 point per annum to par thereafter. The selling concession is 1-3/8 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct. The issue is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 stg and will be listed in Luxembourg. The payment date is April 2.  REUTER &#3;
