UNOCAL <UCL> UNIT BEGINS PLANT CONSTRUCTION

Unocal Corp said its Desert Power subsidiary began construction of a 47,500 kilowatt geothermal plant in Southern California's Imperial Valley.     The company said Desert Power expects the plant to be operational by early 1989.     The new plant will be located at the southern end of the Salton Sea, about one-half mile from an existing 10,000 kilowatt generating plant owned and operated by Southern California Edison Co <SEC>, Unocal also said.
