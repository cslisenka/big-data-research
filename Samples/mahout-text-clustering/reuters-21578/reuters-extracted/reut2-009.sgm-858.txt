CANADA DETAILS TWO BOND AUCTIONS

The finance department said it will sell 500 mln dlrs of three-year 3-1/2 month bonds to primary distributors in a previously announced auction March 31.     It said another 400 mln dlrs of seven-year 3 month bonds will be auctioned April 1.  Reuter &#3;
