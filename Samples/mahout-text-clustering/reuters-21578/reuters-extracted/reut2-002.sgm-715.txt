VALERO ENERGY KNOWS OF NO REASON FOR STOCK GAIN

Valero Energy Corp <VLO> said it knows of no reason for its stock activity today.     The stock rose 1-5/8 to 10-7/8, a gain of about 17.5 pct.     A spokesman for Valero, Steve Fry, said the company knows of no reason for the increased volume and sharp gain in the stock.  Reuter &#3;
