LTV <QLTV> GETS MARINE CORPS CONTRACT

LTV Corp said it has received a 23.5 mln dlr fixed-price contract to rebuild 923 M54 five-ton cargo trucks into more modern M813 cargo trucks.     It said it will deliver 120 conversion kits a months, starting late in the second quarter and ending early in the first quarter of 1988.  Reuter &#3;
