SECURITY PACIFIC <SPC> DOWNGRADED BY MOODY'S

Moody's Investors Service Inc said it downgraded 3.7 billion dlrs of debt of Security Pacific Corp, lead bank Security Pacific National Bank and other units.     Moody's said it is concerned over the outlook for improvement in the lead bank's earnings and asset quality, which have been burdened by high levels of nonperforming assets and charge-offs.     Cut were the parent's senior and guaranteed debt to Aa-3 from Aa-2 and subordinated debt to A-1 from Aa-3 and the lead bank's senior debt and long-term deposits to Aa-2 from Aaa.  Reuter &#3;
