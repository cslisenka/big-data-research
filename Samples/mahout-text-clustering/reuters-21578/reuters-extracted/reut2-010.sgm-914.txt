NYSE SAYS SANTA FE <SFX> DECLINES COMMENT

The New York Stock Exchange said Santa Fe Southern Pacific Corp declined to comment on inquiries regarding the unusual activity in its stock.     Earlier, analysts and traders said the stock rose sharply after Henley Group <HENG> disclosed in a securities and Exchange Commission filing that it holds almost eight mln shares in the railroad and real estate conglomerate.     The stock was trading at 38-3/4, up 2-5/8 on early afternoon turnover of over one million shares.  Reuter &#3;
