GM <GM> DEALERS ORDER 125,000 NEW CHEVROLETS

General Motors Corp said almost 125,000 of Chevrolet's two newest cars - the four-door Corsica sedan and two-door Beretta Sport Coupe - have been ordered by dealers in advance of their official introduction Thursday.     It said on hand in dealer inventory and for immediate delivery were 35,000 of the two models. Another 8,000 were in transit from assembly plants.  Reuter &#3;
