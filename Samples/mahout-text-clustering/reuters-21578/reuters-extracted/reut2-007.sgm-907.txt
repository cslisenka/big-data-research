FIRST UNION <FUNC> BANKS ELECTRONICALLY LINKED

First Union National Bank of Florida, a unit of First Union Corp, said it has electronically linked its Florida and North Carolina 24-hour banking machines.     The bank said the service, which starts April 15, will let First Union customers make withdrawals and balance inquiries at any of its locations in Florida and North Carolina.     "This link-up will give customers immediate access to their accounts without additional cost whether they are in Florida or in North Carolina," said B.J. Walker, vice chairman of First Union Corp and chairman, president and chief executive officer of First Union National Bank.  Reuter &#3;
