AXLON <AXLN> NAMES CO-CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

Axlon Inc said Austin C. Marshall has been named president and co-chief executive officer, sharing power in the latter post with founder and chairman Nolan K. Bushnell.     The company said Marshall, who had been executive vice president of New York toy company Playtime, will have responsibility for the distribution of toys and Bushnell will concentrate on research and development and licensing.
