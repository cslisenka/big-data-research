VIACOM <VIA> EXECUTIVE RESIGNS

Viacom Inc said Gordon E. Belt has resigned as vice president, chief financial officer of Viacom Interntional Inc, effective Nov 13, 1987.     The company said a successor will be announced shortly.     Viacom said Belt has not announced immediate plans after his resignation.      Reuter &#3;
