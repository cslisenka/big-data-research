TENNECO <TGT> TO REDEEM 11 DLR PREFERRED

Tenneco Inc said it will redeem all of its outstanding 11.00 dlr cumulative preference stock on April eight at 102.24 dlrs a share including accrued dividends.     It said 2,515,400 shares of the stock will be redeemed.  Reuter &#3;
