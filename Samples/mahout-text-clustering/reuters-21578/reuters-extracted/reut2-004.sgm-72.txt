HYDRO QUEBEC ISSUES CANADIAN DLR BOND

Hydro Quebec is issuing a 125 mln Canadian dlr bond due April 21, 1997 paying nine pct and priced at 100-3/4 pct, lead manager Merrill Lynch International said.     The bond is available in denominations of 1,000 and 5,000 dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg.     Fees comprise 1-1/4 pct selling concession and 3/8 pct each for management and underwriting. Pay date is April 21.  REUTER &#3;
