S/P AFFIRMS NATIONAL WESTMINSTER DEBT

Standard and Poor's Corp said it affirmed the AAA senior debt and A-1-plus commercial paper of National Westminster Bank Plc <NWBL.L>.     The AA long-term and A-1-plus short-term certificates of deposit of National Westminster Bank USA were also affirmed.     S and P cited the bank's decision to increase group provisions for sovereign debt exposure by 466 mln stg. The action raised total provisions against 35 countries with payment difficulties to 29.8 pct from 13 pct, S and P said. S and P said this puts the bank's debt coverage in line with that of its international peers.  REUTER &#3;
