CANADA 7-YEAR BONDS AVERAGE 7.97 PCT YIELD

The Canadian government's auction of 400 mln dlrs of 7-year and 3-month bonds yielded an average 7.97 pct and were priced at an average 98.787 dlrs, the finance department said.     The coupon rate was 7.75 pct on the issue.  Reuter &#3;
