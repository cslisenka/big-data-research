CAPITAL MARKET BORROWING FALLS IN MARCH, OECD SAYS

Borrowing on the international capital markets contracted significantly in March with 23.1 billion dlrs of medium and long-term funds raised, down 3.4 billion from February and about nine billion dlrs less than in March 1986, the Organisation for Economic Cooperation and Development said in its latest monthly report.     Borrowing on external bond markets totalled 19.7 billion dlrs, some 1.1 billion below the February figure.     The market for floating rate notes saw further setbacks and the volume of new offerings fell to only 800 mln dlrs from 1.4 billion in February.  Reuter &#3;
