SIEMENS AG UNIT AND NEC <NIPNY> UNIT IN PACT

Siemens Information Systems Inc, a unit of <Siemens AG> of West Germany, has reached an agreement in principle with NEC America Inc, a unit of NEC Corp of Japan, allowing Siemens Information's Tel Plus Communications Inc subsidiary to continue distributing NEC's NEAX2400 Information Management System.     Siemens Information said the three year agreement will allow TPC to market all current and future NEAX2400 models over the contract's term.  Reuter &#3;
