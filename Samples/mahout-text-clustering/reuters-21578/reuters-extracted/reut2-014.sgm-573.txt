GENERAL PHYSICS <GPHY> TO PURCHASE SHARES

General Physics Corp said it will purchase up to 100,000 shares of its common stock from time to time in open market transactions.     The purchase would represent approximately 16 pct of the shares in the public float, the company said. Yesterday, the last bid price of General Physics common stock as quoted by Nasdaq was 15 dlrs, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
