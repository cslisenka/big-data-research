UNITED SCIENTIFIC UNIT LANDS 4.3 MLN DLR CONTRACT

Electro-optronic equipment makers <United Scientific Holdings Plc> said the Advanced Products Division of its U.S. Subsidiary <OEC Dallas, Texas> has been awarded a 4.3 mln dlr contract by the U.S. Army.     The contract is for the production of high energy laser protection filters for binoculars. The three year contract already includes options to the value of eight mln dlrs.     United reported pretax profit of 3.2 mln stg on turnover of 117.9 mln stg in the year ended September 1986. The company's shares were unchanged on Friday's close of 243p following today's announcement.     REUTER &#3;
