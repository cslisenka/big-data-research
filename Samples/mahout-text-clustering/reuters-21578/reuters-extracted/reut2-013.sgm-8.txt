NYFE SEAT SELLS FOR 1,500 DLRS

The New York Stock Exchange said a seat on the New York Futures Exchange sold for 1,500 dlrs, which is up 250 dlrs from the previous sale yesterday.     The Exchange said the current bid is 1,250 and the current offer is 1,500 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
