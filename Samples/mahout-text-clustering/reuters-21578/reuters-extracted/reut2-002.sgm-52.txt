EQUATORIAL <EQUA> SIGNS PACT WITH CONTEL UNIT

Equatorial Communications Co, a satellite data network concern, said it has signed an agreement with Contel Corp's <CTC>, Contel ASC, unit allowing Contel ASC to buy 3.6 mln shares of Equatorial stock at 3.25 dlrs per share.     In addition, Equatorial said under the agreement Contel ASC would buy a minimum of 10 mln dlrs of equipment from Equatorial, loan Equatorial up to six mln dlrs and assume a portion of Equatorial's rights and obligations under a satellite transponder lease.
