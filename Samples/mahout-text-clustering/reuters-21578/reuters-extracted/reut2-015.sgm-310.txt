RAYTECH <RAY> BUYS WEST GERMAN COMPANY

Raytech Corp said it acquired <Raybestos Industrie-Produkte GmBH> for 7.5 mln dlrs.     Raybestos, with manufacturing facilities in Radevormwald, West Germany, produces friction materials for use in clutch and braking applications.  Reuter &#3;
