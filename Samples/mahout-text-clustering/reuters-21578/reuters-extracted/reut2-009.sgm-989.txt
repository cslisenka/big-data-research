RICOH REORGANIZES U.S. UNITS

Ricoh Corp, the U.S. unit of Japan-based <Ricoh Ltd>, said it merged Ricoh Systems Inc into Ricoh Corp.     The U.S. unit also purchased Ricoh Electronics Inc from the parent company.     The company said the reorganization unites the sales, research and development, and manufacturing operations of all the U.S. units.  Reuter &#3;
