DE LAURENTIIS FILM <DFP> IN FIRST PAYOUT

De Laurentiis Film Partners LP said it will pay an initial quarterly dividend of 37.3 cts per unit to holders of record on June 30, payable within 90 days of that date.     The partnership said De Laurentiis Entertainment Group Inc <DEG>, which owns one third of its shares, will defer its share of the payout to maximize funds available to the partnership for future film investments.
