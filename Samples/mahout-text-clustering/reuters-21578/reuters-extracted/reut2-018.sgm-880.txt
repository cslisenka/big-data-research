TRITON GROUP <TRRO.O> PRESIDENT RESIGNS

Triton Group Ltd said John Stiska has resigned as its president. He will be replaced by Triton Chairman Charles Scott.     Stiska will return to private law practice and will continue to serve as special counsel to Triton and to Intermark Inc <IMI>.     Intermark, which owns 41 pct of Triton's common stock, took a controlling interest in the company in March, 1986.  Reuter &#3;
