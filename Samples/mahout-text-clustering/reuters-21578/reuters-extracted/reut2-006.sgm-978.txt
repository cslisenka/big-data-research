NORTH KOREA WELCOMES CONTACT WITH U.S. DIPLOMATS

North Korea said it welcomed new contacts between Korean and U.S. Diplomats and would impose no restrictions on them, the New China News Agency reported from Pyongyang.     A North Korean Foreign Ministry statement said it hoped diplomatic contact would help "promote sincere conversations with goodwill and broad-mindedness," the agency said.     The United States on March 9 lifted its ban on its diplomats speaking with North Korean diplomats on neutral territory.  REUTER &#3;
