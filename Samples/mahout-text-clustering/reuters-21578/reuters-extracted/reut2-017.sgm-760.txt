TORONTO SUN <TSP.TO> RAISES DIVIDEND

Toronto Sun Publishing Corp said it raised its semi-annual dividend to five cts per share, pay June 15, record June nine     The company said the annual dividend rate, now ten cts per share, is an increase of 33 pct after adjusting for a two for one split of the common shares.  Reuter &#3;
