VMS SHORT TERM <VST> TO FUND LOAN INCREASE

VMS Short Term Income Trust said it agreed to commit to fund a 1.8 mln dlrs loan increase for 1420 K Street, a 12-story, 60,000 square foot office building in Washington, D.C.     The trust previously approved a 2.9 mln dlr loan for the property, making the total loan 4.7 mln dlrs.     VMS said the increased financing will have an initial term of six months and will include a one pct participation in the net appreciation of the property upon sale or refinancing.  Reuter &#3;
