TRIBUNE CO <TRB> TO SELL MEDIUM-TERM NOTES

Tribune Co said it is offering up to 100 mln dlrs of medium-term notes.     The notes will have maturities from nine months to 15 years, the company said. The terms of each deal will be set at the time of sale.     They will be offered on a continuing basis through Salomon Brothers and Merrill Lynch, the firm added.     Proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
