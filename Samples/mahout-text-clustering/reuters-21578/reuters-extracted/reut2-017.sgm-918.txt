JAPAN HIGHWAY CORP PLANS TO ISSUE ECU BOND

Japan Highway Public Corp planned to issue a seven-year government-guaranteed 120 mln European currency unit (ecu) bond with coupon at 7.375 pct late this month, a corporation spokeman told Reuters.     Issue price would be 101.125 pct to yield 7.165 pct.     Lead manager was Bank of Tokyo Capital Market Ltd, a subsidiary of the Bank of Tokyo in Britain and co-lead manager Credit Commercial de France, he added.    He declined to give other details.
