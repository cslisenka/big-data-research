MFS MANAGED MUNCIPAL BOND TRUST SETS PAYOUT

<MFS Managed Muncipal Trust Bond> said it declared a monthly payout income distribution of 5.7 cts a share compared with 5.6 cts for the previous month.     It said the distribution is payable March 20 to shareholders of record March two.  Reuter &#3;
