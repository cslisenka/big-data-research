XEROX <XRX> LAUNCHES MORE POWERFUL SOFTWARE

Xerox Corp said it is launching in May a more powerful version of its Xerox Ventura Publisher desktop computer software called Release 1.1. that offers 80 new features.     It said the new software, used for both long and short documents in IBM PCs, includes conversion for Hewlett-Packard Soft Fonts and more sophisticated graphics and page layout applications.
