HECHINGER <HECHA> BONDS UPGRADED BY S/P

Standard and Poor's Corp said it raised to A-minus from BBB-plus Hechinger Co's 19.9 mln dlrs of industrial revenue bonds.     Yesterday, S and P upgraded to BBB-plus from BBB the company's 86 mln dlrs of convertible subordinated debentures of 2009 and assigned a BBB-plus rating to Hechinger's planned 100 mln dlr issue of convertible debt due 2012.     In its release yesterday, S and P cited what it termed an outstanding record of sales and earnings growth by Hechinger.  Reuter &#3;
