MITSUBISHI RAISES TRUCK PRICES 1.1 PCT

<Mitsubishi Motor Sales of America Inc> said it is increasing suggested retail prices of its line of two- and four-wheel drive trucks by an average 1.1 pct, or an average of 74 dlrs, effective immediately.     It said price increases range from 0.6 pct, or 40 dlrs, on the Mighty Max two-wheel drive truck to 1.9 pct, or 190 dlrs, on the four-wheel-drive SPX.  Reuter &#3;
