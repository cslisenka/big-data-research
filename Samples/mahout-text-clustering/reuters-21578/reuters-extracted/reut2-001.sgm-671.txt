TRANS LUX <TLX> IN STOCK REPURCHASE

Trans Lux Corp said it will   repurchase up to 350,000 shares common and/or class B stock over an indefinite period in the open market.     It also declared a five pct stock dividend payable April nine to holders of record march 20 and a regular quarterly dividend of two cts per share, payable APril nine to holders of record March 16.
