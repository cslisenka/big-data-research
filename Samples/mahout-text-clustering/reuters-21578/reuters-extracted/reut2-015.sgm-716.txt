COLONIAL BANCGROUP <CLBGA> MAKES ACQUISITION

Colonial BancGroup said it has signed letter of intent to acquire Community Bank and Trust of Hartselle, Ala., with assets of 26 mln dlrs, for undisclosed terms, subject to approval by regulatory authorities and Community Bank shareholders.     Completion is expected within the next year, it said.  Reuter &#3;
