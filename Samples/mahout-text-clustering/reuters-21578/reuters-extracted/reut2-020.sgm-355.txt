ARISTECH <ARS> SEES 1987 PROFITS OVER 2.50/SHR

Aristech Chemical Corp, the former chemicals subsidiary of USX Corp <X>, said it expects its earnings for 1987 to exceed analysts estimates of up to 2.50 dlrs a share.     Aristech earlier reported net income of 18.7 mln dlrs or 72 cts a share compared with 14.4 mln dlrs or 56 cts a share in the year-earlier period on a pro forma basis.     Last year, Aristech earned 43.9 mln dlrs on a pro forma basis, as if the company were a stand-alone entity effective January one, 1986.  Reuter &#3;
