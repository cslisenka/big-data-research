3COM CORP <COMS> OFFERS SHARES OF COMMON

3Com Corp said it is offering 1.2 mln shares of its common stock for sale at 23.50 per share in a public offering.     It said the sale is being co-managed by Goldman, Sachs and Co and Montgomery Securities.     The company said it granted the underwriters an option to buy 180,000 shares to cover over-allotments.     Net proceeds from the sale will be added to the company's working capital to fund future growth, 3Com said.  Reuter &#3;
