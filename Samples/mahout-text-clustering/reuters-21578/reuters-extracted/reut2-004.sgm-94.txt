TOYOTA MOTOR CREDIT ISSUES 23 BILLION YEN BOND

Toyota Motor Credit Corp is issuing a 23 billion yen eurobond due April 10, 1992 paying 4-1/2 pct and priced at 101-1/2 pct, lead manager Nomura International Ltd said.     The bond is available in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in Luxembourg. Payment is April 10.     Fees comprise 1-1/4 pct selling concession and 5/8 pct for management and underwriting combined.  REUTER &#3;
