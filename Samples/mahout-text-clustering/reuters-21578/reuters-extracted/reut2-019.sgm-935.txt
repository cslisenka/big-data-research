BANK OF NEW YORK <BK> DEBT AFFIRMED BY MOODY'S

Moody's Investors Service Inc said it affirmed 1.2 billion dlrs of debt of The Bank of New York Co Inc and units.     Affirmed were the parent's A-1 senior notes and preferred stock, A-2 subordinated capital notes and prime-1 commercial paper. Also affirmed were The Bank of New York's Aa-2 long-term deposits and prime-1 short-term obligations.     Moody's cited the firm's announced 135 mln dlr addition to the loan loss reserve as a reflection of exposure to borrowers in less-developed countries. The addition adjusts the firm's financial statements to reflect economic reality, it said.  Reuter &#3;
