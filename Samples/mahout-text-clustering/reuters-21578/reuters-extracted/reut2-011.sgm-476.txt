BUTLER <BTLR> TO BUY SKYLIGHT MANUFACTURER

Butler Mfg Co said it signed a memorandum of intent to acquire Naturalite Inc, a manufacturer of skylights.     It said Garland, Texas-based Naturalite had sales in 1986 of about 20 mln dlrs.     Terms were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
