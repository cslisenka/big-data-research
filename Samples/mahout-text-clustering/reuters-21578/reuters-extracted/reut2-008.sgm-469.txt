ITT <ITT> LONG DISTANCE UNIT LOWERS WATS RATES

International Telephone and Telegraph Corp said its long distance unit lowered rates for two of its WATS services.     ITT said it lowered its SMART-WATS rate an average of 9.3 pct, and its preferred WATS rates four pct.     In addition to the rate reduction, ITT said it also lowered its monthly preferred WATS service charge for each location to 925 dlrs, a reduction of 75 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
