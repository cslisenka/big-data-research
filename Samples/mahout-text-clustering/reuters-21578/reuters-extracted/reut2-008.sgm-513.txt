MHI GROUP <MH> AFFILIATED WITH CEMETARY OFFER

MHI Group Inc said it is affiliated with several investors who have entered into a letter of intent providing for the purchase of Star of David Memorial Gardens and Cemetery.     The company said those investors will, under certain circumstances, cede their rights under the letter of intent and any definitive agreement to purchase the Fort Lauderdale, Fla., funeral home and cemetery business to MHI.
