UNISYS <UIS> GETS ORDER FROM SWEDEN

Unisys Corp said it received a 4.5 mln dlrs computer order from Rikskatteverket, the Swedish equivalent of the U.S. Internal Revenue Service.     The computer is planned for delivery in May this year and will be installed in the agency's Stockholm headquarters.  Reuter &#3;
