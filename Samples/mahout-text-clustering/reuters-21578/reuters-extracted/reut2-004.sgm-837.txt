NORSK HYDRO EURODOLLAR BOND INCREASED TO 125 MLN

The 10-year eurodollar bond issue launched yesterday for Norsk Hydro at 8-1/4 pct and 101-5/8 pct has been increased to a total of 125 mln dlrs from the original 100 mln amount, Swiss Bank Corp International said as lead.     The issue is due on April 9, 1997.  REUTER &#3;
