INTEGRATED RESOURCES SETS UP BRANCHES IN HONG KONG

U.S.-based insurance and investment broker <Integrated Resources Inc> has set up two subsidiaries in Hong Kong, a company statement said.     <Integrated Resources Insurance and Investment Services Ltd> will focus on insurance marketing and <Integrated Resources (HK) Ltd> on stock broking.     The statement gave no financial details for the local firms.  REUTER &#3;
