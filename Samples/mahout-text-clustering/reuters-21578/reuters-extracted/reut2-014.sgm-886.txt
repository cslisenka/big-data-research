JAPAN TO HOLD FORMAL FINANCIAL TALKS WITH FRANCE

The Japanese Finance Ministry said it plans to hold formal financial talks with France on April 17 in Tokyo, the second such meeting after a first session in May last year. The agenda has not yet been decided.     The Japanese side will be headed by Toyoo Gyohten, Vice Finance Minister for International Affairs, while the French delegation will include Daniel Lebegue, Director General of the Treasury.
