KAPOK CORP <KPK> IN TECHNICAL DEFAULT

Kapok Corp said it is in techical default of its loans from Southeast Banking Corp <STB> and Murray Steinfeld but is negotiating with the lenders.     It said neither has declared the loans due.     The company said it has agreed to sell the Peter Pan Restaurant in Urbana, Md., for 1,100,000 dlrs, or one mln dlrs after the payment of expenses.  Reuter &#3;
