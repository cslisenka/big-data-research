MR. JAX FASHIONS IN PRIVATE PLACEMENT

<Mr. Jax Fashions Inc> said it agreed to sell 600,000 common shares to Pemberton Houston Willoughby Bell Gouinlock Inc and Dominion Securities Inc, which the buyers will offer at 13-5/8 dlrs per share to institutional investors.     Closing is expected on April 30, subject to completion of necessary regulatory filings. Mr. Jax said proceeds will be used for internal growth and potential acquisitions.  Reuter &#3;
