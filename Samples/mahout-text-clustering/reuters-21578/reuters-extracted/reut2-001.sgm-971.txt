MITSUI AND CO TO ISSUE EURODOLLAR WARRANT BONDS

Mitsui and Co Ltd said it plans to issue 300 mln Eurodollar warrant bonds in two tranches with payment on March 30 through a syndicate led by Nomura International Ltd.     One tranche is of five-year, 150 mln dlr bonds while the rest is of seven-year, 150 mln dlr bonds, but other issue terms have not yet been decided.  REUTER &#3;
