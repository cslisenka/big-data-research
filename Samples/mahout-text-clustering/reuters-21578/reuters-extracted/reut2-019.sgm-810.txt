GETTY RESOURCES (GEY.TO) COMPLETES FINANCING

Getty Resources Ltd said it completed its previously announced sale of 9.8 mln dlrs worth of subordinated notes convertible into 930,000 common shares of the company at 10.50 dlrs each.     Getty said it plans to file a prospectus with the Ontario Securities Commission and will be able to force conversion of the notes into common shares.     Getty said it plans to use the money for Canadian mineral projects it is currently exploring.  Reuter &#3;
