SPRINT II ISSUES 35 MLN DLR REPACKAGED FLOATER

Sprint II, a special purpose corporation based in the Cayman Islands, is issuing 35 mln dlrs of floating rate bonds due November 6, 1992 and priced at 100.10, said Fuji International finance Ltd as lead manager.     The bonds are backed by a pool of 48.7 mln dlrs of Japanese ex-warrant bonds. The bonds are priced to yield 20 basis points over the six month London Interbank Offered Rate. They are available in denominations of 100,000 dlrs and are payable on November 6. There is a seven basis point management and underwriting fee, an eight basis point selling concession and a two basis point praecipuum.  REUTER &#3;
