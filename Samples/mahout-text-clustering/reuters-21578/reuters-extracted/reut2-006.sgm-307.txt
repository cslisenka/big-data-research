PUERTO RICAN COMPANY GETS JET FUEL CONTRACT

The Puerto Rico Sun Oil Co is being awarded a 42.4 mln dlr contract for jet fuel, the Defense Department said.     It said work on the contract, which was awarded as part of a multi-contract procurement program, is expected to be completed by April 1988.  Reuter &#3;
