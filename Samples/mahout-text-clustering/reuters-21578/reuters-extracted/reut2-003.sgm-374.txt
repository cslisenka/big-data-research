TEXAS AIR <TEX> UNIT TO EXPAND SERVICE

Texas Air Corp's Continental Airlines said it will begin service in April to Bakersfield, Calif., and Eugene and Medford, Ore., from its hub at Denver.     From Denver, Continental will fly a daily nonstop and a one-stop flight to each of the three cities, the company said. Service to the Oregon cities will start April 5 and to Bakersfield, April 22.
