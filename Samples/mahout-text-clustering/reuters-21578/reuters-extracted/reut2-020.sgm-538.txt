NORTHROP <NOC> BACKLOG UP SLIGHTLY

Northrop Corp said its backlog at September 30 stood at 4.37 billion dlrs, up slightly from the 4.32 billion recorded a yeaer earlier.     Northrop also reported a third quarter profit of 34.1 mln dlrs, or 73 cts per share, compared with a year-earlier loss of 30.5 mln dlrs, or 65 cts per share.     The company said its operating profit for the quarter totaled 100.8 mln dlrs, compared with a 17 mln dlr operating loss a year ago.     Sales for the period climbed to 1.46 billion dlrs from 1.26 billion last year, Northrop said.  Reuter &#3;
