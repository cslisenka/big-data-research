METALLGESELLSCHAFT PLANS COMMODITY FUTURES VENTURE

Metallgesellschaft AG <METG.F> said it is founding a company to work in commodities futures and options trading and financial services linked to these.     The new company, to be called <MG Commodity Corp>, will be based in Zug, Switzerland, and operate from a branch in Hamburg.     Metallgesellschaft will hold 80 pct of the company, and the other 20 pct will be held by Dieter Worms.  REUTER &#3;
