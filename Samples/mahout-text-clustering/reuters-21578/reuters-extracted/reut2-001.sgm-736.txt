TEXAS INSTRUMENTS <TXN> EXECUTIVE TO RETIRE

Texas Instruments Incorp said its executive vice president Grant Dove will take an early retirement effective July.     The company said Dove will become chairman and chief executive officer of <Microelectronics and Computer Technology Corp> of Austin. He will have worked 28 years with Texas Instruments, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
