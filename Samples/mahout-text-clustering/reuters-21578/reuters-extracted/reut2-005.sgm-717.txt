NEW ENGLAND POWER TO REDEEM PREFERRED SHARES

New England Power Co said it will redeem all 250,000 shares of its Preferred Stock cumulative, 100 dlr par value, 13.48 pct series in two stages.     First on July 1, it will redeem 225,000 shares at 110.86 dlrs per share.     Then, on September 1, it will redeem the remaining 25,000 shares pursuant to the sinking fund provisions at 100.75 dlrs plus accrued and unpaid dividends of 2.25 dlrs per share, it said.
