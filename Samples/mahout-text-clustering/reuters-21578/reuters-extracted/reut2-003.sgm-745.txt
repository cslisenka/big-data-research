<CATO CORP> FILES FOR INITIAL OFFERING

Cato Corp said it has filed for an initial public offering of about three mln common shares.     Cato said the offering, to be made in late April or early May, is expected to raise 35 to 45 mln dlrs.     Cato, which had been publicly held several years ago before going private, operates a chain of women's specialty stores in small to medium-sized towns.  Reuter &#3;
