SESOSTRIS SELLS 18,000 TONNES BARLEY TO GREECE

Sesostris, the Spanish subsidiary of the international grain trader Dreyfus, sold 18,000 tonnes of barley to Greece for delivery from Mediterranean ports from April 14 to 30, a company spokesman said. He gave no details on price.     The barley has a 14 pct humidity.  Reuter &#3;
