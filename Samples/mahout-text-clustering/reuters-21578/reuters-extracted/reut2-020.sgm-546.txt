REDKEN LABORATORIES <RDKN.O> TO BUY BACK STOCK

Redken Laboratories Inc said its board authorized management to repurchase up to 600,000 of the company's common shares.     The repurchase program may be implimented over the next 18 months, or sooner, depending on whether or not the shares are bought in the open market or in privately negotiated transactions, Redken said.     The program will be funded with cash on hand and future cash flow, the company also said.  Reuter &#3;
