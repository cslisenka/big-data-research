SURVIVAL TECHNOLOGY <SURV> GETS CONTRACT

Survival Technology Inc said it has received a U.S. Defense Department contract with a ceiling price of 14.4 mln dlrs for the produyction of ComboPen automatic injectors and Mark I Antidote Kits for the self-administration of antidotes to chemical warfare under combat conditions.     It said deliveries are expected to start this month and be completed by August.  Reuter &#3;
