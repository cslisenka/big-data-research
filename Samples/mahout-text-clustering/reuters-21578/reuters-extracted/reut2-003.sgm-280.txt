TCN COMPLETES INSTALLATION OF SWITCHING SYSTEM

<TCN Inc>, a long distance phone service, said it has completed installing it state-of-the-art switching system.     TCN also said it began building a fiber optic transmission network to initially link San Antonio, Austin, Dallas and Houston, Texas.     TCN said the service will expand during the second quarter 1987 to include service to California and Florida.  Reuter &#3;
