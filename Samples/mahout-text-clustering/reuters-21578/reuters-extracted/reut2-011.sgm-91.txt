MARRIOTT <MHS> FRANCHISEES FORM MANAGEMENT CO

The 102 owners of Straw Hat Pizza Restaurant franchises said in a joint statement that they are forming a management corporation to assume complete control of their own operations.     The group said terms of an arrangement with the francisor, Marriott Corp, call for Marriott to pay the new cooperative  corporation 3.5 mln dlrs to fund initial operations.  Reuter &#3;
