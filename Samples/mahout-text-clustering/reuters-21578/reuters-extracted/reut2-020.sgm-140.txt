GREENSPAN SAYS FED READY TO PROVIDE LIQUIDITY

The U.S. Federal Reserve is ready to provide liquidity to support the economy and the financial system, Fed Chairman Alan Greenspan said in a statement.     The brief statement by Greenspan issued by the Fed said, "the Federal Reserve, consistent with its responsibilities as the nation's central bank, affirmed today its readiness to serve as a source of liquidity to support the economic and financial system."
