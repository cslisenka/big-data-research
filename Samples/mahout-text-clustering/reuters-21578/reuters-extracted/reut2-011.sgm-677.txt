U.S. EXTENDS HIDE EXPORT PERIOD FOR YUGOSLAVIA

The Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) has extended the export period for Yugoslavia to ship U.S. hides and skins under a line of credit from the Export Credit Guarantee Program, the U.S. Agriculture Department said.     The shipping period has been extended from September 30, 1987 to December 31, 1987, the U.S. Agriculture Department said.  Reuter &#3;
