SHARP CORP <SHRP.T> TO SET UP PLANT IN THAILAND

Sharp Corp will set up a 100 pct-owned subsidiary in Thailand to make microwave ovens and refrigerators for export to the U.S. And western Europe, a company spokesman said.     The company, to be named <Sharp Appliances (Thailand) Ltd>, will be capitalised at 2.1 billion yen and start operations in October, he told Reuters.     Sharp hopes the subsidiary, which will use parts from Japan and southeast Asia, will eventually produce other electric appliances, he said.  REUTER &#3;
