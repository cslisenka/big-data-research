WALGREEN <WAG> SEES MARCH SALES UP 11.2 PCT

Walgreen Co said preliminary figures show that its sales for March will be up 11.2 pct over March 1986, when it had 313.4 mln dlrs in sales.     Final March retail figures are expected Thursday April 9.     It also said it expects a strong Easter selling season.  Reuter &#3;
