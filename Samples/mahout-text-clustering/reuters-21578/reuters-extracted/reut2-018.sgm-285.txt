SWEET VICTORY <SVICC.O> CONTINUES ON NASDAQ

Sweet Victory Inc said its common stock will continue to be listed in Nasdaq via an exception from the capital and surplus requirement.     The company said while it failed to meet this requirement as of April 6, 1987, it was granted a temporary exception subject to it meeting certain conditions.  Reuter &#3;
