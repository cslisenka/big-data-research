ANHEUSER-BUSCH <BUD> TO BUILD PLANT

Anheuser-Busch Cos' Metal Container Corp said it plans to build a beverage can manufacturing plant at Chester, N.Y.     The facility is expected to become operational in late 1988.     Metal Container operates six can and lid plants in the U.S., and plans to build three more, including the Chester facility. Facilities are under construction at Windsor, Colo., and Riverside, Calif.     The Anheuser-Busch unit said it expects to produce six billion cans and eight billion lids in 1987.  Reuter &#3;
