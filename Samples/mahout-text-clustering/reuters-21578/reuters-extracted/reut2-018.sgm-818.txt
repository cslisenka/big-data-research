BANKAMERICA'S <BAC> COOPER JOINS ISFA

ISFA Corp said Thomas A. Cooper, who recently resigned as president of BankAmerica Corp, has been named chairman and chief executive officer of ISFA.     ISFA operates Invest, a full-service brokerage program for banks, and Insure, a retail insurance program.  Reuter &#3;
