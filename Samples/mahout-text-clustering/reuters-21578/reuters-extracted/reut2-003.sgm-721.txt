BUSINESSLAND <BUSL> COMPLETES OFFERING

Businessland Inc said it sold 50 mln dlrs of 5-1/2 pct convertible subordinated debentures due 2007 in a registered public offering.     The company said each debenture is convertible into shares of Businessland common stock at a conversion price of 20.50 dlrs.     Net proceeds will be used to repay amounts outstanding under the company's bank credit line and for working capital, the company also said.  Reuter &#3;
