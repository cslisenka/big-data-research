THERMO ELECTRON <TMO> UNIT GETS TURBINE ORDERS

Thermo Electron Corp said its British subsidiary, Peter Brotherhood Ltd, received five mln dlrs in order for steam turbine and turbine generator systems during the first quarter of fiscal 1987.     It said Peter Brotherhood's current backlog is 20 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
