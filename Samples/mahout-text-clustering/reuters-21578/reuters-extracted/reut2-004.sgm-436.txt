PARKER DRILLING <PKD> SUSPENDS PAYOUT

Parker Drilling Co said its board of directors voted to suspend the payment of dividends to shareholders.     Parker Drilling said it has been paying a quarterly dividend of one ct a share of common stock.     The company said it expects to save 1.3 mln dlrs a year through the dividend suspension.
