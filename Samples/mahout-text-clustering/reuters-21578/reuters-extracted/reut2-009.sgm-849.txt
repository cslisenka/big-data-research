BP <BP> TO HOLD NEW YORK PRESS CONFERENCE

British Petroleum Co PLC said it has scheduled a New York press conference for 1300 EST/1800 gmt today at which senior management will discuss the company's proposed acquisition of the 45 pct of Standard Oil Co <SRD> that it does not already own for 70 dlrs per share.     The offer is worth about 7.4 billion dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
