DAHLBERG <DAHL.O> COMPLETES SEARS<S> AGREEMENT

Dahlberg Inc said it completed a previously announced agreement with Sears, Roebuck and Co to operate hearing aid centers in sears stores on a national basis.     Dahlberg currently operates some 142 hearing aid centers on a concession basis in Sears stores.     In connection with the program, the company said it granted Sears an option to buy up to 300,000 shares of Dahlberg stock at 12 dlrs a share.  Reuter &#3;
