SUN <SUN> IN NORTH DAKOTA OIL FIND

Sun Co Inc said the O.M. Steel Federal Number One well in Williams County, N.D., flowed 660 barrels of oil and 581,000 cubic feet of natural gas per day through a 13/64 inch choke from depths of 13,188 to 13,204 feet.     Sun said it has a 50 pct interest and Comdisco Resources Inc the remainder.  An offset well is under consideration, it said.  Reuter &#3;
