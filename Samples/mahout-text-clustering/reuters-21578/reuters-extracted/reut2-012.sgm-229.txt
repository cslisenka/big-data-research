NORTH CENTRAL LABS <NCLB> DISCUSSES AIDS TESTS

North Central Laboratories Inc said it has had preliminary discussions with MikroMed Inc about providing laboratory testing services to screen the presence of the AIDS virus.     It said MikroMed is developing a kit which it would distribute to its customers and which would be used by its customers to draw samples of their own blood. These samples would be mailed to a laboratory facility for AIDS screening.  Reuter &#3;
