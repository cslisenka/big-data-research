HAWKEYE <HWKB> HOLDERS APPROVE DEBT PLAN

Hawkeye Bancorporation said shareholders at the annual meeting approved the bank holding company's debt restructuring package with its principal creditors.     The plan calls for the company to issue new class of preference stock to Hawkeye's principal creditors in exchange for 32.3 mln dlrs of debt, and an exchange offer to preferred holders.
