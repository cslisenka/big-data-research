C.O.M.B. <COQ> TO HAVE OPTIONS ON AMEX

C.O.M.B Inc commenced trading put and call options on the American Stock Exchange, according to the Exchange.     The company's options will trade with initial expiration months of April, May, July and October, with position and exercise limits at 5,500 contracts on the same side of the market, according to the Exchange.
