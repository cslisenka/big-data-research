MEDICAL RESEARCH FUND SAYS ASSETS ROSE

Medical Research Investment Fund Inc said net assets as of February 28 were 2,359,722 dlrs, up 189.5 pct from a year earlier.     It said net asset value per share rose to 14.64 dlrs from 12.14 dlrs, based on 161,187 shares outstanding compared with 67,136 shares.                  Reuter &#3;
