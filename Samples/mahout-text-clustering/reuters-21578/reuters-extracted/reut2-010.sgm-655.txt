NEW ZEALAND TREASURY BILL TENDER CANCELLED

New Zealand's Reserve Bank said it has cancelled the regular weekly Treasury bill tender scheduled for tomorrow.     The bank said in a statement that it forecasts a small net cash injection into the system over the settlement week.     However, it said it will conduct open market operations during the week. After these the cash balance should fluctuate around 30 mln N.Z. Dlrs.  REUTER &#3;
