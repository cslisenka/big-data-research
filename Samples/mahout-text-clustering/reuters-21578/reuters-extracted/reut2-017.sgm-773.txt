FIRST PHILADELPHIA UNIT FILES FOR CHAPTER 11

<First Philadelphia Corp> said its Computone Systems Inc unit filed a chapter 11 plan for with the U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Atlanta.     Subject to confirmation by the court of the plan, the assets of Computone's two operating divisions would be transferred to CPX Inc, a wholly-owned subsidiary of First Philadelphia, the company said.     First Philadelphia said it is in the process of raising 1,750,000 dlrs to complement it initial investment of 750,000 dlrs to fund the cost of the reorganization and provide ongoing capital for existing Computone businesses.
