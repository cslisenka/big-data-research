MARS STORES <MXXX> FEBRUARY SALES OFF 1.6 PCT

Mars Stores Inc said sales for the four weeks ended February 28 were off 1.6 pct to 6,307,096 dlrs from 6,411,103 dlrs a year earlier.     The company said sales were hurt this year by store closings caused by snowstorms.  Reuter &#3;
