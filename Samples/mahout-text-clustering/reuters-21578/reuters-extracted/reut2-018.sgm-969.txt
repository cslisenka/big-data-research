NYSE TO DISCLOSE IMBALANCES TWICE TOMORROW

The New York Stock Exchange said in connection with the triple expirations of stock index futures, options and individual options it will disclose imbalances in 50 major stocks tomorrow at 0900 EDT and again at 1530 EDT.     The information will be disclosed via financial news services.  Reuter &#3;
