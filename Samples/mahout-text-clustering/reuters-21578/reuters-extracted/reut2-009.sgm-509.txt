OWENS-CORNING FIBERGLAS <OCF> SELLS FOAM UNIT

Owens-Corning Fiberglas Corp said it sold its controlling interest in its French foam insulation producing subsidiary to a Lafarege Corp <LAF> subsidiary.     Owens-Corning said it sold its interest in Sentuc Porxpan SA for an undisclosed price.  Reuter &#3;
