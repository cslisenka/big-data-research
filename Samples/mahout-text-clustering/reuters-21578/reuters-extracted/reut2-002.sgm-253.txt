SHOE-TOWN <SHU> FEBRUARY SALES UP 21.8 PCT

Shoe-Town Inc said <shu> it had record February sales of 10,052,000 up 21.8 pct from 8,252,000 dlrs in the year earlier months.     The company said sales for the eight weeks ended February 28 were 19.5 mln dlrs up 19.9 pct 14.1 mln dlrs a year earlier.  Reuter &#3;
