PLAYBOY <PLA> GETS LICENSING PACT IN INDIA

Playboy Enterprises Inc said its Playboy Licensing unit has entered into an agreement with Fashionport Private Ltd of India to market Playboy and Playmate fashions and accessories in India, the Middle East and several Eastern bloc countries.     Fashionport will produce men's and women's apparel, leather garments, footwear, small leather goods and accessories, it said.     Fashionport is based in Bombay, India. No other details were available.  Reuter &#3;
