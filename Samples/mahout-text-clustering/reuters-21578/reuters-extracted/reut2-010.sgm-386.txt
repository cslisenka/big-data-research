MACMILLAN BLOEDEL <MMB> STOCK SPLIT APPROVED

MacMillan Bloedel Ltd said shareholders authorized a previously announced three-for-one stock split, applicable to holders of record April nine.     The company said its stock will begin trading on a split basis on April 3, subject to regulatory approvals.  Reuter &#3;
