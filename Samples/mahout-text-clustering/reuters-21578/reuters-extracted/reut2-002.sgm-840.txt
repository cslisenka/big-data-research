REXON <TEXN> FILES FOR STOCK SALE

Rexon Inc said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission covering the primary sale of 1.25 mln common shares through an underwriting group managed by Hambrecht and Quist.     Rexon, a maker of tape drives, subsystems and computer-related products, said it plans to use proceeds from the sale for working capital and for capital expenditures, primarily related to the company's tape drive products.
