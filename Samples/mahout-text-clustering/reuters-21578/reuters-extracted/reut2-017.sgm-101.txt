EXXON <XON> RAISES HEATING OIL PRICE, TRADERS SAID

Oil traders in the New York area said Exxon Corp's Exxon U.S.A. subsidiary raised the price it charges contract barge customers for heating oil in the New York harbor by 0.50 cent a gallon, effective today.     They said the 0.50 cent a gallon increase brings Exxon's contract barge price to 49.25 cts a gallon.  Reuter &#3;
