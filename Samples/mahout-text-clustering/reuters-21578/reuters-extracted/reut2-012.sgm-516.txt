ELECTROMEDICS <ELMD> TO TAKE 1ST QTR CHARGE

Electromedics Inc said it expects to take a 150,000 dlr charge against first quarter results due to the conversion of debentures.     The company said, however, it expects "positive" operating comparisons for the period.  Electromedics lost 6,000 dlrs in last year's first quarter.  Reuter &#3;
