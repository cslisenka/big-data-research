SIMMONS <SIMM> FEBRUARY LOAD FACTOR DECLINES

Simmons Airlines Inc said its load factor for February declines to 41.8 pct from 46.1 pct a year ago.     Traffic increased 31.3 pct to 15 mln revenue passenger miles from 11.4 mln, while capacity increased 45.1 pct to 35.8 mln available seat miles from 24.7 mln.  Reuter &#3;
