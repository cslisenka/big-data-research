FIRST CAPITAL <FCH> ENTERS JOINT VENTURE

First Capital Holdings Corp said it and Robert M. Bass Group formed a joint venture, called FCB Partners, to acquire and manage real estate investments.     The joint venture partners also intend to create new products which will be offered through its Pilgrim Group Inc mutual funds subsidiary, First Capital said.  Reuter &#3;
