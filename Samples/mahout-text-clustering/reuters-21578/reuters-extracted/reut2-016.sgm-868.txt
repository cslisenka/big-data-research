JAMES RIVER <JR> TO REBUILD PAPER MACHINE

James River Corp said it will rebuild its Number Three paper machine at the Wauna pulp and paper mill near Clatskanie, Ore., at a cost of about 31.8 mln dlrs to significantly improve newsprint quality and expand its uncoated groundwood specialties paper product line.     It said completion is expected in late 1988.  Reuter &#3;
