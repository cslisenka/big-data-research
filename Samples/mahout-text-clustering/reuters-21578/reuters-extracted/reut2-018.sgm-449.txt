TOYOTA LIKELY TO ISSUE 800 MLN DLR WARRANT BOND

Toyota Motor Corp <TOYO.T> is likely to raise 800 mln dlrs through a Eurodollar bond with warrants in mid-July, underwriting sources said.     A final official decision on the issue has yet to be made and detailed terms will be announced later, they added.     The funds are likely to be used for investments to increase productivity both domestically and abroad, they said.  REUTER &#3;
