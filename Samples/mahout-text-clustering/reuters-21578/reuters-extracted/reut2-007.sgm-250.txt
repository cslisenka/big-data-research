GERMAN COMPANY INSOLVENCIES DOWN IN JANUARY

West German corporate insolvencies totalled 1,099 in January, 0.7 pct less than in the same month of 1986, the Federal Statistics Office said.     The number of insolvencies fell by 3.6 pct in the building industry and by four pct in manufacturing industry but rose by 1.8 pct in the services sector.     Overall insolvencies, including those of private individuals, totalled 1,522 in January, 1.4 pct down on the same month of last year.  REUTER &#3;
