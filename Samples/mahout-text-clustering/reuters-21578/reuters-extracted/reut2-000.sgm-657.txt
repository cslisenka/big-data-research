AMERICAN NURSERY <ANSY> MAKES ACQUISITION

American Nursery Products Inc said it has purchased Heini's Nursery Inc of Miami, which grows indoor foliage plants for wholesale distribution and had sales of 4,472,0000 dlrs for the year ended August 31.     Terms were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
