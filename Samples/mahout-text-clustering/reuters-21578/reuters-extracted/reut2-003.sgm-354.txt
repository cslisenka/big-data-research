SYSTEMHOUSE <SHKIF> WINS DATA CENTER CONTRACT

Systemhouse Inc said it won a contract valued at 2.6 mln dlrs from Genstar Stone Products, a Hunt Valley, Md., supplier of building materials, to design, install and operate Genstar's data center in Baltimore.     The data center is scheduled to begin operating in May. The pact is a three-year deal, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
