PAYLESS CASHWAYS <PCI> SETS SHARE BUYBACK PLAN

Payless Cashways Inc said it will repurchase up to one mln of its common shares from time to time in the open market.     The company, which has 34.7 mln shares outstanding, called the stock buyback a good investment at current prices. Its stock closed at 12-1/2, down 1/8, following a 3-3/8 point drop on Monday.
