KNOWLEDGE DATA SYSTEMS <KDSI> NAMES CHAIRMAN

Knowledge Data Systems Inc said it named John Kerr chairman of the company, and named its former chairman, Jon Jacoby, vice chairman.     The company said Kerr was formerly president and co-founder of <Mediflex Systems Corp>, and Jacoby is also its chief financial officer.  Reuter &#3;
