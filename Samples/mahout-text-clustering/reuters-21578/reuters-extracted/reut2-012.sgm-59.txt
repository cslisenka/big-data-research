UNITED CITIES <UCIT> PRIVATELY PLACES BONDS

United Cities Gas Co said it placed privately on March 18 20 mln dlrs of 8.69 pct first mortgage bonds.     Proceeds will be used to retire short-term debt and fund the company's current construction program, United Cities said.     The company said more than 30 lending institutions participated in the bidding for the bonds and that 25 pct of the issue was placed with the U.S. unit of a Canadian firm.  Reuter &#3;
