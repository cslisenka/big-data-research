BALTIMORE GAS AND ELECTRIC <BGE> FILES DEBT

Baltimore Gas and Electric Co said it filed a registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission to sell 100 mln dlrs of unsecured debt.     The debt filing was registered under the SEC's shelf procedure which gives the utility up to two years to sell its debt.     Once the registration becomes effective Baltimore Gas and Electric will be able to issue debt on short notice as market or corporate conditions warrant.      Reuter &#3;
