COMMONWEALTH REALTY <CRTYZ> IN REFINANCING DEAL

Commonwealth Realty Trust said it has transferred its ownership of six buildings at the Valley Forge Executive Mall in Valley Forge, Pa., to an equally-owned joint venture with Pennsylvania Public School Employees' Retirement System as part of a refinancing.     The trust said it received five mln dlrs for its half interest in the buildings.     It said it also received a 32 to 33 mln dlr eight-year 9.375 pct mortgage loan on the buildings, with the entire principal payable at the end of the term.  After associated costs, it said it realized net proceeds of about 20 mln dlrs in cash on the transactions, 100 mln dlrs of which it will use to repay debt under its credit lines.  Reuter &#3;
