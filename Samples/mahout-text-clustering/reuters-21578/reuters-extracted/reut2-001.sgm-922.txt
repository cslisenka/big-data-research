GUNZE SANGYO SETS 25 MLN SWISS FRANC NOTES ISSUE

Gunze Sangyo Inc of Tokyo is launching a 25 mln Swiss franc convertible notes issue due May 31, 1992 with a coupon indication of 1-5/8 pct, lead manager Swiss Volksbank said.     Conditions will be set on March 11 and payment date is March 30.  REUTER &#3;
