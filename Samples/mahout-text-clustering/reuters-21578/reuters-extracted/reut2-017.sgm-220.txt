FIRE DAMAGES PAKISTAN SUGAR STOCK

A fire damaged a large stock of imported sugar stored in a customs warehouse at Karachi on Saturday night, customs officials said.     They said the warehouse contained about 15,000 tonnes of sugar in 150,000 bags but they did not know how much had been destroyed or damaged. They said the cause of the fire was unknown.
