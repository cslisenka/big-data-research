HERITAGE <HHH> SAYS MOST WARRANTS EXERCISED

Heritage Entertainment Inc said 419,867 of its 745,976 warrants were exercised at the reduced price of 3.75 dlrs a share under an offer that expired March 12.     The company said the exercise price will now be 4.50 dlrs a share though the end of 1987 and five dlrs for 1988.  Reuter &#3;
