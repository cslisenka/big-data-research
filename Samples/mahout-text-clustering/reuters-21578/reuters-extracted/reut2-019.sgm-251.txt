GREAT WESTERN <GWF> UNIT SELLS 10-YEAR NOTES

Great Western Bank, a unit of Great Western Financial Corp, is offering 200 mln dlrs of notes due 1997 yielding 9.60 pct, said lead manager Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.     The notes have a 9-1/2 pct coupon and were priced 99.365 to yield 130 basis points more than comparable Treasury securities.     Non-callable to maturity, the issue is rated A-2 by Moody's and A by Standard and Poor's. The gross spread is 6.50 dlrs, the selling concession is four dlrs and the reallowance is 2.50 dlrs. Shearson Lehman co-managed the deal.  Reuter &#3;
