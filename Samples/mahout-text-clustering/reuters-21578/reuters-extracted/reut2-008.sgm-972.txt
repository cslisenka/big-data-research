LEECO DIAGNOSTICS <LECO> AWARDED FEDERAL GRANT

Leeco Diagnostics Inc said the National Institutes of Health have awarded it a two-year, 478,568 dlr federal grant to develop a new medical test kit to diagnose and monitor diabetes mellitus.     Leeco said the Institutes made the grant under their small business innovation research program.     The company said the research, beginning April 1, will focus on developing a more effective technique for detecting hemoglobin A1C in the blood. High levels of hemoglobin A1C can indicate uncontrolled diabetes.  Reuter &#3;
