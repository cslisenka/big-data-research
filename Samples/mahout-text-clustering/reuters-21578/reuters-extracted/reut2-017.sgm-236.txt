BANGLADESH PAYMENTS DEFICIT NARROWS IN NOVEMBER

Bangladesh's overall balance of payments deficit narrowed to 3.03 mln U.S. Dlrs in November from 8.3 mln in October and 22.63 mln in November 1985, Central Bank officials said.     The current account deficit increased to 28.68 mln dlrs in November against 10.69 mln in October and 55.19 mln in November 1985.     The trade deficit rose to 88 mln dlrs from 36.36 mln in October and 86.2 mln in November 1985.  REUTER &#3;
