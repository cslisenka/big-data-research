OXFORD FINANCIAL BUYS CLANCY SYSTEMS

<Oxford Financial Inc> said it acquired <Clancy Systems International Inc> for an undisclosed sum.     The company said Clancy has developed a fully automated parking citation system, currently in use in Oklahoma City, the University of California at Sacramento, and in a pilot program in San Francisco.
