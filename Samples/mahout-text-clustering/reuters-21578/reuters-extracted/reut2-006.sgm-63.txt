BRUNSWICK <BC> FILES FOR 300 MLN DLR SHELF

Brunswick Corp said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission on a shelf offering of 300 mln dlr in senior debt securities.     It said proceeds from the sale will be used mainly for to replace variable rate indebtedness, primarily privately placed commercial paper, incurred in connection with the acquisitions of Bayliner marine Corp and Ray Industries Inc.
