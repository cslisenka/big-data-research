EXERCISE PRICE SET ON NOKIA EQUITY WARRANT BOND

The exercise price for the warrants attached to the 100 mln dlr eurobond for Finland's Nokia Oy <NOKS.HE> has been fixed, lead manager Morgan Guaranty Ltd said.     The five year, par-priced, deal was launched last Thursday and pays five pct. Each 5,000 dlr bond has 134 warrants attached which are each exercisable into one Nokia free preferred share at a price of 169.4 Finnish markka. This compares with today's closing price of 178 markka.  REUTER &#3;
