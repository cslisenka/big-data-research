RIEDEL ENVIRONMENT <RETI> UNIT GETS EPA CONTRACT

Riedel Environment Technologies Inc's subsidiary Riedel Environmental Services Inc <RES> received a contract to clean up and remove hazardous substances from contaminated sites in 23 states west of the Mississippi River.     The contract, which could total more than 100 mln dlrs over the next four years, is with the Federal Environmental Protection Agency, the company said.     Among the states covered in the contract are Alaska and Hawaii, as well as the Pacific Trust Territories, Riedel said.  Reuter &#3;
