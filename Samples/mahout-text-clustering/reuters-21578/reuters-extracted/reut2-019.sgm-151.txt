TOTAL ERICKSON <TLE.T> BUYS MINING PROPERTY

Total Erickson Resources Ltd and Consolidated Silver Standard Mines Ltd <CDS.V> said that Total Erickson has purchased all Consolidated's interests in its Dome Mountain property for 60,000 Total Erickson shares and 70,000 dlrs in cash.     The companies said the property has several gold-bearing veins and has considerable exploration potential.  Reuter &#3;
