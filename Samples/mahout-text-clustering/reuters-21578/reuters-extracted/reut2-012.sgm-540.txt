SWISSAIR REVENUE FALLS IN FEBRUARY

Swissair <SWSZ.Z> said that its traffic  volume rose six pct in February compared with a year earlier but revenues were down seven pct because of currency movements.     Cargo traffic rose 11 pct and passenger traffic was up six pct compared with February 1986.     The overall load factor slipped to 60 pct from 61 pct and the seat load factor fell to 56 pct from 58 pct in February 1986.     Costs before depreciation were eight pct lower.  REUTER &#3;
