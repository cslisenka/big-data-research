TOYOTA AND NISSAN EXPORTS FALL IN MAY

Toyota Motor Corp <TOYO.T> and Nissan Motor Co Ltd <NSAN.T> both said May vehicle exports fell from a year earlier mainly due to the yen rise against the dollar.     Toyota's exports fell 9.5 pct from a year earlier to 161,266 vehicles in May while Nissan's fell 17.6 pct to 100,221.     Toyota's exports to the U.S. Fell 13.9 pct from a year earlier to 90,096 in May and those to Europe fell 2.8 pct from a year earlier to 34,876.     Nissan's exports to the U.S. Fell 25.4 pct to 53,604 but those to Europe rose 0.7 pct to 29,721.  REUTER &#3;
