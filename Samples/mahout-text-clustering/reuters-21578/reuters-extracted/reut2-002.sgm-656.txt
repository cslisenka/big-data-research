AMERICAN WEST <AWAL> HAS LOWER LOAD FACTOR

America West Airlines said its February load factor slipped to 57.9 pct from 58.1 pct a year earlier and the year-to-date load factor was up slightly at 55.5 pct compared with 55.4 pct a year earlier.     February revenue passenger miles rose to 367.6 mln from 209.1 mln and year-to-date revenue miles were up to 730.3 mln from last's year 421.9 mln.     Available seat miles for the month increased to 634.4 mln from 360.1 mln a year earlier and for the two-month period available miles totaled 1.32 billion, up from the 761.7 mln reported for the comparable period in 1986, America West said.
